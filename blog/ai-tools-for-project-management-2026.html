Pick your board first

The core choice is still simplicity vs structure. Trello gets a team productive on day one with visual boards and AI summaries. Asana and ClickUp add dependencies, timelines, and goals for multi-team programs. Notion AI is the wildcard — docs-first teams often consolidate planning and wikis into one workspace.

Close the meeting loop

The biggest PM time-sink is the meeting tax. An AI notetaker like Fireflies captures decisions and action items, then pushes them into your board automatically — turning "we should" into a tracked task without manual entry. That single integration returns more hours than any board feature.

The bottom line

Start with the board your team will actually open, then add an AI notetaker to feed it. The stack that ships is the one with the least manual status-updating — let the AI write the updates.
