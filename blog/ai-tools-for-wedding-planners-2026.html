How we picked

Every tool on this list is used by working wedding planners managing 20+ events a year. We scored on time saved per wedding, client-facing polish, and price-to-value. We cut anything that added more back-and-forth than it removed.

The verdict

Start with Canva for design and Tally or JotForm for intake and RSVPs. Add Notion for run-of-show templates and HubSpot for the client pipeline. The whole stack stays under $100/month and frees you to take on more weddings.
