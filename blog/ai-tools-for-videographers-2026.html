How we picked

We tested each tool with 10 professional videographers who shoot weddings, corporate, and commercial content. Scoring criteria: (1) hours saved per project, (2) output quality vs manual editing, (3) learning curve, and (4) ROI relative to subscription cost. Tools that produced AI artifacts or required constant manual correction were eliminated immediately.
