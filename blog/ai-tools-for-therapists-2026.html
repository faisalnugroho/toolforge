HIPAA compliance checklist

Before using any AI tool with client data:

  1. Get a BAA. A Business Associate Agreement is legally required. No BAA = don't use it with PHI.
  2. Informed consent. Clients must know AI is being used and consent to it.
  3. De-identify when possible. Remove names, DOBs, and identifiers before using AI tools without a BAA.
  4. Review everything. AI-generated notes are drafts. You are the clinician of record. Always review and edit.
  5. Check your licensing board. Some states have specific guidance on AI use in therapy.
