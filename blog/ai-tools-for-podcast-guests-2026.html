How we picked

We used these to go from 'want to be on more shows' to a booked calendar and a clip per episode. Only tools we'd actually pay for again made the cut — no filler, no 'AI-powered' logos without substance.

When to use which

Use ChatGPT + Perplexity to find and prep for shows. During recording, run Otter for a transcript. After, Descript + Opus Clip turn 45 minutes into 5 shareable clips. HeyGen and ElevenLabs extend reach into other languages and formats. Notion AI keeps the pipeline organized.

Bottom line

You don't need a booking agent to become a repeat guest — you need a repeatable system. These 9 tools take you from pitch to promo clip in an afternoon, and most have free tiers to start today.
