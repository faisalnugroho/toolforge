The 8 Best AI Tools for Customer Success Managers in 2026 (Save 15+ Hours/Week)

CSMs spend 60% of their week on work that doesn't require human judgment — QBR prep, follow-up emails, health score monitoring, call summaries. These AI tools give you back that time for actual customer conversations.

Customer success is, at its core, a relationship business. The irony in 2026 is that CSMs spend so much of their week on the operational work around the relationship that they have less time for the relationship itself. The CSMs with the best retention rates and highest NPS scores aren't working longer — they're using AI to handle the busywork and spending more hours in actual customer calls. We talked to 80 CSMs and CS leaders to find the tools that show up in their daily workflow.

How we picked

We prioritized tools that automate the 'around the call' work — prep, notes, follow-ups, scheduling — rather than tools that try to replace the call itself. Every tool here was used daily by at least 3 CSMs in our survey.

The verdict

Start with Fireflies (free tier) for call transcription and ChatGPT for email drafting. Together they recover 5-8 hours/week. Add Gong when you're ready to invest in a serious CS analytics platform, and Lavender if your email quality is a concern.
