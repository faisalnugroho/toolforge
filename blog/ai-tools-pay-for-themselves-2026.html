The math behind the ROI

Loaded hourly cost for knowledge workers: $50-100/hour. AI tools that save 2+ hours per week hit ROI in week 1. Tools that save 30+ minutes per week hit ROI in month 1. Tools that save less than 30 minutes/week never hit ROI — even at $5/mo. The tools above all save 2+ hours per week for their target users. That's why they make the list.

How to measure your own ROI

Before buying any AI tool, ask: 'What task will I do with this that I currently spend 2+ hours on?' If you can name a specific task, the tool will pay for itself. If you can't, you don't need the tool. The 8 tools above all have clear use cases. Buy one, use it for 7 days, measure time saved. If positive, keep. If not, cancel.
