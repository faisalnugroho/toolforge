How we picked

We ran a 4-week webinar series using this exact stack and measured seat fill, no-show follow-up, and replay watch time. Tools were kept only if they saved at least an hour per event or measurably lifted attendance or pipeline.

The verdict

Keep it simple: Zoom to host, Descript to edit the replay, and Zapier to fire follow-up emails from HubSpot. Add Runway and Pictory once you're repurposing into social clips. HeyGen is a luxury for avatar-led promo.
