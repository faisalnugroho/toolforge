Recipe development without the guesswork

ChatGPT and Claude are now solid at scaling a 4-cover recipe to 200 covers, converting units, and flagging allergens. The trick: feed it your existing recipe and ask for a scaled, costed version with a substitution plan for out-of-stock items.

Inventory and food cost

Pair a voice tool like Otter.ai to capture what your sous-chef shouts during a rush, then drop it into a spreadsheet AI. Margin leaks show up fast when the data is clean.

Training the line

NotebookLM turns your standard recipes and HACCP docs into a podcast your team can listen to on the commute — surprisingly effective for new hires.
