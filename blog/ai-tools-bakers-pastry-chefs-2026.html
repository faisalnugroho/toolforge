How we picked

Every tool below is actively used by at least 3 bakery owners doing $25K+/month. We tested each for 3 weeks, scored on labor hours saved, output quality, ease of use, and price. We removed anything that needed a 'tech person' to set up. The list skews toward small/independent bakeries — chains and commissaries will need different tools.

Start with **ChatGPT Plus** for recipe work and **Square AI Insights** for the bake-vs-skip decision. Add **Midjourney** for wedding cake pitches and **Photoroom** for social content. Frequently asked questions

Do I really need AI tools if I'm a solo bakers & pastry chef?

No. AI tools are force multipliers, not magic. If you're doing 5 hours of work a week, AI won't help much. If you're doing 50+ hours and your bottleneck is post-production/marketing/admin (not the actual bakers & pastry chefs work), AI can cut that by 30-50%.

What's the cheapest way to try this stack?

Start with just ChatGPT Plus ($20/mo) and Photoroom (free tier). Those two alone will save 5+ hours/week. Add other tools only when you hit specific bottlenecks.

Will clients notice I use AI?

Most clients can't tell the difference between AI-assisted and fully human work — what they notice is faster turnaround, more revision options, and lower prices. Be transparent if asked; most clients are impressed, not disappointed.
