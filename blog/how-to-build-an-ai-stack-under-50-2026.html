The $50 build

Start with ChatGPT Plus at $20 — it covers writing, coding help, and image generation. Add Suno at $10 for any music or jingles. That’s $30. The rest — Claude, Notion, Grammarly, QuillBot, Otter, Canva — all have free tiers solid enough for daily solo use. Total paid: $30/month, comfortably under $50, with room to swap Suno for another $10 tool if you don’t need music.

Where to spend the last $20

If your work is video, trade Suno for CapCut Pro or HeyGen. If it’s research, add Perplexity Pro. The principle: pick ONE paid generalist (ChatGPT or Claude) and ONE paid specialist that matches your bottleneck. Everything else rides free tiers until a limit actually bites.

The verdict

A $30-50/mo stack now does what a $5,000/mo agency retainer did in 2020. Spend on one generalist + one specialist, keep the rest free, and upgrade only when a free limit blocks real work.
