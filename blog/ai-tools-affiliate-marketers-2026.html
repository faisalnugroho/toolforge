Why these tools

1. Jasper — $49/mo

AI marketing writer with templates for product reviews, comparison posts, email sequences, and ad copy. Built for affiliate workflows. Best long-form AI writer for affiliate content. Trained specifically on marketing copy, with Brand Voice to match your site tone.

2. Surfer SEO — $89/mo

AI-powered SEO content optimization. Tells you exactly what to add to rank for a target keyword. Pairs perfectly with Jasper. The only SEO tool that gives you a real-time content score as you write. Top affiliates use it to hit page 1 in 30-60 days consistently.

3. AdCreative.ai — $29/mo

Generates hundreds of ad creative variations in minutes. Trains on your highest-performing ads to keep improving. Replaces a $2K/mo designer. Generates 100+ Meta/TikTok ad variations trained on your best performers. CTR typically jumps 30-50%.

4. Anyword — $24/mo

AI copywriting with predictive performance scores. Knows which version of your ad will convert before you spend a dollar. The 'predictive score' is genuinely useful — it tells you which headline will perform best before you run the ad. Cuts testing costs by 50%.

5. Frase — $15/mo

AI for SEO research and content briefs. Generates a full content outline (with H2/H3s, keywords, FAQ schema) in 60 seconds. The fastest way to go from keyword to ranked article. Top affiliates use it to brief writers (or AI) and ship 30+ posts/month.

6. Midjourney — $10/mo

Best AI image generation. Affiliate marketers use it for hero images, product mockups, and social cards. Replaces stock photo subscriptions. Generate unlimited product mockups, lifestyle images, and social cards that don't look like AI.

7. ElevenLabs — $5/mo

AI voice for VSLs (video sales letters), YouTube reviews, and podcast ads. Most affiliates run 50+ voice variations per VSL. VSLs convert 3-5x better than text, and ElevenLabs lets you produce them at scale. $5/mo vs $500 for a voice actor.

8. Instantly — $30/mo

AI-powered cold email for affiliate outreach. Partner with SaaS companies, get review placements, and negotiate commission bumps. Top affiliates use cold email to land exclusive deals and JV partnerships. Instantly's AI sequences book 3-5 meetings/week on autopilot.

The bottom line

You don't need all of these — start with the one that addresses your biggest bottleneck, measure the impact, and add the next. AI tools compound when they connect to each other (e.g., transcription feeds your docs, your docs feed your chatbot, your chatbot deflects tickets), so plan for a stack, not isolated purchases.
