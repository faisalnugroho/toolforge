How we picked

Every tool on this list is currently used by at least 5 paralegals at firms billing $400+/hour. We tested each for 6+ weeks on real matters, scored on accuracy of legal citations, time saved per task, and ABA Model Rule compliance. We removed anything that couldn't verify citations (a hard requirement — hallucinated cases get paralegals fired and attorneys sanctioned). The legal-specific AIs (Casetext, Harvey, Lexis) come first — they understand privilege, work product, and citation format. The general AIs (ChatGPT, Perplexity) are for non-billable admin work.

The verdict

If you're at a firm with a Westlaw or Lexis subscription, you already have access to Casetext CoCounsel or Lexis Protégé — start there. If your firm doesn't have an enterprise AI tool, push for one (most offer free trials). For non-billable admin — emails, calendar, formatting — ChatGPT Enterprise with privacy mode enabled is the right tool. The biggest ROI in 2026 is document review — paralegals using AI contract review report cutting doc review from 8 hours to 90 minutes. That's billable time you can reallocate to higher-value work.
