What teachers actually need

Top teacher needs: (1) Lesson plans aligned to standards, (2) Differentiated materials for different reading levels, (3) Quick assessment creation, (4) Parent communication, (5) Administrative documentation. The tools above address each. MagicSchool is the only one built specifically for teachers. The rest are general tools that work exceptionally well for education.

How to start

Start with MagicSchool AI — it's free and built for teachers. Use it for lesson plans and IEPs. Add ChatGPT free for parent emails. Add Canva AI for visuals. The rest is nice-to-have. Don't use AI to grade essays — that's a student trust issue. Use it to draft feedback, then edit. The right line is: AI does the heavy lifting, you do the human part.
