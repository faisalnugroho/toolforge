How to combine them

Most data engineers run Cursor or Copilot for authoring, Snowflake Cortex or BigQuery ML for in-warehouse generation, dbt + AI for transformation, and Monte Carlo for monitoring. Keep model inference inside your warehouse wherever possible — it avoids egress costs and keeps governance tight.

The verdict

Start with Cursor + your warehouse's native AI (Cortex or BigQuery ML) + Monte Carlo. That trio alone cuts pipeline build time roughly in half. Add dbt AI and Hex as your team scales.
