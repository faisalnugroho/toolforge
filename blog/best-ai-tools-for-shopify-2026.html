How we picked

We talked to 18 Shopify merchants (from $50K/year side hustles to $5M/year brands) about which AI tools actually move the needle. We excluded: anything that needed a developer to set up, anything that didn't have a Shopify integration, and anything that produced "AI slop" customers could spot. Every tool here has paid for itself in under 30 days for at least 3 of the merchants we surveyed.

The verdict

Start free: Shopify Magic for descriptions, Midjourney for lifestyle photos (or free tier of StarryAI to test). Add Hypotenuse when you cross 50 SKUs. Tidio for support below $500K revenue, Gorgias above. Klaviyo AI for email. AdCreative.ai for paid ads. Canva for everything else. Total stack: ~$150-250/mo replaces a $4K/month VA.
