Tier 1 — pays for itself in a day

ChatGPT ($20) and Claude Pro ($20) are the anchors: they touch every task, so the payback is immediate. ElevenLabs ($5) replaces per-narrator voiceover fees the first time you use it. These are non-negotiable.

Tier 2 — pays for itself in a week

Midjourney ($10) kills stock-image budgets. Descript (free tier) removes hour-long editing grunt work. HeyGen ($24) and Synthesia ($22) replace studio shoots for routine video. Jasper ($39) earns its keep only if you ship high volumes of marketing copy.

The verdict

Spend on Tier 1 first — that’s $45/mo for two generalists and voiceover. Add Tier 2 tools only when a specific workflow justifies them. Nothing here breaks $50, and the right combination returns its cost many times over.
