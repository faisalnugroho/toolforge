How we picked

We surveyed 80+ tattoo artists across the US, UK, and Australia — solo artists, 2-3 person shops, and 10+ artist studios. Tools were scored on (1) time saved per design, (2) output quality that clients actually accept, and (3) commercial licensing safety. We cut anything that produced generic AI-looking tattoos clients turned down.

Start with Midjourney ($10/mo) for design mockups and Square Appointments ($29/mo) for booking. Add Recraft ($12/mo) if you need vector stencils. The full design + booking stack runs under $60/mo — about 1 hour of chair time — and easily pays for itself the first week.
