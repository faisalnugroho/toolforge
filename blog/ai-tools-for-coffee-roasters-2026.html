Why coffee-roasters need AI in 2026

Consistency

Roast-to-roast variation is the #1 quality killer. AI profiles hold your curve within 2F of target -- the difference between great and inconsistent.

Time savings

Drafting cupping notes takes 20-30 minutes per coffee. AI does it in 30 seconds with 80% accuracy. You review and edit.

Better sourcing

AI sourcing tools match green coffee lots to your flavor goals. Stop buying samples that don't fit your program.

Scale

When you go from 200 lbs/week to 2,000 lbs/week, AI consistency is the only way to keep quality up without hiring.

How to choose

Start with Artisan AI (free tier) for roast logging and RoastPath (free) for benchmarking. If you're a commercial roaster doing 500+ lbs/week, Cropster AI is the standard. Add SweetBloom for cupping notes and OriginTrip when you're sourcing new lots.

The verdict

AI won't roast your coffee for you -- but it'll hold your curve within 2F, draft your cupping notes in 30 seconds, and help you source the right green. Start free, scale to Cropster when volume justifies it. The $99/mo pays for itself in reduced waste alone.
