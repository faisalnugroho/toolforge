How we picked

Tools were tested across corn/soy, wheat, cotton, vegetables, livestock, and specialty crop operations. We prioritized tools that work without perfect cell service, that integrate with the equipment farmers already own, and that don't lock data behind enterprise contracts. Free and cheap options were given bonus weight.

The verdict

ChatGPT at $20/mo is the highest-ROI tool for any farmer — agronomy Q&A, market analysis, equipment troubleshooting, all in your pocket. Microsoft Copilot ($30/mo) wins if you're already an Excel power user for farm financials. Granola ($14/mo) is the sleeper hit — voice notes in the cab beat typing on a phone with gloves on.
