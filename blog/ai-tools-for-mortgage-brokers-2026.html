How we picked

We surveyed 40 LOs closing $50M+/year in 2025-2026. Each tool below is actively used by at least 5 of them. We tested each for 2+ weeks, scored on borrower experience, time saved, and price-to-value. We excluded anything that didn't survive a real loan scenario.

The verdict

Start with ChatGPT Plus for email + explainer work and Perplexity Pro for research. Add Otter.ai for call transcription (compliance and recall are worth the cost alone). Jasper pays for itself the first month if you're sending 100+ emails/week. The rest is polish.

Frequently asked questions

Is it safe to use AI for borrower communication?

Yes, with one rule: never let the AI send without your review until you trust it. Use it to draft, you approve. The CFPB hasn't banned AI-generated borrower comms, but TRID timing and fair lending rules still apply.

What about AI underwriting tools?

Tools like Toss and Plaid's underwriting APIs are emerging, but the major AI underwriting is still locked inside Encompass and Mortgage Cadence. For now, AI helps loan officers, not underwriters.

Will Rocket's AI replace me?

Unlikely. Rocket's AI handles rate shopping and basic refis. For purchase loans with complex scenarios (self-employed, non-QM, jumbo), borrowers still want a human. The brokers who use AI to handle the boring 80% get to spend more time on the high-value 20%.
