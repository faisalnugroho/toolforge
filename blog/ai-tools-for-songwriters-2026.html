From hum to demo in an afternoon

Hum a melody into Suno or Udio and get a full arrangement back. Use it as a sketch — steal the chord change you like, throw the rest away. ChatGPT and Claude help when the second verse won't come. When the bones are right, ElevenLabs sings a guide vocal so you can pitch the demo without booking a studio.

Keep the copyright clean

Read each platform's terms before you release — training and ownership differ. The safe play: use AI for inspiration and demos, record the final with humans, and you keep full rights to a song that's actually yours.
