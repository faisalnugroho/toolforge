How we picked

We surveyed 52 executive assistants across finance, tech, healthcare, and law firms. Every tool on this list was actively used by at least 5 of them. We removed anything that put sensitive executive communications at risk, anything that required sending data to a third-party LLM without enterprise SSO, and anything that took more than 30 min to learn.

The verdict

Start with Superhuman for inbox, Motion for calendar, and Granola for meetings. Add ChatGPT + Claude for drafting and summarization. Make handles the rest. The EAs who adopt this stack consistently say they reclaim 8-12 hours per week — and most use it to take on a second executive.

Discretion matters

One rule the experienced EAs agreed on: never paste raw confidential content (board decks, M&A docs, personnel reviews) into a consumer AI tool. Use enterprise-tier ChatGPT Enterprise or Claude for Work — they offer zero-retention, SSO, and audit logs. Several EAs we spoke with said their company requires this.
