How designers are using AI in 2026

The biggest shift: client presentations have gone from 2D mood boards to photorealistic 3D renders. Designers using Midjourney or Interior AI can show clients exactly what their space will look like — before buying a single piece of furniture. This closes deals faster and reduces revision cycles from weeks to hours.

Virtual staging (uploading an empty room photo and furnishing it with AI) has become standard in real estate. Tools like Interior AI and Remini do in seconds what used to cost $200+ per room from a staging company.

The verdict

Start with Midjourney ($10/mo) for concept generation. Add Interior AI ($29/mo) for virtual staging and room redesigns. Use Canva (free) for mood boards and client presentations. That's a complete interior design AI stack for under $50/month.
