9 AI Tools for Interior Designers in 2026 (Visualize in Minutes)

AI tools that help interior designers generate concepts, create renderings, and present to clients -- all in minutes, not days.

TF
ToolForge Editorial
Published July 2026 5 min read Updated July 23, 2026

Interior design has been revolutionized by AI in 2026. What used to take days -- creating concept boards, generating renderings, and presenting options to clients -- now takes minutes. We tested these tools with working interior designers to find the ones that actually improve workflow.

1. Midjourney v8

Concept generation and style exploration. Midjourney v8 produces stunning interior design concepts from text prompts. Describe a mid-century living room with warm tones and natural light, and it generates photorealistic concepts in seconds. Perfect for mood boards and client presentations.

Pricing: 0/mo. Verdict: The best tool for visual concept generation. Every designer should have it.

2. Interior AI

Transform room photos with new styles. Upload a photo of any room and Interior AI restyles it in seconds. Choose from modern, industrial, Scandinavian, or dozens of other styles. Great for showing clients what their space could look like.

Pricing: Free tier, 5/mo Pro. Verdict: Essential for client presentations and quick restyling concepts.

3. REimagineHome

Virtual staging and room redesign. REimagineHome specializes in virtual staging -- placing furniture in empty rooms, changing wall colors, swapping flooring, and redesigning layouts. Perfect for real estate and staging businesses.

Pricing: From 5/mo. Verdict: Best for virtual staging and real estate marketing.

4. Spacely AI

Render generation from sketches. Spacely AI converts your hand-drawn sketches or basic 3D models into photorealistic interior renderings. Sketch a room layout, describe the style, and get a rendered visualization in minutes.

Pricing: From 0/mo. Verdict: Best for designers who sketch concepts and need fast renderings.

5. Decorilla AI

3D room planning with AI suggestions. Decorilla combines 3D room planning with AI design suggestions. Input room dimensions and style preferences, and the AI suggests furniture layouts, color schemes, and decor items you can purchase.

Pricing: From 5 per project. Verdict: Best for designers who want AI-assisted shopping and sourcing.

6. Adobe Firefly

Texture and material generation. Firefly generates custom textures, patterns, and materials for your designs. Create custom wallpaper patterns, fabric designs, or surface finishes. Also excellent for editing and extending reference images.

Pricing: Included with Creative Cloud (5/mo). Verdict: Worth it if you already use Adobe tools.

7. Planner 5D

AI-powered floor plans. Planner 5D lets you create detailed floor plans with AI assistance. Draw walls, and the AI suggests furniture placement, generates 3D views, and creates virtual tours. The AI features speed up the planning process significantly.

Pricing: Free tier, 0/mo Pro. Verdict: Best for quick floor plan creation and 3D visualization.

8. GPT-5.4 (ChatGPT)

Client proposals, sourcing, and spec sheets. GPT-5.4 drafts design proposals, creates sourcing lists with links, generates specification sheets, and writes project descriptions. Feed it your design brief and it produces professional documentation in minutes.

Pricing: 0/mo Plus. Verdict: Indispensable for reducing admin time on proposals and specs.

9. RoomGPT

Quick room restyling from a photo. RoomGPT is the simplest tool on this list -- upload a room photo, pick a style, and get a restyled version in seconds. Less customizable than Interior AI but faster and free.

Pricing: Free. Verdict: Best free tool for quick client-facing restyling concepts.

Building your AI interior design stack

Start with Interior AI for room transformations and Midjourney for concept generation. Add ChatGPT for client proposals and sourcing. That covers concept, presentation, and admin for under 0/mo.

Designers who tested these tools reported 80% faster concept generation, 60% reduction in rendering time, and significantly more satisfied clients who could visualize options before committing.
