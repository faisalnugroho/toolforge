How we picked

Every tool was actively used by licensed architects shipping real projects. We tested each for 4+ weeks on actual client work, scored on output quality, integration with Revit/SketchUp/Rhino, control over results, and price-to-value. Tools that generated 'AI-looking' renders or couldn't handle technical constraints were cut.

The verdict

Start with Veras or PromeAI for rendering from your existing models. Use Midjourney for mood boards and competition imagery. Bring in Spatia for generative design inside your BIM tool. Maket for early-stage floor plan exploration. The rest is supplementary.
