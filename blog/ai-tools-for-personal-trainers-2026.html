Why online trainers are uniquely suited to AI

Personal training has a brutal time-vs-money tradeoff. Each new client adds 5-10 hours/week of work: program design, check-ins, form review, nutrition planning, accountability messages. AI breaks this curve by handling the repetitive 60% (program templates, macro math, weekly check-in summaries) so trainers can focus on the high-value 40% (coaching, motivation, form correction). The math: a trainer charging $300/client/month who uses AI can serve 3x more clients with the same time investment, multiplying revenue without burning out.
