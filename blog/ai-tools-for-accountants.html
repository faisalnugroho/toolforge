Kill the data entry

PDF AI and similar extraction tools turn bank statements, invoices, and 1099s into structured data you can paste into a ledger — no manual keying. Zapier strings the whole flow together: new document in the inbox, extracted, logged, and a summary dropped in Slack. That’s the single biggest time saver for a small practice.

Research and review

Perplexity and scite keep your authorities honest — critical when a position hinges on the exact wording of a standard. Claude’s long context is perfect for reviewing a full return or a multi-year statement package. Motion keeps the deadline treadmill manageable during close.

The verdict

A one-person firm can run this stack for roughly $90/month and handle 2-3x the clients. Start with PDF extraction + Zapier, then layer research tools. The human still owns the sign-off — AI just removes the typing.
