How we picked

Every tool below is actively used by at least 3 florists running a working shop. We tested each for 2+ weeks, scored on time saved, output quality, ease of use, and price. We prioritized tools that pay for themselves in <30 days. We removed anything that needed a design background to operate or had a steep learning curve.

The verdict

Start with ChatGPT Plus for wedding mood boards and Midjourney for design mockups. Add OptimoRoute for delivery routing — this is the single biggest time-saver during Valentine's and Mother's Day. The rest is nice-to-have as your shop scales. Most florists on this list spend <$80/mo total on AI tools and recover 6-10 hours/week.
