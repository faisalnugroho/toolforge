How to learn AI (the right way)

Don't try to learn all 1,000 AI tools. Don't read every AI blog. Don't watch every YouTube tutorial. Instead: pick ONE tool, use it for 30 days, daily. Once you feel you've gotten 80% of its value, add the next one. This 'depth before breadth' approach is what separates AI-productive people from AI-overwhelmed people. The 7 tools above in that order = a 6-month curriculum that actually builds real skills.

When you're ready to level up

After you've used the free tools above for 30 days each, you'll know what you actually need. Most people will find they want: ChatGPT Plus ($20) for daily use, Claude Pro ($20) for serious writing, and 1-2 specialized tools for their work. Don't buy anything until you've used the free version for 30 days. The hype is real, but the value comes from daily use, not from having more subscriptions.
