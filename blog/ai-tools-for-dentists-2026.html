Why dental practices are uniquely suited to AI

Dentistry is one of the most AI-disruptable professions in healthcare. The work is highly visual (x-rays, intraoral photos, 3D scans), heavily document-driven (SOAP notes, insurance codes, treatment plans), and repetitive (insurance verification, recall reminders, post-op instructions). The 200+ dental AI tools on the market in 2026 fall into 5 categories: clinical decision support, clinical documentation, patient communication, imaging/diagnostics, and practice operations.
