How we picked

We surveyed 40 practicing veterinarians (general practice, ER, specialty) and credentialed vet techs across 12 states. Tools were scored on HIPAA-equivalent client confidentiality, time saved per day, accuracy on clinical questions, and — most importantly — whether they help or hurt the doctor-patient relationship. We excluded anything that "hallucinated" dosages or couldn't cite sources. Every tool on this list has a clear use case that doesn't replace veterinary judgment.

The verdict

Start with ChatGPT Plus for the universal second opinion and SOAP drafting. Add Perplexity Pro for research with citations (critical when you need to defend a treatment plan). The biggest single time-saver is Scribe — most vets save 1-2 hours/day of charting immediately. Notion AI handles the templates and client handouts. Fireflies transcribes staff meetings and CE lectures. Grammarly polishes the client-facing writing. Descript is for the practice that wants to build a YouTube/social presence. Elicit is for the vet doing clinical research or staying current on the literature.
