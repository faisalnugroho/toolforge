How we picked

We interviewed 30 licensed bail agents across 12 counties (CA, TX, FL, NY, GA, IL, OH, PA, NC, MI, NJ, AZ). Tools were scored on (1) reduction in skip rate, (2) increase in bond volume capacity, (3) time saved on intake/admin, and (4) reduction in forfeiture losses. We excluded tools that required technical setup only a CTO could deploy.

What we left out (and why)

We skipped generic CRMs and any tool that did not have bail-specific features. We also skipped any "AI" tool that was just a ChatGPT wrapper without skip tracing data. The bail industry is too regulated for toy tools.

The verdict

Start with BailTec as your bail-specific CRM -- it pays for itself in the first avoided forfeiture. Add PRYME for skip tracing. Layer in Otter for call compliance. Build the after-hours intake with a ChatGPT-powered chatbot only if you write 100+ bonds a year -- below that, an answering service is cheaper.
