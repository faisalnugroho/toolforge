Why these tools

1. Figma AI — $15/mo

AI features inside Figma — auto-layout suggestions, content generation, and first-draft wireframes from prompts. If you're already in Figma, the built-in AI tools have crossed the quality bar. Best for teams that want AI without leaving their existing workflow.

2. Uizard — $19/mo

Generate mockups from text descriptions or screenshots. Great for non-designers and rapid prototyping. Best-in-class text-to-mockup. Type 'a SaaS dashboard for a fitness app with dark mode' and get 4 variants in 30 seconds.

3. Maze — $99/mo

AI-powered user research platform. Generate tests, run them with real users, and get sentiment analysis on open feedback. Replaces UserTesting for fast, affordable research. AI summarizes 1,000+ open responses into themes in minutes.

4. Galileo AI — $39/mo

Generate UI from text prompts, train it on your design system, and export directly to Figma. The highest-quality text-to-UI generator. Trained on real Dribbble-level designs, not generic Material UI templates.

5. Krea — $24/mo

AI image and video generation with real-time canvas. Great for hero images, illustrations, and design mood boards. Best-in-class real-time generation — paint a rough sketch and see the AI fill it in live. Massive time saver for mood boards.

6. Notion AI — $10/user/mo

AI for research docs, user interview notes, PRDs, and design specs. Stays in your team workspace. The 'glue' AI — turns 8 hours of meeting notes into a 1-page design brief. Every UX team needs this for research synthesis.

7. Midjourney — $10/mo

Best-in-class AI image generation for hero illustrations, concept art, and visual design exploration. Still the highest aesthetic quality for design exploration. Use it for mood boards, hero illustrations, and concept variations.

8. Khroma — Free

AI color palette generator trained on thousands of popular palettes. Discover combinations you'd never think of. Free, fast, and the best tool for breaking out of your color comfort zone. Train it on palettes you like and it generates infinite variations.

9. Relume — $32/mo

AI sitemap and wireframe generator. Describe your product and get a complete IA + low-fi wireframes in seconds. The fastest way to start a project. Generate a 50-page sitemap and wireframes from a 1-paragraph brief — clients love the speed.

The bottom line

You don't need all of these — start with the one that addresses your biggest bottleneck, measure the impact, and add the next. AI tools compound when they connect to each other (e.g., transcription feeds your docs, your docs feed your chatbot, your chatbot deflects tickets), so plan for a stack, not isolated purchases.
