AI Tools for Physical Therapists: Save 10+ Hours/Week in 2026

Documentation and home-program handouts eat your week. These AI tools give it back. Free and paid options reviewed for physical therapists.

TF
ToolForge Editorial
AI Tool Researchers

Documentation is the silent tax on physical therapy. Between SOAP notes, home-exercise programs, and patient education, the laptop steals time from hands-on care. In 2026, AI tools can claw back 10+ hours a week. We tested dozens and kept the 6 that PTs actually use.

1. ChatGPT — Home-exercise plans and handouts

ChatGPT drafts progressive home-exercise programs and plain-language handouts you can paste into your EMR. The $20/mo Plus plan handles nuanced rehab instructions.

Use case: Use case: "Build a 4-week knee-replacement home program with sets, reps, and precautions."

2. Otter.ai — Session notes by voice

Otter transcribes your verbal session notes in real time, so you dictate instead of typing. The free tier gives 300 minutes/month.

Use case: Use case: "Dictate a SOAP note right after a session and copy it into the chart."

3. Canva — Exercise infographics

Canva's Magic Write turns rehab concepts into shareable infographics and printouts for your clinic wall. Free for most therapists.

Use case: Use case: "Design a poster on proper squat form for the gym area."

4. Descript — Exercise demo videos

Descript lets you trim and caption demo videos by editing text, then publish to your patient portal. Free tier included.

Use case: Use case: "Caption a glute-activation demo and embed it in the home program."

5. Notion AI — Protocol and resource library

Notion AI organizes your rehab protocols, reference sheets, and onboarding docs into one searchable workspace. The $10/mo add-on turns Notion into a clinical assistant.

Use case: Use case: "Summarize the latest tendinopathy research into a one-page protocol."

6. Fireflies — Team and case-conferencing notes

Fireflies auto-transcribes team huddles and complex case reviews so nothing is lost. The free tier covers most clinics.

Use case: Use case: "Record a multidisciplinary case discussion and get action items."

The 3-tool starter stack (free)

If you are just starting, grab these three free tools:

  • ChatGPT Free — home-program drafts
  • Otter.ai Free — voice session notes
  • Canva Free — exercise infographics

That is $0/month and saves 5+ hours/week immediately.

Upgrade when you have budget

When the ROI is clear (or you are ready to buy back your time), add:

  • ChatGPT Plus ($20/mo) — nuanced rehab copy
  • Notion AI ($10/mo) — searchable protocol library
  • Fireflies Pro ($10/mo) — unlimited meeting notes
