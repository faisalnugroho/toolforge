The 12 Best Free AI Tools for Podcasters (2026)

Starting a podcast on $0? These 12 free AI tools handle recording, editing, transcription, and clips — no budget required.

TF
ToolForge Editorial
✍️ Published June 2026⏱️ 4 min read🔄 Updated June 16, 2026

You don't need a $400 DAW and an editor to launch a podcast in 2026. A stack of free AI tools now handles recording, cleaning audio, transcribing, and clipping — good enough to sound pro from episode one. Here are the 12 we'd use on a zero budget.

Record + clean for free

Riverside and Cleanfeed capture studio audio in the browser. Adobe Podcast's free enhancer removes echo and noise from any recording.

Edit by transcript

Descript's free tier lets you cut audio by deleting words. No timeline wrangling required.

Transcribe + repurpose

Otter.ai and Whisper give you free transcripts you can turn into show notes and blog posts. Opus Clip and Captions make shareable vertical clips.

Don't overthink it

Grab two or three of these, hit record, and ship. The best podcast tool is the one you actually use.
