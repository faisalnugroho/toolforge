Compliance reminders

  • AI output is not tax advice. Always review AI-generated research against primary sources (IRC, Treasury Regs, Rev. Procs.) before client-facing use.
  • PTIN and EAs must sign returns. AI cannot legally prepare or sign returns without credentialed human review. Use AI as drafting assist, not replacement.
  • Client confidentiality. Use enterprise/business-tier AI plans with DPAs. Never paste full SSNs or sensitive client data into consumer ChatGPT.
  • Document your AI use. IRS Circular 230 §10.35 requires competence and diligence. Maintain logs of AI assistance for complex positions.
