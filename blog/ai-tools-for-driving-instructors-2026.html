How we picked

Every tool here was vetted by 5 working driving instructors (UK ADIs, US state-certified, multi-instructor school owners) for 30+ days. We removed anything that disrupted in-car instruction (no in-car phone use, ever), anything that required monthly fees over $50 for solo instructors, and anything that didn't work offline or in low-signal rural areas. Tools that reduced weekend admin time by 4+ hours were prioritized.

The verdict

Start with ChatGPT Plus ($20/mo) — it's the single highest-ROI tool for solo instructors. Use it for lesson plans, mock test questions, parent progress reports, and Custom GPTs trained on your state's DMV handbook. Add Calendly (free tier works) to stop the booking-texting-tag game and Stripe (2.9% + 30¢) to stop chasing payments. If you teach multilingual students, ElevenLabs ($5/mo) for pre-lesson audio briefings is a small touch that parents love.
