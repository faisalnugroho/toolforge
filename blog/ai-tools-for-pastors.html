AI Tools for Pastors: Save 10+ Hours/Week in 2026

Sermon prep, graphics, and comms eat your week. These AI tools give it back. Free and paid options reviewed for pastors.

TF
ToolForge Editorial
AI Tool Researchers

Pastors wear the preacher, admin, and communicator hats at once. In 2026, AI tools can return 10+ hours a week to people, not screens. We tested the stack and kept the 6 that help without replacing the heart of the work.

1. ChatGPT — Sermon prep and outlines

ChatGPT drafts sermon outlines, illustrations, and small-group questions from a passage. The $20/mo Plus plan handles nuanced theological tone.

Use case: Use case: "Outline a 30-minute sermon on Psalm 23 with 3 points and a story."

2. Canva — Sermon graphics and social

Canva's Magic Write produces sermon-series art, announcement graphics, and verse cards fast. Free for most churches.

Use case: Use case: "Design a 4-week sermon-series social set."

3. Descript — Sermon clips and captions

Descript edits sermon video by transcript and auto-captions clips for online audiences. Free tier included.

Use case: Use case: "Cut a 60-second sermon highlight with captions."

4. ElevenLabs — Voiceovers for announcements

ElevenLabs generates warm voiceovers for video announcements without a narrator. Free tier gives 10 minutes/month.

Use case: Use case: "Voice a 30-second weekly announcement clip."

5. Notion AI — Planning and notes

Notion AI organizes sermon archive, volunteer schedules, and meeting notes. The $10/mo add-on auto-summarizes.

Use case: Use case: "Turn a staff meeting into action items."

6. Beehiiv — Church newsletter

Beehiiv's AI helps you publish a weekly newsletter that keeps the congregation connected. Free to start.

Use case: Use case: "Draft a Monday devotion and announcement email."

The 3-tool starter stack (free)

If you are just starting, grab these three free tools:

  • ChatGPT Free — sermon outlines
  • Canva Free — graphics
  • Beehiiv Free — newsletter

That is $0/month and saves 5+ hours/week immediately.

Upgrade when you have budget

When the ROI is clear (or you are ready to buy back your time), add:

  • ChatGPT Plus ($20/mo) — sermon prep
  • Notion AI ($10/mo) — planning hub
  • Descript Pro — sermon clips
