What actually helped

Indyx and Save Your Wardrobe win on closet organization — they make you wear what you already own, which is the cheapest style upgrade there is. Wishi adds a human-in-the-loop stylist for specific events. Style DNA’s color analysis is a fun, genuinely useful starting point for building a capsule.

Choosing your stylist

Maximize your closet: Indyx or Save Your Wardrobe. Event help: Wishi. Learn your palette: Style DNA. Shop smarter: The Yes or GetWair.

Reality check

None of these replace a great thrift run or a tailor. But for $0–$10 they remove the "I have nothing to wear" paralysis better than any mirror pep-talk.

FAQ

Are AI stylist apps free?

Most have free tiers (Wishi, Indyx, Save Your Wardrobe, GetWair, The Yes). Style DNA charges about $10 for deep color analysis.

Do they need a photo of my closet?

Yes — you upload or screenshot items and the app catalogs them. The better the catalog, the better the outfit picks.

Can an AI stylist shop for me?

Apps like The Yes and GetWair surface shoppable items matched to your taste and closet gaps, but you approve every purchase.
