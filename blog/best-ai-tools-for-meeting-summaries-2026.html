The standouts

Fireflies is the most universal — it works on virtually any call and pushes notes to your CRM. Fathom is shockingly good for free and lives inside Zoom. Jamie is the pick for privacy purists: no bot joins, it listens locally. Read.ai adds sentiment, which managers love.

How to choose

Most platforms: Fireflies. Zoom-only & free: Fathom. Privacy: Jamie. CRM-driven sales: Read.ai or tl;dv. Content/clips: Grain.

One caveat

Always tell participants a notetaker is present where local law requires it, and review auto-drafted follow-ups before they go out.

FAQ

Are AI meeting notetakers free?

Several have free tiers — Fathom, Jamie, Tactiq, tl;dv, and Read.ai all offer real value at $0. Fireflies and Grain charge for higher volume.

Do they join the call as a bot?

Most do, but Jamie listens locally without a bot, and some (Otter, Tactiq) can run as a sidecar. Pick based on your privacy needs.

Which is most accurate?

Accuracy is now high across the board. Fireflies and Otter lead on multilingual transcription; Fathom and Jamie lead on summary usefulness.
