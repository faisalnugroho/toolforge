The one-shoot-to-five-assets loop

Film once. In Descript, cut the usable takes. Ask ChatGPT for 10 scroll-stopping hooks and pick three. Drop into CapCut for auto-captions, then Opus Clip lifts the best 30 seconds into a TikTok-ready short. Canva spins a thumbnail. You shipped five assets from one session.

Don't automate the charm

Brands pay for your personality. Use AI for captions, resizing, and ideation — never to fake authenticity. The creators who last are the ones who sound like a person, just faster.
