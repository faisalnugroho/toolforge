How we tested

We gave each builder the same prompt: "Create a website for a boutique marketing agency in Austin, TX." We scored on design quality, mobile responsiveness, load speed, SEO defaults, and customization options. Every tool on this list produced a site we'd actually use for a real business.

Best by use case

Fastest launch: Durable — generates a complete site in 30 seconds. Perfect for solopreneurs who need something up today. Best design: Framer — AI-generated sites that look like a $5,000 designer built them. Most professional: Webflow — enterprise-grade builder with AI assistance, used by companies like Dell and Zendesk. Best free option: Gamma — surprisingly good AI websites from a tool that started as a presentation maker.

What AI website builders still can't do

AI builders are great for marketing sites, portfolios, and landing pages. They're not ready for complex web applications, ecommerce stores with 1,000+ products, or sites that need custom functionality. For those, you still need Webflow or a developer.

The verdict

If you need a professional website and you're not a developer, AI website builders in 2026 are genuinely good enough. Start with Framer or Durable for speed, graduate to Webflow or Squarespace for scale. The days of paying $5,000 for a basic marketing site are over.
