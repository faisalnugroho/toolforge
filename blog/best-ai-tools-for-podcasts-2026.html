The 9 Best AI Tools for Podcasters in 2026

From editing to show notes to clip generation — the AI stack that turns a 2-hour recording into a published episode in an afternoon.

TF
ToolForge Editorial
✍️ Published July 2026 ⏱️ 4 min read 🔄 Updated July 10, 2026

Podcasting in 2026 is 20% recording and 80% everything-else. The creators releasing weekly are not working harder — they have an AI stack that handles editing, transcripts, show notes, and clips. Here is the exact toolkit.

The recording & editing layer

Start with Descript for transcript-based editing — cut "ums" by deleting words, not waveforms. Pair it with Riverside for studio-quality remote recording, and Adobe Podcast (free) to clean up background noise in one click.

The distribution layer

Once the episode is cut, Otter.ai or Fireflies generate searchable transcripts. Use Captions or Opus Clip to auto-extract 10 short-form clips for TikTok, Reels, and Shorts — this is where most new listeners come from in 2026.

The verdict

If you only add two tools this quarter, make them Descript (editing) and Opus Clip (distribution). Together they collapse a full day of post-production into two hours. Everything else is incremental.
