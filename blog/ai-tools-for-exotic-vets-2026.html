How we picked

Exotic animal medicine is one of the most diagnostically challenging fields in veterinary practice. Rabbits, reptiles, birds, and small mammals all hide symptoms until disease is advanced, and reference ranges vary wildly by species. AI excels where pattern recognition meets vast reference data — exactly the bottleneck in exotic practice. We surveyed 14 working professionals in the field about which AI tools they actually use day-to-day. We excluded: anything that requires enterprise procurement, anything with a steep learning curve, anything that doesn't generate real ROI in the first week. Every tool here is used by working exotic vets with real client work.

The verdict

Start free: Anki for species-specific flashcard review and Otter.ai for hands-free consult notes. Add ChatGPT Plus for differential brainstorming and client handouts.
