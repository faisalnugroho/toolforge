Top picks, explained

ChatGPT

Brainstorm worksheet structures and psychoeducation handouts. Never enter client-identifying information into any public AI — use it for templates and general content only.

Notion

A clean home for your reusable worksheets, referral lists, and business checklists. Keep clinical records in your compliant EHR, not here.

Otter

Capture supervision sessions and peer consults (with consent) so you can focus on the conversation. Redact anything identifying before storing.

ElevenLabs

Generate calming narration for between-session resources you share with clients — a nice complement to your own voice.

Canva

Turn evidence-based frameworks (CBT thought records, grounding sheets) into polished, printable client handouts.

Grammarly

Keep emails to clients and referrers clear and mistake-free without overthinking every sentence.

How to choose

For clinical work the rule is simple: AI assists the clinician, never stands in for one, and never touches identifiable client data.

  • Use AI for templates, handouts, and your own admin — never for diagnosis or therapy itself.
  • Keep all PHI in your compliant EHR; treat ChatGPT/Notion as non-clinical scratch space.
  • Always disclose and get consent before recording any session.
  • Add ElevenLabs/Canva only if you ship client resources regularly.

FAQ

Is it HIPAA-compliant to use ChatGPT?

The public ChatGPT is not a HIPAA-covered environment. Do not enter client names, dates, or details. Use it only for generic templates and your own non-identifying work, or use a vendor with a BAA for anything clinical.

Can AI write my notes for me?

It can draft structure from your own bullet points, but you remain the author and must verify accuracy. Many clinicians use it to speed a first pass, then edit.

Where's the line on client resources?

Generic worksheets and psychoeducation are fair game. Anything personalized to a specific client should be created by you, for that client, in your records system.

Verdict

AI won't (and shouldn't) replace the therapeutic relationship. But ChatGPT + Notion + Canva handily cut the documentation and resource-building load — as long as PHI never touches a public model.
