The free-tier limits to know

Every free tier has limits. The smart move is to combine tools so you never hit a wall. Use ChatGPT for daily chat, Claude for long documents, Gemini for video, Perplexity for research. Switch when you run out of free credits, not before.

Where free still loses to paid

Three areas where the free tier genuinely isn't enough in 2026: (1) high-volume API usage — the free tiers are for chat UIs, not production; (2) advanced agentic features — Claude Sonnet 4 Pro and ChatGPT Plus unlock longer thinking and parallel tool use; (3) commercial-grade media generation — Midjourney v7, Runway Gen-4, Suno Pro still justify their costs for client work.
