How we picked

We asked 30+ independent restaurant owners (1-5 locations, no chains) to share the AI tools they pay for and which they actually open every week. We removed anything that needed a full-time person to operate, anything that stored customer payment data insecurely, and anything that cost more than the time it saved. The list is intentionally broad — front of house, back of house, marketing, and ops.

The verdict

Start with ChatGPT Plus for menu engineering and supplier emails. Add Perplexity for competitive research. Scribe is the sleeper hit — most kitchens waste 5+ hours/week writing the same prep procedures that already exist somewhere. For marketing, Canva and Midjourney are the power combo. Skip the rest until month 2.
