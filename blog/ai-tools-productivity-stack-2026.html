5 workflows, not 15 tools

The 15 tools above map to 5 workflows: (1) Communication & Writing: ChatGPT + Claude + Grammarly. (2) Research & Information: Perplexity. (3) Visual & Audio: Midjourney + Runway + ElevenLabs. (4) Automation: Make. (5) Organization: Notion + Motion. Plus role-specific add-ons: Cursor for devs, Jasper for marketers, Descript for video creators, Sudowrite for writers. Pick 5-7 tools, use daily, ignore the rest.

Total cost vs total value

The full 15-tool stack costs ~$280/mo. Most people don't need the full stack. The 'core 5' (ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Gamma, Grammarly) costs $80/mo and handles 80% of knowledge work. Add Make ($9) for automation, Notion AI ($10) for docs, Motion ($34) for scheduling = $133/mo for 95% coverage. That's the right target for most people. The other tools are nice-to-have, not need-to-have.
