Research that cites itself

Perplexity and scite are the two tools that change how Associates approach a memo. Perplexity returns answers with inline citations you can verify; scite goes further and tells you whether a cited case is supported or contradicted by later rulings — the difference between a confident citation and a sanctions risk. Use Elicit when you need systematic review across many sources rather than a single answer.

Drafting and review

Claude’s 200K-token context is ideal for dropping an entire contract set and asking targeted questions. ChatDoc and ChatDoc-style PDF chat let you query a single agreement. For high-stakes clause review, LegalOn and similar contract playbooks codify your firm’s preferred positions so redlines are consistent. Treat the output as a first draft — never file AI-generated text unchecked.

The verdict

A solo practitioner can run the whole stack for under $60/month and reclaim 5-10 hours a week. Start with ChatGPT Plus + Claude, add Perplexity and scite for research, and layer contract tools as volume grows. The firms that win in 2026 are the ones that use AI to bill more for judgment and less for grunt work.
