How we picked

We used each daily for a month on identical tasks and scored blind where possible. Rankings reflect real output quality, not marketing. We don't take payment for placements — see our disclosure.

When to use which

Reach for ChatGPT when you want the broadest toolset (image, voice, plugins). Reach for Claude when the task needs careful writing or long, nuanced reasoning. Reach for Gemini when you're deep in Google Workspace or need massive context windows. Most power users keep all three open.

Bottom line

There's no single winner in 2026 — only the right tool for the task. Start with the free tiers of all three, notice which you open by habit, and pay for that one first. For most people, that's ChatGPT or Claude; Gemini wins inside Google.
