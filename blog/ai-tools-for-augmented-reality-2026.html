The Best AI Tools for Augmented Reality in 2026

From on-device segmentation to spatial prompt editors — the AI stack AR creators and developers are actually using this year.

TF
ToolForge Editorial
✍️ Published July 2026⏱️ 3 min read🔄 Updated 2026-07-16

Augmented reality in 2026 is less about headsets and more about the AI pipelines that generate, place, and animate 3D content in real time. Here are the tools worth your time.

The shortlist

  • Meshy / Tripo — text- and image-to-3D asset generation for instant AR props.
  • Luma Ray — neural capture and relighting for real objects.
  • Adobe Firefly — 3D-aware generation that drops into Aero.
  • On-device segmentation models — background/object masks that run at 60fps.

How to pick

Match the tool to the deployment target: web AR wants lightweight glTF exports (Meshy), while headset work wants USDZ and spatial anchors. Build a pipeline, not a single app.
