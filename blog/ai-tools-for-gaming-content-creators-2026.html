The content multiplication workflow

Step 1: Stream. Use OBS with AI noise suppression and auto-scene switching for a professional-looking stream. Step 2: Clip. Upload the VOD to Opus Clip — it finds the 10 best moments and creates short-form clips with captions. Step 3: Edit. Polish clips in CapCut with trending effects and transitions. Step 4: Thumbnails. Generate eye-catching thumbnails in Midjourney — dramatic lighting, bold text, and expressive characters. Step 5: Publish. Use ChatGPT to write SEO-optimized titles and descriptions for each platform. One 3-hour stream becomes 10+ clips, 2-3 YouTube videos, and a week of social media content.

The verdict

Start with CapCut (free) for editing and ChatGPT (free) for copywriting. Add Opus Clip ($15/mo) when you're streaming regularly — it's the biggest time-saver. Add Midjourney ($10/mo) for professional thumbnails. Total cost: $0-25/mo. The content multiplication effect is real — one stream becomes a week of content across every platform.
