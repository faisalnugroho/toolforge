AI Tools for Event Planners: Save 10+ Hours/Week in 2026

Timelines, proposals, and invites eat your week. These AI tools give it back. Free and paid options reviewed for event planners.

TF
ToolForge Editorial
AI Tool Researchers

Event planning is a thousand moving parts and one deadline. In 2026, AI tools can return 10+ hours a week to focus on the experience. We tested the stack and kept the 6 planners reach for.

1. ChatGPT — Timelines and contracts

ChatGPT drafts run-of-show timelines, vendor emails, and contract language fast. The $20/mo Plus plan is ideal for client-ready copy.

Use case: Use case: "Build a minute-by-minute 200-guest wedding timeline."

2. Canva — Invites and signage

Canva's Magic Write produces invitations, menus, and signage in your event brand. Free for most planners.

Use case: Use case: "Design a coordinated invite and table-number set."

3. Notion AI — Planning hub

Notion AI runs your vendor list, budget, and checklist in one workspace. The $10/mo add-on auto-summarizes changes.

Use case: Use case: "Turn a client call into a task list with owners and dates."

4. Calendly — Client and vendor calls

Calendly removes scheduling friction with booking links and reminders. Free tier covers one calendar.

Use case: Use case: "Share a link for venue walkthroughs."

5. Mailchimp — Guest communications

Mailchimp's AI writes RSVP reminders and event updates with smart segments. Free up to 500 contacts.

Use case: Use case: "Send a 3-touch RSVP reminder sequence."

6. Gamma — Proposals and decks

Gamma turns a brief into a polished client proposal or mood board in minutes. Free tier included.

Use case: Use case: "Build a 12-slide corporate-event proposal."

The 3-tool starter stack (free)

If you are just starting, grab these three free tools:

  • ChatGPT Free — timelines
  • Canva Free — invites
  • Calendly Free — booking

That is $0/month and saves 5+ hours/week immediately.

Upgrade when you have budget

When the ROI is clear (or you are ready to buy back your time), add:

  • ChatGPT Plus ($20/mo) — client copy
  • Notion AI ($10/mo) — planning hub
  • Mailchimp paid — guest comms
