How we picked

Every tool above was used by our team to run a real micro-SaaS in 2026. We kept only what we'd pay for again. No tool made the list because it was hyped — only because it removed a bottleneck (design, code, research, or content) that would otherwise need a contractor.

When to use which

Use ChatGPT + Claude as your always-on co-founders for writing and decisions. Use Cursor/Lovable/v0 to build — pick one primary builder and learn it well. Use Perplexity for research and Notion AI to keep the company brain in one doc. Add ElevenLabs only when you need voice. Stack the free tiers first; upgrade as revenue appears.

Bottom line

You don't need a co-founder or a big seed round to start in 2026 — you need a tight AI stack and the discipline to actually ship. The 12 tools above kept our monthly tool spend under $120 while replacing roughly three contractors. Start free, upgrade on proof.
