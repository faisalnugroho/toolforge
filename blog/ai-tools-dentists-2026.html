7 Best AI Tools for Dentists in 2026 (Automate Patient Care)

AI tools that automate appointment scheduling, X-ray analysis, patient communication, and treatment planning for dental practices. Tested on real workflows.

ToolForge Editorial
Published June 2026 4 min read Updated June 24, 2026

Dentistry is one of the medical fields most ripe for AI disruption. Between X-ray interpretation, patient scheduling, insurance verification, and treatment planning, there is a massive amount of repetitive work that AI can handle. Here are the 7 tools dental practices are actually adopting in 2026.

1. Pearl - AI X-Ray Analysis

Price: Custom pricing | Best for: Detecting cavities, bone loss, and pathology

Pearl's FDA-cleared AI analyzes dental X-rays in real-time, highlighting areas of concern with 95%+ accuracy. It acts as a second set of eyes for every radiograph, reducing missed diagnoses and providing visual evidence for treatment plans. Dentists report catching 15-20% more pathology in the first month.

2. Weave - Patient Communication Platform

Price: From $399/mo | Best for: Phone, text, reviews, and patient engagement

Weave is the all-in-one communication platform for dental offices. AI-powered phone system handles missed calls with text-back, automated appointment reminders reduce no-shows by 30%, and review generation fills your Google profile with 5-star reviews. The VoIP phone system replaces your office phone entirely.

3. Arini - AI Dental Receptionist

Price: From $249/mo | Best for: 24/7 call handling and scheduling

Arini is an AI receptionist built specifically for dental offices. It answers calls, books appointments, verifies insurance, and handles patient questions 24/7. Unlike generic AI phone systems, Arini understands dental terminology and integrates with Dentrix, Eaglesoft, and Open Dental.

4. TrueLark - AI Scheduling and Engagement

Price: From $199/mo | Best for: Filling cancellations and automated scheduling

TrueLark's AI fills your schedule by automatically contacting patients on your waitlist when cancellations occur. It handles appointment confirmations, rescheduling, and follow-ups via text and email. Practices report a 25% reduction in no-shows and 15% increase in schedule utilization.

5. Yapi - Dental Practice Management

Price: From $299/mo | Best for: Digital forms, reminders, and review management

Yapi digitizes your entire patient intake process. Digital forms sync with your PMS, automated reminders go out via text and email, and review requests are sent after every appointment. The iPad-based check-in system eliminates clipboard paperwork entirely.

6. Simplifeye - Digital Patient Intake

Price: From $199/mo | Best for: Patient forms and payment processing

Simplifeye lets patients complete all intake forms on their phone before arriving. Insurance verification happens automatically, copays are collected digitally, and treatment plans are presented with visual aids. Reduces front-desk workload by 40%.

7. Dentistry.AI - Treatment Planning

Price: Custom pricing | Best for: AI-assisted treatment plan generation

Dentistry.AI analyzes patient history, imaging, and clinical notes to suggest optimal treatment plans. It helps younger dentists learn faster and gives experienced dentists a second opinion on complex cases. Integrates with major PMS systems.

The verdict

Start with Arini or TrueLark for scheduling automation - these have the fastest ROI (typically 60-90 days). Add Pearl for X-ray analysis if you want to improve diagnostic accuracy. Weave is the all-in-one choice if you want phone, text, and reviews in one platform. Most practices see 3-5x ROI within the first year.
