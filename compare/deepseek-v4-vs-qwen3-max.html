The verdict

Pick DeepSeek V4 if your workload is English-heavy reasoning, math, or code, or if you're self-hosting on a tight GPU budget — its MoE efficiency and unbeatable API price make it the default for cost-per-token. Pick Qwen3 Max if you need native vision, multilingual (especially Chinese) quality, or a 1M-token context window. For most builders, DeepSeek V4 is the daily driver and Qwen3 Max is the specialist.

Winner: DeepSeek V4 on value · Qwen3 Max on breadth

How we tested

Both models were run via their official APIs and self-hosted weights over 4 weeks across 40 real tasks: 10 math, 10 coding, 8 multilingual, 6 long-context, and 6 agentic/tool-use.
