Frequently asked questions

Is o3-mini better than Claude Sonnet 4 for math?

Yes — o3-mini uses chain-of-thought reasoning that makes it stronger on pure math problems (AIME, GSM8K, MATH benchmarks). Claude Sonnet 4 is good at math but doesn't have the explicit reasoning chain that o3-mini uses.

Is o3-mini better than Claude Sonnet 4 for coding?

Claude Sonnet 4 is widely considered the best coding model in 2026. It produces cleaner, more idiomatic code with better multi-file reasoning. o3-mini is competitive on algorithmic/competitive programming but Claude wins on practical software engineering tasks.

Can I use both?

Yes, and many developers do. Use o3-mini for math, logic, and hard reasoning problems. Use Claude Sonnet 4 for coding, writing, and long-context tasks. The total cost ($40/mo for both Pro tiers) gives you the best reasoning + the best coding model.
