Frequently asked questions

Can 10Web host sites I built outside WordPress?

10Web is WordPress-only. If you have an existing non-WordPress site (Webflow, Wix, Squarespace), use 10Web's AI Recreate to clone it as a WordPress site, then transfer the domain. For non-WP businesses, Durable is the faster path.

Does Durable have a CMS for blogging?

Yes, but limited. Durable's blog is fine for 1-2 posts per month. For serious content marketing (10+ posts/month, multiple authors, content workflows), 10Web's WordPress is the better CMS by 10x.

Which is cheaper for a small business site?

Durable wins on price ($15/mo includes CRM, marketing, invoicing). 10Web's $20/mo Starter is WordPress hosting only — you'd add CRM ($30+/mo), invoicing ($20+/mo), and marketing tools separately. For an equivalent stack, 10Web costs $60-$100/mo more.
