The verdict

Use ChatGPT for everyday AI work — best app polish, DALL-E integration, GPTs, voice mode. Use Gemini 2.5 Pro if you need to analyze long documents (100+ pages), work heavily in Google Workspace, or analyze video. For most people, ChatGPT Plus ($20) is the right default. Power users should have both.

How we tested

We used ChatGPT (Plus, $20/mo) and Gemini 2.5 Pro (AI Premium add-on, $20/mo) daily for 30+ days across real workflows: 50-page PDF analysis, code reviews, marketing copy, video understanding, and Google Docs integration.
