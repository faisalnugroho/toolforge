Frequently asked questions

Is ElevenLabs worth the higher price per minute?

If you're producing 5-15 minutes of high-stakes voiceover per month (audiobook chapter, brand video, documentary), yes. ElevenLabs' quality gap is real and worth $0.50-$1.00/min. If you're producing 60+ minutes of e-learning, YouTube narration, or social content, Lovo's $24/100 min plan is the better deal.

Can Lovo clone voices as well as ElevenLabs?

Lovo's cloning is fast and decent quality, but ElevenLabs' cloning is more accurate and expressive. For creator/personal use, Lovo is fine. For audiobook-level clone work, ElevenLabs wins.

Which has better non-English voices?

Lovo by a wide margin — 100 languages vs ElevenLabs' 29. For Spanish, Mandarin, Hindi, Arabic, or other Tier-1 non-English languages at production quality, Lovo is the only choice. ElevenLabs recently expanded but still lags significantly.
