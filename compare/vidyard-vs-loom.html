The verdict

Pick Vidyard if you're in B2B sales, do cold outreach, or run account-based marketing. The engagement analytics alone justify the price. Pick Loom AI for general team communication — it's cheaper, has a better mobile app, and the AI summaries are good enough for internal use.

Winner: Vidyard for sales, Loom for everything else
