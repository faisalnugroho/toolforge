Overview

Sora 2 is built for accessibility: type a prompt, get a 60-second clip with matching audio. It's the model to recommend to anyone who has never made a video before. Runway Gen-4 is built for control: camera moves, character references, motion brushes, inpainting, multi-track timeline. It's the model for professionals who already know what they want.

The fundamental difference: Sora 2 interprets, Runway Gen-4 obeys. Sora is more creative (sometimes too creative). Runway is more literal (sometimes too literal).

When to use Sora 2

You want to generate video from a single prompt and get usable output fast. You need native audio (dialogue, ambient sound, SFX) generated in the same pass. You're making social content, ads, or explainers where 1080p is enough.

When to use Runway Gen-4

You're a professional filmmaker, agency, or studio. You need character and scene consistency across multiple shots. You want to integrate AI clips into a traditional editing workflow (Premiere, DaVinci, Final Cut). You need fine-grained control over motion, camera, and style.

Pricing

Sora 2 is included in ChatGPT Plus ($20/mo) with limited generations, and ChatGPT Pro ($200/mo) for heavy use. Runway Gen-4 Standard is $35/mo, Pro is $76/mo. Heavy users will find Runway's credit system more predictable than Sora's rate limits.

The verdict

Pick Sora 2 if you're new to AI video, want the easiest path, and need audio in the same generation. Pick Runway Gen-4 if you're a pro who needs consistency, control, and a real editing suite. For most creators, Sora 2 will be the one you reach for first. For agency and film work, Runway is still the gold standard.

How we tested

Both tools were used for 30-60 days across real-world tasks. We scored on quality, ease of use, features, pricing, and support. We don't accept payment for reviews — see our full disclosure.

FAQ

Which has better video quality?

Runway Gen-4 wins on cinematic quality and consistency. Sora 2 is more hit-or-miss but its hits are stunning.

Which is better for ads and social content?

Sora 2, because the native audio alone saves hours. Most social content doesn't need the granular control Runway offers.

Can I use clips from either commercially?

Yes on both with paid plans. Read each platform's commercial terms carefully — Runway is more permissive for client work.
