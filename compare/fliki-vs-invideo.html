The verdict

Use Fliki for voice-driven video — podcasts, audiobooks, multilingual social clips, faceless YouTube. Use InVideo for template-driven video — brand explainers, longer YouTube content, polished marketing videos. Both are great; they target different parts of the same workflow.

Winner: Fliki for voice, InVideo for templates
