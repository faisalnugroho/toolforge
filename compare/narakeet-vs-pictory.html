Narakeet vs Pictory (2026)

Two script-to-video tools. Narakeet is the budget champion at $9/mo. Pictory is the marketing team favorite at $23/mo.

The short answer

Narakeet wins on price ($9/mo for 60 min) and narration quality. Pictory wins on blog-to-video workflows, stock footage library, and visual polish. For marketers repurposing blog content into video, Pictory is the better fit. For educators, course creators, and anyone producing voice-heavy explainer videos, Narakeet is unbeatable on price.

Narakeet in depth

Narakeet is the script-to-video tool that almost no one talks about and everyone who tries it loves. It's specifically built for voice-led video: 600+ AI voices in 95 languages, slide-style editor that turns a Markdown script into a video in 3 minutes, and $9/mo gets you 60 minutes of video. There's no AI b-roll — the video style is "slides with voiceover," which is exactly what educators, course creators, and internal trainers actually want. The voice quality is excellent (Narakeet uses Microsoft Azure + ElevenLabs voices under the hood).

Pictory in depth

Pictory is the marketer's script-to-video tool. Paste in a blog post URL or a script, and Pictory uses AI to extract the key sentences, find matching stock footage from a library of millions, add captions, and produce a cinematic-looking social video. The $23/mo Starter gets you 30 videos/month — enough for most social media calendars. Pictory's strength is the visual polish: the resulting video looks like a real editor cut it, not a slideshow. Where Pictory loses: voice quality is weaker than Narakeet, and you can't produce pure voiceover-style content without visuals.

When to pick which

✓ Pick Narakeet if…

  • You're producing educational or training content where voice is primary
  • You need 95 languages and 600+ voice options
  • Slide-style video fits your use case (think: explainer, lecture, internal training)
  • Price matters — $9/mo for 60 minutes is unbeatable

✓ Pick Pictory if…

  • You're a marketer repurposing blog posts into social videos
  • You need cinematic b-roll and stock footage integration
  • Your audience is on social media where visual polish matters more than narration
  • You produce 30+ videos/month and need a real timeline editor

The verdict

These two tools serve different jobs. Narakeet is a voice-first explainer tool — think Duolingo lessons, corporate compliance training, online courses. Pictory is a blog-to-social tool — think LinkedIn posts turned into 60-second videos. If you're producing voice-led content at scale and price matters, Narakeet is unbeatable at $9/mo. If you're a marketing team that lives on content repurposing, Pictory's blog-to-video workflow is the right answer.
