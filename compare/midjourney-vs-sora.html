Midjourney v7 vs Sora 2

Last updated June 16, 2026 · Reviewed by ToolForge Editorial

Midjourney v7 and Sora 2 are the two most-discussed generative AI tools of 2026. They make different things (still images vs video) but they're often compared because they're both the flagship of their category and both made by the same category of company. The honest comparison: they do completely different jobs. Here's when you'd want which.

Overview

Midjourney v7 makes single still images (and now multi-image grids) that look like art. It's text-to-image, optimized for aesthetics, with a Discord-based workflow. Sora 2 makes video clips up to 60 seconds long with native audio. It's text-to-video, optimized for cinematic motion and physics.

You wouldn't use a hammer on a screw, and you wouldn't use Midjourney when you need a video. The real question is: when do you need a still image, and when do you need a video?

When to use Midjourney

You need a hero image for a blog post, ad, or landing page. You're creating concept art, illustrations, or mood boards. You need consistent style across a series of images (Midjourney's --sref and --cref handle this well). Your final output is a still image.

When to use Sora 2

You need a video clip — an ad, a social post, a product demo, a short film. The motion is the message, not the still. You need native audio (dialogue, SFX, ambient). You want the result to look like real footage, not illustration.

Quality comparison

Midjourney v7 is the gold standard for AI images. Sora 2 is the gold standard for AI video. Both have hit the uncanny valley of quality where most viewers can't tell it's AI on first glance. Where they fail: Midjourney still struggles with text and hands. Sora 2 still struggles with long-shot character consistency and complex multi-character scenes.

The verdict

Pick Midjourney v7 for still images. Pick Sora 2 for video. The only real overlap is if you're a creator who needs both — in which case, yes, you need subscriptions to both. They're complementary tools, not competitors. Trying to pick one over the other is like choosing between a camera and a microphone.

FAQ

Can Sora 2 generate a single image from a prompt?

Yes, it can output a single frame. But Midjourney will give you higher quality and more control over the still image.

Can Midjourney generate video?

No, still images only. For video, you need Sora, Runway, Kling, Pika, or Luma.

Which is better for brand assets?

Midjourney, because you need consistent still images for logos, social graphics, and ads. Video is supplemental.
