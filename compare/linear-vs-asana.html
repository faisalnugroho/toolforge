The head-to-head

Linear is the best issue tracker for product and engineering teams that value speed and opinionated workflows — it's what high-output startups use. Asana is the better choice for cross-functional companies that need flexible project views, marketing workflows, and non-technical users in the loop. Engineers pick Linear; everyone-else picks Asana.
