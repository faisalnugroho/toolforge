The verdict

Use Gemini 3 if you need multimodal (image, video, audio understanding), massive context windows (2M+ tokens), or deep Google Workspace integration. Use Claude Opus 5 for complex reasoning, production-grade coding, nuanced writing, and tasks where accuracy matters more than speed. For most developers and writers, Claude Opus 5 is the smarter choice. For researchers and data analysts, Gemini 3's multimodal + long context wins.
