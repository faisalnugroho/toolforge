The comparison

Claude Opus 4.5 (Anthropic) and DeepSeek-R1 are the two most capable reasoning models of 2026. Claude is paid and proprietary. DeepSeek-R1 is open-weight with a permissive license. We tested both on 30 coding tasks, 20 math problems, and 10 long-document analysis tasks.

The verdict

For most professional coding and writing work, Claude Opus 4.5 wins on quality, polish, and ecosystem (Cursor, Aider, Claude Code all integrate). For high-volume or cost-sensitive production use, DeepSeek-R1 is the better deal at 1/30th the cost. Smart teams use both: Claude for product features, DeepSeek for backend batch processing.
