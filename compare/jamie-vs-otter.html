Jamie vs Otter

Jamie vs Otter head-to-head. Two leading meeting AIs with very different philosophies: Jamie runs no-bot with EU hosting, Otter is the polished bot-based incumbent.

Ja
Option A

Jamie

No-bot meeting AI that works on Zoom, Meet, Teams, phone, and in-person — EU hosted

Price: $24/mo Pro plan
Best for: EU teams, regulated industries, in-person meetings
✓ Pros
  • No bot joins your calls — invisible to attendees
  • Works on phone calls and in-person meetings (unique)
  • GDPR + EU data residency (genuine, audited)
  • 7-section structured summaries (most thorough)
✗ Cons
  • No live transcription — summary arrives 2-5 min after call
  • No Linux app
  • Smaller integration catalog than Otter (30 vs 60+)
  • Meeting library search is paywalled to Business ($39/mo)

Otter

The OG meeting AI. Live transcription, speaker ID, polished UI

Price: $16.99/mo Pro plan
Best for: High-volume virtual calls, US teams, live transcription needs
✓ Pros
  • Cheaper ($16.99 vs $24/mo)
  • Best live transcription in the industry
  • Mature speaker diarization
  • Live captions for in-meeting accessibility
✗ Cons

  • No in-person meeting support
  • US-hosted (not GDPR-clean for EU)

Try Otter →

The verdict

Pick Otter if you want the cheapest polished meeting AI with live transcription and don’t mind a bot joining. Pick Jamie if you work in a regulated industry, sell to EU enterprise, or run in-person meetings. Both are excellent — Jamie wins on privacy and structure, Otter wins on price and live transcription.

Winner: Tie — Jamie for privacy/EU, Otter for value/virtual
