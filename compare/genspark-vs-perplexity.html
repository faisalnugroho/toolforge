Genspark vs Perplexity

Genspark and Perplexity are the two leading AI search engines of 2026, and they take opposite approaches. Perplexity gives you a quick cited answer in a chat thread. Genspark builds a full custom mini-website for every query. We tested both on 50+ research tasks (product research, trip planning, news, technical questions). Here's who wins and when.

The verdict

Use Perplexity for fast factual Q&A, news, and follow-up questions. Use Genspark when you need a deep, structured answer that would normally take 30 minutes of clicking through links. For product comparisons, trip planning, and complex research, Genspark's Sparkpage format is genuinely better. For quick lookups, Perplexity is faster. Power users should have both — and the good news is both have generous free tiers.
