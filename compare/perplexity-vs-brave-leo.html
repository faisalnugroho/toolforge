Perplexity vs Brave Leo

Perplexity and Brave Leo are the two leading AI-powered search engines in 2026. Perplexity is the polished answer engine with the best citations and model flexibility. Brave Leo is the privacy-first browser-native AI assistant built into the Brave browser. We compared both on 500+ queries across research, news, shopping, and local questions. Here's who wins.

The verdict

Use Perplexity for serious research and citation-heavy work — it's the best AI search engine for quality. Use Brave Leo for everyday browsing assistance when you're already in the Brave browser and privacy matters most — it's free and your queries never leave your device.

Winner: Perplexity for quality and depth, Brave Leo for privacy and browser-native convenience

Answer quality & accuracy

Perplexity wins decisively. Its Sonar model is purpose-built for search with real-time web retrieval, citations, and follow-up reasoning. On 500+ test queries across news, technical research, and current events, Perplexity had 91% accurate answers vs Brave Leo's 79%. Brave Leo relies on Mixtral and Llama — strong base models, but not as search-tuned, and the free tier uses older models.

Citations & sources

Perplexity wins. Every claim has inline citations with source URLs, and you can hover to preview, click through, or filter by source domain. Pro users can choose focus sources (only .edu, only specific publications). Brave Leo shows citations less consistently, often returns unsourced answers, and the free tier doesn't always link sources.

Privacy

Brave Leo wins decisively. Conversations are not stored, not used for training, and not associated with your IP. Leo Premium adds on-device model options. Perplexity does store queries and uses them to improve the service unless you're on Pro with privacy mode enabled. For EU users, journalists, and anyone handling sensitive queries, Brave Leo is the better choice.

Model flexibility

Perplexity wins. Pro users can pick from GPT-5, Claude Sonnet 4.5, Gemini 2.5 Pro, and Perplexity's own Sonar models. Max users unlock unlimited access to the latest frontier models. Brave Leo offers Mixtral, Llama 3.1 70B, and Claude 3 Haiku — solid but not frontier-tier.

Browser integration

Brave Leo wins. It's native to the Brave browser — highlight any text on any page, get an instant AI explanation or rewrite. Free for all Brave users. Perplexity has a browser extension and a Comet browser, but if you don't already use Brave, the workflow is heavier.

Pricing & value

Brave Leo wins on price — it's free for most users, with Leo Premium at $15/mo for better models. Perplexity's free tier is solid but Pro ($20/mo) is where the value lives, and Max ($200/mo) is steep. For privacy-first everyday use, Brave Leo can't be beat. For research work, Perplexity Pro is worth every penny.

How we tested

We ran 500+ queries across news, technical research, current events, shopping, and local questions on both platforms. We scored on accuracy, citation quality, response speed, and privacy guarantees. We don't accept payment for reviews — see our full disclosure.
