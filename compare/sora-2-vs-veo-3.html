The verdict

Use Sora 2 for cinematic shots, storytelling, and any project that needs native audio/music/dialogue. Use Veo 3 for high-fidelity short clips, scientific content, and tight Google Workspace integration. For YouTube creators and marketers, Sora 2 wins on audio-visual cohesion. For filmmakers and documentary work, Veo 3 wins on visual realism. Most creators should have access to both via their existing Google/OpenAI subscriptions.

Winner: Tie — Sora 2 for audio+cine, Veo 3 for visual fidelity
