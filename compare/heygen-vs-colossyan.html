The verdict

HeyGen wins for creators, marketers, and sales teams — polished UI, Photo Avatar, video translation. Colossyan wins for L&D and corporate training — SCORM export, LMS integration, 80+ languages, training templates. If you're producing sales or marketing videos, HeyGen. If you're producing training content at scale, Colossyan.

How we tested

We produced 10 sales outreach videos with HeyGen and 8 internal training videos with Colossyan. We measured: avatar realism (blinded review by 12 reviewers), translation quality across 5 languages, LMS export reliability, and total production time per video.
