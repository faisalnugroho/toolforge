The verdict

Use Lindy if you want a no-code drag-and-drop agent builder with templates for sales, support, and ops workflows. Use Manus if you need autonomous agents that handle long multi-step tasks (research, browser automation, code execution) without supervision. Lindy is for builders. Manus is for people who want AI to just do the thing.

Frequently asked questions

Is Manus better than Lindy?

For autonomous tasks where you want to hand off a complex job and come back to a result, yes. For repeatable business workflows you want to control and refine, Lindy is better.

Can Lindy agents replace employees?

For high-volume repetitive work — lead enrichment, meeting follow-ups, support triage — yes, Lindy agents can replace or augment junior roles. They're not magic, but they save real hours.

Can Manus agents replace employees?

For research and one-off complex tasks, Manus can replace 5-10 hours/week of analyst work. It's less suited to ongoing operational workflows than Lindy.

Which has a better free tier?

Lindy offers 400 free tasks/month which is enough to test real workflows. Manus offers $5 credits which is roughly 5-10 tasks. Lindy wins for evaluation.
