The verdict

Pick Gladia if you need the fastest streaming transcription, broadest language coverage, and a generous free tier. Pick AssemblyAI if you need the highest accuracy on real-world noisy audio plus built-in sentiment and entity detection for voice agents and call analytics. Both beat Deepgram and Whisper on different benchmarks.

Winner: Gladia for speed and price, AssemblyAI for accuracy and features
