The verdict

Use Klaviyo if you run an ecommerce brand on Shopify and need email + SMS that drive measurable revenue. Use ActiveCampaign if you're B2B or run a service business and need complex automations + a built-in CRM. For 70% of ecommerce, Klaviyo wins. For 70% of B2B, ActiveCampaign wins.

Winner: Klaviyo (ecommerce) · ActiveCampaign (B2B/automation)

Ecommerce features

Klaviyo was built for Shopify from day one — deep integration with orders, customers, products, browse abandonment, post-purchase flows. ActiveCampaign added ecommerce features in 2024 but they're bolted on, not native. If you sell online, Klaviyo's revenue attribution alone is worth the switch.

Automation depth

ActiveCampaign's visual automation builder is the gold standard — conditional branching, lead scoring, site tracking, and CRM triggers. Klaviyo's automation is more linear but covers every ecommerce scenario out of the box. For complex multi-step B2B nurture sequences, ActiveCampaign wins. For abandoned cart + post-purchase ecommerce flows, Klaviyo wins.

AI features (2026)

Both added AI in 2026: Klaviyo's predictive segments + send-time AI vs ActiveCampaign's AI content generator + lead scoring. Klaviyo's AI is more ecommerce-aware (predicts lifetime value, churn risk, next purchase). ActiveCampaign's AI is better for B2B (drafts personalized sales emails, scores intent).

Pricing

ActiveCampaign is cheaper at low list sizes (100 free contacts vs Klaviyo's 250, but ActiveCampaign paid starts at $29/mo vs Klaviyo's $45/mo). However, Klaviyo includes SMS in the base price; ActiveCampaign charges extra for SMS. At 5K+ contacts, Klaviyo becomes more expensive per contact but the ROI is usually higher for ecommerce.

How we tested

We ran both platforms for 60 days on real campaigns (an ecommerce brand and a B2B SaaS). We scored on deliverability, automation power, AI quality, ease of use, support, and total cost. We don't accept payment for reviews — see our full disclosure.
