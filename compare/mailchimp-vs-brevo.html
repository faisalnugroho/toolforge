The head-to-head

Mailchimp is the better pick if email is your whole world and you want the best templates, segmentation, and brand polish — but you'll pay for it. Brevo wins on value: cheaper email, plus SMS and a lightweight CRM in one plan. Choose Brevo if you're cost-sensitive or want multichannel without stacking tools; choose Mailchimp if email sophistication is the priority.
