Read AI vs Fathom

Read AI and Fathom are both top-tier meeting AIs, but they have wildly different philosophies. Read AI is the analytics powerhouse. Fathom is the free, simple, no-nonsense note-taker. We tested both on 50+ meetings — and the answer depends entirely on whether you need analytics or just notes.

The verdict

Pick Fathom if: You want free, reliable meeting notes and don't need analytics. Fathom is the best free meeting AI in 2026 by a wide margin. Pick Read AI if: You're running a sales team, executive team, or any function where meeting analytics directly impact revenue. The talk-time and coaching data is genuinely transformative. Most individual contributors should start with Fathom and upgrade to Read AI when they need analytics.
