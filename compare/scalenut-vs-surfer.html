The verdict

Use Scalenut if you want one tool for the entire SEO content workflow — keyword research, content brief, AI writing, and optimization in one. Cheaper too. Use Surfer SEO if you already have a writing workflow (Jasper, Claude, ChatGPT) and just want the on-page optimization layer that tops every benchmark. For new SEO teams, Scalenut wins; for established teams with a writer stack, Surfer is the bolt-on.

Frequently asked questions

Is Scalenut as good as Surfer for on-page optimization?

Close, but Surfer still wins narrowly on the pure optimization layer. Surfer's NLP engine has been trained on more SERPs and the Content Score correlates more strongly with rankings. Scalenut's score is more actionable for newer writers; Surfer's is more rigorous for SEO pros.

Can I use both Scalenut and Surfer together?

Yes, and some teams do. Use Scalenut for Cruise Mode (research + brief + first draft) and run the final draft through Surfer for the optimization pass. Costs ~$120/mo total. Worth it only for high-stakes content — most teams pick one and stick with it.

Which has better topical authority tools?

Scalenut, by a wide margin. The Topic Cluster Hub generates 30+ supporting articles, parent topics, and internal linking maps in one click. Surfer's Topical Authority tool is newer and less developed. If site-level SEO strategy matters to you, Scalenut wins.
