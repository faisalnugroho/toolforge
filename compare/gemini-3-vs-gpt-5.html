The verdict

Use Gemini 3 if you want the best free tier, Google Workspace integration, and top-tier multimodal capabilities (image, video, audio understanding). Use GPT-5 if you need premium writing quality, the best coding assistant, and the most polished overall experience. Budget-conscious users: Gemini 3's free tier is unbeatable.

Winner: Gemini 3 for budget + multimodal, GPT-5 for writing + polish

How we tested

Both models were used for 30-60 days across real-world tasks: writing blog posts, coding Python/JS, analyzing data, understanding images, and answering research questions. We scored on quality, speed, features, pricing, and ecosystem. We don't accept payment for reviews — see our full disclosure.
