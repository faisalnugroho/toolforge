The verdict

Use **Air AI** if you're a sales team that wants to book 100+ meetings a month and don't want to build infrastructure. Use **Vapi** if you're a developer who wants to embed AI voice into your own product and need fine control over the conversation logic. For non-technical sales teams, Air AI wins on time-to-value. For developers, Vapi is more flexible and cheaper at scale.

Winner: Air AI for non-technical sales teams · Vapi for developers
