The head-to-head

If your team's pain is 'I need a flexible database for marketing campaigns / hiring pipelines / content calendars', choose Airtable. If your pain is 'we need a wiki + docs + meeting notes that everyone can find', choose Notion. The integration story has improved — both have proper APIs now — but the core UX philosophies haven't converged.

Migrated a 12-person marketing team's campaign tracker from each platform. Ran for 30 days on each. Measured time-to-launch for new campaign types, friction for non-technical users, and how often the team 'escaped' to Google Sheets or Docs.
