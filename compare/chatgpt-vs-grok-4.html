The verdict

Use ChatGPT if you want the most polished, capable, all-round assistant with image generation and the biggest plugin ecosystem. Use Grok 4 if you live on X and want an assistant that reasons over live social data with far less hedging. For most people ChatGPT Plus is the safer daily driver; Grok is the specialist for 'what's happening right now.'

Winner: ChatGPT for general use; Grok 4 for real-time X context
