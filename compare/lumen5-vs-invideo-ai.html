The verdict

Use Lumen5 when you have existing written content (blogs, articles, scripts) you want to repurpose into video with consistent brand styling. Use InVideo AI when you start with just an idea or topic and want AI to generate the full video — script, scenes, voiceover, music — from a single prompt. For B2B marketers repurposing content, Lumen5 wins on polish. For faceless YouTube channels and high-volume content, InVideo AI wins on speed.

Winner: Lumen5 for blog-to-video and brand consistency. InVideo AI for prompt-driven ideation and high-volume content.

Workflow

Lumen5 follows a "text-in, video-out" pattern: paste an article or URL, and Lumen5 extracts the best sentences, matches them to stock footage, and ships a polished video. The 10M+ Shutterstock-powered library is the biggest advantage. InVideo AI works differently: type "60-second explainer on cold email" and the AI generates the script, picks scenes, adds voiceover, and ships. Less hand-holding, more "AI does it for you."

Stock library and quality

Lumen5's 10M+ Shutterstock library is broader and more polished than InVideo AI's stock. For brand-consistent B2B content where every frame matters, Lumen5 wins. InVideo AI uses a smaller library but compensates with AI-generated b-roll in some categories — useful when stock doesn't have what you need.

Pricing

Lumen5's $19/mo Creator tier includes 20 videos/mo at 1080p with no watermark. InVideo AI's $25/mo Plus tier includes 50 mins/mo of AI generation. For high-volume output, InVideo AI gives more minutes per dollar. For brand-quality output, Lumen5 is the better investment.

How we tested

We generated 25+ videos on each platform: blog-to-video (Lumen5's strength), prompt-to-video (InVideo AI's strength), LinkedIn clips, Instagram Reels, and TikTok Shorts.
