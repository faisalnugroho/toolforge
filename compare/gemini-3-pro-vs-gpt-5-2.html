The verdict

Pick Gemini 3 Pro if you live in Google Workspace, need a 2M-token context window, or want the most generous free tier in 2026 — it's the best no-cost AI experience available. Pick GPT-5.2 if you're building agents, want the biggest plugin ecosystem, or need the most reliable computer-use. If you only pay for one, GPT-5.2 is the safer all-rounder; if you pay for none, Gemini 3 Pro is the clear free winner.

Winner: Gemini 3 Pro on free value · GPT-5.2 on agents

Both models were used daily for 4 weeks across 45 real tasks: 10 reasoning/math, 10 coding, 8 multimodal, 7 long-context, 6 agentic, and 4 Workspace/productivity.
