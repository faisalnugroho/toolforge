The verdict

Use Gamma if you need a polished deck in 5 minutes from a single prompt — investor pitch, sales one-pager, internal update. Their AI generation is genuinely a generation ahead of Pitch. Use Pitch if your team lives in decks, needs shared brand templates, and cares about real-time collaboration and version control. Pitch's design-system thinking is what enterprise teams need; Gamma is what solo founders and small teams need.
