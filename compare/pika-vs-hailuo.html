Pika vs Hailuo

Two viral video AI tools that took over TikTok in 2025. Pika is known for its Pikaffects (the squish, the inflate, the melt). Hailuo is known for being free, fast, and great at keeping characters looking the same across generations.

Updated June 17, 2026 · 10 min read · 🏆 Pika wins for creative effects · Hailuo wins for free, character consistency

✓ Pika pros

  • Pikaffects are the most viral video AI feature of 2025-2026
  • Strong motion control — specify camera moves precisely
  • Active Discord community of 700K+ creators
  • Easy editing — regenerate any section of a clip
  • Best for social-media creators who want shareable content

✗ Pika cons

  • Effects-heavy output can feel gimmicky for serious work
  • Short clips (5-10s) limit narrative use
  • Quality ceiling is below Runway or Sora
  • Credits drain fast on the Standard plan

✓ Hailuo pros

  • Free tier is genuinely useful — 6s 1080p clips daily
  • Best character consistency of any free video model
  • Strong prompt adherence — does what you ask
  • Good for storyboarding and pre-visualization
  • MiniMax is investing aggressively in video

✗ Hailuo cons

  • 6-second max is limiting for anything narrative
  • No built-in editing or effects library
  • Slower than Pika for some prompt types
  • Less polished UI than Pika's web app

The verdict

If you're making TikTok content and want to ride trends, Pika is the right default — the Pikaffects are unbeatable for viral content. If you want a free, reliable video model for character work, storyboarding, or concept visualization, Hailuo is the smarter choice. Many creators use both: Hailuo for free concept work, Pika for the final social-ready clip.
