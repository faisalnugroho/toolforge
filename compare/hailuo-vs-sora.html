Hailuo vs Sora

Hailuo (by MiniMax) is the viral Chinese AI video model that shocked the world with its physics accuracy. Sora (by OpenAI) is the original flagship video model that set the standard in 2024-2025. Both generate 1080p video from text. Here's how they compare in 2026 — and which one wins for your use case.

The verdict

For viral social content, product demos, and physics-heavy shots (water, fabric, sports), Hailuo wins on realism and price. For cinematic narrative shots, character consistency, and ChatGPT integration, Sora 2 is still the more polished choice. If you can only afford one, pick based on what kind of videos you make most often.

Winner: Tie — Hailuo wins physics, Sora wins narrative

When to choose Hailuo

  • You make short-form social content (TikTok, Reels, Shorts)
  • You need realistic physics — water, fabric, sports, food
  • You want fast iteration — Hailuo generates in under 2 minutes
  • You want to test many concepts cheaply before committing
  • You don't need character consistency across shots

When to choose Sora 2

  • You're producing longer narrative content (15+ second shots)
  • You need character consistency across a storyboard
  • You want cinematic camera moves and lighting
  • You already pay for ChatGPT Plus and want it included
  • You're doing pre-vis for film or animation
