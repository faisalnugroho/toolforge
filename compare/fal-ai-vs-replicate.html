The verdict

Use fal.ai if you're shipping a product that needs fast cold starts, real-time UX, and the newest SOTA models. Use Replicate if you need community models, want the bigger ecosystem, or are doing one-off experimentation. For most production apps in 2026, fal.ai wins on speed and price. For research and hobby work, Replicate's model catalog is unmatched.

Winner: fal.ai for production, Replicate for research/hobby
