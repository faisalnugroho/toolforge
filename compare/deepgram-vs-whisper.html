The verdict

Use Deepgram if you're building production voice agents, need real-time streaming, or work with noisy audio (calls, podcasts). Use Whisper if you need offline transcription, are privacy-constrained, or want to fine-tune on your own audio. Whisper is technically free but you'll pay in GPU hours. Deepgram is technically paid but the time savings are massive at scale.

Winner: Deepgram for production; Whisper for privacy/hobby
