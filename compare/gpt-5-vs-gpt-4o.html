The head-to-head

GPT-5 is the better model on reasoning, coding, and long-context tasks — but GPT-4o remains fast, free, and more than enough for everyday chat, drafting, and quick questions. Use GPT-4o for speed and cost; reach for GPT-5 when the task is hard, long, or requires real multi-step reasoning. Most free users won't notice a difference; power users will.
