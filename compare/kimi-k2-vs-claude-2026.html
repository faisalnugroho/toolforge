The verdict

Kimi K2 is a remarkable achievement for open-source — it matches or beats Claude on coding and factual tasks. But Claude still wins on nuanced writing, instruction following, and multi-turn conversations. Choose Kimi K2 if you want to self-host or avoid vendor lock-in. Choose Claude if you want the best quality and don't mind paying.

Winner: Claude for quality, Kimi K2 for open-source value

How we tested

50 identical prompts across coding (LeetCode-style), creative writing, reasoning puzzles, and factual Q&A. Kimi K2 ran on a local H100 cluster. Claude was accessed via API. Both were tested in July 2026.
