MeetGeek vs Tactiq

MeetGeek vs Tactiq head-to-head. Both meeting AIs, but one runs as a bot, the other as a Chrome extension. Which is right for your team in 2026?

Price: $19/mo Pro plan
Best for: Cross-platform teams (Zoom/Meet/Teams), Slack integrations

The verdict

Pick MeetGeek if you want a true set-and-forget meeting AI that handles every platform and ships summaries to Slack/HubSpot automatically. Pick Tactiq if you’re a Chrome user who wants free or cheapest meeting AI and don’t mind a manual flow. For most teams running 5+ meetings/week, MeetGeek wins on time saved.

Winner: MeetGeek for teams · Tactiq for solo
