Opus Clip is a viral clip factory — drop in a 60-min video, get back 10-15 short-form clips with virality scoring. We tested both for 30+ days across YouTube-to-shorts repurposing and marketing team workflows.

Key features compared

Capsule strengths

  • Full AI video editor — captions, highlights, transitions
  • Brand-kit lock-in for teams (fonts, colors, lower thirds)
  • Silence removal + jump-cut automation
  • Team collaboration with comments + approvals
  • Strong for marketing teams + agencies

Opus Clip strengths

  • Drop in long video → get 10-15 shorts in minutes
  • Virality scoring (predicts which clip will perform)
  • Auto-reframes to 9:16 with face-tracking
  • AI-generated captions + emojis per clip
  • Built for creators + podcasters doing YT-to-TikTok

The verdict

Capsule wins for marketing teams editing long-form content — brand-kit, full editor, team collab. Opus Clip wins for creators repurposing long videos into shorts — virality scoring + auto-reframe is genuinely magic. If you're a creator podcasting + posting 10+ shorts/week, Opus Clip. If you're a marketing team editing webinars + product demos, Capsule.

How we tested

We ran the same 60-minute webinar through Opus Clip, and edited a separate 60-min product demo with Capsule. We measured: time saved, output quality, virality score correlation with actual performance (we posted both), and per-clip editing time.
