The verdict

Pick Mem0 if you want to ship an AI product with memory this week. The managed option saves you weeks of engineering. Pick Letta if you need full control, plan to self-host, or have strict data-residency requirements. Both are excellent — the choice is managed speed vs. open-source control.

Winner: Mem0 for speed, Letta for control
