The verdict

Use Durable if you run a solo service business and need a professional site online by tonight — $15/mo includes CRM, invoicing, and AI marketing. Use Framer if you're a designer, agency, or growing brand that needs full design control, a real CMS, and a site that scales from 5 pages to 50. Durable is for owners; Framer is for designers.

Winner: Durable for service businesses (faster + all-in-one); Framer for designers + growing brands (more control + CMS)

Frequently asked questions

Can I switch from Durable to Framer later?

Not directly — there's no migration tool. You'd rebuild the site in Framer (or vice versa). Plan to start in the tool that matches your business stage. Solo service: Durable. Growing brand with a designer: Framer.

Does Framer have AI features like Durable?

Yes, but more limited. Framer's AI Wireframer generates a layout from a prompt; Framer's AI Translate and AI Style tools help with copy and design polish. Durable's AI goes further — full business profile to site, CRM, marketing, invoicing in one click.

Which is better for SEO?

Framer wins for SEO. Native CMS, better page speed, structured data controls, and sitemap support. Durable's SEO is basic — fine for local service businesses targeting a city, weak for content marketing plays.
