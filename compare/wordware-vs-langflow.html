The verdict

Pick Wordware if you want to ship AI products fast with a team that includes non-engineers. The built-in deploy + auth is a huge time-saver. Pick LangFlow if you're an engineer who wants full control, needs to self-host for compliance, or already lives in the LangChain ecosystem.

Winner: Wordware for team product velocity, LangFlow for open-source control
