LangChain vs LlamaIndex

LangChain vs LlamaIndex head-to-head. The two leading LLM orchestration frameworks compared on agents, RAG, ease of use, and production readiness.

The verdict

Pick LangChain if you’re building agents, tool-using apps, or multi-step LLM workflows. Pick LlamaIndex if your app is fundamentally a RAG pipeline over documents (legal, finance, healthcare, research). For most teams in 2026, LangChain is the safe default — use LlamaIndex if your data is mostly documents and PDFs.

Winner: LangChain for agents · LlamaIndex for RAG
