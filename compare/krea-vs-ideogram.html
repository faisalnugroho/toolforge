Krea vs Ideogram

Krea and Ideogram are both AI image generators that solve specific problems other models don't. Krea is the real-time, sketch-to-image tool — start drawing and the AI completes it live. Ideogram is the typography specialist — it generates readable text in images, which most other AI image models (including Midjourney) still struggle with. We tested both on 50+ prompts spanning illustration, design, and text-heavy graphics.

The verdict

Use Ideogram if you need text in your images — posters, logos, book covers, social graphics, product mockups. It's the only AI image tool that consistently produces readable, well-typeset text. Use Krea for everything else — real-time sketch-to-image, AI video (their new Luma partnership), and faster ideation. They complement each other more than they compete.

Winner: Ideogram (for text) · Krea (for real-time + video)

How we tested

We generated 50+ images on each: 20 with text in them (logos, posters, social posts), 20 illustrations (no text), and 10 UI/UX mockups. We scored on prompt adherence, text legibility, aesthetic quality, and speed. Krea was tested on the $10/mo plan; Ideogram on the $7/mo Basic plan.
