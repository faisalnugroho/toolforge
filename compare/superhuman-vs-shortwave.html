Superhuman vs Shortwave

Two premium AI email clients fighting for the fast-inbox crown. Superhuman is the speed-obsessed incumbent; Shortwave is the AI-native challenger. We tested both for two weeks.

Head-to-head

Aspect Superhuman Shortwave
AI features Command bar + AI write/summarize AI threads, summaries, and an assistant inbox

The verdict
