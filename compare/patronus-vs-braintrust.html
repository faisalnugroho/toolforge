Patronus AI vs Braintrust

If you're an AI team in 2026, you've probably narrowed your eval tool choice to Patronus AI or Braintrust. They're the two most-mentioned platforms in the LLM eval space, but they have fundamentally different philosophies. Patronus is safety-first: pre-built evaluators for hallucination, toxicity, PII, RAG grounding. Braintrust is engineering-first: per-PR scoring, experiment tracking, dataset versioning. We tested both for 30+ days across a customer support RAG pipeline. Here's who wins for which team.

Key features compared

Patronus AI strengths

  • Pre-built hallucination, toxicity, PII evaluators
  • Out-of-the-box RAG grounding scores
  • Online monitoring at scale (100% of prod traffic)
  • Open-source evaluator library (even without paid)
  • Used by Notion, JetBlue, Accenture

Braintrust strengths

  • Per-PR scoring in CI — catch regressions before merge
  • Custom scorers: LLM-as-judge, code, human
  • Experiment tracking + dataset versioning
  • Synthetic test case generation from prod traffic
  • SOC 2, HIPAA, EU residency on paid tiers

The verdict

Patronus wins for safety/compliance buyers — pre-built hallucination + PII evaluators save weeks of custom work. Braintrust wins for engineering velocity — per-PR scoring + experiments are a 10x for active dev teams. If your biggest risk is a hallucination in a regulated industry, Patronus. If your biggest bottleneck is shipping reliable LLM features fast, Braintrust.

How we tested

We built a customer support RAG pipeline, ran both tools' evaluators over 200 production queries, and measured: setup time, eval quality (correlated with human review), how each catches prompt regressions, and total cost at scale. We don't accept payment for reviews — see our full disclosure.
