The verdict

Use Windsurf if you want a polished, production-ready AI editor with the Cascade agent — it's the closest Cursor competitor. Use Trae if you want a free AI editor powered by ByteDance's models and don't mind a newer, less mature product. Windsurf has the edge on stability and features; Trae wins on price.

Winner: Windsurf for production · Trae for free access

How we tested

Both tools were used for 28 days across real-world coding tasks. We scored on AI quality, speed, multi-file editing, stability, and pricing. We don't accept payment for reviews — see our full disclosure.
