The comparison

Suno and Meta’s MusicGen both turn text prompts into music, but they target completely different users. Suno is a polished consumer product with vocals, lyrics, and a beautiful UI. MusicGen is Meta’s open-source research model you run locally. Here’s how they compare in 2026.

Feature Suno v5 MusicGen (Meta)
Output length Up to 4 minutes Up to 30 seconds (extend by chaining)
Vocals/lyrics Yes (full songs) No (instrumental only)

Commercial use Paid plans only ($24/mo) Yes (under Meta license)

The verdict

For 95% of creators — musicians, content creators, podcasters — Suno wins on quality, ease of use, and full songs with lyrics. For developers building music products or indie hackers who want full ownership and zero monthly fees, MusicGen is the foundation you should build on.
