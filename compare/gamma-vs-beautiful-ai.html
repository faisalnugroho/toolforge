The verdict

Use Gamma if you want AI to generate 80% of the deck and you just want to tweak and present. Use Beautiful AI if you want to build a polished deck slide-by-slide with smart templates that auto-arrange content beautifully. Different speeds for different needs.

Winner: Gamma (speed/AI) · Beautiful AI (design polish)

AI capabilities

Gamma's AI is genuinely best-in-class: type a prompt, get a full 10-slide deck in 30 seconds with images, charts, and formatting. Beautiful AI added AI features in 2025 but they're more assistive (generate an outline, suggest layouts) — you still build slide-by-slide. If you want pure AI speed, Gamma wins.

Design polish

Beautiful AI's smart templates have been refined for 8+ years. Every slide automatically applies beautiful spacing, alignment, and visual hierarchy. Gamma's AI-generated decks are good but need tweaking for client-facing work. For sales pitches, Beautiful AI still wins on polish.

Output formats

Gamma exports to PowerPoint, PDF, and shareable links. It also functions as a website (publish a deck as a landing page). Beautiful AI exports to PowerPoint and PDF. Gamma wins on output flexibility.

Brand consistency

Beautiful AI has better brand kit management — logos, fonts, colors applied across all decks. Gamma added brand kits in 2025 but the implementation is less mature. For teams that need consistent branding, Beautiful AI wins.

How we tested

We created 10 decks on each tool (sales pitches, training materials, board updates, internal docs). We measured: time-to-first-draft, design polish score, AI quality, collaboration features. We don't accept payment for reviews — see our full disclosure.
