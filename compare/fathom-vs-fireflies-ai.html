Free tier comparison

Fathom wins decisively on free tier. Unlimited recordings, unlimited transcription, unlimited storage — all free, for 1-5 users. Fireflies' free tier is also generous (unlimited recordings for 1 user) but limits storage to 800 minutes.

AI summary quality

Fathom's AI summaries are more concise. Fireflies' summaries are longer and more comprehensive but often feel bloated. Both are accurate, but Fathom's are shareable as-is.

CRM integrations

Fireflies wins on CRM. Native HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive, Zoho integrations. Fathom's CRM integrations are decent but Fireflies' are deeper, with automatic field mapping.

UI and UX

Fathom's UI is cleaner. Fireflies' UI is more functional but denser. Both have mobile apps that are serviceable.

Compliance

Both HIPAA, GDPR, SOC 2. Fireflies has a slight edge on enterprise features (audit logs, SSO).

The verdict

Pick Fathom if you want a free meeting recorder with great AI summaries. Pick Fireflies if you live in a CRM and need deep sync. For most people, Fathom's free tier is hard to beat.

How we tested

Both tools were used for 30-60 days across real-world tasks. We scored on quality, ease of use, features, pricing, and support. We don't accept payment for reviews — see our full disclosure.

FAQ

Does the bot show up on calls?

Both tools use a bot that joins the call. The bot's name is editable in settings. Some users find this annoying — if you do, try Granola (no bot, opt-in audio).

Which is better for HIPAA?

Both are HIPAA-compliant on paid plans. Fireflies' Business plan is more enterprise-friendly.

Can I use both?

Yes. Some teams use Fireflies for the CRM sync and Fathom as the lightweight free option for personal use.
