VEED vs Kapwing

VEED and Kapwing are the two leading browser-based video editors in 2026. VEED wins on AI features (subtitles, TTS) and solo creator UX. Kapwing wins on team collaboration and template library. We edited the same video on both — here's who wins for your workflow.

VE
Option A

VEED

Browser-based video editor with strong AI features

Price: From $12/mo Lite
✓ Pros
  • Auto-subtitles in 100+ languages — best in class
  • AI text-to-speech with stock voices
  • Screen recording + teleprompter built-in
  • Background removal is fast and accurate
  • Clean, modern UI
✗ Cons
  • Watermark on free tier
  • Limited advanced editing vs Premiere/Final Cut
  • AI image gen is basic
  • Stock library smaller than Kapwing

Kapwing

Collaborative video editor for teams

Price: From $16/mo Pro
✓ Pros
  • Real-time team collaboration (Google Docs style)
  • Smart cut auto-removes silence
  • AI script-to-video from text
  • Massive template library (10,000+)
  • Auto-resize for social platforms
✗ Cons
  • Slower than VEED for solo editing
  • Subtitle accuracy slightly behind VEED
  • No native screen recording
  • Pricing is per-seat, expensive for teams
Try Kapwing →

The verdict

Use VEED if you're a solo creator who needs best-in-class subtitles, screen recording, and a clean editing experience. Use Kapwing if you're a team that collaborates on video and needs real-time editing + templates. Both are excellent browser-based editors — pick based on team vs solo.

How we tested

We edited the same 10-minute YouTube video on both platforms, adding subtitles, B-roll, transitions, and a thumbnail. Measured editing time, subtitle accuracy, AI feature quality, collaboration features, and final export quality.
