Lumen5 vs Pictory

Lumen5 and Pictory are both script-to-video AI tools with millions of users. But they target different workflows. Lumen5 is the blog-repurposing tool: paste a URL, get a video with stock footage + captions in 5 minutes. Pictory is the script-to-video tool: paste a script or upload a webinar, get a polished short. We tested both for 30+ days across blog-to-video, script-to-video, and webinar-to-shorts workflows.

Key features compared

Lumen5 strengths

  • Blog-to-video in 5 minutes (paste URL)
  • 10M+ stock footage library
  • Brand kit lock-in (fonts, colors, watermark)
  • Auto-suggests scenes + visuals from blog content
  • Strong for content marketers repurposing blogs

Pictory strengths

  • Script-to-video with auto voiceover
  • Webinar/long-video → shareable shorts
  • Auto-captions in 30+ languages
  • AI-powered scene selection + transitions
  • Strong for coaches, trainers, sales teams

The verdict

Lumen5 wins for content marketing teams repurposing blogs — paste a URL, get a video in 5 minutes. Pictory wins for coaches/trainers with scripts — auto scene selection + captions produce more polished script-to-video output. If your content starts as a blog, Lumen5. If your content starts as a script or webinar, Pictory.

How we tested

We ran 10 blog posts through Lumen5 and 5 scripts/webinars through Pictory. We measured: time to first usable video, output quality (reviewed by 3 marketers), brand consistency, and how each handles technical content. We don't accept payment for reviews — see our full disclosure.
