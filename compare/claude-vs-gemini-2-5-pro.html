The verdict

Claude excels at nuanced writing and careful reasoning. If your work involves long-form writing, code review, or tasks that require careful thought over flashy speed, Claude is the better choice. Its writing has more personality and its reasoning is more transparent.

Gemini 2.5 Pro wins on multimodal tasks and massive context. The 1M+ token context window means you can feed it entire codebases, long documents, or hours of video. The native Google Search integration and multimodal capabilities (video, audio) are unmatched.

Our pick: Claude for writing, coding, and professional work where quality matters. Gemini 2.5 Pro for research, multimodal tasks, and anything requiring massive context. Many power users subscribe to both.
