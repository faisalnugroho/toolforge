Lemlist vs Instantly.ai

Lemlist vs Instantly.ai head-to-head. Both are cold outreach platforms, but one does multichannel with personalized videos, the other does raw volume with AI reply prediction.

The verdict

Pick Lemlist if you want personalized images/videos, multichannel sequences (email + LinkedIn), or you sell to EU enterprise with strict GDPR. Pick Instantly.ai if you want the cheapest volume-led cold email with AI reply prediction. There’s no wrong answer — try both free trials.

Winner: Lemlist for personalization · Instantly for volume
