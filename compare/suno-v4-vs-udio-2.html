The verdict

Suno v4 for end-to-end songs and ease of use. Udio 2 for producers who need stems, MIDI, and mixing control.

Where Suno v4 wins

  • Polished end-to-end tracks, vocals, easier UI
  • Better consumer UX — fewer steps from prompt to song
  • Stronger vocal quality and lyric interpretation

Where Udio 2 wins

  • Stem export, MIDI, mixing controls, producer-grade
  • Better for professional music production workflows
  • More genre flexibility and stylistic control
