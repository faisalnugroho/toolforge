The head-to-head

n8n is the better choice for developers and teams that want to self-host, write custom code, and go AI-native without per-operation billing. Make is the better choice for non-technical users who want a beautiful UI and 1,000+ ready app integrations. Pick n8n for control and cost-at-scale; pick Make for speed-to-build and breadth of apps.
