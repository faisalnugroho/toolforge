Cognee vs Mem

Cognee and Mem0 are both memory layers for AI agents, but they have very different philosophies. Cognee goes deep (knowledge graphs, temporal reasoning, entity resolution). Mem0 stays simple (memories as records, semantic search).

The head-to-head

Cognee is the deep option — if you're building a research agent that needs to reason across what it learned weeks ago, Cognee handles it. Mem0 is the pragmatic option — five lines of code, your chatbot remembers users between sessions. Most teams should start with Mem0 and only graduate to Cognee when Mem0's flat-memory model starts losing context.

Built a personal-assistant agent in each that needed to remember: (1) user preferences stated weeks ago, (2) facts that changed over time ('old job' vs 'new job'), (3) relationships between entities ('Alice works at the company Bob founded'). Measured recall accuracy, setup time, and query latency.
