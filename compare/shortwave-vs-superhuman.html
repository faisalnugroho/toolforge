The verdict

Use **Shortwave** if you want AI email features (summaries, drafts, bundles) at a fair price. The free tier alone is better than most paid email apps. Use **Superhuman** if you send 100+ emails a day and value the keyboard-shortcut-driven UX, read receipts, and status as the 'default exec email app.' For 90% of people, Shortwave wins on value. For 10% who genuinely use it 4+ hours/day, Superhuman pays for itself.

Winner: Shortwave on value · Superhuman on premium UX
