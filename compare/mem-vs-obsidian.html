The verdict

Mem is the fastest way to capture and resurface notes with AI — great if you hate organizing. Obsidian is the power user's vault: total ownership, infinite plugins, but you build the system. Pick Mem for speed, Obsidian for control.

Winner: Obsidian for control, Mem for speed
