The verdict

Use NotebookLM for short-term research: upload a PDF, ask questions, get AI-generated summaries and podcast overviews. Use Obsidian for building a long-term knowledge base: write notes, link ideas, and grow your second brain over years. They serve different needs — many people use both. NotebookLM for quick research; Obsidian for permanent knowledge.

Winner: NotebookLM for research, Obsidian for knowledge management

How we tested
