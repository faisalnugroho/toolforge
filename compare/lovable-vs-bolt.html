Lovable vs Bolt

Lovable and Bolt are the two leading AI full-stack app builders in 2026. Both let you build web apps from natural language prompts, but they have different strengths. We built the same app on both platforms to compare.

The verdict

Lovable produces more polished frontends. If your app is UI-heavy and you want it to look production-ready from day one, Lovable's design sense is noticeably better. The Supabase integration is seamless.

Bolt is faster for full-stack prototypes. It supports more frameworks (Next.js, Vue, Svelte) and gives you more control over the tech stack. If you need to build something complex quickly and don't mind polishing the UI later, Bolt is the better choice.

Our pick: Lovable for client-facing apps and MVPs where design matters. Bolt for internal tools and rapid prototyping where speed matters more than polish.
