The verdict

Choose watsonx when governance, audit trails, and hybrid deployment are non-negotiable — finance, healthcare, public sector. Choose Bedrock when you want maximum model choice with AWS-native guardrails and already run on Amazon. For most regulated buyers, IBM's governance edge wins.
