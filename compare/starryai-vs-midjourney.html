The verdict

Use Midjourney for professional, commercial-grade images. Use StarryAI for casual mobile creation and when you want a usable free tier. They serve different audiences — Midjourney for serious creators, StarryAI for everyone with a phone.

Winner: Midjourney (quality) · StarryAI (portability + free tier)

Image quality

Midjourney v7 produces noticeably sharper, more photorealistic, and more artistically coherent images. StarryAI's models (Altair, Orion, Argo) are competent but visually a generation behind. For thumbnails, social posts, and personal projects, StarryAI is fine. For client work and print, Midjourney is mandatory.

Mobile vs desktop

StarryAI's mobile apps (iOS + Android) are polished and fast. Midjourney's web interface works on mobile but feels cramped. If you generate on the go, StarryAI wins by a mile.

Free tier

StarryAI gives 5 free credits every day — about 150 per month. Midjourney offers a free trial of ~25 images, then requires $10/mo minimum. StarryAI wins on free tier generosity by a long shot.

How we tested

Both tools were used for 30 days. We generated the same prompts across both, scored on quality, ease of use, mobile experience, and pricing. We don't accept payment for reviews — see our full disclosure.
