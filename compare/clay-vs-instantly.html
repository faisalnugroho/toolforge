Clay vs Instantly

Clay and Instantly are the two breakout outbound platforms of 2025-2026. Clay is the AI-powered data enrichment and personalization engine. Instantly is the high-volume deliverability-focused cold email sender. We tested both on 50K+ outbound sends across 30 campaigns. Here's who wins.

The verdict

Use Clay if personalization quality and data depth matter more than volume — niche campaigns, ABM motions, and AI-personalized copy at scale. Use Instantly if you need to send 50K+ emails/month across many accounts and deliverability is everything. Many teams use both — Clay for enrichment + copy, then push to Instantly for sending.

Winner: Clay for personalization and data, Instantly for volume and deliverability

Data enrichment

Clay wins decisively. With 75+ data providers (Apollo, ZoomInfo, Clearbit, LinkedIn, Crunchbase, and 70 more) plus AI-powered "Claygent" web scraper, Clay can enrich a contact with 50+ data points in seconds. Instantly has basic enrichment (email, name, company) but relies on you to bring enriched lists. For "I want to know their funding, tech stack, recent hires, and a custom data point" — Clay is the only option.

AI personalization

Clay wins. Its AI Email Copywriter pulls from your enriched data and generates personalized intros, PS lines, and CTAs that reference the lead's company, role, or recent activity. Output quality is genuinely good — not "Hi {first_name}, I noticed your company {company} is in {industry}" template-y. Instantly has AI personalization (added 2025) but it's simpler and works off basic fields.

Sending & deliverability

Instantly wins decisively. Built for high-volume senders — unlimited email accounts on most tiers, the strongest deliverability infrastructure in the category (built-in warm-up across its own network of 200K+ inboxes), inbox rotation, and auto-stop on reply. Clay has sending capability but it's basic — most Clay users connect to Instantly or Smartlead for actual sends.

Pricing & value

Instantly wins on entry price ($30/mo vs Clay's $149/mo). At higher tiers, both are competitive — Instantly Unlimited at $96/mo is the sweet spot for solo senders, while Clay Pro at $349/mo is best for ABM teams that need the data layer. Credit-based pricing on Clay can feel unpredictable; Instantly's unlimited model is simpler.

Integrations & workflow

Clay wins on integration breadth — push enriched contacts to Instantly, Smartlead, HubSpot, Salesforce, Lemlist, and 100+ destinations via webhook. Instantly integrates natively with the major CRMs but is more of an endpoint than a hub. Most advanced teams use Clay → Instantly as a workflow.

How we tested

We enriched 5,000 leads on Clay, pushed 50K+ sends through Instantly across 30 campaigns, and tracked reply rates, deliverability (inbox placement), and time-to-meeting. We don't accept payment for reviews — see our full disclosure.
