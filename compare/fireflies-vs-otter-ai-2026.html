Where they differ in 2026

Transcription accuracy: Otter still edges Fireflies on live, low-latency transcription — its on-screen captions arrive ~1 second faster and handle heavy accents slightly better. Fireflies has closed the gap a lot in the last 12 months, but Otter remains the safer pick for noisy calls.

AI summaries & action items: Fireflies pulls ahead here. Its 2026 "AskFred" follow-ups generate cleaner action-item lists, sales-coaching insights (talk/listen ratio, objections), and push directly into HubSpot, Salesforce, and Notion. Otter's summaries are good but more generic.

Integrations: Fireflies connects to 60+ tools (CRM-heavy). Otter focuses on calendar + Slack + the meeting platforms. If you live in a CRM, Fireflies wins; if you just want notes, Otter is simpler.

Privacy: Otter lets you exclude sensitive meetings and offers a no-training mode on business plans. Fireflies added SOC 2 + GDPR controls, but defaults to ingesting everything — check settings.

Price: Fireflies Pro is $10/mo (annual); Otter Pro is $16.99/mo. Free tiers are similar (~300 min/mo).

The verdict

Pick Fireflies.ai if you're a sales or revenue team that lives in a CRM — the post-call automation and coaching insights pay for themselves. Pick Otter.ai if you want the most accurate live notes for yourself or a small team and don't need a CRM pipeline.

Winner: Fireflies for teams · Otter for individuals

How we tested

Both bots joined the same 40+ calls across Zoom, Google Meet, and Microsoft Teams over 6 weeks.
