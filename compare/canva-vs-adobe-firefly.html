The verdict

Use Canva if you want to design end-to-end without touching pro software. Use Adobe Firefly (inside Photoshop/Express) if you need commercially-safe generation and pixel-level control. Non-designers: Canva. Pros: Firefly.

Winner: Canva for ease; Firefly for pro control

Head-to-head details

  • Use case: Canva = full editor; Firefly = generative engine
  • Pricing: Canva $15/mo; Firefly $10/mo
