The verdict

Pick Sembly if you have multilingual meetings or you care more about decisions and action items than raw transcripts. Pick Otter if you just want the most accurate English transcription and the most-recognized brand. For most international teams, Sembly wins on the "Sembly Moments" extraction alone. For US/UK teams doing English-only meetings, Otter's accuracy advantage is meaningful.

Winner: Sembly (multilingual + smarter summaries); Otter (English accuracy + brand recognition)
