Read AI vs Otter AI

Read AI and Otter are the two biggest names in AI meeting transcription — but they take very different approaches. Read AI is the analytics-first meeting coach (talk time, sentiment, meeting score). Otter is the pure transcription-and-summary tool. We tested both on 50+ meetings over 4 weeks. Here's who wins for which use case.

✓ Read AI pros

  • Best-in-class talk time and engagement analytics
  • Meeting score feature trains teams to run better meetings
  • Strong Salesforce/HubSpot integrations for sales
  • Sentiment analysis flags at-risk deals from customer calls

✗ Read AI cons

  • Smaller community than Otter — fewer tutorials
  • Free tier limited to 5 meetings/mo
  • Some users find the analytics overwhelming

✓ Otter AI pros

  • Longest-running meeting AI — most reliable transcription
  • Generous free tier (300 min/mo is enough for most)
  • Live transcription during meetings is rock-solid
  • OtterPilot auto-joins Zoom calls and takes notes

✗ Otter AI cons

  • No analytics or coaching features
  • English is the strongest language
  • Some privacy concerns (always-listening assistant)

The verdict

For sales teams, execs, and anyone who runs coaching conversations: Read AI wins on analytics alone. The talk-time data, meeting score, and sentiment analysis will transform how your team runs meetings. For general meeting notes and solo professionals: Otter wins. It's more reliable, has a better free tier, and does one thing really well (transcription). Both are good. Pick based on whether you need analytics or just notes.
