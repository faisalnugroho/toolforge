Fathom vs Fireflies

Fathom and Fireflies are the two meeting AI tools that have come to dominate the 'Otter alternative' conversation in 2026. Fathom is famous for being genuinely free (no usage limits). Fireflies is famous for its massive integration ecosystem and aggressive pricing. We tested both on real sales calls, team standups, and customer interviews for 6 weeks. Here's the honest breakdown.

The verdict

Pick Fathom if you want a genuinely free meeting AI with no usage limits and a clean, simple UI — it's the best free option in the category. Pick Fireflies if you need deep CRM/sales integrations (Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive) or want AI-powered search across your entire meeting history. For most individuals and small teams, Fathom wins on price. For sales teams and customer success orgs, Fireflies wins on integrations.

Winner: Fathom (free) · Fireflies (integrations)

How we tested

Both tools were used for 30-60 days across real-world tasks. We scored on quality, ease of use, features, pricing, and support. We don't accept payment for reviews — see our full disclosure.

Fathom pros & cons

✓ Fathom strengths

  • Genuinely free — unlimited recordings, unlimited minutes, no time cap
  • Cleanest UI of any meeting AI — feels like Notion for meetings
  • AI summaries are concise and actionable
  • Syncs to HubSpot, Salesforce, Notion, Slack automatically
  • HIPAA-compliant on the Team plan

✗ Fathom weaknesses

  • AI search across meetings (Fathom Chat) is newer and less mature than Fireflies' AskFred
  • Fewer integrations than Fireflies overall
  • No mobile recording (web/Zoom only)
  • No AI voice agents or call coaching

Fireflies pros & cons

✓ Fireflies strengths

  • Massive integration ecosystem — 40+ tools including Salesforce, HubSpot, Slack, Zapier
  • AskFred AI search across all your meeting history is best-in-class
  • Generous free tier (800 min/mo of storage)
  • Soundbites let you share 1-min AI-generated clips from meetings
  • Mobile app records in-person meetings well

✗ Fireflies weaknesses

  • Free tier caps storage at 800 min (then you lose old recordings)
  • $10/mo Pro is more expensive than Fathom's free tier
  • UI is more cluttered than Fathom's minimalist design
  • AI summaries can be too long — needs more human editing before sharing

Who should pick Fathom?

Individuals and small teams who want a genuinely free, polished meeting AI with no usage caps. Especially great for founders, consultants, and small agencies who don't want another subscription.

Who should pick Fireflies?

Sales teams that need deep Salesforce/HubSpot integration, customer success orgs that want AI search across hundreds of customer calls, larger teams that need granular admin controls, and anyone who values Fireflies' AskFred for searching meeting history.
