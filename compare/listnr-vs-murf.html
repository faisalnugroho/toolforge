Listnr vs Murf (2026)

Two AI voice generators. Listnr is the all-in-one podcasting play. Murf is the studio-quality voiceover platform.

The short answer

Murf wins on studio-quality voice for marketing video and e-learning. Listnr wins on podcast hosting, all-in-one workflow, and 140 languages. For YouTubers and course creators who need voiceover-only, Murf is sharper. For podcasters who need voice + hosting + distribution in one tool, Listnr is the answer.

Listnr in depth

Listnr started as a podcast hosting platform and added AI voice in 2023. In 2026, it's the only all-in-one tool that does AI voice generation AND podcast hosting AND distribution to Spotify/Apple/Google in one dashboard. 900+ voices in 140+ languages covers every conceivable niche. The free tier is generous (1,000 words/month, no credit card). Where Listnr loses: voice quality is "very good" but not the studio-grade polish that Murf delivers. If your voiceover is the whole product (a podcast, an audiobook), Listnr is great. If your voiceover supports a visual product (a marketing video, an explainer), the quality gap matters.

Murf in depth

Murf is the studio-grade voice platform. 200+ voices in 20+ languages, but every voice is tuned for warmth, pacing, and emphasis — Murf voices sound like professional voice actors, not synthesized TTS. The editor lets you adjust pitch, speed, pause length, and emphasis per word. Integrations with Canva, Google Slides, and Adobe Captivate make it the go-to for e-learning teams. Enterprise tier adds voice cloning, SSO, and a dedicated account manager. Where Murf loses: 20 languages is a hard ceiling if you produce multilingual content at scale.

When to pick which

✓ Pick Listnr if…

  • You're producing podcasts and need hosting + distribution in one tool
  • You need 140+ languages — for global content, this is unmatched
  • You want generous free tier (1,000 words/month, no card)
  • You're a multilingual creator and voice quality is "good enough," not "studio perfect"

✓ Pick Murf if…

  • Voice quality is the product — marketing video, e-learning, explainer
  • You need studio-grade voices that pass for human voice actors
  • You're producing primarily in English (or 1-2 other languages)
  • You need word-level control over pitch, emphasis, and pacing

The verdict

If you're producing podcasts or multilingual content at scale, Listnr is unbeatable — the all-in-one hosting + AI voice + distribution workflow saves real time. If you're producing English marketing video, e-learning modules, or any content where voice quality is the brand, Murf's studio-grade voices justify the higher price. Both are good. The decision is "all-in-one podcasting play" vs "studio voiceover for visual content."
