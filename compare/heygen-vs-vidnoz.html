HeyGen vs Vidnoz (2026)

Two AI avatar tools. One starts at $22/mo, the other at $24/mo. Which is worth it?

The short answer

HeyGen wins on avatar quality and lip-sync. Vidnoz wins on price, avatar library size, and the most generous free tier in the category (1 free minute/day, no credit card). For most solo creators, Vidnoz is the new default. For premium enterprise avatars where every frame matters, HeyGen still wins.

HeyGen in depth

HeyGen has been the avatar-quality leader since 2023. The lip-sync is the best in the industry — better than Synthesia, Colossyan, and D-ID. At $24/mo Creator, it includes 175+ stock avatars, voice cloning in 40 languages, and 1080p export. The 4K export and brand kit come in at the higher tiers ($72/mo Business). The 2025 launch of "Avatar Pro" custom cloning ($99 one-time + ongoing subscription) is genuinely the best-looking custom avatar you can get without a Hollywood capture stage.

Vidnoz in depth

Vidnoz is the dark horse of the avatar category. While HeyGen and Synthesia compete on premium enterprise pricing, Vidnoz undercuts them with a $22/mo Starter tier, a $52/mo Pro tier, and — most importantly — a free tier that gives you 1 minute of 1080p video every day with no credit card. The avatar library (1,500+) is 8x deeper than HeyGen's, and voice clone quality is comparable. Where Vidnoz loses: lip-sync is 90% as polished as HeyGen, which matters for "talking head" customer-facing videos but is invisible for internal training and most social content.

When to pick which

✓ Pick HeyGen if…

  • You're producing customer-facing videos where lip-sync quality is non-negotiable
  • You need 4K export and brand kit control
  • You're an enterprise that needs SSO, SCORM, or Salesforce integrations
  • Your scripts are short (<60s) and price is secondary to polish

✓ Pick Vidnoz if…

  • You're a solo creator or small team producing 2-10 videos/month
  • You want the deepest avatar library in the category (1,500+)
  • You publish in 5+ languages and need 140+ voice options
  • Budget matters and the free tier (1 min/day) actually fits your workflow

The verdict

If you're an enterprise producing training videos where lip-sync precision matters, HeyGen is still the best in class and worth the $24/mo. For everyone else — solo creators, marketing teams, course sellers, agencies doing social content at scale — Vidnoz is the smarter choice. $22/mo vs $24/mo is comparable, but the 1,500+ avatars and the real free tier (1 min/day, no card) make Vidnoz the most flexible option in the category.
