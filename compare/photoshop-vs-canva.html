The head-to-head

Photoshop is irreplaceable for professional retouching, compositing, and anything pixel-precise — designers, photographers, and studios need it. Canva is the right tool for 90% of business design: social posts, decks, flyers, and quick edits made by people who aren't designers. Use Canva by default; reach for Photoshop when the job demands real image manipulation.
