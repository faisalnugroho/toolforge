Fireflies and tl;dv are the two leaders in the "join your Zoom/Meet/Teams, transcribe, summarize, search" category. The verdict

Use Fireflies if you're in sales, customer success, or any role where meeting notes need to flow into HubSpot, Salesforce, or Pipedrive automatically. Their integration graph is wider than anyone's. Use tl;dv if you're in product, UX research, or design — the clip-and-tag UI, multi-language transcription, and topic-based highlights are the best in category. tl;dv is also better for async teams because clips are instantly shareable and the UI doesn't scream "enterprise."

Winner: Fireflies for sales/CRM · tl;dv for product/research

How we tested

100+ meetings over 6 weeks across Zoom, Google Meet, and Teams. Scored on transcription accuracy (measured against human transcripts), summary quality, integration breadth, clip/tag UX, search speed, and price-to-value.
