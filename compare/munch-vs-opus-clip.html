The verdict

Use Opus Clip if you're a solo creator or small team that just needs good clips at a fair price — $19/mo is unbeatable. Use Munch if you're a marketing team or agency that wants the trend analytics and multi-language support — the $49/mo pays for itself with better-performing clips. Both produce solid output; the question is whether you need the analytics layer.

Winner: Opus Clip for solo creators, Munch for teams
