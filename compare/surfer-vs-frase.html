Surfer vs Frase

Surfer and Frase are the two most popular AI-powered SEO content tools of 2026. Both optimize content for search ranking, both have AI writers, both score your drafts against the SERP. But they take very different approaches: Surfer is a full-stack platform with a content audit engine and topical authority scoring; Frase is a focused writer's tool with AI briefs and an integrated writing UI. We tested both on 30+ articles across 8 months.

The verdict

Pick Surfer if you're an agency or content team running 50+ articles/month and need clustering, audit, and topical authority at scale. Pick Frase if you're a solo blogger or small team writing 5-20 articles/month and want an affordable AI writing + brief tool. Surfer wins on data depth; Frase wins on price-to-value.

Who should pick Surfer?

Agencies managing 10+ clients, in-house content teams at SaaS companies, SEO power users who want the deepest SERP analysis. Surfer's topical authority and cluster features are unmatched. The $89/mo is steep but pays for itself if you're publishing >50 articles/month.

Who should pick Frase?

Solo bloggers, niche site builders, small content teams (1-3 people), anyone who wants AI writing + SEO briefs without the agency-tier price. Frase's $14.99/mo is one of the best values in SEO tools in 2026. The UI is simpler and the AI writer is genuinely good for blog-style content.
