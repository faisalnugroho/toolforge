GPTZero vs Originality.ai

GPTZero and Originality.ai are the two leading AI content detectors in 2026. GPTZero is the teacher default with a generous free tier. Originality.ai is the publisher default with bundled plagiarism check. We tested both on 200 documents — here's who wins and the limits you must know.

G0
Option A

GPTZero

Best for teachers and individuals — free tier, sentence highlighting

Price: From $10/mo Essential
✓ Pros
  • Most-used detector on the market — 2.5M+ users
  • Sentence-level highlighting is uniquely useful
  • LMS integrations (Canvas, Blackboard, Moodle) are best-in-class
  • Generous free tier (10,000 words/mo)
  • Updates fast when new LLMs ship
✗ Cons
  • False positive rate ~5-15% on short texts (<200 words)
  • No detector is definitive — must pair with human judgment
  • Ethical concerns — relying on detectors for academic punishment is risky
  • Doesn't catch heavily edited AI text

Try Originality.ai →

The verdict

Use GPTZero if you're a teacher or individual — free tier, sentence highlighting, and LMS integrations are perfect for education. Use Originality.ai if you're a publisher or agency — the bundled plagiarism + fact-check + team features pay for themselves. NEVER rely on either as a smoking gun — both must be paired with human judgment.

How we tested

Both detectors were tested on 200 documents: 50 human-written, 50 AI-generated (ChatGPT/Claude/Gemini), 50 mixed, and 50 borderline cases (heavy editing, paraphrasing). We measured true-positive rate, false-positive rate, and confidence scores.
