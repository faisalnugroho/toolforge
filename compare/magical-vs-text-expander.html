Magical vs TextExpander

Last updated June 16, 2026 · Reviewed by ToolForge Editorial

Magical vs TextExpander is the modern vs classic matchup. Magical is browser-first, free for the core features, and adds unique cross-app Autofill (move LinkedIn data to Salesforce in 3 seconds). TextExpander is more mature, works on desktop too, and supports powerful scripting/AppleScript. Magical wins for browser-based SDRs and recruiters; TextExpander wins for power users needing desktop coverage.
