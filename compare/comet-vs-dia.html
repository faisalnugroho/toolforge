The verdict

Pick Comet if...

Comet wins if you live in research mode -- reading long articles, comparing products, doing competitive analysis. The Perplexity integration is best-in-class.

  • Perplexity search built into every tab
  • Agent can book flights, shop, fill forms
  • Best answer quality of any browser AI
  • Tab organization is genuinely useful
  • Free tier exists (invite-only)

Pick Dia if...

Dia wins if you want a polished, fast browser with ChatGPT available everywhere. The Arc DNA means tab management is genuinely better than Chrome.

  • Built on Arc's award-winning foundation
  • Cleaner UI than Comet
  • ChatGPT sidebar on every page
  • Better tab management (Arc DNA)
  • Faster than Comet on heavy pages

Bottom line

Comet for research and AI-heavy browsing. Dia for a polished daily driver with ChatGPT everywhere.
