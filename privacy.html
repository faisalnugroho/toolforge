Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-15

What we collect

ToolForge uses Plausible Analytics, a privacy-friendly analytics tool. We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any cross-site tracking. We collect:

  • Page URLs you visit (e.g. /tools/jasper.html)
  • Referrer (e.g. Google, Twitter, direct)
  • Country (derived from IP, not stored)
  • Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)

We do not collect your IP address, fingerprint your browser, or track you across other sites.

Cookies

We use zero advertising cookies and zero third-party tracking cookies. Plausible Analytics is cookieless by design.

Affiliate links

Some links on this site are affiliate links. When you click one and sign up for a product, we may earn a commission. This never costs you extra — and is the only way we monetize. We only recommend tools we'd pay for ourselves. Read our full disclosure →

Newsletter

If you sign up for our newsletter, we store your email. We never sell, share, or rent it. You can unsubscribe with one click from any email.
