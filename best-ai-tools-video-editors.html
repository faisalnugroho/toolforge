The video editor's AI workflow

For most YouTubers and short-form creators, the daily workflow now looks like: (1) Record raw footage in 4K. (2) Import to Descript, transcribe automatically. (3) Edit by deleting words from transcript. AI removes filler words. (4) Auto-generate 5-10 short clips for TikTok/Reels. (5) Use ElevenLabs to translate to other languages. (6) Use Runway or Kling for B-roll that doesn't exist. Total time: 2-3 hours for what used to take 8-10 hours.

What top video editors are using in 2026

Three categories: (1) Editors who refuse AI: doing 1 video/week, falling behind. (2) Editors who use AI for the boring 80%: shipping 3-5 videos/week, growing. (3) Editors who let AI do everything: producing generic content that doesn't differentiate. The sweet spot is #2 — AI for assembly, human for creative decisions, transitions, and final polish.
