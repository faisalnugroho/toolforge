Why we exist

The AI tool space is broken. Every "top AI tools" listicle is the same — ranked by who paid the writer, padded with tools that haven't been updated since 2023, full of affiliate links that don't disclose they're affiliate links.

We started ToolForge because we got tired of it. We're a small team of developers, designers, and marketers who use AI tools every day. We review the ones that earn their place in our stack, and we ignore the rest. No fluff, no padding, no BS.

Every tool on this site has been used by us for at least 30 days. Every review is updated quarterly. Every "best" pick has been stress-tested in real production workflows. If a tool doesn't earn its place, it doesn't get listed.

— How we curate

The methodology

5 criteria every tool must pass to make the list

1

30-day hands-on test

No tool is reviewed before we've used it for at least 30 days. We test in real workflows, not 5-minute demos.

2

Output quality

Does the output actually hold up? We test edge cases, failure modes, and consistency across multiple uses.

3

Real pricing transparency

We show actual cost, free tier limits, and where you'll hit paywalls. No hidden fees, no surprise charges.

4

Active development

AI moves fast. We only list tools that ship updates regularly. Abandoned products get cut.

5

Ethical considerations

We won't list tools with serious privacy issues, exploitative pricing, or harmful use cases — even if they're popular.

★

Community signal

We cross-reference reviews on Reddit, Twitter, and product communities. If real users hate it, we don't list it.

Affiliate disclosure

Transparency first: some links on this site are affiliate links. If you click one and sign up, we may earn a commission at no cost to you. This is how we keep the site free and the content honest.

Crucially: affiliate revenue does not influence our rankings or recommendations. We have, and will continue to, recommend tools that don't pay us affiliate commissions over tools that do, when the non-paying tool is better. The worst tool on this site is better than many tools that pay 50% recurring — because we don't list them.

If you find a tool on this site that doesn't live up to our review, please tell us. We'll re-test it, and if it falls short, we'll update the review or remove it entirely.

No paid placements. No "sponsored reviews." No "this post contains affiliate links" hidden in fine print at the bottom. Every review is independent and editorially controlled.
