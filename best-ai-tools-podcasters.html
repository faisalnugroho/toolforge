The podcaster's AI workflow

For a 60-minute weekly episode, the AI workflow looks like: (1) Record in studio. (2) Drop raw audio in Descript. Auto-transcribes in 2 min. (3) Edit by deleting filler words and mistakes. AI removes 15-20% of runtime automatically. (4) Generate 5-10 short video clips for social (Descript auto-clips). (5) Use ChatGPT to write show notes, episode titles, social posts. (6) Use Perplexity to research guest facts. (7) Use ElevenLabs for foreign language versions if international audience. Total: 2 hours vs 8 hours traditional.

What pro podcasters do differently

Top 1% podcasters (the ones making $10K+/month): (1) They don't use AI to make more episodes — they use it to make each episode better. More research, better editing, more promotion. (2) They invest in AI for promotion, not just production. AI-generated social clips drive 70%+ of their growth. (3) They use AI for repurposing — one 60-min episode becomes 30+ pieces of content across platforms. (4) They keep the human element. AI does the boring 80%, they focus on the valuable 20% (interviewing, storytelling, connection).
