Team headshots that actually look consistent

HeadshotPro specializes in one thing: turning selfies into professional headshots that look like they came from the same studio session. Upload photos of each team member, pick a style and background, and you get a set of uniform headshots ready for your About page, email signatures, and Slack — no scheduling conflicts, no photographer fees.

Who it's for: Startups, agencies, remote teams, and growing companies that need consistent headshots across the org without flying everyone to a studio.

Key features

Team Bulk upload

Upload photos for every team member at once. Each person gets 50+ headshots in the same style, background, and lighting — perfect for company directories.

100+ Backgrounds & outfits

Choose from corporate, creative, healthcare, outdoor, and studio backgrounds. Swap outfits — suits, blazers, casual — without anyone changing clothes.

4K High resolution

Download in 4K for print, web, and large displays. No watermarks. Royalty-free commercial usage included.

2 hrs Fast turnaround

Headshots are ready in under 2 hours. Re-generate variations instantly if someone doesn't like their first set.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Best-in-class for team consistency — everyone gets the same look
  • Bulk pricing makes it cheaper than individual sessions for 5+ people
  • Wide range of professional backgrounds and outfits
  • 4K downloads with commercial usage rights
  • 2-hour turnaround beats any photographer

✗ What doesn't

  • Individual plan ($29) is pricier than Aragon for the same output
  • AI can struggle with glasses, facial hair, and darker skin tones
  • Limited creative/fun styles — very corporate-focused
  • No API or Zapier integration for automated onboarding

Verdict

If you're a team of 5+ needing consistent headshots, HeadshotPro is the strongest choice. For individuals, Aragon AI offers more style variety at a similar price. For teams, HeadshotPro's bulk workflow and uniform output make it the winner.
