The fastest way to build internal tools in 2026

Retool is the platform engineering teams use when they need to build admin panels, dashboards, CRMs, and internal tools without the overhead of a full frontend framework. In 2026, Retool shipped Retool AI — a native AI chat component that connects to your database, your APIs, and your business logic. Ask natural language questions, get answers from your live data. No more Slack-threading your data team for a SQL query.

Who it's for: Engineering teams, ops teams, and startups that need internal tools fast. If you have data in Postgres, MySQL, MongoDB, or any API, Retool turns it into apps in hours, not weeks.

Key features

AI Chat Native AI chatbot component

Embed a chatbot that queries your database, calls your APIs, and returns formatted answers. Connect to GPT-4, Claude, or self-hosted models. Guardrails and RBAC included.

Drag-and-drop 100+ UI components

Tables, forms, charts, maps, file uploaders, and more. Drag them onto a canvas, wire them to your data sources, and ship. No CSS fights.

Code when you need Custom JavaScript & SQL

Every component supports custom JS transforms. Write SQL queries inline. When drag-and-drop isn't enough, code fills the gap.

Integrations Connects to everything

Postgres, MySQL, MongoDB, Snowflake, BigQuery, REST APIs, GraphQL, S3, Stripe, Salesforce, and 50+ more pre-built connectors.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Build internal tools in hours, not weeks — massive time savings
  • Retool AI chatbot is genuinely useful for ops teams querying live data
  • Free tier is generous — 5 apps, unlimited users, full features
  • Enterprise-grade RBAC, audit logs, and SSO for larger teams
