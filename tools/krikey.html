Motion capture that does not need a suit

Krikey lets anyone animate a 3D character by recording a short video of themselves — no mocap suit, no rigging knowledge. The AI estimates body pose, retargets it onto a library of characters, and exports clean FBX/GLB for games, films, or social posts.

Who it's for: Indie game devs, animators, educators, and video creators who need character animation without a 3D studio or motion-capture budget.

Key features

Video→Anim Video to animation

Record a 10-second clip on your phone; Krikey maps the motion onto a 3D avatar with editable keyframes.

Character Lib 3D character library

Dozens of rigged humanoid and creature characters, or upload your own GLB to retarget motion onto.

Editing Timeline & editing

Trim, blend, and smooth clips in a browser timeline, then layer multiple takes into one sequence.

Export Game-engine export

Export FBX/GLB to Unity, Unreal, Blender, or download MP4 for video and social.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • No mocap suit or 3D expertise required
  • Fast turnaround — minutes, not days
  • Clean exports into Unity/Unreal/Blender
  • Fun, approachable UI for non-animators

✗ What doesn't

  • Quality depends on camera angle and lighting
  • Character library is mostly humanoid
  • Free tier limits export resolution
  • Fine facial/ hand motion still limited

Verdict

Krikey is the easiest on-ramp to real 3D character animation in 2026. For game jams, explainer videos, and quick cuts it is magic; for film-grade facial performance you will still want a specialist. The free tier is a great place to start.
