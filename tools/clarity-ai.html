The analytics layer your ad stack was missing

Clarity AI plugs into your ad accounts and creative library, then uses computer vision to score every asset on predicted performance. Instead of waiting two weeks for an A/B test to declare a winner, you get a conversion-propensity score the moment you upload a banner or video.

Who it's for: Performance marketers, agency creative leads, and growth teams who burn budget testing creative blind.

Key features

Creative scoring Predictive grades

Every image or video gets a 0-100 likelihood-to-convert score, with the specific elements (face, CTA, color) driving the number.

Benchmarks Competitive intel

See how your creative performs against anonymized category benchmarks from thousands of campaigns.

Attribution Multi-touch modeling

Clarity's AI re-weights credit across touchpoints so you stop over-paying for last-click noise.

Integrations Native connectors

Meta, Google, TikTok, and Shopify pipe in automatically — no spreadsheet exports.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Creative scoring saves real ad-spend on bad assets
  • Benchmarks are genuinely useful for agencies pitching clients
  • Setup is fast — connectors just work
  • Clear, visual reporting non-analysts understand

✗ What doesn't

  • $49/mo entry is steep for solo creators
  • Predictions are directional, not a guarantee
  • Limited value if you run tiny ad budgets
  • UI can feel busy at first

Verdict

If you spend more than $5K/month on paid social, Clarity AI pays for itself by killing weak creative before launch. Below that budget, the free analytics in the ad platforms are usually enough.
