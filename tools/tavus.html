The personalization engine behind outbound video

Tavus is the only tool that solves a specific, expensive problem: how do you send a personal video to 5,000 leads without recording 5,000 videos? You record one template, Tavus clones your face and voice, then synthesizes a unique video per recipient that mentions their name, company, and custom fields. Recipients can't tell it's AI-generated — that's the point. Reply rates on cold outbound jump from 3% to 18%.

Who it's for: B2B sales teams, customer success orgs, marketing teams doing ABM, and recruiters running high-volume outreach. Less useful for one-off creators.

Key features

Clone Voice + face replica

Record 2 minutes of training footage. Tavus creates a high-fidelity digital twin of your face, voice, and gestures. Quality is genuinely unsettling — most people can't tell.

Variables Dynamic personalization

Drop CSV with names, companies, and custom fields. Tavus swaps variables in your script and re-generates the lip-sync, eye contact, and pronunciation in real time. Spanish names, company acronyms, all work.

API Programmatic generation

Trigger video generation from your CRM (HubSpot, Salesforce), your outbound tool (Outreach, SalesLoft), or a webhook. Generate a video when a lead opens an email. Wild stuff.

Stock Avatars No-camera option

Don't want to use your own face? Tavus ships 30+ stock AI avatars (diverse, professional, voice-matched). Useful for product demos and support videos at scale.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Reply rates on cold outbound: 3% text → 18% personalized video. Documented.
  • Clone quality is best-in-class — lip sync, eye contact, micro-expressions all on point
  • API + CRM integrations make it slot into existing sales stacks in hours, not weeks
  • Stock avatar library is huge — useful for non-camera-shy teams
  • Compliance features: consent capture, opt-out, audit logs for every video

✗ What doesn't

  • $225/mo Starter is steep for solo founders — minimum commitment is real
  • Clone quality depends on your recording — bad lighting = bad clone
  • Variable swapping only handles simple substitutions; complex logic needs API work
  • Rendering 1,000 videos takes 6-12 hours — not real-time, plan ahead
  • Some recipients find AI personalized video creepy — A/B test before scaling

Verdict

Tavus is the only AI video tool that actually delivers on the "personalize at scale" promise. For B2B sales and CS teams doing high-volume outreach, the math works on day one: even 1 extra meeting per week is worth $50K+ in pipeline. For one-off creators, it's overkill. Test the 50-minute free trial — record a clone, send 10 personalized videos, and watch the reply rates yourself.
