Clipping without the decision fatigue

Vidyo.ai finds the most shareable moments in a long video, cuts them to vertical, adds captions and transitions, and queues them for every platform. It competes directly with Opus Clip and Munch, with a slightly stronger auto-resize and template system.

Who it's for: Podcasters, YouTubers, and marketers who want a steady clip pipeline without editing each short by hand.

Key features

Auto-clips Highlight detection

Branded templates tuned for Reels, Shorts, and TikTok.

Captions Burned-in text

Social scheduler Direct publish

Queue clips to multiple platforms from one place.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Fast, accurate clip selection
  • Good caption styling
  • Free tier to test
  • Scheduler is a nice bonus

✗ What doesn't

  • Credit system caps volume
  • Edits sometimes need a tweak
  • Less control than manual
  • Premium needed for bulk

Verdict

Vidyo.ai is a strong Opus Clip alternative with better templates. The free tier lets you validate before the $15/mo Creator plan.
