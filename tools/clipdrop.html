One app instead of five

Clipdrop, now under Stability AI, bundles the AI photo utilities you'd otherwise cobble together: uncrop to expand a frame, cleanup to erase objects, relight to add studio lighting after the shot, and an upscaler that turns thumbnails into 4K. It runs in the browser, on macOS, and on mobile.

Who it's for: Marketers, real-estate photographers, and casual editors who need fast fixes without opening Photoshop.

Key features

Uncrop & Outpaint Expand frames

Extend any image beyond its borders with AI-generated, context-aware content.

Cleanup & Remove Erase objects

Delete people, objects, and text; Clipdrop fills the gap seamlessly.

Relight Studio after the fact

Add virtual studio lighting to a flat product shot after you've already taken it.

Image Upscaler 4K from thumbnails

Turn a 512px export into a crisp 4K image for print and web.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Broad toolkit in a single app
  • Backed by Stability AI research
  • Great macOS, Windows, and mobile apps
  • Free tier is actually usable
  • Fast processing

✗ What doesn't

  • Per-tool credit limits on the free tier
  • Quality is uneven across features
  • Now behind Stability AI's paywall
  • No layer-based editing
  • Some features duplicate what Photoshop already does

Verdict

Clipdrop is the fastest way to fix a photo without opening Photoshop. The free tier covers casual use; $11.99/mo unlocks the full studio.
