Design-grade websites, AI-fast

Framer started as a prototyping tool that designers loved for its animations and micro-interactions. In 2023 they shipped Framer AI — a text-to-website generator that builds production-ready marketing sites in minutes. In 2026 it's the most designer-friendly AI site builder: you can start with a prompt, then drop into a real Figma-like canvas to refine every pixel. Better CMS, better SEO, better hosting than Wix. Better AI than Webflow.

Who it's for: Designers building client sites, indie hackers launching MVPs, agencies shipping marketing pages at speed, and anyone who wants Webflow's power without Webflow's learning curve.

Key features

AI generate Text-to-website

Type "SaaS landing page for an AI scheduling tool" and Framer generates a complete responsive site with copy, hero section, features, and pricing. You can then edit everything visually — no locked templates.

Design canvas Pixel-perfect control

Unlike Wix or Squarespace, Framer's editor is genuinely Figma-grade. Custom layouts, vector tools, component system, design tokens. Designers actually want to use it.

Translate AI site translation

One click translates your entire site into 100+ languages with proper locale handling. The AI keeps your design intact while adapting copy, units, and cultural references.

CMS + SEO Built for production

Real CMS for blogs, case studies, and dynamic content. Server-side rendering for SEO. CDN hosting with edge functions. Custom domains. Analytics built in. Not a toy — production-grade.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Best-in-class AI site generation — produces designer-quality output, not template slop
  • Full design control after generation — no vendor lock-in to AI layouts
  • Native CMS, SEO, and form handling — production-ready out of the box
  • Free tier is generous (3 sites, framer subdomain, basic CMS)
  • Lightning fast hosting on a global CDN
  • Animation and interaction tools that beat Webflow

✗ What doesn't

  • Mini plan at $15/mo caps you at 1,000 visitors — scales get expensive
  • No native e-commerce yet (use Shopify embed instead)
  • Memberships and gated content are limited vs Webflow
  • AI generation is great for marketing sites, weak for complex apps
  • Steep price for high-traffic sites ($30/mo Basic for 10K visitors)

Verdict

If you're a designer building marketing sites, Framer AI is the best tool of 2026 — period. The AI gets you 80% there in 5 minutes, the design canvas takes you the rest of the way. For non-designers, Wix or Squarespace is friendlier. For complex apps, use Webflow or code. For beautiful marketing sites at speed, Framer wins.
