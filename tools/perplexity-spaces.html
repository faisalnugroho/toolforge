A research wiki that answers back

Spaces are Perplexity's collaborative research feature: you create a named workspace, pin sources and files, and then ask questions that are answered with citations drawn from that space plus the live web. It is part notebook, part chatbot, part shared knowledge base - ideal for due diligence, competitive research, and team Q&A.

Who it's for: Researchers, analysts, and teams who repeatedly query a curated set of sources and want answers with receipts.

Key features

🗂️ Pinned sources

Add files, links, and past threads as the space's knowledge base.

🔎 Cited answers

Every response links to the source it used, web or pinned.

👥 Shared workspaces

Invite teammates to a single living research space.

🤖 Pro models

Spaces tap Perplexity's Sonar and flagship models.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Answers always cited
  • Great for teams
  • Combines files + web
  • Free to start

✗ What doesn't

  • Best features on Pro
  • Not a full wiki
  • Can get cluttered
  • Web focus over deep docs

Verdict

Spaces turn Perplexity from a one-shot answer engine into a reusable research layer. If your team keeps re-asking the same questions, this pays off fast.
