The smartest "AI notetaker" in your meeting

Sembly is the AI meeting assistant that started the "AI joins your Zoom call" category in 2020. By 2026, it has grown into a full meeting intelligence platform: it joins as a silent attendee, records multi-speaker audio, transcribes with speaker labels in 40+ languages, and pulls out action items, decisions, and risks using proprietary "Sembly Moments." For teams that live in meetings and want more than a transcript, Sembly is the most operationally mature option.

Who it's for: Project managers, consultants, agency operators, sales teams, and anyone running 5+ recurring meetings/week who wants searchable meeting history and automated follow-ups.

Key features

🤖AI attendee

Send "Sembly" as a meeting invite. It joins silently on Zoom, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, and Webex, records the call, and posts the summary to your workspace within 60 seconds of the call ending.

📝Sembly Moments

Beyond transcription: it identifies decisions, action items, risks, dates, and key topics automatically. Tagged in the sidebar so you can jump to the right moment without scrubbing audio.

🌐40+ languages

Better multilingual support than Otter. Handles code-switching (e.g., English+Spanish in the same meeting) more reliably than competitors. Useful for international teams.

🔍Meeting search

Search every past meeting across your team by keyword, person, project, or decision. Like a Google for your meeting history. The killer feature once you've accumulated 6+ months of meetings.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Mature integrations — Zoom, Meet, Teams, Webex, and calendar all "just work"
  • Sembly Moments (decisions/action items) is genuinely smarter than Otter's basic summary
  • Best multilingual support in the category — 40+ languages with code-switching
  • Team workspace with shared meeting library and search
  • Reasonable price — $10/mo is half of Fireflies' Team plan

✗ What doesn't

  • Transcription accuracy ~92% — behind Otter's ~96% on clean English audio
  • Free tier limited to 60 min/mo — barely enough for a week of 1:1s
  • No real-time coaching (Gong/Chorus territory) — this is post-call intelligence
  • Enterprise governance requires $20/mo Team plan minimum

Verdict

For teams running 5+ meetings/week across multiple languages, Sembly is the best price-to-feature meeting AI of 2026. Otter wins on raw transcription accuracy, Fireflies wins on CRM sync, and tl;dv wins on free tier — but Sembly's combination of decision/action-item extraction, multilingual support, and $10/mo pricing makes it the strongest "all-rounder" for SMBs.
