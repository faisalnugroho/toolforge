See your feed before you post it

Later started as an Instagram scheduling tool and evolved into a full visual content platform. Its killer feature is still the visual planner — drag and drop posts into a grid to preview how your Instagram feed will look before publishing. Add AI caption writing, hashtag suggestions, and one of the best link-in-bio tools on the market, and you have a complete social media toolkit for visual brands.

Who it's for: Instagram-first creators, e-commerce brands, photographers, and visual businesses that need to maintain a cohesive feed aesthetic.

Key features

Planner Visual Feed Planner

Drag and drop posts into a visual grid to preview your Instagram feed. See exactly how your profile will look before publishing anything.

AI AI Caption Writer

Generate platform-specific captions from a topic or image. Later's AI suggests hashtags too — and actually ones that are relevant, not just popular.

Link Link in Bio

Later's Linkin.bio creates a clickable version of your Instagram feed. Each post links to a specific URL — products, blog posts, booking pages.

Best Time Best Time to Post

Later analyzes your audience's activity and suggests optimal posting times for each platform. Customized to your specific account, not generic data.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Visual feed planner is unmatched for Instagram aesthetic planning
  • Linkin.bio is one of the best link-in-bio tools — better than Linktree
  • 7M+ users means it's battle-tested and reliable
  • AI caption writer with relevant hashtag suggestions
  • Supports Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, Twitter/X, Pinterest, LinkedIn, YouTube

✗ What doesn't

  • Free tier is very limited (10 posts/mo, 1 profile per platform)
  • Analytics are basic on lower tiers
  • No social listening or competitor tracking
  • AI features still catching up to Buffer and Ocoya
  • $25/mo Starter plan limits you to 30 posts/mo per profile

Verdict

Later is the best tool for Instagram-first creators who care about feed aesthetics. The visual planner alone justifies the price if Instagram is your primary channel. For multi-platform scheduling with more AI features, Buffer or Ocoya might be better. But if you need to see your grid before you post — Later is king.
