Email at the speed of thought

Superhuman is a premium email client (Gmail and Outlook) built around keyboard shortcuts, split-second search, and AI that drafts replies and summarizes threads. It is opinionated and fast.

Who it's for: Executives, founders, and anyone who processes hundreds of emails a day and bills by the hour.

Key features

Shortcuts Keyboard-only flow

Triage a packed inbox in minutes with muscle-memory commands.

Split Inbox AI priorities

Important senders surface; newsletters wait.

Write AI draft and polish

Generate a reply from a one-line intent; fix tone instantly.

Remind Follow-up nudges

Superhuman pings you if nobody replies.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Blazing fast
  • Excellent search
  • AI compose saves time
  • Beautiful UX
  • Great support

✗ What doesn't

  • $30/mo is pricey
  • Gmail and Outlook only
  • Learning curve on shortcuts
  • No free tier to trial casually

Verdict

Superhuman is a luxury, and like most luxuries, it is hard to go back. If email is your job, the $30/mo buys back hours a week. If you touch email twice a day, Gmail is fine.
