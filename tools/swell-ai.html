Turn your podcast into 10 pieces of content

Swell AI is a podcast content engine — drop in an audio or video file and it generates transcription, show notes, titles, descriptions, social posts, and short video clips in minutes. For podcasters who struggle with the content marketing side, it's a one-stop shop that turns one episode into a week of social content.

Who it's for: Podcasters, video creators, and content teams who want to repurpose long-form audio into clips, blog posts, and social media content without manual editing.

Key features

Clips AI clip generation

Upload a 60-minute episode and Swell AI identifies the most engaging 30-90 second clips, adds captions, and formats them for TikTok, Reels, and Shorts. No manual scrubbing through the timeline.

Notes Show notes & titles

Auto-generated show notes with timestamps, key takeaways, and SEO-optimized titles and descriptions. Upload and publish — no copywriting needed.

Transcribe Accurate transcription

Speaker detection, 99% accuracy on clean audio, and export to TXT, SRT, VTT. Integrate with your existing podcast workflow or CMS.

Social Multi-platform posts

Generates Twitter threads, LinkedIn posts, newsletter teasers, and blog summaries from each episode. One upload = a week of content.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Clip selection is genuinely smart — finds the moments you'd pick manually
  • All-in-one: transcription, notes, clips, and social posts in one tool
  • $15/mo is competitive for the feature set
  • Integrates with Buzzsprout, Libsyn, Spotify for Podcasters
  • Video clip export with captions saves hours of editing

✗ What doesn't

  • Clip quality varies — some "viral" picks feel forced
  • Caption styling is basic compared to Opus Clip or Submagic
  • Transcription accuracy drops on noisy audio or heavy accents
  • Social post templates can feel generic — need human editing
  • No built-in video editor for manual clip adjustments

Verdict

Swell AI is the best all-in-one podcast content tool for creators who want to maximize each episode's reach without hiring an editor. At $15/mo it pays for itself if it saves you 2 hours of post-production per episode. For pure clip quality, Opus Clip is better — but Swell AI's breadth (transcription + notes + clips + social) makes it the better value for solo podcasters.
