The bottom line

Gong is the original revenue intelligence platform and still the category leader. It records every sales call (Zoom, Meet, Teams, in-person), transcribes with 95%+ accuracy, and uses AI to surface patterns: which objections kill deals, which demos convert, which reps close above quota. If you're running a B2B sales team above 25 reps, Gong is the most-deployed conversation intelligence tool on the planet for a reason.

Who it's for: B2B sales leaders (VP Sales, CRO) at companies with 25+ AEs who want data-driven coaching and deal inspection.

Key features

🎙️ Call Recording & Transcription

Auto-joins every Zoom, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, WebEx, and in-person meeting. Multi-language transcription with speaker ID. 95%+ accuracy in English, 90%+ in Spanish/French/German.

🧠 Deal Intelligence

Tracks every mention of competitors, pricing, objections, next steps. Flags deals at risk by detecting negative language patterns across the last 2-3 calls.

📈 Market Intelligence

Aggregates customer language across thousands of calls to surface what buyers actually say about your product, your competitors, and the market. The closest thing to having a 1,000-rep focus group.

🎯 Sales Coaching

Scorecards on every call. Track which reps hit discovery milestones, handle objections, and pitch value. Library of call snippets for onboarding and training.

🤝 Integrations

Salesforce, HubSpot, Outreach, Salesloft, Apollo, LinkedIn Sales Navigator, Slack. Bidirectional sync means every call is logged to the CRM automatically.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Largest dataset in the category — patterns emerge that smaller tools simply can't detect
  • The 'deal warnings' feature alone has saved teams millions by catching stalled deals early
  • Best-in-class transcription accuracy and speaker ID (works on multi-language calls)
  • Board-ready analytics: win rates, average deal size, sales cycle length by segment
  • Compliance and security certifications (SOC 2 Type II, HIPAA, GDPR, EU data residency) make it enterprise-ready

✗ What doesn't

  • Expensive: $100-$160/user/mo, with most teams needing $1,500+/mo minimum
  • Implementation takes 4-6 weeks to tune (categories, trackers, scorecards)
  • UI is dense — new reps need 2-3 weeks of training to use effectively
  • Limited to sales calls — doesn't analyze support, customer success, or product feedback
  • No free trial; only paid pilots (most teams get a 30-day pilot)

Verdict

Gong is the gold standard for B2B revenue intelligence and the right choice if you have 25+ reps and a sales-led GTM motion. The price is steep but the ROI is real: teams using Gong report 12-20% lift in win rates within 6 months, primarily from coaching and deal inspection. If you're a sub-10-rep team, Chorus or Avoma gives you 80% of the value at 30% of the price. If you're enterprise, there's no real alternative — it's Gong or roll your own.
