Why this Gmail replacement has 500K paying users

Shortwave is the AI-native email client built by the team that made Google Inbox. After Inbox was killed in 2022, the team rebuilt the same AI-first philosophy from scratch — only this time with GPT-4 baked in for summarization, draft replies, and triage. It works on top of your existing Gmail (and now Outlook) and turns email from a 3-hour daily drain into a 45-minute skim.

Who it's for: Knowledge workers, founders, execs, sales teams, and anyone who gets 100+ emails a day and wants to stop living in their inbox. Especially powerful if you live in Gmail but want AI to handle the busy work.

Key features

AI AI Summaries

One-line TL;DR of every long email thread. Open a 30-message chain and Shortwave gives you the key decisions and action items in 2 seconds.

Sma Smart Bundles

Group related emails (newsletters, receipts, pitches) into a single swipeable stack. The original Inbox feature, rebuilt with AI auto-categorization.

AI AI Draft Replies

Hit reply, type a 1-line instruction ("decline politely, say I'm OOO next week"), and Shortwave writes a 3-paragraph response in your voice.

Sen Send Later & Scheduled Send

AI suggests optimal send times based on recipient timezone and historical open patterns. Stop sending Friday afternoon emails that get buried.

Con Conversations View

Modern threaded view that actually makes sense. Inline images, collapsible quoted text, no more scrolling through 12 quoted replies.

Sha Shared Inboxes

Built-in team collaboration — assign emails, leave internal notes, @mention teammates. Replaces Help Scout or Front for small teams.

✓ What we like

  • Built by the actual Google Inbox team — they know email UX cold
  • AI summaries and drafts genuinely save 1-2 hours/day for heavy email users
  • Bundles are the best newsletter/receipt management we've seen anywhere
  • Native apps for macOS, Windows, Linux, iOS, Android, and web
  • Free tier is genuinely useful (unlimited email, 10 AI replies/day)

✗ What we don't

  • Only works with Gmail and Outlook — no IMAP support for other providers
  • No calendar integration yet (big miss for execs)
  • AI can be over-eager with auto-categorization in the first week
  • Mobile app is good but not as polished as the desktop client

The verdict

If you get more than 50 work emails a day and you're still using vanilla Gmail, you're leaving 1-2 hours on the table. Shortwave is the single highest-ROI AI tool we recommend for knowledge workers in 2026. Start with the free tier — if you process more than 30 emails a day, you'll be on Pro within a week.
