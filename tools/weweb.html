Ship a real app without writing the UI

WeWeb is a no-code builder for the frontend of serious web apps. Point it at any REST or GraphQL backend (or Supabase/Xano), design in a visual editor, and publish to your own hosting. No lock-in on the data layer.

Who it's for: Founders, agencies, and product teams who need app UIs fast without a React hire.

Key features

🎨 Visual Editor

Pixel-level control with reusable components and design tokens.

🔗 Any Backend

Bind to REST, GraphQL, Supabase, Xano, or your own API.

🚀 Self-Hosting

Export and deploy to your own infrastructure.

🤖 AI Builder

Scaffold pages and workflows from a text prompt.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Works with your existing backend — no data lock-in
  • Production-grade output, not a toy
  • Self-hosting keeps you in control
  • Strong workflow/state tooling

✗ What doesn't

  • Steeper learning curve than pure landing-page builders
  • Backend logic lives elsewhere
  • Higher tiers needed for teams
  • Design system setup takes upfront time

Verdict

WeWeb is the no-code choice when you've outgrown the toy tools but don't want to hire a frontend team. Bind it to Supabase and you can ship a real SaaS in a weekend.
