The bottom line

Chorus.ai was founded in 2015 — the same year as Gong — and built the conversation intelligence category alongside it. Acquired by ZoomInfo in 2021 for $575M, Chorus is now bundled into ZoomInfo's sales platform and offers the tightest native Zoom integration of any tool in this space. The AI surfaces what works on calls: objection handling patterns, talk ratios, competitor mentions, and the moments that close deals.

Who it's for: B2B sales teams already using ZoomInfo, or teams that want the simplest, cleanest Zoom-native conversation intelligence.

Key features

📹 Zoom-Native Recording

Records meetings directly in Zoom with one click — no bot joining the call. Cleaner attendee experience. Works with Google Meet, Teams, WebEx, and BlueJeans via the bot.

💡 Smart Themes

Auto-tags every call with topics (objections, pricing, competition, next steps) so you can search across thousands of calls. The best search UI in the category.

⚠️ Deal Risk Detection

Flags deals where sentiment is dropping, next steps are vague, or buyer engagement has slowed. Integrates with Salesforce to update deal stages automatically.

🎓 Coaching Workflows

Scorecards, peer review, and library of best moments. Managers can leave timestamped feedback directly on calls — the closest competitor to Gong's coaching layer.

🔗 ZoomInfo Integration

Bundle pricing for ZoomInfo customers. Buyer intent data from ZoomInfo combines with call data to surface which accounts are heating up.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Best-in-class Zoom integration — recordings happen invisibly with no bot
  • Cleaner, more modern UI than Gong (closer to Notion or Linear)
  • Cheaper if you're already a ZoomInfo customer (bundle pricing)
  • Excellent search across call transcripts — finds moments faster than Gong
  • Coaching workflows are arguably better than Gong's for managers who want a light-touch review process

✗ What doesn't

  • Limited utility if you're not already a ZoomInfo customer (stand-alone pricing is comparable to Gong)
  • Smaller dataset than Gong — pattern detection is less powerful on call volumes below 1,000/mo
  • Fewer integrations than Gong (no Apollo, no Outreach native sync, no Slack workflows)
  • No native market intelligence layer (Gong's 'company-level' insights are unmatched)
  • Acquisition integration has slowed product velocity — feature releases are slower than Gong's

Verdict

Chorus is the right choice if you're a ZoomInfo customer or you prioritize UI/UX over feature depth. The Zoom-native recording is genuinely better than Gong's bot-joins-call approach — clients don't notice the recording happening, which matters for cold calls and executive conversations. But for pure analytics depth, deal inspection, and market intelligence, Gong is still ahead. Choose Chorus if ZoomInfo is your GTM stack and you want conversation intelligence bundled in. Choose Gong if you're building a sales-led motion from scratch.
