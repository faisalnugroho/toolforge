The AI design tool for people who hate design tools

Hotpot.ai is what happens when someone looks at Canva, Figma, and Midjourney and asks: what if non-designers could just describe what they want? The result is a suite of focused AI tools — app icon generator, AI headshot generator, social media post maker, photo upscaler — each doing one thing well. Not the most beautiful output, but the fastest path from idea to file.

Who it's for: Indie hackers, solo founders, small business owners who need decent visuals fast. Less ideal for design professionals who want pixel-perfect control.

Key features

📱 App icon generator

Generate iOS/Android app icons in 10+ styles. Pre-cropped, gradient-corrected, store-ready. Saves indie devs hundreds of dollars.

🤳 AI headshots

Upload 5 selfies, get LinkedIn-ready headshots in 30+ styles. Cheaper than a photographer, faster than scheduling a shoot.

🖼️ Marketing graphics

Social media posts, blog headers, ad creatives. Choose a template, customize the AI output, export. Beats hiring a designer for one-offs.

✨ Photo upscaler & restorer

Upscale low-res photos, restore old family photos, colorize black-and-white. Surprisingly good for a side feature.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Best-in-class app icon generation — genuinely useful
  • AI headshots are shockingly good for $11
  • All-in-one suite — no tool-hopping
  • Beginner-friendly UI, zero learning curve
  • Cheaper than buying multiple specialized tools

✗ What doesn't

  • Output quality trails dedicated tools (Midjourney for art, Canva for design)
  • Limited customization once image is generated
  • Free tier is heavily watermarked
  • No vector output (everything is raster)

Verdict

Hotpot.ai is the perfect "first AI design tool" for non-designers. The app icon generator alone is worth the $11/mo if you ship apps. The AI headshots are an absolute steal. For professional design work, you'll still want Midjourney + Canva. For everything else, Hotpot covers it.
