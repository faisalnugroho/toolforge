What MailerLite does well

The most affordable email platform with AI features. Clean UI, generous free tier, fair pricing.

Who it's for: Small businesses, solopreneurs, and nonprofits that want pro email tools without paying ConvertKit prices.

Key features

AI Writing Assistant GPT-4-powered

Draft subject lines, body copy, and preview text from a prompt. Built into the composer.

Drag-and-drop Editor No HTML needed

Beautiful email templates, simple drag-and-drop editor. The best editor UX in the category.

Automation Visual flows

Welcome series, abandoned cart, birthday emails — all visual builder. Free tier includes basic automations.

Landing Pages Built-in

No-code landing pages, pop-ups, and embedded forms. Great for lead gen on a budget.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Most affordable paid plan in the category ($10/mo)
  • Free tier is actually useful (<1K subscribers, 12K emails/mo)
  • Cleanest UI of any email tool
  • AI features work well for SMB use cases

✗ What doesn't

  • Limited advanced segmentation vs. Klaviyo or ActiveCampaign
  • No native SMS (email only)
  • A/B testing less sophisticated than ConvertKit
  • Integrations catalog smaller than Mailchimp

Verdict

MailerLite is the value pick for email in 2026. If you're a solopreneur, small business, or nonprofit that wants pro email tools without $25-45/mo subscriptions, MailerLite's $10/mo plan is unbeatable. For heavy ecommerce automation, Klaviyo wins.
