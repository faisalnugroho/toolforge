The AI assistant already in your pocket

Meta AI is the assistant Meta ships inside its family of apps — Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp, and Messenger — plus a standalone web and mobile app. In 2026 it runs on the Llama 4 family of models and is completely free, with no usage caps. It generates images inline (via Emu), answers questions with live web access, and understands photos you send it. For the billions of people already inside Meta's ecosystem, it's the zero-friction default AI.

Who it's for: Casual users who want a free, no-signup AI for quick questions, image generation, and social planning. Great as a secondary assistant alongside ChatGPT or Claude.

Key features

🌐 Live web search

Grounded answers pulled from the real-time web — sports scores, news, and current events without leaving the chat.

🎨 Emu image generation

Type 'imagine' and Meta AI paints images inline, then animates them into short looping videos.

💬 Cross-app presence

Lives inside WhatsApp, Instagram, and Messenger — ask it anything mid-conversation.

🆓 Truly free

No paywall, no message caps. The only major assistant you can use unlimited for $0.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Completely free with no usage limits
  • Already installed for billions of Meta app users
  • Strong multimodal: text, image, and photo understanding
  • Real-time web access for current facts
  • Inline image generation with animation

✗ What doesn't

  • Less capable than GPT-4o or Claude on complex reasoning
  • No serious coding agent or IDE integration
  • Privacy trade-offs — Meta trains on interactions
  • Weaker long-form writing than dedicated tools
  • No API-tier product for developers (use Llama directly)

Verdict

The best free AI assistant for everyday questions and image generation, especially if you already live in Meta's apps. Power users should still keep ChatGPT or Claude for heavy lifting, but Meta AI is the smart default for 'just ask something quick.'
