monday AI

Project management with AI that's actually woven into the workflow — not bolted on.

The work OS, now with AI in every column.

monday.com was already the most flexible work management platform — boards for projects, CRM, marketing, HR, anything. In 2026, AI Blocks drop into any column to summarize text, extract entities, classify items, generate content, or call external LLMs. The AI assistant handles one-off queries like 'summarize all overdue items.' It's the most usable AI-in-PM I've tested.

Who it's for: Operations teams, marketing teams, agencies, PMs running 5+ projects in parallel, and any team that lives inside spreadsheets.

Key features

AI BlocksDrop-in AI columns

Add an AI Block to any column — summarize emails, extract action items from meeting notes, classify leads by ICP, rewrite copy. No prompt engineering needed.

AI AssistantConversational queries

Ask 'what's blocking the launch?' or 'draft a status update for the leadership team' — pulls live data from your boards. Saves 5-10 hours/week on reporting.

AutomationsRecipe-based AI workflows

Trigger AI actions on status changes, due dates, or new items. Auto-classify inbound leads, auto-summarize Slack threads, auto-route support tickets.

Integrations200+ app ecosystem

Native Slack, Gmail, Outlook, Salesforce, HubSpot, Jira, GitHub, Figma, Zoom. Two-way sync means AI Blocks can act on data from anywhere.

TemplatesPre-built AI workflows

Marketing campaign planner, sales pipeline analyzer, content production tracker, customer onboarding — all with AI Blocks wired in. Clone and customize.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • AI Blocks feel native — not a separate ChatGPT sidebar you have to remember to use
  • Best UI in PM tools — colorful, fast, genuinely pleasant to use
  • AI credit system is generous: 500 actions/mo on Pro, 2,500 on Enterprise
  • AI Assistant can write formulas for you — finally solves the 'how do I build a SUMIF' problem
  • Strong mobile apps (iOS + Android) with full AI Block support
  • SOC 2 + GDPR + HIPAA available on Enterprise plans

✗ What doesn't

  • AI credits run out fast on heavy usage — need to upgrade to Enterprise ($19+/seat) for unlimited
  • Still expensive at scale: 50-person team = $9,500/yr minimum on Pro
  • AI Blocks can't call external APIs yet (Asana's AI Workflows can)
  • Reporting/dashboards less powerful than ClickUp's white-label dashboards
  • Time tracking is a $6/seat add-on (ClickUp includes it free)

Verdict

If you're a team that lives in PM tools and you want AI that actually shows up in your daily work — not a separate dashboard you forget about — monday AI is the best option in 2026. The AI Blocks UX is the right pattern. If you need more AI flexibility or unlimited usage on a budget, ClickUp AI is the better value play.
