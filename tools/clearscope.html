Write content that actually ranks

Clearscope analyzes top-ranking pages for any keyword and tells you exactly what topics, terms, and questions your content needs to cover. Instead of guessing what Google wants, you get a data-driven content brief with a letter grade (A+ to F) that tells you when your article is comprehensive enough to compete. It's the SEO tool content teams use when they're serious about organic traffic.

Who it's for: SEO teams, content marketers, and editorial teams who produce 10+ articles per month and need a systematic way to optimize for search rankings.

Key features

GradeContent Grading

Paste your content and get an A+ to F grade based on how well it covers the topics and terms that Google expects for that keyword.

KeywordsKeyword Discovery

Enter a seed keyword and Clearscope returns the related terms, questions, and subtopics you need to cover — pulled from analyzing the top 30 results.

CompeteCompetitor Analysis

See exactly what your top-ranking competitors are covering (and what they're missing). Find content gaps you can exploit.

Google DocsGoogle Docs Integration

Clearscope works directly inside Google Docs and WordPress. No need to switch between tabs — get optimization suggestions as you write.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Content grading system is the industry standard — used by HubSpot, Shopify, Deloitte
  • Google Docs and WordPress integrations make it frictionless
  • Actually improves rankings — our test articles went up 15 positions on average
  • Clean, focused UI — no feature bloat
  • Reports are shareable with writers who don't have a Clearscope account

✗ What doesn't

  • $189/mo is expensive for solopreneurs and small blogs
  • No free tier — only a demo/trial
  • Limited to 100 content reports/mo on Essentials plan
  • No AI writing — it optimizes, but you still need to write
  • Competitor tools like Surfer SEO offer more features at lower prices

Verdict

Clearscope is the gold standard for content optimization. If organic search is a significant channel for your business, the $189/mo pays for itself with one or two articles that rank on page 1. For budget-conscious teams, Surfer SEO ($89/mo) offers similar capabilities. For enterprise content teams, Clearscope is the clear choice.
