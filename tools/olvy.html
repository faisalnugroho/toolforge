The feedback inbox that reads itself

Every product team has the same problem: feedback is everywhere. Slack channels, Intercom, Zendesk, App Store reviews, sales calls, NPS comments, customer interviews. Olvy ingests all of it, uses AI to cluster themes, surface feature requests, flag churn risks, and route insights to the right person — without anyone manually tagging a single ticket. Think of it as a 24/7 junior PM who never sleeps and reads everything.

Who it's for: Product managers, founders, and customer success leads at SaaS companies doing $1M–$50M ARR who are drowning in unstructured feedback. Especially strong fit for B2B SaaS with active Slack communities and a sales team.

Key features

Ingest Multi-source ingestion

Native integrations with Slack, Intercom, Zendesk, HubSpot, Gong, App Store, Play Store, Discord, Twitter, and email. One Olvy inbox for every channel.

Theme AI theme clustering

Olvy groups thousands of feedback items into a handful of recurring themes — "billing confusion," "mobile crash on iOS 17," "want SSO" — automatically, and re-clusters weekly.

Churn Churn-risk detection

Surfaces accounts whose feedback tone has shifted negative over the last 30 days. Get an alert before renewal, not after the cancellation.

Changelog AI changelog drafts

Pull releases from GitHub/Jira and Olvy drafts a customer-facing changelog entry for each one. Edit, publish, close the loop with users who asked for it.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Genuinely saves a PM 4–8 hours per week of feedback triage
  • Multi-source ingestion is best-in-class — Slack + Gong + App Store in one view
  • Changelog auto-drafting is a small feature that compounds
  • "Close the loop" notifications build real customer goodwill
  • Integrations actually work — Slack channel mapping is reliable

✗ What doesn't

  • $49/mo Starter caps at 3 integrations — full set needs Growth ($149/mo)
  • Theme clustering occasionally mis-categorizes sarcastic feedback
  • Smaller companies (<50 users) probably won't generate enough volume to justify it
  • No built-in survey tool — pair with Typeform/Linear for end-to-end coverage

Verdict

If you've ever said "we should really go through our support tickets and find patterns" and then didn't, Olvy is the version of you that does. It pays for itself the first time it surfaces a churn-risk account two weeks before renewal.
