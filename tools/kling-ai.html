The longest AI videos you can generate today

Kling AI, built by Kuaishou (the Chinese tech giant behind one of the world's biggest short-video platforms), generates up to 2 minutes of AI video from text or images. That's 4-5x longer than most competitors. The motion quality is excellent — faces are expressive, physics are mostly correct, and camera movements feel intentional.

Who it's for: Content creators needing short-form video, social media marketers, indie filmmakers testing concepts, and anyone who wants AI video that doesn't look like a slideshow.

Key features

🎬 Text-to-video

Describe a scene in natural language and Kling generates cinematic video. Supports detailed prompts with camera angles, lighting, and character descriptions.

🖼 Image-to-video

Upload a still image and Kling animates it — camera pans, character movements, environmental effects. Great for bringing photos and artwork to life.

🖌 Motion brush

Paint over areas of your image to control exactly what moves and how. Precise control over animation without complex keyframing.

🎥 Camera control

Specify camera movements — pan, zoom, tilt, dolly — in your prompt. Makes generated footage feel shot by a cinematographer, not randomly assembled.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Longest AI video clips available — up to 2 minutes
  • Excellent motion quality and facial expressions
  • Generous free tier with 66 credits daily
  • Motion brush gives real creative control
  • Fast processing compared to Runway/Pika

✗ What doesn't

  • Free plan has long queue times during peak hours
  • Occasional physics artifacts (fingers, reflections)
  • No native audio generation — silent by default
  • Chinese hosting raises data privacy questions for some users
  • Interface only in English and Chinese

Verdict

Kling is the best free AI video generator in 2026, period. The 2-minute clip length is unmatched, and the quality rivals tools costing 3x more. If you need AI video on a budget, start here. Only upgrade to Runway if you need professional-grade consistency and audio.
