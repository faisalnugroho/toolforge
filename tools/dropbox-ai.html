Stop searching 10 different apps for one file

Dropbox Dash is the AI-powered universal search tool that connects to all your work apps — Dropbox, Google Drive, Notion, Slack, Asana, GitHub, Jira, and 100+ more. Ask a question in natural language, get results from everywhere. It also generates AI answers by synthesizing information across your connected tools. Think of it as an AI librarian for your entire digital workspace.

Who it's for: Knowledge workers drowning in files across multiple platforms. If you have content in Dropbox AND Google Drive AND Notion AND Slack, Dash finds it all in one search. Especially useful for teams of 10-500 people.

Key features

Universal Search 100+ app integrations

Search across Dropbox, Google Drive, Notion, Slack, Asana, GitHub, Jira, Confluence, and 100+ more from one search bar. Results ranked by AI relevance.

AI Answers Natural language Q&A

Ask "What was the Q3 revenue target?" and Dash synthesizes an answer from your docs, spreadsheets, and Slack messages. Citations included.

Stacks Smart collections

Organize related content across apps into "Stacks" — a project brief from Notion, the deck from Drive, the specs from GitHub, all in one view.

AI Dashboards Personalized feeds

Get AI-curated updates on what changed in your most important projects. Like a personal assistant monitoring your work.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Searches 100+ apps — no other tool has this breadth of integration
  • AI answers are genuinely useful for finding "that thing someone said in Slack"
  • Stacks feature is clever — organize cross-platform content by project
  • Included with Dropbox Business plans — no extra cost if you're already a customer
  • Fast — results appear as you type, no waiting for AI processing

✗ What doesn't

  • Newer product — features still maturing compared to Glean or Guru
  • AI answer quality depends heavily on your data hygiene
  • Requires admin setup to connect all your tools — not plug-and-play
  • $15/user/mo standalone is expensive if you don't already use Dropbox
  • Privacy concerns — Dash reads everything across your connected tools

Verdict

Dropbox Dash is the most ambitious universal search tool in 2026. If your team uses 5+ work apps and wastes time finding information, Dash pays for itself in the first week. The AI answers feature is the real differentiator — it doesn't just find files, it synthesizes information across them. If you're already on Dropbox Business, try it immediately. Standalone buyers should compare with Glean first.
