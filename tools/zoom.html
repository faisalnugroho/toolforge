What Zoom AI Companion is built for

Zoom AI Companion is the meeting assistant baked into Zoom — no separate subscription, no bot to admit. It summarizes every meeting with action items, catches you up if you join late, and even drafts replies in Team Chat.

Who it's for: Teams already on Zoom who want meeting notes without adding another tool.

Key features

Summary Meeting Summaries

AI Companion recaps every meeting with action items — no recording needed.

Catch up Catch me up

Join late? Get an instant summary of what you missed.

Inbox AI in Team Chat

Draft replies, summarize threads, and surface action items in chat.

Whiteboard AI Whiteboard

Turn rough sketches into organized diagrams and lists.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Free with paid Zoom plans
  • No separate subscription
  • Solid, reliable summaries
  • Multilingual support
  • Deeply integrated

✗ What doesn't

  • Only works inside the Zoom ecosystem
  • Summaries weaker than Fireflies/Otter on accuracy
  • Some features need a paid Zoom account
  • Privacy controls vary by org

Verdict

If you already pay for Zoom, AI Companion is the no-brainer meeting assistant — it's free and good enough for most notes. Power users who live across Meet and Teams, or need sharper accuracy, should pair it with Fireflies or Otter.
