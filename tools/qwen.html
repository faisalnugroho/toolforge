Qwen (通义千问) is Alibaba's flagship AI family.

Qwen (通义千问) is Alibaba's flagship AI family. The Qwen2.5 and Qwen3 series consistently rank in the top 3 open models on the LMSYS Chatbot Arena leaderboard, and the Qwen-VL multimodal model is the best open vision-language model. The consumer product (Qwen Chat) is China's #1 AI chat product with 100M+ users, and there's a free image generation model (Qwen-Wanx) that's surprisingly good.

Who it's for: Developers building with open models (Qwen 2.5 72B is widely considered the best mid-size open model), Chinese-speaking users, and anyone who needs a capable free chat with image generation.

Key features

Qwen 3 Frontier open model

Qwen3 (235B parameters, MoE) is the best open model for code and math — matches DeepSeek R1 on most benchmarks, with much better Chinese-language performance.

Qwen-VL Best open vision model

Qwen2-VL and Qwen2.5-VL are the best open-weight vision-language models — they read documents, analyze images, and understand video better than Llama 3.2 Vision.

Wanx Free image generation

Qwen's Wanx image model is the best free image generator outside of the US — high quality, decent text rendering, no daily limit for most users.

Qwen Chat Free consumer chat

Qwen Chat is free with image generation, file uploads, and web search included. No account required to try. The best free ChatGPT alternative outside the US.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Best open model for code and math (Qwen3 235B-A22B)
  • Best open vision model (Qwen2.5-VL) — handles documents, charts, video
  • Qwen Chat is genuinely free with image gen and web search
  • Best Chinese-language AI by a wide margin
  • Active development — new models monthly

✗ What doesn't

  • Chinese company — same data concerns as DeepSeek for Western users
  • Less polished English UX than ChatGPT/Claude
  • Image generation can struggle with Western cultural references
  • Larger models need serious hardware to self-host (235B is ~140GB)

Verdict

Qwen is the unsung hero of open AI in 2026. The Qwen3 series matches DeepSeek R1 on most benchmarks, Qwen-VL is the best open vision model, and Qwen Chat is the most capable free AI outside the US. Western developers underuse it — try Qwen3-235B before assuming DeepSeek is the only serious open option.
