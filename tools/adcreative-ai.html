The bottom line

AdCreative.ai is the AI ad creative generator for performance marketers. Connect your brand assets, pick your objective (purchase, lead, signup), and the AI produces a batch of conversion-scored ad creatives — images, copy, and headlines — in under 60 seconds. Each creative comes with a predicted conversion score, so you know which to launch.

Who it's for: Performance marketers, DTC brands, and agencies running paid social at scale ($10K+/mo ad spend).

Key features

Batch 100 creatives in 60s

One prompt produces 50-100 ad variants. Each gets a unique image, copy, and CTA. The end of staring at Canva for an afternoon.

Score Conversion prediction

AdCreative's AI scores every creative 1-100 on predicted conversion rate, trained on billions of real ad impressions. Top-scoring 10 typically outperform human-designed baselines.

Brand On-brand by default

Upload your logo, colors, and fonts once. The AI applies your brand kit to every creative automatically. No 'wait, that's the wrong blue' reviews.

Integrations Meta + Google + Pinterest

Push approved creatives directly to Meta Ads Manager, Google Ads, and Pinterest. Cuts the export-upload-step cycle entirely.
