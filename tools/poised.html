The honest take

AI communication coach for video calls. Gives you real-time feedback on filler words, pace, energy, and clarity during meetings.

Who it's for: Anyone who presents in meetings regularly: founders, sales reps, PMs, consultants, public speakers.

Key features

Live Real-time feedback

Subtle on-screen nudges while you talk: 'slower', 'less filler', 'more energy'. Optional — can be turned off.

Report Post-call reports

After every call, get a scorecard on your filler words, speaking pace, energy, clarity, and inclusivity.

Trend Trends over time

See how your communication has improved week over week. Track filler word %, talk:listen ratio, and confidence.

Calls Works on all calls

Joins Zoom, Meet, Teams, Slack huddles, WebEx. Silent on your end — no one else sees it.

Pros and cons

✓ What works

  • Feedback is genuinely useful, not annoying
  • Real-time nudges are subtle, not distracting
  • Trend data is motivating
  • Free tier: 5 calls/mo is enough to try it

✗ What doesn't

  • Real-time feedback is opt-in, you have to turn it on per call
  • Privacy-conscious users will find it creepy
  • Reports can feel like being graded
  • Mac only, no Windows yet

Verdict

Poised is the AI that actually makes you a better communicator. Most people don't realize they say 'um' 50 times in a meeting until they see the report. The trend data is the killer feature — it shows real improvement.
