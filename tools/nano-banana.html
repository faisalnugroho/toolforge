The image model nobody expected

Nano Banana is the internal codename for Google's Gemini 3 image generation model. It turned heads in 2025-2026 for one thing competitors struggled with: rendering clean text inside images, and editing existing photos without wrecking them. It's fast, cheap, and shockingly consistent.

Who it's for: Designers, marketers, and creators who need quick image edits, product mockups, and on-brand visuals without opening Photoshop.
