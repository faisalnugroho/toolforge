The fastest way to test 30 creative angles on Meta, TikTok, and YouTube.

Performance marketers live by the rule: test 30+ creatives per ad set, kill the losers, scale the winners. Arcads turns that workflow into a 30-minute job instead of a 3-week production cycle. Paste a product URL, pick an audience and tone, and Arcads generates 30+ UGC-style video ads featuring realistic AI actors — each with different hooks, angles, and visual styles. The 2026 update added "winning ad rewriter" — paste a competitor's ad URL and Arcads generates 10 variations on the same proven hook.

Who it's for: DTC brand marketers, performance agencies, and growth teams running paid social at scale. Not for brand-led campaigns that need a specific actor or on-location shoot.

Key features

URL URL → 30 ads

Paste a product URL (Shopify, Amazon, custom). Arcads reads the page, extracts benefits and visuals, then generates 30+ UGC-style video ads with diverse AI actors, hooks, and CTAs.

UGC AI UGC actors

Library of 300+ realistic AI actors — diverse ages, ethnicities, settings, and energy levels. Pick the actor that matches your audience or let Arcads auto-match.

Rewrite Winning ad rewriter

Paste a competitor's ad URL or your own winning creative. Arcads generates 10 variations with the same proven hook but new actors, visuals, and angles. Killer for scaling winners.

Script AI scripts & hooks

100+ proven UGC hook templates (problem-solution, before-after, testimonial, listicle). Arcads picks the right hook for your product and writes a 15-30 second script.

Format All formats & ratios

Output in 9:16 (TikTok, Reels, Shorts), 1:1 (Feed), 16:9 (YouTube). Different lengths (15s, 30s, 60s). Captions burned in. Royalty-free music included.

Brand Brand kit & custom actors

Train custom AI actors on your brand ambassador's likeness (with consent). Custom fonts, colors, and product shots. Brand-safe output.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Fastest path from product URL to 30+ ad variations — replaces 90% of UGC agency work
  • Actor diversity is realistic and broad — passes most platform "AI disclosure" requirements
  • Winning ad rewriter is a real moat — no one else does this well
  • Quality is good enough for paid social testing on Meta and TikTok
  • Custom actor training is a game-changer for brand consistency
  • 30+ video output in ~15 minutes is wild

✗ What doesn't

  • Output quality is good but not premium — still loses to real UGC at high CPMs
  • Pricing is steep ($100/mo entry) — only worth it if you spend $5K+/mo on ads
  • Actors can still feel uncanny on close-ups — best for medium shots
  • Limited to UGC-style ads — no brand-story or cinematic work
  • Some platforms (Meta) increasingly flag AI-generated UGC
  • Music library is limited vs Epidemic Sound

Verdict

If you're a DTC marketer or performance agency spending $5K+/month on Meta and TikTok ads, Arcads pays for itself in a single winning creative. The "URL to 30 ads" workflow is unmatched, and the winning-ad rewriter is a genuine moat. Not for brand-led campaigns — but for performance testing volume, nothing else comes close.
