Europe's answer to ChatGPT is faster than you'd think

Le Chat is the consumer chat interface for Mistral AI's models, the French AI lab that's raised €600M+ from Microsoft, Andreessen Horowitz, and others. Where ChatGPT is American, censored-by-policy, and hosted on US servers, Le Chat is European, more open, and hosted in EU data centers under GDPR. In 2026, it's gone from 'interesting also-ran' to genuinely competitive — Mistral Large 2 matches GPT-4o on most reasoning benchmarks, and Le Chat's response latency is the fastest we've measured.

Who it's for: European users who care about data sovereignty, developers who want a ChatGPT alternative with less ideological filtering, and anyone who wants the fastest token streaming on the public web.

Key features

Mis Mistral Large 2

The flagship 123B parameter model. Matches GPT-4o on MMLU, beats it on coding (HumanEval) and multilingual tasks. Available on the Pro plan.

Mis Mistral Small & Medium

Free tier runs Small (fast, capable) and Medium (better reasoning). Both are genuinely good — not crippled 'free' models.

Web Web search & citations

Live web search with proper source citations. Built into the chat UI. Comparable to Perplexity's answer engine.

Can Canvas mode

Side-by-side writing workspace. AI writes on the left, you edit on the right. Markdown export, version history, easy code blocks.

Ima Image generation (Flux Pro)

Built-in image generation via Black Forest Labs' Flux Pro. Free tier gets 10 images/day, Pro gets unlimited.

Cod Code interpreter

Python sandbox for data analysis, file uploads, CSV visualization. Runtimes up to 5 minutes on Pro.

✓ What we like

  • Fastest response latency of any major AI chat (we measured ~200ms to first token)
  • EU-hosted, GDPR-compliant — real story for European enterprise
  • Mistral Large 2 is genuinely competitive with GPT-4o and Claude 3.5
  • Less aggressive content filtering than ChatGPT for creative/literary work
  • Free tier is actually useful (Mistral Small + Medium, not crippled)

✗ What we don't

  • Ecosystem is smaller — no native plugins, no Sora-equivalent video, no Advanced Voice
  • Image generation is via third-party (Flux), not native like GPT-4o
  • Voice mode is functional but a step behind ChatGPT's Advanced Voice
  • Mobile app is good but the iOS app crashes more than ChatGPT's

Pricing

The verdict

Le Chat is the most credible ChatGPT alternative from outside the US. The latency is genuinely faster, Mistral Large 2 is genuinely competitive, and the EU data residency story is real. It won't replace ChatGPT for power users who depend on the plugin ecosystem or voice mode — but for privacy-conscious users and developers, it's the best second AI assistant you can add to your stack.
