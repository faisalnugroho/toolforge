Make videos from a sentence

Pika lets anyone generate and edit short videos from prompts or images. Pika 2.0 added Pikaffects, scene consistency, and lip-sync - making it a favorite for social clips and product teasers.

Who it's for: Creators, marketers, and founders who need scroll-stopping video without a camera or editor.

Key features

🎬 Text & image to video

Describe a scene or upload an image; Pika animates it.

😎 Pikaffects & lip-sync

Fun effects plus sync audio to character mouths.

🎨 Scene consistency

Keep characters and styles stable across shots.

✏️ Video editing

Modify regions, extend frames, and restyle existing clips.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Easiest entry into AI video
  • Strong style consistency
  • Fun effects drive shares
  • Free daily credits

✗ What doesn't

  • Short clips only (seconds)
  • Not cinematic like Sora or Veo yet
  • Credits burn fast on Pro
  • Occasional morphing artifacts

Verdict

Pika is the best on-ramp to AI video for non-editors. At $10/mo it is cheaper than Runway and more playful. For longer, cinematic output, graduate to Sora or Kling.
