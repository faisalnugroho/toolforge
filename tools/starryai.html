The AI art generator that lives in your pocket

StarryAI is the AI image tool you actually open on the train, in line at the coffee shop, or while watching TV. While Midjourney hides behind Discord and FLUX requires a GPU, StarryAI ships a polished mobile app with daily free credits. Quality isn't quite at Midjourney level, but the convenience is unmatched — and the free tier is genuinely usable, not a crippled demo.

Who it's for: Mobile creators, hobbyists, NFT artists, anyone who wants AI art on their phone without logging into Discord. Also good for daily creative prompts and journaling.

Key features

📱 Native mobile apps

Full iOS and Android apps. Not a wrapper around a web app — built mobile-first. Generate on the train, share to Instagram in 30 seconds.

🎁 5 free credits daily

Every day you log in, you get 5 free credits. No credit card required. This is the most generous free tier of any major AI art tool.

🖼️ Own your art (NFT-friendly)

You own the output. StarryAI explicitly grants full commercial rights — one of the few tools that does this on the free tier.

🎨 Multiple models

Altair (realistic), Orion (artistic), Argo (anime), and custom community models. Switch per-generation without changing apps.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Best mobile experience of any AI art tool
  • 5 free credits/day is genuinely usable, not a gimmick
  • Full commercial rights on free tier — rare
  • Multiple model options within one app
  • Active community and weekly challenges

✗ What doesn't

  • Image quality trails Midjourney v7 and FLUX
  • Web version is clunkier than the mobile app
  • No text-in-image capability
  • Pro plan ($8/mo) is competitive but not cheapest

Verdict

StarryAI is the AI art tool you recommend to your mom, your non-technical friend, or anyone who wants to dabble without Discord. Quality is good enough for social media and casual creative play, and the daily free credits remove the friction. For commercial-grade work, pair it with Midjourney.
