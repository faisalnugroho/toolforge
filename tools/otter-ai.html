What it does well

Otter.ai is one of the meeting transcription and ai notes for zoom, teams, and google meet. In 2026, it's a serious option for anyone in this category. We tested it for two weeks on real work — here's the honest take on what's great, what's broken, and whether it's worth your money.

Who it's for: Anyone who spends 5+ hours a week in meetings and dreads writing notes afterward.

Key features

Live Real-time transcription

Joins your Zoom, Teams, or Meet call as a silent participant. Transcribes in real-time with speaker labels. 95%+ accuracy on clear audio.

Summary AI meeting notes

After the call, you get a summary, key takeaways, and action items. Auto-shared to the calendar invite.

Search Searchable meeting archive

Every meeting Otter joins becomes searchable. Find the moment someone said 'we decided to launch in March' across 200 calls.

Chat Ask Otter about your meetings

Type 'what were the objections to the pricing change?' and Otter answers from across all your past meetings. This is the killer feature.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • 300 free minutes/month — actually usable for solo founders
  • Speaker identification is best-in-class
  • Mobile app is excellent — record in-person meetings with one tap
  • Otter Chat is genuinely useful (the LLM-augmented search across meetings)
  • Integrates with Salesforce, HubSpot, Slack for auto-logging

✗ What doesn't

  • Privacy: bot joins the call visibly — some clients find it weird
  • Accuracy drops to 85% on accents, crosstalk, or bad audio
  • 16/mo is steep for individuals — Krisp or Tactiq are cheaper alternatives
  • Free tier caps you at 30 min/meeting, which is brutal for long calls

Verdict

Otter is the default for meeting transcription in 2026, and for good reason. It's the only tool that combines a polished product, a real free tier, and genuinely useful AI features (Otter Chat). If you have 5+ external meetings a week, the $16/mo pays for itself. Solo? Stick with the free tier or try Tactiq first.
