The honest take

✓ What works

  • Reliable live transcription across platforms
  • Genuinely useful AI summaries save post-meeting time
  • Generous free tier
  • Strong integrations (Zoom, Teams, Slack)

✗ What doesn't

  • Accuracy dips in noisy or accented audio

  • Speaker labels sometimes wrong
  • Privacy concerns for sensitive calls

Verdict
