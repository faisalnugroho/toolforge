Know where every billable hour goes

Toggl Track is the time tracker people do not quit. One keyboard shortcut starts a timer; the rest is automatic. In 2026 it layers on AI that turns raw time data into project profitability and capacity forecasts.

Who it is for: Freelancers, agencies, and consultants who bill by the hour and need to know which projects actually make money. Also useful for anyone trying to find where the workday goes.

Key features

Timer One-click tracking

Start, stop, and label timers from the desktop app, browser extension, or a global hotkey, no timesheet to fill in later.

AI Smart summaries

Toggl AI groups similar entries, drafts week notes, and flags under-billed clients automatically.

Reports Profitability

See margin per project and per client by mapping tracked hours to your rates.

Idle Background detection

Idle detection and a timeline view reconstruct your day even if you forget to start a timer.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Genuinely frictionless, highest adoption of any tracker
  • Excellent free tier
  • AI summaries save Friday admin
  • Great mobile, desktop, and browser coverage
  • No credit card to start

✗ What does not

  • Starter plan caps some reports
  • AI features are newer and occasionally rough
  • Not a full project-management suite
  • Team features need the $9/mo+ plan

Verdict

For solo workers and small teams, Toggl is the default for a reason, it is the tracker you will actually keep using. The 2026 AI additions make the weekly review almost automatic. Start free; upgrade only when you need the reports.
