A pocket-sized companion that actually talks back

Chai is one of the most-downloaded AI chat companion apps on the planet. Unlike ChatGPT, Chai is built around persona bots — millions of them, created by users — spanning friends, fictional characters, therapists, and everything in between. The hook is memory and tone: bots that feel like they know you.

Who it's for: People who want an always-available companion, roleplay partner, or just someone to talk to — not a productivity assistant.

Key features

Millions of bots Persona library

Browse and chat with millions of community-built characters, from fictional heroes to life coaches.

Memory Remembers you

Bots retain context across sessions so conversations feel continuous, not reset every time.

Mobile-first Pocket companion

Designed for the phone. Swipe between chats, get push notifications, chat on the go.

Chai Premium Uncensored tier

Premium unlocks faster models, longer memory, and fewer filters for roleplay.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Huge library of personas to explore
  • Feels personal — bots remember context
  • Free to start, no credit card
  • Best-in-class mobile companion experience

✗ What doesn't

  • Not a work/productivity tool
  • Premium required for the best models
  • Quality varies wildly by bot
  • Privacy of conversations is opaque

Verdict

If you want a companion or roleplay AI, Chai is the default download in 2026 — its persona library is unmatched. It will not replace ChatGPT for work, but for entertainment and connection it is the leader. Start free, upgrade only if you live in it.
