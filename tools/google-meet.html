The simple, reliable meeting caller — now AI-assisted

Google Meet is the video layer of Google Workspace. The 2025-2026 AI additions bring automatic meeting summaries (in Docs), live translated captions, and adaptive noise cancellation. It's the easiest way to meet for anyone already in Gmail and Google Calendar, and the AI notes land straight in your Google ecosystem.

Who it's for: Google Workspace users and anyone who wants frictionless video calls with AI recaps that sync to Google Docs.

Key features

📄 AI meeting notes

Summaries saved to Google Docs for Workspace users.

🌐 Live translated captions

Real-time captions translated across languages.

🔇 Adaptive noise cancellation

Filters background noise on the fly.

📆 Calendar-native

One-click join from Google Calendar.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Zero setup for Google users
  • Clean, reliable call quality
  • AI notes drop into Google Docs
  • Free tier is generous

✗ What doesn't

  • Best AI features need Workspace paid
  • Fewer integrations than Teams
  • No native long-form editing
  • Recording limited on free tier

Verdict

Meet is the path of least resistance for Google shops, and the AI summaries are genuinely useful. Pair it with a dedicated note tool if you need searchable transcripts.
