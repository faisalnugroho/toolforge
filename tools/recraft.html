Recraft is one of the image tools that consistently shows up in top creator and team stacks. What makes it different: focus on the specific pain point it solves, with features that actually work as advertised.

Key features

Generate both vector (SVG) and raster images. Unique in the market — most AI tools are raster-only.

🏷️ Brand Kit

Save brand colors, fonts, and styles. Every generation stays on-brand automatically.

🔄 Style Consistency

Lock a style and apply it to multiple generations. Perfect for design systems.

Generate t-shirts, business cards, posters, packaging with realistic mockups.
