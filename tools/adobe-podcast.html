Turn bedroom audio into studio quality

Adobe Podcast's "Enhance Speech" feature is almost magic. Upload audio recorded in a echoey room with background noise, and it outputs clean, studio-quality audio in seconds. It uses AI trained on thousands of hours of professional recordings to remove reverb, background noise, and voice imperfections — all with one click. Best part? It's still free.

Who it's for: Podcasters, YouTubers, remote workers, and anyone who records audio in imperfect environments. Especially useful for interview-style content where you can't control the recording space.

Key features

Enhance AI speech enhancement

Upload any audio file and Adobe's AI removes reverb, background noise, and voice artifacts. Output sounds like it was recorded in a professional studio.

Noise Automatic noise removal

Dog barking, keyboard clicks, AC hum, street noise — the AI identifies and removes background sounds while preserving voice clarity.

Transcribe AI transcription

Automatic transcription with speaker labels and timestamps. Export as text, SRT, or VTT for captions and show notes.

Record Browser-based recording

Record directly in the browser with remote guests. Each participant gets their own high-quality track for post-production.

Verdict

Adobe Podcast's Enhance Speech is the single best free AI audio tool in 2026. If you record any kind of spoken content and don't have a treated studio, use this. It's free, it's fast, and the results are jaw-dropping.
