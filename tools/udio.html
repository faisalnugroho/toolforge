The Suno competitor with full-song capability

Udio started as a Suno alternative focused on better vocals and song structure. In v2, it generates full songs (3+ minutes) with coherent verses, choruses, and bridges — something Suno still struggles with. For songwriters and musicians, Udio is the new default for AI music.

Who it's for: Musicians, songwriters, podcast producers who need full songs with vocals.

🎵 Full songs (3+ min)

Generate complete songs with intro/verse/chorus/bridge/outro structure.

Vocals sound less 'AI' than any competitor. Multiple voice styles.
