The AI storyteller fiction writers actually use.

NovelAI started in 2021 as a GPT-powered story-writing tool and has since become the default for fiction authors, roleplayers, and worldbuilders. The custom-trained Kayra and Clio models understand narrative voice, genre tropes, and prose rhythm far better than vanilla GPT. The 2026 release adds high-quality anime-style image generation bundled into every plan — no extra subscription needed.

Who it's for: Fiction authors, fanfic writers, TTRPG game masters, light novel writers, and worldbuilders who want prose that sounds like prose.

Key features

KayraProse-tuned LLM

Custom 30B+ parameter model trained on literary fiction, light novels, and high-quality web fiction. Dramatically better prose than vanilla GPT or Claude for narrative.

LorebookWorldbuilding memory

Persistent worldbuilding database. Characters, locations, magic systems, plot threads — Kayra references them naturally in generated prose. Beats prompt-engineering every time.

Image GenBundled anime-style art

Generate character portraits, scene illustrations, and cover art in your fiction's style. SDXL-based V3 model trained for anime/illustration aesthetics. ~500 images/mo on Tabletop tier.

MemoryLong context window

8K-32K token context (depending on tier) — Kayra remembers the last 20 pages of your novel without losing the thread. No more 'as you mentioned earlier...' amnesia.

PrivacyUnfiltered & private

Your stories are encrypted, never used for training, and the AI will write anything you ask. No content filters blocking dark fiction, romance, or violence.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Kayra model produces genuinely good prose — better than GPT-4 for fiction in blind tests
  • Lorebook is the killer feature for long-form worldbuilding — solves the context problem elegantly
  • Image generation included — no extra Midjourney/Stable Diffusion subscription
  • Unfiltered: writes any genre including dark romance, grimdark, and explicit content
  • Active Discord community of 100K+ fiction writers sharing prompts and tricks
  • Memory module tracks plot continuity across sessions

✗ What doesn't

  • Not great for non-fiction — Claude and ChatGPT still win for business writing
  • Image gen is anime/illustration-focused — not photorealistic
  • UI feels dated compared to Sudowrite or Novelcrafter
  • No built-in publishing or export to EPUB (you'll need to copy/paste to Vellum or Atticus)
  • Token-based pricing gets expensive on long novels ($25/mo Opus for unlimited)

Verdict

NovelAI is the best AI fiction-writing tool in 2026, hands down. The Kayra model + Lorebook combo is uniquely powerful for novelists and worldbuilders. If you write fiction — even casually — and you've been frustrated by ChatGPT's 'corporate prose' voice, try NovelAI. The free trial (100 generations) is enough to know within an hour.
