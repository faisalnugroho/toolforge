The fastest way to get more from You.com

You.com reimagines search as a private, app-like experience. Instead of ten blue links, you get cited answers, AI chat, and creation tools in one tab — without the ad tracking that funds the rest of the web.

Who it's for: Privacy-minded searchers, researchers, and power users who want cited answers and an app-like SERP instead of a wall of links.

Key features

YouChat Conversational answers

Ask anything and get cited, sourced answers. YouChat runs on leading models with web access baked in.

Smart Search Result cards

Search results reimagined as app-style cards — code, news, sports, stock, and social — not just links.

YouWrite & YouImagine Create in-app

Draft emails and blog posts with YouWrite, and generate images with YouImagine without leaving the tab.

Privacy first No ad tracking

You.com does not build an ad profile from your searches. Your data stays yours — a rare stance in search.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Leading cited answers with real sources
  • Genuinely privacy-respecting (no ad tracking)
  • YouPro unlocks GPT-4-class and custom models
  • Clean, app-like SERP beats ten blue links
  • Solid free tier with unlimited chat

✗ What doesn't

  • Smaller index than Google for obscure queries
  • YouPro at $20/mo is pricier than free search
  • Image model trails Midjourney and SD for fidelity
  • Occasionally slower under peak load

Verdict

If you want a search engine that respects your privacy and shows its work, You.com is one of the few that delivers. The free tier is generous, and YouPro ($20/mo) unlocks GPT-4-class models and custom assistants. For most daily search, it pays for itself in time saved.
