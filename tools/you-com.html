What it does well

You.com is one of the ai search engine with cited answers, custom ai agents, and a developer api. In 2026, it's a serious option for anyone in this category. We tested it for two weeks on real work — here's the honest take on what's great, what's broken, and whether it's worth your money.

Who it's for: Researchers, analysts, students, and professionals who need cited answers, not just chat.

Key features

YouChat AI search with citations

Ask anything, get an answer with inline source links. Pulls from web results in real-time — no training cutoff anxiety.

Agents Custom AI agents

Build a 'Python tutor' or 'crypto analyst' agent with specific instructions, tools, and data sources. The killer feature.

AIMode Multi-model switcher

Switch between GPT-4, Claude, Gemini, Llama, and You.com's own models mid-conversation. Great for comparing outputs.

Pro Priority models, unlimited

Get access to the latest models, higher rate limits, image generation, and 100+ apps in the marketplace.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Real-time web search integrated with chat — answers cite live sources
  • Custom agents are powerful and shareable (the 'GPT store' done right)
  • Multi-model access in one place — pay once, use many
  • Developer API is well-documented and reasonable pricing
  • Free tier is genuinely useful for casual searches

✗ What doesn't

  • Brand recognition lags Perplexity — feels like the 'other' AI search
  • Custom agent builder has a learning curve
  • Pro tier at $15/mo is less compelling than Perplexity Pro at $20 with more polish
  • UI can feel cluttered with all the modes and apps

Verdict

You.com is the AI search engine for power users and builders. If you want a single subscription that gives you access to multiple frontier models + live web search + the ability to build and share custom agents, You.com is the best deal. Casual users will be happier with Perplexity's cleaner UI, but You.com is more flexible.
