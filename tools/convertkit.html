What ConvertKit does well

Email marketing built for creators. AI subject lines, automations, and the best deliverability in class.

Who it's for: Newsletter operators, course creators, podcasters, and creators who monetize with email.

Key features

AI Subject Lines GPT-4-powered

Generate 5 subject line variants per email, scored on predicted open rate. Lifts opens 15-25% on average.

Visual Automations No-code flows

Drag-and-drop automation builder. Welcome sequences, sales funnels, webinar reminders — all visual.

Deliverability 99%+ inbox rate

Best-in-class deliverability. Built-in domain authentication, sunset flows, and bounce cleanup.

Commerce Sell digital products

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Best deliverability in the creator email space
  • AI subject lines genuinely lift open rates
  • Visual automations beat Mailchimp for complex flows
  • Free tier for <1K subscribers is real

✗ What doesn't

  • Tagging system is powerful but confusing for new users
  • No built-in social media scheduler (Mailchimp has it)
  • Reporting less customizable than ActiveCampaign
  • Landing page builder is basic

Verdict

ConvertKit is the creator-economy default in 2026. If you're a newsletter operator, course creator, or podcaster, ConvertKit's deliverability and automation tools make it the right choice. For pure ecommerce, Klaviyo is the pick.
