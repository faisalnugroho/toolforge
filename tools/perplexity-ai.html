Search, but it answers

Perplexity is the answer engine that cites its sources. Ask a question, get a synthesized response with footnotes you can click. Spaces, file upload, and focus modes make it a research powerhouse.

Who it's for: Researchers, students, and analysts who want trustworthy answers with citations, not just a chatbot.

Key features

🔎 Cited answers

Every claim links to a source - no hallucinated facts without a trail.

📁 Spaces & files

Upload PDFs and build shared research spaces with your team.

🌐 Focus modes

Toggle Academic, Wolfram, Reddit, or YouTube for targeted results.

🤖 Pro models

Route hard questions to GPT-4, Claude, or Sonar for deeper reasoning.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Citations build real trust
  • Faster than 10-tab Googling
  • Great for academic research
  • Clean, ad-free UI

✗ What doesn't

  • Not a creative writing tool

  • Sometimes shallow on niche topics
  • Can lag on breaking news

Verdict

For research and just answer my question, Perplexity beats Google and ChatGPT. The $20/mo Pro is worth it if you research daily. For open-ended brainstorming, keep ChatGPT handy.
