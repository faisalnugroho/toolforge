Product photography without a photographer

Photoroom started as a background remover and grew into a full product-image studio. Upload a raw product shot and it strips the background, then drops your item onto a generated, on-brand scene — a marble counter, a studio gradient, a lifestyle setting. For marketplace sellers, it turns a phone photo into a listings-ready image in 20 seconds.

Who it's for: Ecommerce sellers, marketplace vendors, and social sellers who need hundreds of consistent product images.

Key features

Cutout Background removal

One-click subject isolation with clean, hair-aware edges — the core feature that built the brand.

Instant AI backgrounds

Generate contextual scenes (studio, lifestyle, seasonal) behind your product automatically.

Batch Bulk editor

Apply the same cutout + background to an entire catalog in one pass via the API or desktop app.

Retouch Smart edits

Erase shadows, fix wrinkles, and upscale resolution without leaving the canvas.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Background cutout quality is best-in-class
  • Batch + API make catalog work trivial
  • Mobile app is genuinely useful for on-the-go sellers
  • Templates keep listings visually consistent

✗ What doesn't

  • $13/mo Pro is required for HD exports
  • AI backgrounds can look generic without tuning
  • Less flexible than Photoshop for fine art direction
  • Free tier watermarks exports

Verdict

If you sell anything online, Photoroom is the highest-ROI image tool you can subscribe to. The $13/mo pays back on the first handful of listings you don't have to outsource.
