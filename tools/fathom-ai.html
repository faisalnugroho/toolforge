The free meeting recorder that actually works

Fathom is an AI meeting assistant that auto-joins your Zoom, Google Meet, and Microsoft Teams calls, records them, transcribes in real-time, and generates a summary with action items. What sets it apart: the free tier is genuinely free — unlimited recordings, unlimited transcription, no watermarks. Most individual users and small teams never need to upgrade. The summaries are well-structured (not just a wall of text) and action items are clearly extracted with owners and deadlines.

Who it's for: Individual professionals and small teams who want AI meeting notes without paying $16.99/mo for Otter or $10/mo for Fireflies. Particularly popular with consultants, freelancers, and startup founders who take many calls but don't need enterprise features.

Key features

Auto-Join Records meetings

Fathom joins your Zoom, Meet, and Teams calls as a silent participant and records audio + screen share. No manual start button. Works with calendar integration — knows which meetings to join and which to skip.

AI Summaries Structured notes

Generates summaries with: 1-line overview, key points, decisions made, and action items with owners. Summaries are better structured than Otter's — feels like a human took meeting minutes, not an AI regurgitating transcript.

Clip Sharing Share highlights

Create 30-second clips from any part of a meeting and share with a link. Great for sharing decisions or customer feedback with teammates who weren't in the meeting. Clips include auto-captions.

CRM Sync Push to Salesforce/HubSpot

Premium tier auto-pushes meeting notes and action items to Salesforce, HubSpot, or Close CRM. Meeting notes attach to the right contact and deal automatically. Eliminates manual CRM data entry.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Free tier is genuinely free — unlimited recordings and transcription
  • Summaries are the best-structured of any meeting AI we've tested
  • Action item extraction is accurate — catches who said they'd do what
  • Clip sharing is genuinely useful for async team communication
  • Setup takes 2 minutes — connect calendar and you're done

✗ What doesn't

  • Transcription accuracy lags Otter slightly for accented English
  • No mobile app for recording in-person meetings (Otter has this)
  • Free tier doesn't include CRM sync or team features
  • Search across past meetings is limited on free tier
  • Privacy: recordings stored on Fathom's servers (some industries may object)

Verdict

Fathom is the best free AI meeting assistant available. If you're an individual or small team that doesn't need enterprise features, there's no reason to pay for Otter or Fireflies — Fathom's free tier covers 90% of use cases. The summary quality is genuinely better than competitors, and the clip-sharing feature is a unique differentiator. Upgrade to Premium only if you need CRM sync, team workspaces, or advanced search. For most users, free is enough.
