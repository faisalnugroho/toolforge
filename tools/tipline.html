The bottom line

Tipline is a new category of meeting tool: instead of just recording and summarizing after the call, Tipline listens live and surfaces relevant context in real-time — competitor battle cards, objection-handling prompts, pricing comparisons, and customer-specific notes. Built for B2B sales teams who want a 'second brain' during live conversations. The 2026 evolution of conversation intelligence.

Who it's for: B2B sales teams that want real-time coaching during calls, not just post-call analysis.

Key features

⚡ Live AI Assistant

Listens to the conversation and surfaces relevant information in a side panel: competitor battle cards, product specs, case studies, pricing. Answers questions like 'what did we charge Acme last year?' without breaking eye contact.

🎯 Objection Detection

When a buyer raises an objection ('too expensive', 'we use X already'), Tipline suggests the right response in real time. Trained on your team's best reps' call patterns.

📚 Knowledge Base Sync

Connects to your Notion, Confluence, Google Drive, and CRM. Searches across all of them in <500ms when answering live questions. The more content you have, the smarter Tipline gets.

📝 Auto-Follow-Up

Generates personalized follow-up emails in your writing style within 60 seconds of call end. Pulls action items from the conversation.

🔒 Privacy Controls

All live processing happens in your environment — no call audio is sent to Tipline's servers. SOC 2 Type II, GDPR compliant.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Real-time coaching is genuinely novel — the closest thing to having a sales coach on every call
  • Objection handling prompts have measurably improved close rates in early customer data
  • Connects to your existing knowledge base — no need to maintain a separate content library
  • Privacy architecture is better than competitors — no audio leaves your environment
  • Follow-up email quality is close to ChatGPT level after a 30-minute training on your style

✗ What doesn't

  • Still early — fewer integrations than mature tools (Gong, Chorus)
  • Higher price point than Grain or Fireflies ($49/mo vs $15-$18/mo)
  • Requires a robust knowledge base to be useful — bare-bones CRM teams won't get value
  • Live suggestions can be distracting for new reps who aren't used to the workflow
  • Limited post-call analytics compared to full revenue intelligence tools

Verdict

Tipline is the most interesting new meeting AI of 2026. Real-time coaching during live calls is a fundamentally different approach than the Gong/Chorus post-call analytics model, and early customer data suggests it has a measurable impact on close rates. The right tool if you have a strong knowledge base (Notion/Confluence/CRM with rich content) and a sales team that needs in-the-moment support. Not yet ready to replace Gong or Chorus for full revenue intelligence, but the closest thing to a 'co-pilot for sales' that exists in 2026.
