What Podcastle is built for

All-in-one podcast creation and AI audio editing. We put it through real daily use to see where it shines and where it falls short.

Who it's for: Audio professionals who want AI to handle the repetitive work so they can focus on judgment and creativity.

Key features

Record Studio-quality capture

Multi-track remote recording in the browser - no downloads, up to 4K video.

Magic AI audio cleanup

One-click noise removal, leveling, and 'Magic Dust' mastering on every track.

Transcribe Auto transcripts

Generate accurate transcripts and turn them into show notes or blog posts.

Revoice AI voice clones

Create a digital twin of your voice to fix mistakes without re-recording.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Zero-install browser recording - great for remote guests
  • AI cleanup makes cheap mics sound professional
  • Generous free tier for new podcasters
  • Transcription and editing are tightly integrated

✗ What doesn't

  • Heavy editing still easier in Audition/Reaper
  • AI revoice needs careful consent
  • Export options limited on free plan
  • Occasional browser lag on long sessions

Verdict

Podcastle is the fastest path from 'I have a mic' to 'I have a published episode.' The AI cleanup alone justifies the subscription for anyone recording remotely.
