A full 3D pipeline that hides the hard parts

Masterpiece Studio is a no-code 3D suite that turns text or images into rigged, animatable characters. Where Sloyd focuses on props, Masterpiece focuses on characters and scenes — generation, auto-rigging, and animation in one browser app. It targets creators who want to ship avatars to VR or games without a modeling team.

Who it's for: Indie game studios, VR creators, metaverse builders, and marketers who need animated characters but lack rigging or animation expertise.

Key features

Text to Character Generate avatars

Prompt a character and get a mesh with baked materials, ready to refine.

Auto-rig One-click skeleton

Masterpiece auto-rigs generated characters so they can be posed and animated immediately.

Animation presets Built-in motion

Apply walk, idle, and talk cycles without keyframing by hand.

VR export Quest & WebXR

Export directly to VR platforms and WebXR scenes.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Rigging alone saves days of work
  • No-code workflow is approachable
  • Good VR/WebXR export path
  • Decent free trial to validate

✗ What doesn't

  • Character quality lags hand-sculpted art
  • Subscription is pricey for hobbyists
  • UI has a learning curve
  • Occasional mesh artifacts need cleanup

Verdict

For teams shipping VR or game characters without a 3D artist, Masterpiece Studio removes the two hardest steps — rigging and animation. Worth the $29/mo the moment you have a real character deadline.
