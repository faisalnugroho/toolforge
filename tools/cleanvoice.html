The short take

Cleanvoice is an AI podcast post-production tool that takes your raw recording and removes “um”, “uh”, mouth clicks, long silences, and background noise — all in one click.

Who it’s for: Podcasters, YouTube narrators, course creators, audiobook narrators, and anyone who records spoken-word audio and hates editing it.

Key features

🚫 Fill Filler word removal

Detects and removes “um”, “uh”, “like”, “you know”, “so basically” in 10+ languages. Sensitivity is tunable so it doesn’t cut intentional pauses.

⏸️ Dead Dead air removal

Trims silences longer than 0.5s down to a natural length. One hour of raw audio becomes a tight 45-minute episode.

👄 Mou Mouth sound cleanup

Removes lip smacks, mouth clicks, and breathing (optional). This is what makes Cleanvoice different from a basic noise gate.

🎙️ Mult Multitrack mode

Process each speaker track separately for podcasts with multiple remote guests. Reduces cross-talk and preserves natural dialogue flow.

📤 Exp Export & timeline

Export cleaned WAV/MP3, or download an EDL (edit decision list) for Descript, Audacity, or Adobe Audition to fine-tune manually.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Saves 2-3 hours per podcast episode
  • Multitrack mode handles remote guests gracefully
  • Multilingual filler detection (English, German, French, Spanish)
  • EDL export lets editors fine-tune in their DAW
  • Free tier gives 30 minutes of cleaning per month

✗ What doesn’t

  • Struggles with heavy accents in non-English languages
  • No real-time monitoring (upload-and-wait model)
  • Less precise than manual editing for broadcast-quality audio
  • No video editing (use Descript for that)

Verdict

If you’re a podcaster who dreads editing, Cleanvoice at $10/mo is the most underrated tool in your stack. Pair it with Descript for transcripts and you’ll publish 2x faster.
