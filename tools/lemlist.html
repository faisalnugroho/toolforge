The short take

Honest, hands-on review of Lemlist in 2026. Multichannel cold outreach with personalized images, videos, and LinkedIn steps. EU-based, 10+ years on the market.

Who it's for: The multichannel cold outreach platform that sends personalized images, videos, and LinkedIn steps.

Key features

🎨 Pers Personalized images & videos

Lemlist’s signature feature — auto-generate custom images and 15-second video thumbnails with the prospect’s name, company, and website. Open rates 2-3x plain text.

🔄 Mult Multichannel sequences

Email + LinkedIn (connect, message, InMail) + manual tasks + phone calls in one sequence. Lemlist handles the timing and throttling automatically.

Lemwar Lemwarm — built-in warmup

Pool of 30K+ inboxes warming each other daily. Reaches 95%+ inbox placement in 2-3 weeks. Better than Instantly for older domains that need extra care.

📇 450M B2B lead database

450M+ contacts with verified emails (Lemlist recently acquired a data provider). Filter by industry, role, company size, tech stack.

🇪🇺 EU EU-based, GDPR-clean

Headquartered in Paris. All data hosted in EU. Trusted by EU enterprise sales teams with strict GDPR requirements.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Personalized images/videos are unmatched — best open rates in cold email
  • Multichannel (email + LinkedIn) in one sequence is rare
  • Lemwarm is the best warmup for older/reputation-sensitive domains
  • EU-based and GDPR-clean — wins EU enterprise deals
  • 10+ years on the market — mature, stable, well-supported

✗ What doesn't

  • Pricier than Instantly ($59 vs $30/mo for similar sending volume)
  • No AI reply prediction (vs Instantly’s Lead score)
  • LinkedIn steps require a separate LinkedHelper subscription for full automation
  • Per-seat pricing hurts as teams scale

Verdict

Lemlist is the choice when you need personalized video/image thumbnails or you sell to EU enterprise with strict GDPR requirements. If you’re a US startup sending 10K+ cold emails/month on raw volume, Instantly is cheaper. Lemlist’s $59/mo Email Pro plan is the entry point.
