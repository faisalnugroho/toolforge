The smart citation tool

Scite shows you how papers are cited — not just how many times, but whether other papers support, contrast, or simply mention the cited paper. For researchers, this is invaluable for understanding the state of evidence on any topic. The new AI assistant in 2026 lets you ask questions about citation patterns.

Who it's for: Academic researchers, science journalists, evidence-based decision makers.

Key features

📊 Smart citations

See if citations are supporting, contrasting, or mentioning. Color-coded.

🤖 AI research assistant

Ask questions about citation patterns. 'What evidence supports X?'

🔍 1.2B citations

Massive database of citation statements from full-text papers.

📄 Chrome extension

See citation context on any paper page. Free with basic plan.
