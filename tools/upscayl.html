The free upscaler that beats paid tools

Upscayl is the desktop app that took the AI upscaler market by surprise. Free, open-source (GPLv3), runs on your GPU, no cloud upload required. Wraps multiple models (Real-ESRGAN, UltraMix, UltraSharp, Digital Art) in a clean, native UI. The output is often indistinguishable from paid tools like Topaz Gigapixel and Let's Enhance. Why pay $9-199/mo when this exists?

Who it's for: Anyone with a GPU who wants unlimited AI upscaling without subscriptions. Photographers, digital artists, print shops, e-commerce. Privacy-conscious users who don't want photos uploaded to the cloud. Not for users without a discrete GPU (use Let's Enhance instead).

Key features

Free Completely free, MIT-style license

No trials, no "free tier" with watermarks, no upsells. Download the binary for Windows, Mac, or Linux. The code is on GitHub. Donations support development.

Local 100% offline, no upload

Your photos never leave your machine. Critical for wedding photographers, medical/legal work, or any privacy-sensitive context. Real-ESRGAN runs locally at 30-60s per 4K image.

Models 8+ AI models

Real-ESRGAN (general), UltraMix (balanced), UltraSharp (sharpest), Digital Art (illustrations), Remacri (portraits), and more. Pick the model per-image. Custom model support.

Batch Batch processing

Drop in a folder of 1000 images, click Start, walk away. Output is saved with "_upscayl" suffix. Native UI is built with Tauri (Rust), so it starts in <1 second.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Truly free — no asterisks
  • Output quality rivals $200 paid tools
  • Privacy-respecting (local only)
  • Active development, new models added monthly

✗ What doesn't

  • Requires a GPU (RTX 10-series or newer recommended)
  • No denoising or sharpening — upscale only
  • Slower than cloud tools for high-volume batch
  • No face-specific model (use Remini for that)

Verdict

Upscayl is the best free upscaler in 2026 and a legitimate Topaz competitor. If you have a decent GPU and only need upscaling (not denoise/sharpen), this is all you need. For more features, pair with Remini (faces) or Topaz (full suite). For no GPU, use Let's Enhance cloud. But for a free, unlimited, private upscaler, Upscayl is unbeatable.
