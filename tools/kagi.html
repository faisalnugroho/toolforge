The search engine you pay for (and love)

Kagi is a paid search engine built on a radical idea: you're the customer, not the product. No ads, no tracking, and a results ranking tuned for relevance over engagement. It layers in AI features — Summarizer, Ask, and Universal Summaries — that read the pages for you and hand back the answer. Once you switch, going back to ad-laden search feels like stepping into a spam folder.

Who it's for: Researchers, developers, writers, and privacy-minded professionals who run 20+ searches a day and are done with SEO sludge.
