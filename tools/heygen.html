The end of filming spokesperson videos

HeyGen creates realistic AI avatars that speak your script in 40+ languages. Upload a 2-minute video of yourself, and HeyGen creates a digital twin that looks and sounds like you — but can deliver any script in any language, with perfect lip-sync. Used by sales teams for personalized outreach, L&D teams for training videos, and content creators for faceless YouTube channels. The v3 model (late 2025) made uncanny valley concerns largely moot.

Who it's for: Sales teams (personalized video outreach at scale), L&D departments (training videos in multiple languages), content creators who don't want to be on camera, and businesses that need to localize video content.

Key features

👤 Avatar Creation

Upload a 2-minute video of yourself. HeyGen creates a photorealistic AI avatar that can deliver any script. The v3 model is nearly indistinguishable from real video.

🌍 40+ Languages

Your avatar speaks in any language, with native pronunciation and lip-sync. Same person, 40 different languages, identical delivery.

📝 Script-to-Video

Type a script, pick an avatar (yours or one of 100+ stock), pick a background, get a video in 5 minutes. No filming, no editing.

🎭 Video Translate

Upload an existing video. HeyGen translates it to another language, matching the original speaker's voice, lip movements, and style.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • v3 avatars are remarkably realistic — most viewers can't tell it's AI
  • 40+ languages with native-quality lip-sync
  • Stock avatar library for faceless content (100+ options)
  • API for embedding in your own product
  • Massive time savings for sales teams (1 hour of video in 10 minutes)

✗ What doesn't

  • Avatar quality varies — some stock avatars are clearly AI
  • Custom avatar creation needs good lighting and clean audio in source video
  • Gestures and expressions can feel subtle compared to real humans
  • Free tier is essentially a demo (1 credit, ~1 minute of video)

Verdict

The right choice for any business that needs spokesperson-style video at scale. If you're a solo creator doing TikTok, use Runway. If you need to produce 50+ training/sales/marketing videos per month, HeyGen pays for itself in the first week.
