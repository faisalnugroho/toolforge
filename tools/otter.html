The original AI meeting assistant — and still the best

Otter.ai pioneered real-time meeting transcription and AI-generated notes. In 2026 it's still the gold standard: live captions in Zoom/Teams/Meet, automatic speaker identification, action item extraction, AI chat ('What did marketing decide about the launch?'), and searchable transcripts of every meeting you've ever had. The free tier (300 min/month) is generous enough for most individual users. For sales teams, journalists, and anyone who lives in meetings, Otter pays for itself in the first week.

Who it's for: Anyone who spends 5+ hours/week in meetings. Sales teams, journalists, consultants, project managers, recruiters. Less ideal for highly technical meetings with lots of jargon (use Fireflies for that).

Key features

🎤 Live Transcription

Real-time captions in Zoom, Teams, Google Meet, and Webex. Sub-second latency.

🤖 Otter AI Chat

Ask questions about past meetings: "What did Sarah say about the budget?" AI answers with citations.

📝 Auto Summaries

After every meeting, get a structured summary with action items, decisions, and key quotes.

🔍 Searchable History

Every meeting becomes searchable text. Find that one decision from 6 months ago in seconds.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Best-in-class transcription accuracy (95%+ in clean audio)
  • Live meeting chat that actually works (vs competitors)
  • Auto-joins Zoom/Meet calls and takes notes for you
  • Free tier is genuinely usable (300 min/month)
  • Salesforce/HubSpot integration for sales teams

✗ What doesn't

  • Accuracy drops on heavy accents or overlapping speakers
  • 300-min free tier won't cover heavy meeting users
  • Privacy concerns — bot joins meetings by default
  • Mobile app less reliable than desktop

Verdict

Otter.ai remains the best AI meeting assistant in 2026. If you only try one tool from this list, make it this. The free tier alone saves 3-5 hours/week of note-taking. Pro at $20/mo is a no-brainer for anyone in 10+ meetings a week.
