What Decagon does best

AI customer support agents that actually resolve tickets. GPT-4-class AI that integrates with your helpdesk, knowledge base, and order system. Used by Hertz, Eventbrite, Bilt.

Who it's for: Mid-market and enterprise customer support teams handling 10K+ tickets/month. Companies spending $500K+/year on BPOs or L1 support agents.

Key features

🤖 Resolves, not deflects Closes tickets autonomously

Unlike chatbots that deflect to humans, Decagon resolves 60-80% of L1/L2 tickets without escalation. Refunds, order changes, account updates — it does the work.

🔌 Helpdesk-native Zendesk, Intercom, Salesforce

Drops into your existing stack. Reads ticket context, queries your knowledge base, performs actions via APIs, writes back to the ticket. Zero rip-and-replace.

🛡️ Guardrails Safe to deploy at scale

Configurable guardrails: which tickets the AI handles, when to escalate, what tone to use, what data to never share. Compliance-grade audit logs.

📊 Real-time analytics See what AI is doing

Watch AI-handled tickets in real-time, review responses, see resolution rates, identify knowledge gaps. AI suggests knowledge base updates from missed tickets.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • 60-80% autonomous resolution — saves real headcount, not just deflection
  • Native to existing helpdesks (Zendesk, Intercom, Salesforce)
  • Enterprise security: SOC 2, HIPAA, GDPR compliant
  • Backed by a16z, Bain Capital Ventures, Elad Gil — well-funded
  • Used by Hertz, Eventbrite, Bilt, Substack — proven at scale
