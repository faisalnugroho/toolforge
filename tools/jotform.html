Forms for everything, no code required

JotForm is the Swiss Army knife of online forms. Its drag-and-drop builder, 10,000+ templates, and huge widget library mean you can stand up a lead form, appointment scheduler, or internal approval flow in minutes. Built-in approvals and 100+ integrations make it a genuine workflow tool, not just a form collector.

Who it's for: Small businesses, educators, and operations teams that need forms, payments, and approvals without a developer.

Key features

Builder Drag-and-drop editor

Compose forms visually with 300+ field types and a live preview — no HTML required.

Templates 10,000+ library

Start from a vetted template for almost any use case, then tweak to your brand.

Approvals Workflow routing

Add multi-step approval flows so submissions route to the right person automatically.

Integrations 100+ apps

Connect to Google Sheets, Salesforce, Slack, Stripe, and more out of the box.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Massive template and widget library saves hours
  • Approvals turn forms into real workflows
  • Generous free tier for casual use
  • Payments and e-signatures built in
  • Polished mobile app for on-the-go edits

✗ What doesn't

  • Paid plans add up as you need more forms/submissions
  • Interface feels dense next to minimalist rivals
  • Branding removal requires a paid plan
  • Heavy forms can feel sluggish on old devices

Verdict

JotForm is the safest default when you need a form that does more than collect text. Start free, move to Bronze ($34/mo) once you're past five forms.
