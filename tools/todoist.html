Capture and finish, faster

Todoist is the task manager people actually stick with. Quick-add with natural language ("Submit report every Fri at 9am"), organize by project and label, and let the AI Assistant reschedule, prioritize, and break down work for you.

Who it's for: Busy professionals and students who want a fast, reliable capture-to-complete system without enterprise overhead.

Key features

Quick Add Natural language

Type "Call plumber tomorrow 3pm" and Todoist parses the date, project, and priority automatically.

AI Assistant Smart planning

Ask the assistant to reschedule overdue tasks, plan a project, or suggest what to focus on today.

Projects & labels Flexible structure

Organize by project, context label, and filter — then save custom views.

Sync & Karma Cross-platform

Syncs everywhere; Karma gamifies streaks and productivity.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Fast, keyboard-first capture
  • Excellent mobile and watch apps
  • Natural-language dates save real time
  • Free tier is usable

✗ What doesn't

  • AI Assistant is a paid add-on
  • Free tier limits file uploads and reminders
  • No built-in calendar view
  • Subtask hierarchy is shallow

Verdict

The best lightweight task manager on the market. Pair the $4/mo Pro with the AI Assistant add-on and you get a system that thinks about your week for you.
