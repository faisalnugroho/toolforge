An AI that does your research for you

Gemini Deep Research is Google's answer to Perplexity and ChatGPT's Deep Research — but with a key advantage: it has direct access to Google Search, Google Scholar, and the full web. Give it a research question, and it autonomously searches 100+ sources, reads and synthesizes the findings, and generates a comprehensive report with citations.

The workflow is simple: you ask a complex question ("What are the best AI tools for real estate agents in 2026?"), Gemini creates a research plan, executes it by searching and reading multiple sources, then delivers a structured report with inline citations. The whole process takes 3-10 minutes depending on complexity.

Who it's for: Researchers, journalists, analysts, students writing papers, and anyone who needs to quickly get up to speed on a complex topic with verified sources.

Key features

100+ Sources per research

Autonomously searches and reads 100+ web sources, academic papers, and documents. More thorough than any human researcher in the same time.

Plan Auto-generated research plan

Before searching, Gemini creates a structured research plan you can review and edit. You control the scope and direction.

Cite Inline citations

Every claim in the report is linked to its source. Click through to verify. No hallucinated references.

Export Google Docs integration

Export research reports directly to Google Docs. Perfect for team collaboration, academic papers, or client deliverables.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Access to Google Search and Scholar — best source coverage
  • Autonomous research saves hours of manual work
  • Inline citations make it trustworthy and verifiable
  • Google Docs export for easy collaboration
  • Research plan review gives you control over scope

✗ What doesn't

  • Requires Gemini Advanced ($19.99/mo) — no free tier
  • 3-10 minute wait for results (can't be rushed)
  • Reports can be verbose — need editing for conciseness
  • Not great for niche/technical topics with limited web sources

Verdict

Gemini Deep Research is the best AI research tool for people who need thorough, cited reports. The Google Search integration gives it an edge over Perplexity and ChatGPT for source coverage. If you do research professionally — journalism, analysis, academic work — it's worth the $19.99/mo for the time it saves alone.
