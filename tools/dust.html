What it does well

Dust is one of the enterprise ai agents for teams — build custom assistants that connect to your company data. In 2026, it's a serious option for anyone in this category. Key features

Build Custom assistants

Build AI assistants in minutes that know your company. Connect Notion, Slack, Google Drive, GitHub, Linear.

Team Shareable across the team

Publish an assistant once, your whole company can use it. Permissions, versioning, audit logs built in.

Workflows Multi-step workflows

Chain multiple agents together — research agent → writing agent → review agent. Automation that feels like having more teammates.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Fastest way to give a whole team AI assistance on internal data
  • Multi-model support (Claude, GPT-4, Mistral, Llama)
  • Strong data privacy story for regulated industries
  • Active development, ship weekly
  • Based in Paris, EU data residency available

✗ What doesn't

  • Still maturing — some features in beta
  • Less polished than Glean or Slack AI for enterprise IT
  • Pricing jumps at scale
  • Smaller community than Stack AI or Voiceflow

Verdict

Dust is the best AI platform for companies that want to deploy internal AI agents to non-technical teams fast. If you've been wowed by what ChatGPT can do but want it on your own data, Dust is the answer.
