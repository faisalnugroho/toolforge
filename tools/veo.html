The honest take

Google's answer to Sora. 4K AI video with native dialogue, sound effects, and 8-second cinematic shots.

Who it's for: Creators and studios already in the Google ecosystem, or anyone who needs 4K output.

Key features

Quality 4K output

Generate up to 4K resolution, 60fps. No other consumer tool matches this in 2026.

Dialogue Native dialogue

First model that puts words in characters' mouths convincingly. Lip sync and voice in one pass.

Long Longer coherence

Up to 8-second clips with consistent character identity and scene continuity.

Flow Flow editor

Google's dedicated video editor for stitching Veo clips with traditional footage. Free for Veo subscribers.

Pros and cons

✓ What works

  • Highest resolution of any consumer video model
  • Dialogue and lip sync that actually works
  • Native 4K saves upscaling headaches
  • Tight Google Workspace integration

✗ What doesn't

  • Google AI Ultra is expensive ($250/mo for full access)
  • Clips max at 8 seconds (Sora does 60s)
  • Less 'artistic' style range than Runway
  • Long queue times during peak hours

Verdict

If you need 4K, 60fps, or believable dialogue in your AI video, Veo 3 is the only game in town. For social content where 1080p is fine, Sora 2 wins on cost. Pick by use case.
