Text that actually renders

Stable Diffusion 3 introduced a new MMDiT architecture that finally solved AI image generation's oldest embarrassment: legible text in images. It also improved prompt adherence and multi-subject composition. The smaller SD3 Medium is open-weight, making it a strong choice for designs that need real words on screen.

Who it's for: Designers and developers who need accurate text, logos, and typography inside generated images.
