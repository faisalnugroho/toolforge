The AI design partner for Framer and Webflow

Relume solves the most painful part of web design: the boring 80%. Describe a project in plain English and it generates a complete sitemap, wireframes for every page, and a working component library — all styled and ready to drop into Framer or Webflow. Designers spend their time on the 20% that matters (brand, copy, polish) instead of dragging boxes around.

Who it's for: Web designers, freelancers, and small agencies working in Framer or Webflow. Especially powerful for landing pages, marketing sites, and SaaS product pages.

Key features

Sitemap AI sitemap generator

Type "B2B SaaS landing page for HR software" and get a full sitemap in 30 seconds: home, features, pricing, about, blog index. Saved pages for each section.

Wireframe Auto wireframes

Each sitemap page gets a complete wireframe — hero, features grid, testimonials, CTAs, footer. Pre-built with semantic components that match modern best practices.

Figma Figma + Framer sync

Export to Figma as a working component library, or push directly to Framer with one click. Webflow export coming in 2026. The same components work in both.

10h Saves 10+ hours per project

Real number from 200+ designer interviews. Most landing pages take 12-18 hours to wireframe — Relume gets you to 80% in under an hour.
