The short take

Beatoven.ai is the AI composer for content creators. Pick a mood, a genre, and a length — it composes a unique track tailored to your video, podcast, or game scene.

Who it’s for: YouTubers, indie game developers, course creators, podcasters, and short-form video creators who need unique background music that fits the mood of their content.

Key features

🎭 Mood Mood-based generation

Pick from 16 moods (calm, energetic, suspenseful, romantic, etc.) and 8 genres. Beatoven composes a track with intro, buildup, climax, and outro structure.

🎬 Vide Video sync

Upload a video and Beatoven analyzes scene changes, then composes music that transitions with the visuals. Game devs use this for trailer cuts.

🎚️ Stem Stem export

Download individual stems (drums, bass, melody) so you can remix or fine-tune in your DAW. Pro+ plan only.

📜 Lice Commercial license

All paid plans include a full commercial license for YouTube, TikTok, podcasts, games, and client work. Indie-friendly.

🔁 Loop Loop-friendly exports

Tracks are designed to loop seamlessly, perfect for game levels, study streams, or ambient podcast backgrounds.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Cheapest royalty-free AI music ($9/mo vs Mubert $14/mo)
  • Video-aware composition is unique — no other tool does this well
  • Mood + genre matrix produces surprisingly varied output
  • Free tier gives 5 tracks/month at 1-min length
  • Indian music roots mean excellent sitar/tabla samples

✗ What doesn’t

  • Tracks feel “template-y” — less unique than Suno output
  • Limited control over instrumentation
  • No API for high-volume users
  • Vocals/instrumental lyrics not supported (use Suno for that)

Verdict

Beatoven is the best cheap option for ambient/background music. If you’re scoring a documentary, podcast intro, or game level, $9/mo is unbeatable. For tracks with lyrics or more creative range, Suno is the upgrade.
