Personalization that used to take 20 minutes, now takes 20 seconds

SmartWriter automates the part of outreach nobody likes: the research. It crawls a lead’s website, LinkedIn, and recent posts, then writes a cold email that references something specific — a funding round, a blog post, a job change. We A/B tested it against templated blanks and saw a 3.1x lift in reply rate.

Who it's for: Sales reps, agencies, and founders running cold outbound who want personalization at scale without a research VA.

Research Auto lead enrichment

Generates first-touch, follow-up, and LinkedIn sequences referencing real context about the lead.

LinkedIn Social personalization
