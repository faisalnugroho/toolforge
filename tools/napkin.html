From sentence to diagram, instantly

Napkin reads your text — a paragraph, a process, a list — and generates editable diagrams, flowcharts, and infographics. It's the fastest way to make a meeting recap or blog post visual without opening Figma.

Who it's for: Marketers, founders, and educators who need visuals but aren't designers.

Key features

Text to visual One click

Paste text; Napkin proposes several visual layouts you can pick from.

Editable Tweak anything

Change colors, icons, labels, and layout without design skills.

Brand kits Stay on-brand

Save brand colors and fonts so every visual matches your company.

Export PNG, SVG, PDF

Download or embed anywhere — docs, decks, social, blogs.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Incredibly fast for non-designers
  • No Figma skills required
  • Free tier is generous
  • Great for docs and social posts
  • Editable, not just static

✗ What doesn't

  • Not a replacement for detailed design work
  • Limited fine-grained control vs Figma
  • Best on simple/structured text
  • Pro needed for brand features

Verdict

Napkin is the quickest path from 'I wrote a paragraph' to 'I have a diagram.' For anyone who ships content but isn't a designer, the free tier alone is a win; $10/mo unlocks brand consistency.
