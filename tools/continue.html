The Tabnine alternative that's actually good

Continue is the open-source alternative to Copilot and Tabnine that doesn't lock you in. Connect any LLM (Claude, GPT, local), customize with your own prompts, and run it in VSCode or JetBrains. The 'no vendor lock-in' pitch is real — you can self-host the whole thing.

Who it's for: Developers who want Copilot-like features without the vendor lock-in or per-seat pricing.

Key features

🔓 Open source

MIT licensed. Read the code, run it yourself, no surprises.

🔌 Any model

Claude, GPT-4, Llama, Ollama. Switch providers per project.

🧩 Customizable

Write your own prompts, share with team. Fine-tune to your codebase.

🛡️ Privacy-first

Self-host option means your code never leaves your server.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Free and open source
  • No vendor lock-in
  • Self-host for full privacy
  • Works in both VSCode and JetBrains

✗ What doesn't

  • Setup more complex than Copilot
  • Less polished UX than Cursor
  • Smaller community than commercial alternatives
  • BYOK model = you pay API costs directly
