AI writing that sounds like you, not a robot

GrammarlyGO is the generative AI layer built into Grammarly — the writing tool 30 million people already use daily. Unlike standalone AI writers that produce generic output, GrammarlyGO learns your voice, your brand, and your context. It generates drafts, rewrites paragraphs, adjusts tone, shortens text, and explains complex topics — all while maintaining your personal writing style. In 2026, GrammarlyGO expanded to work in every app where Grammarly runs: Gmail, Google Docs, Slack, Notion, VS Code, and more.

Who it's for: Anyone who writes professionally — marketers, executives, support agents, students, journalists. If you already use Grammarly (and 30M+ people do), GrammarlyGO is the AI upgrade you get for free or with Premium.

Key features

Voice Profile Learns your writing style

GrammarlyGO analyzes your past writing to match your tone, vocabulary, and structure. Generated text sounds like you wrote it, not an AI.

Generate Drafts From prompt to first draft

Describe what you need — a follow-up email, a blog intro, a product description — and GrammarlyGO writes it in your voice, ready to edit.

Contextual Rewrite Improve existing text

Select any text and ask GrammarlyGO to make it more concise, more formal, more persuasive, or adjust the tone. Works inline, no copy-paste needed.

Universal Access Works in every app

GrammarlyGO works in Gmail, Google Docs, Slack, Notion, VS Code, and 500,000+ websites. It's the AI writing assistant that follows you everywhere.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Voice profile makes output sound genuinely like you wrote it
  • Works everywhere Grammarly works — no new tool to learn
  • Free tier includes 100 GrammarlyGO prompts/month
  • Integrated with grammar, clarity, and plagiarism checking
  • Enterprise plan includes brand voice and style guide enforcement

✗ What doesn't

  • Full GrammarlyGO access requires Premium ($12/mo) or Business ($15/mo)
  • Generation quality is good but not as strong as Jasper or Copy.ai for marketing copy
  • Voice profile needs 10+ documents to calibrate properly
  • Can be aggressive with suggestions — sometimes you just want it to be quiet
  • No image generation or research capabilities — pure text AI

Verdict

GrammarlyGO is the AI writing assistant for people who already have a writing workflow. It doesn't ask you to switch apps or learn a new interface — it enhances the tool you already use. The voice profile is the real differentiator: generated text actually sounds like you. If you're paying for Grammarly Premium, GrammarlyGO is the best feature you're already paying for. If you're not, the free 100 prompts/month is enough to test it.
