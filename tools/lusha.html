Find the right person real contact info

Lusha enriches LinkedIn profiles and company pages with verified work emails and direct dials, plus firmographic and intent signals. It is the enrichment layer most outbound stacks are built on.

Who it is for: SDRs, recruiters, and growth teams doing cold outreach who need accurate contacts without buying a giant data warehouse.

Key features

Enrich Chrome extension

Reveal email and phone on any LinkedIn profile or company page in one click.

Bulk List enrichment

Drop a list of domains or names and get contacts back at scale via the API or CSV.

Intent Buying signals

Lusha intent data flags accounts actively researching your category.

CRM Native sync

Push enriched contacts straight to Salesforce, HubSpot, or Outreach.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • High email-verification accuracy
  • Generous free tier (25 credits)
  • Fast Chrome extension
  • Good CRM integrations
  • Strong EU compliance (GDPR)

✗ What does not

  • Mobile numbers less reliable than email
  • Costs climb fast at volume
  • Coverage thins outside US, UK, EU
  • Free tier is tiny for teams

Verdict

Lusha remains a top-three contact-enrichment tool in 2026 for accuracy and ease of use. The free 25 credits let you validate fit before paying. Use it with Smartlead or Instantly to keep your cold pipeline full.
