Find the papers you didn't know you needed

Research Rabbit maps the citation graph visually — add a paper and it surfaces related work, co-citation networks, and the labs behind them. Researchers use it to go down rabbit holes productively instead of drowning in Google Scholar.

Who it's for: PhDs, literature reviewers, and anyone building a citation network for a paper or grant.

Key features

🕸️ Visual graph

See how papers connect at a glance.

🔗 Co-citation maps

Find seminal works via citation clusters.

📚 Collections

Build and share reading lists.

🔔 Alerts

Get notified when new related work drops.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Free and nonprofit-friendly
  • Far better discovery than Scholar
  • Visual, intuitive
  • Great for literature reviews

✗ What doesn't

  • No full-text search of paywalled PDFs
  • Smaller than Semantic Scholar index
  • Web only, no mobile

Verdict

The best free tool for discovering and mapping academic literature. Pair it with Semantic Scholar or Connected Papers for a complete research workflow.
