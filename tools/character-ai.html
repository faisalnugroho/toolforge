The platform that made AI characters mainstream

Character.AI is the platform that turned 'chatting with an AI' from a productivity tool into an entertainment medium. Founded in 2022 by Noam Shazeer and Daniel De Freitas — the Google engineers who built LaMDA (the model that sparked the 'is it sentient?' Blake Lemoine drama) — it now hosts 20M+ user-created characters ranging from a friendly Mario to historical figures to therapy bots to NSFW roleplay (filtered but findable). In 2026, it's the dominant character-chat platform, with 200M+ monthly visits and a mobile app that 40M+ people open every day.

Who it's for: Anyone who wants to talk to AI versions of historical figures, fictional characters, or community-created personas. Big audience: teens (with parental concerns), roleplay enthusiasts, language learners practicing with native-speaker characters, and lonely people who want casual conversation.

Key features

20M 20M+ user-created characters

Anyone can create and publish a character. Top creators make $5K-$20K/mo from the creator rewards program.

Cha Character Voice

AI characters can talk out loud. Some creators use their own voice clones, others use TTS. Quality varies wildly but the best are excellent.

Gro Group chats with multiple characters

Put Sherlock Holmes, Einstein, and a custom 'product manager' character in one room. The characters talk to each other AND to you.

Ima Image generation in chat

Characters can generate images of themselves or scenes mid-conversation. Uses a custom in-house model, not Flux/DALL-E.

Sce Scenario starter prompts

Each character has a greeting and scenario. 'You walk into a coffee shop and the barista is...' — sets the stage for roleplay.

Mem Memory (Character.AI 2.0)

New in 2026: characters remember details from prior conversations for ~30 days. Major improvement over the goldfish-memory v1.

✓ What we like

  • Largest character library on the internet — 20M+ characters, niche ones often surprisingly well-built
  • Free tier is genuinely useful (limited by wait times during peak hours, but free)
  • Group chats are a unique feature nobody else has matched
  • Mobile app is polished and the voice mode is solid
  • Community is creative — thousands of genuinely useful educational and therapeutic characters

✗ What we don't

  • Content filter is aggressive and inconsistent — some innocuous roleplay gets blocked, some borderline stuff slips through
  • Free tier wait times can be 30+ seconds during US evenings
  • Quality varies wildly — top characters are great, 90% of user-created ones are mediocre
  • NSFW policy is restrictive; users complain about erotica being blocked but violence being allowed
  • Not appropriate for kids under 13 — age gate is trivially bypassed

The verdict

Character.AI is the default character-chat platform and it earned that position. The library is unmatched, the group chat feature is genuinely novel, and the new memory system (2026) finally makes long-term character relationships feel real. Parents should be aware this is mostly used by teens, and the content moderation is imperfect. For adults who want character chat, start with the free tier — you'll be hooked within 10 minutes.
