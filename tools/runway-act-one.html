Your face, their character — in seconds

Runway Act-One is a breakthrough in AI video generation. You record yourself on a webcam — talking, making faces, gesturing — and the AI transfers your performance onto any character image. The result: an animated character that moves and speaks exactly like you, with accurate lip sync, facial expressions, and head movements. It's motion capture without the motion capture suit.

Who it's for: Content creators, animators, educators, and marketers who want to bring characters to life without a 3D rig or animation software. Perfect for explainer videos, social media content, and storytelling.

Key features

Perf Capture Webcam performance transfer

Record yourself on any webcam. Act-One captures your facial expressions, head movements, and lip sync, then transfers them onto a character image. No motion capture hardware needed.

Any Character Character-agnostic

Upload any character image — 2D illustration, 3D render, AI-generated art, or photo — and Act-One animates it with your performance while maintaining the original art style.

Lip Sync Accurate lip sync

Speak in any language and the character's mouth movements match your words. The lip sync is frame-accurate and handles fast speech, accents, and multiple languages.

Multi-shot Multi-scene workflows

Record multiple takes and stitch them into a single character video. Cut between different camera angles of the same character performance.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Genuinely uncanny performance transfer — facial expressions are accurate
  • Works with any character image style (2D, 3D, photo, AI art)
  • Lip sync is best-in-class across multiple languages
  • No motion capture equipment — just a webcam
  • Fast turnaround — results in minutes, not hours

✗ What doesn't

  • Body movements are limited — primarily facial and head animation
  • Credits are expensive — heavy users will burn through the Standard plan quickly
  • Occasional artifacts with extreme expressions or fast head movements
  • Character image quality directly affects output quality — garbage in, garbage out

Verdict

Act-One is one of the most genuinely impressive AI video tools of 2026. The performance transfer feels like magic — you record yourself and a character comes to life with your exact expressions. It's not perfect (body movement is limited), but for talking-head character animation, it's the best tool available.
