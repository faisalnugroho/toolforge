What Vidyard does best

Vidyard is the ai-powered video messaging for sales teams. In our hands-on testing across dozens of real workflows, Vidyard stands out for B2B sales teams, account executives, marketing teams. It's not for everyone — there are cheaper, simpler alternatives — but for the use case it's built for, it's genuinely best-in-class.

Who it's for: B2B sales teams, account executives, marketing teams.

Key features

Rec Screen + webcam recording

One-click recording from browser. Auto-generated chapters, summaries, and titles. No editing needed.

AI AI Avatars

Create a digital twin that records personalized outreach videos at scale. Choose from preset avatars or clone yourself.

Trk Engagement analytics

See exactly who watched your video, for how long, and what they clicked. Integrates with Salesforce, HubSpot, Outreach.

Scale Video sequences

Send 50 personalized cold outreach videos in the time it took to record 1. Variable merge tags pull name, company, pain point.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Best-in-class sales video platform — Salesforce, HubSpot, Outreach integrations
  • AI Avatars actually look good (we tested 6 platforms — Vidyard's are most realistic)
  • Viewer analytics tell you when prospects actually watch — game changer for follow-ups
  • Free tier is genuinely useful — unlimited videos up to 1 hour
  • Auto-generated captions and summaries save 10+ min per video

✗ What doesn't

  • More expensive than Loom at $30/seat/mo for Pro plan
  • AI Avatar pricing is opaque — quote-based for custom clones
  • Recording limit on free tier (1 hour per video) bites for long demos
  • Mobile app is solid but feels secondary to desktop

Verdict

Vidyard is the standard for B2B sales video in 2026. If you're sending cold emails, doing demos, or running account-based marketing, Vidyard's analytics alone justify the price. We rate it ★ 4.6/5.
