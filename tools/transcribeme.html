When you need humans, not just a model

TranscribeMe blends automated speech recognition with a global pool of human editors who clean up the output. For medical, legal, and research interviews where a single misheard word is costly, that human pass is the difference between usable and useless.

Who it's for: Researchers, journalists, clinicians, and enterprises with compliance or accuracy requirements automated tools can't meet.

Key features

Human-verified Editor pass

Every automated transcript is reviewed by a trained human editor for spelling, speakers, and punctuation.

First Draft ASR fast lane

Need it now? Get machine-generated first draft in minutes, upgrade to human-verified later.

Translate Multilingual

Transcribe and translate across 100+ languages with native-speaker review available.

Enterprise Compliance

HIPAA and GDPR aligned, with NDA-backed human editors for sensitive content.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Accuracy is the best we've measured for hard audio
  • Human editors handle accents and crosstalk well
  • Strong compliance story for healthcare/legal
  • First Draft gives a fast, cheap option

✗ What doesn't

  • Pay-per-minute adds up on long recordings
  • Human turnaround is hours, not seconds
  • Overkill for clean single-speaker podcasts
  • Interface feels dated vs newer apps

Verdict

For anything where accuracy is legally or reputationally risky, TranscribeMe is worth the per-minute premium. For casual podcast subtitles, an automated tool like Otter or TurboScribe is cheaper and fast enough.
