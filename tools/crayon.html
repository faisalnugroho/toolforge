Competitive intel that runs itself

Crayon monitors millions of web pages, job posts, social signals, and reviews to build a live picture of your competitive landscape. Its AI summarizes competitor changes — pricing updates, new features, messaging shifts — and pushes them to the teams that need them. It's the tool that keeps product marketing from being blindsided on a sales call.

Who it's for: Competitive intelligence, product marketing, and sales enablement teams at B2B companies with crowded markets and active rivals.

Key features

Track Automatic tracking

Crayon crawls competitor sites, newsletters, and job boards continuously — no manual screenshotting.

Battlecards AI battlecards

Auto-generated battlecards that surface your differentiators against each rival in real time.

Alerts Smart alerts

Slack and email alerts when a competitor changes pricing, launches, or rebrands.

Win Win/loss insight

Tie intel to CRM outcomes to learn why you win and lose deals.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Saves hours of manual competitor monitoring
  • Battlecards integrate directly into sales workflows
  • Broad signal coverage beyond just websites
  • Strong Slack/CRM integrations

✗ What doesn't

  • No transparent self-serve pricing
  • Setup and taxonomy tuning takes effort
  • Most valuable only with active, large competitor sets
  • Can surface noisy low-value changes

Verdict

Crayon is the default choice for serious competitive intelligence programs in 2026. If your sales team routinely loses deals to rivals they didn't know had changed pricing, the ROI is immediate. Smaller teams should weigh it against Klue or WatchMyComp first.
