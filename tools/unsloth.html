What Unsloth is for

Unsloth rewrites the kernels behind popular fine-tuning libraries so you can train open models dramatically faster and on much smaller GPUs.

Who it's for: Researchers and builders who want custom models without a cluster.

Key features

Feature 2x Faster

Hand-written Triton kernels speed up training.

Feature 70% Less VRAM

Feature Open Models

One-cell Colab and Kaggle workflows.
