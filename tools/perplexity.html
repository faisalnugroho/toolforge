Google for people who want answers, not 10 blue links

Perplexity is what Google should be in 2026. You ask a question, it gives you a synthesized answer with cited sources inline. No ads. No SEO-stuffed content farms. No '12 Best X of 2026' listicles. The Pro tier gives you access to multiple frontier models (GPT-4, Claude, Sonar), file uploads, image generation, and Pro Search (multi-step reasoning across the web). For research, it has replaced Google for millions of users.

Who it's for: Researchers, analysts, students, journalists, and anyone who needs fast, sourced answers to complex questions. Also a great default search engine replacement for Chrome.

Key features

🔍 Cited Answers

Every response includes inline citations with hyperlinks. Click any claim to see the source. No more 'trust me bro' AI output.

🤖 Multi-Model

Choose between Sonar (Perplexity's own model, optimized for search), GPT-4, Claude 3.5, and others. Pro Search runs multiple queries and synthesizes the best answer.

📁 File Uploads

Upload PDFs, CSVs, and images. Perplexity analyzes them alongside web search. Great for 'summarize this report and find related industry trends'.

🎯 Focus Modes

Filter by source: Academic (peer-reviewed papers), YouTube, Reddit, News, or the entire web. Crucial for research quality.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Cited sources on every answer — no hallucination guessing
  • Multi-model access for $20/mo (GPT-4 + Claude 3.5 + Sonar)
  • Pro Search does multi-step research that would take a human 30 minutes
  • File upload feature is genuinely useful (PDFs, CSVs)
  • Focus modes filter out SEO noise

✗ What doesn't

  • Not a replacement for deep primary research — surfaces web content, doesn't analyze deeply
  • Sometimes cites low-quality sources (medium posts, listicles)
  • Pro Search can be slow (30-60 seconds for complex queries)
  • Free tier is limited (5 Pro searches per day)

Verdict

If you do any kind of research online — work, school, investing, shopping decisions — Perplexity Pro will save you hours a week. The $20/mo is the best ROI of any AI tool in this list.
