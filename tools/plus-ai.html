Bring AI generation into Google Slides — not a new tool to learn.

If your team lives in Google Workspace, switching to a separate AI presentation tool means exporting, sharing, permissions, version control pain. Plus AI is the elegant fix: an add-on for Google Slides (and Docs, Sheets) that adds AI generation and editing inside the native UI. Open an existing deck, click "Plus AI," and ask for "5 more slides on competitive positioning" — it generates them in your template, with your fonts and colors. The killer feature in 2026: Plus AI Snapshots, which turn live Google Sheets data into chart slides that auto-update.

Who it's for: Enterprise teams on Google Workspace who need AI speed without leaving the Slides workflow. Especially strong for revenue ops, sales enablement, and recurring business reviews.

Key features

Generate AI slide generation

Generate slides from a prompt inside Google Slides. Output respects your existing template's fonts, colors, and layout. Insert single slides or full decks.

Edit AI slide editing

Rewrite slide text for tone ("more executive," "more technical"), translate, summarize, or expand. Edit multiple slides at once with a single prompt.

Snapshots Live data snapshots

Turn a Google Sheets range into a slide chart that auto-updates when the data changes. Perfect for weekly business reviews, dashboards, and sales QBRs.

Mix Remixing & templates

Upload a brand deck, and Plus AI uses it as a style template for all future generations. New in 2026: brand-aware tone matching.

Docs/Sheets AI for Docs & Sheets

Same engine for Google Docs (AI writing, summaries) and Google Sheets (AI formulas, analysis, data classification). One license covers all three.

Security Enterprise security

SOC 2 Type II, GDPR, data residency controls, and SSO via Google Workspace. Data is not used to train models. Enterprise tier adds audit logs and admin controls.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Stays inside Google Slides — no new tool for the team to learn
  • Snapshots are a genuine killer feature for recurring business reviews
  • Brand-template respect is best-in-class for AI generation
  • Enterprise security — SOC 2, GDPR, no training on customer data
  • Covers Slides, Docs, and Sheets with one license
  • Solid free tier for personal use

✗ What doesn't

  • Only works inside Google Workspace — no PowerPoint support
  • Less aesthetic output than Gamma or Decktopus (Slides is more rigid)
  • Pricing per seat adds up for large teams
  • Generation quality is good but not creative — needs prompt iteration
  • Snapshots only work with Google Sheets, not external BI tools
  • No offline mode

Verdict

Plus AI is the right AI presentation tool for Google Workspace shops — especially for revenue ops, sales enablement, and teams that build recurring decks from live data. Snapshots alone justify the price for any team doing weekly business reviews. If you live in PowerPoint, look at Beautiful.ai. If you want standalone AI decks, look at Gamma or Decktopus.
