A full AI art department inside Canva

Magic Studio is the bundle of AI features Canva shipped across 2023-2026 — Magic Write, Magic Edit, Magic Expand, and Text to Image. Together they turn Canva from a template tool into a self-contained AI design suite that non-designers actually finish projects in.

Who it's for: Marketers, small-business owners, and anyone already in Canva who wants AI without a new tool. Best for fast, on-brand social and doc visuals.

Key features

Magic Write In-design copy

Draft and rewrite copy directly inside any Canva doc or design.

Magic Edit & Grab Object swap

Erase, replace, or add objects in a photo just by describing them.

Magic Expand Infinite canvas

Extend any image beyond its borders seamlessly for new formats.

Text to Image On-brand generation

Generate visuals from prompts that respect your brand kit.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Lives inside the Canva you already use
  • Generous free tier
  • Easy enough for anyone
  • Brand-kit aware

✗ What doesn't

  • Locked to the Canva ecosystem
  • Per-image generation limits
  • Not a pro-grade Photoshop replacement

Verdict

If you already pay for Canva Pro, Magic Studio is free upside — and if you don't, $14.99/mo gets you a complete beginner-friendly AI design suite. Hard to beat for everyday marketing visuals.
