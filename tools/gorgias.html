What Gorgias actually does

Gorgias is the helpdesk platform built from scratch for ecommerce. Unlike Zendesk or Intercom (built for SaaS), Gorgias pulls in Shopify order data, BigCommerce catalogs, and shipping status so your agents (and now AI) can answer "where is my order" tickets without switching tabs. The 2025 update launched "Automate" — an AI agent that resolves 50%+ of common ecommerce tickets (order status, returns, refunds, shipping) without a human in the loop. Used by 15,000+ DTC brands including Olipop, Beardbrand, and Tushy.

Who it's for: Shopify and BigCommerce merchants doing $500k+/yr who handle enough support volume to justify a dedicated platform but don't want to build a Zendesk stack.

Key features

🛒 Ecommerce-native (Shopify, BigCommerce, Magento)

Pulls order data, tracking, and customer LTV directly into the ticket view. Agents stop alt-tabbing between Shopify Admin and Gmail. Saves 30-60 seconds per ticket.

🤖 Automate AI agent

Resolves order status, returns, refunds, shipping, and FAQ tickets automatically. Customers report 50%+ ticket deflection within the first 60 days.

📊 Revenue attribution

Tracks support-driven revenue: which tickets converted to upsells, which customers came back after a great support interaction. Only ecommerce helpdesk with this.

📲 Multi-channel unified inbox

Email, SMS, Instagram DMs, Facebook Messenger, WhatsApp, live chat — all in one inbox. The reason DTC brands pick Gorgias over plain Shopify Inbox.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Shopify/BigCommerce integration is unmatched — purpose-built for DTC
  • Automate AI agent pays for itself with 2-3 months of ticket deflection
  • Revenue attribution shows the actual ROI of good support
  • Setup is 1-2 hours vs weeks for Zendesk
  • Multi-channel inbox means no customer channel goes dark

✗ What doesn't

  • Per-ticket pricing model punishes growth — popular brands hit $1k+/mo fast
  • Limited to ecommerce — won't work for SaaS, services, or B2B
  • Less flexible than Zendesk for complex routing or custom workflows
  • Voice/phone support requires a third-party integration (not native)

Verdict

If you're a Shopify or BigCommerce merchant doing $500k-$10M/yr and drowning in support tickets, Gorgias is the obvious choice. The Automate AI agent pays for itself within a quarter. If you're a SaaS, B2B, or services company, look at Zendesk or Intercom instead — Gorgias is purpose-built for ecommerce and the limitations show outside that niche.
