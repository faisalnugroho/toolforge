Social media management that proves ROI

Agorapulse is the social media tool that marketing agencies and mid-size teams pick when they need to prove that social media actually makes money. Its ROI reporting tracks how social interactions translate to website visits, leads, and revenue. The AI writing assistant helps generate post copy, and the unified inbox keeps every comment, DM, and mention organized. It's built for teams that take social seriously.

Who it's for: Marketing agencies, mid-size business social media teams, and anyone who needs to prove social media ROI to stakeholders.

Key features

ROIROI Reporting

Track how social media interactions translate to website visits, leads, and actual revenue. Show stakeholders the dollar value of your social work.

InboxSocial Inbox

Unified inbox for all platforms. Assign conversations to team members, label by topic, and achieve inbox zero on every channel.

AIAI Writing Assistant

Generate post captions, reply suggestions, and content variations directly inside the publishing workflow. Tone-aware for brand consistency.

CalendarContent Calendar

Visual calendar with drag-and-drop scheduling, bulk publishing, approval workflows, and content labels for organizing campaigns.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • ROI reporting is unique — no other social tool does this as well
  • Social inbox is excellent for team collaboration
  • Approval workflows are great for agencies and enterprise teams
  • Free tier is generous (3 social profiles, 1 user)
  • Support team is responsive and helpful

✗ What doesn't

  • $79/mo is expensive for solopreneurs
  • UI feels dated compared to Buffer or Later
  • AI writing features still basic compared to Jasper or Copy.ai
  • No TikTok support on lower tiers
  • Limited design capabilities — still need Canva for visuals

Verdict

Agorapulse is the best social media tool for teams that need to prove ROI. If your boss asks "what's the return on social media?" — Agorapulse gives you the answer. For solopreneurs, Buffer or Later are better choices. For agencies and mid-size teams, Agorapulse is worth the $79/mo.
