Where work conversations live — now AI-summarized

Teams is Microsoft's chat, meetings, and calling hub, bundled into Microsoft 365. The 2025-2026 AI layer (Copilot) auto-summarizes meetings, drafts follow-ups, and answers questions about what was said. For organizations already on Microsoft 365, it's the default place meetings happen — and now the place meeting notes write themselves.

Who it's for: Enterprises and teams standardized on Microsoft 365 who want AI meeting recaps without adding another app.

Key features

📝 Copilot recaps

Auto meeting summary, action items, and answers about the conversation.

🔤 Live captions & translation

Real-time captions in 50+ languages.

📅 Deep 365 integration

Outlook, Word, Excel, and OneDrive wired in.

📞 Meetings & calling

HD video, breakout rooms, and PSTN calling.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Already deployed in most enterprises
  • Copilot recaps save real time
  • Strong security & compliance
  • Free tier for personal use

✗ What doesn't

  • Copilot requires the paid add-on
  • Can feel heavy and notification-noisy
  • UI is dense for new users
  • Best value only inside Microsoft 365

Verdict

If your org lives in Microsoft 365, Teams with Copilot is the lowest-friction way to get AI meeting notes. Standalone, it's less compelling than dedicated note tools.
