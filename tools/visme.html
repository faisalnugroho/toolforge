Presentations, infographics, and brand visuals in one no-code design tool. Built for teams, not just designers.

Visme launched in 2013 as an infographic maker and grew into a full visual-content platform. In 2026 it sits between Canva (simpler) and Adobe Express (pro-leaning), with a superpower: live data connected to your charts and a brand kit that keeps whole teams consistent. With 28M+ users, it's the go-to for business teams that need to look like they have a design department.

Who it's for: Marketers, educators, and business teams who need polished visual content without a designer. Presentations, infographics, reports, social posts, and even interactive content — all from templates. The 'we can't afford a design agency but need to look professional' crowd.

Key features

Templates Thousands of templates

Presentations, infographics, reports, social, and documents — all start from a huge, on-brand template library. Most assets are 80% done before you open them.

Charts Data viz

Connect live data to charts and infographics. Pull from Sheets, Excel, or APIs and your visuals update automatically. Rare among design tools.

Brand Brand kit

Lock fonts, colors, and logos so every teammate stays on-brand. Role-based permissions keep the intern from breaking the style guide.

Interactive Interactivity & animation

Add hover effects, pop-ups, links, and animations. Build microsites and interactive presentations, not just static images.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • More than a design tool — also does data viz and interactive content
  • Brand kit keeps non-designers on-brand automatically
  • Live-data charts are a genuine differentiator vs Canva
  • Strong for infographics and presentations specifically
  • Free tier is usable for occasional users

✗ What doesn't

  • Interface is busier than Canva's — more powerful, less intuitive
  • Best features (brand kit, data, interactivity) are on paid tiers
  • Not a Photoshop/Illustrator replacement for pros
  • Performance can lag on heavy interactive projects

Verdict

Visme is the sweet spot between Canva's simplicity and a real design tool's power — especially if you make infographics or data-heavy presentations. The live-data charts and brand kit justify the $29/mo for any team producing visuals weekly. If you only need quick social posts, Canva is enough; if you need connected, on-brand, interactive content, Visme wins.
