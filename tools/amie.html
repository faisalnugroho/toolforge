Scheduling you'll actually enjoy

Amie reimagines the calendar as a playful, unified planner — events, to-dos, habits, and music live together, and booking a meeting is as smooth as sending a link. Beloved for its design, Amie turns the daily grind of scheduling into something that feels light.

Who it's for: Founders, creatives, and anyone who lives in their calendar but hates the sterile enterprise UI of legacy tools.

📅 Unified planner

Events, tasks, and habits in one timeline.
