Voice cloning that sounds human

Resemble AI lets you clone any voice from just 10 seconds of audio. The result is nearly indistinguishable from the original. What sets it apart: real-time streaming for voice agents, emotion control (make it sound excited, sad, or calm), and built-in deepfake detection. It's the API-first choice for developers building voice products.

Who it's for: Developers building voice agents, audiobook platforms, game studios, and enterprises needing custom TTS at scale.

Key features

Clone Rapid voice cloning

Upload 10 seconds of audio and get a usable voice clone. For production quality, 1-5 minutes is ideal. The model captures not just the voice but speaking style and cadence.

Stream Real-time streaming

WebSocket API delivers audio in under 200ms — fast enough for live voice agents and real-time conversations. Perfect for AI phone agents and interactive characters.

Emote Emotion control

Control the emotion of the generated voice with simple tags. Happy, sad, angry, whispering — the same voice clone can express different feelings for different contexts.

Detect Deepfake detection

Resemble includes Percept, their deepfake detection tool. Upload any audio and get a probability score on whether it's AI-generated. Useful for content verification.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Voice quality is excellent — near-indistinguishable from real speech
  • Real-time streaming is genuinely fast (under 200ms)
  • Emotion control adds depth that most TTS tools lack
  • Pay-per-character pricing is fair for variable usage
  • Built-in deepfake detection is a unique differentiator

✗ What doesn't

  • Requires developer knowledge — API-only, no drag-and-drop UI
  • Clone quality varies with source audio quality
  • Per-character pricing can get expensive at high volume
  • No built-in script editor — you need to bring your own text pipeline

Verdict

If you're building a voice product and need cloning + real-time streaming, Resemble AI is the API to beat. The emotion control and deepfake detection are features you won't find elsewhere. Not for casual users — this is a developer tool.
