The enterprise-open LLM from 01.AI

Yi is the model family from 01.AI, founded by AI veteran Kai-Fu Lee. Released under permissive licenses, Yi models are tuned for long context, coding, and bilingual use — a favorite for companies that want to self-host without legal strings.

Who it's for: Engineering teams and enterprises that want open, license-friendly weights with strong coding and long-context behavior.

Key features

📜 Long context

200K-token context on larger variants.

💻 Coding focus

Solid code generation and explanation.

⚖️ Permissive license

Apache 2.0 — safe for commercial self-hosting.

🌐 Bilingual

Strong Chinese + English.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Truly open, commercial-friendly license
  • Great long-context handling
  • Self-hostable

✗ What doesn't

  • Smaller than GLM/Qwen in China
  • English creative writing average
  • Less frequent releases lately

Verdict

For a license-clean, self-hostable LLM with long context, Yi is a strong, underrated pick. Choose Qwen or GLM if you want the biggest open ecosystem.
