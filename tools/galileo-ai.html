What Galileo AI actually does

Galileo AI turns plain English prompts into editable, high-fidelity UI designs. Describe a "fintech dashboard with dark mode" and get a real, usable Figma file in 30 seconds. It learned from thousands of top Dribbble shots, so output actually looks good.

Who it's for: Product designers, founders, and PMs who need fast UI mockups without opening Figma from scratch.

Key features

Prompt Text-to-UI

Type a description, get a complete screen design with realistic content and components.

Export Figma export

One-click export to editable Figma frames — you keep full design control.

Library Component library

Generates from a library of 100+ production-quality UI patterns.

Brand Brand-aware

Upload your brand colors/fonts and Galileo applies them across generated screens.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Output quality is genuinely impressive — most generations need <5 min of tweaks
  • Fastest path from idea to clickable mockup on the market
  • Real Figma export, not just pretty pictures
  • Free trial gives you 3-5 generations to evaluate

✗ What doesn't

  • Not a replacement for a real designer on complex flows
  • Sometimes generates inconsistent component styling across screens
  • Mobile-only UI patterns are still weaker than web
  • No real-time collaboration like Figma has natively

Verdict

The fastest way to go from "what if we built..." to a clickable prototype. For solo founders and PMs, it's the closest thing to instant UI design. Real designers will still want Figma — Galileo is the fast lane.
