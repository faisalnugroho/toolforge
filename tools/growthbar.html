SEO research and drafting without the enterprise price

GrowthBar is the lightweight SEO suite for founders and small teams. Its Chrome extension shows keyword difficulty, traffic, and backlink data on any page, while the AI writer generates blog posts and outlines wired to the keywords you're targeting. It sits between a full suite like Semrush and a bare writing tool.

Who it's for: Solopreneurs, founders, and content marketers who want SEO signal without a $120/mo enterprise login.

Key features

AI content writer

Generates long-form drafts optimized around your target keyword and outline.

Keyword research

Volume, difficulty, CPC, and related terms in one panel.

Competitor spy

See any site's top keywords, traffic, and backlinks instantly.

Chrome extension

Live SEO data on every Google result and webpage you visit.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Far cheaper than enterprise SEO suites
  • AI writing tied directly to keyword targets
  • Extension makes research frictionless
  • Fast onboarding

✗ What doesn't

  • Keyword database shallower than Semrush/Ahrefs
  • Long-form AI still needs editing
  • No technical SEO audit depth
  • Limited historical trends

Verdict

GrowthBar is the right call when you want SEO guidance and drafting in one cheap tool. Graduate to Semrush or Ahrefs once you're spending real budget on content ops.
