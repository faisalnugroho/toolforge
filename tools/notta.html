What it does

Notta is the transcription tool that quietly became the go-to for multilingual teams. It transcribes audio and video in 104 languages with surprising accuracy, generates AI summaries, and exports to Notion, Google Docs, Slack, and 10+ other apps. The web, desktop, and mobile apps are all equally polished — rare for a transcription tool.

Who it's for: Multilingual remote teams, journalists, podcasters, and anyone transcribing international calls or interviews. Especially strong if you need a single tool that works equally well on phone, browser, and Zoom.

Key features

104 104-language transcription

Auto-detect or pin to a language; accuracy is best-in-class on the top 20 languages (English, Spanish, Mandarin, Japanese, German, French). Handles accents better than Otter at the same price.

AI AI summaries & action items

Every transcript gets a 3-bullet summary, key decisions, and a list of action items with timestamps. Export to Notion or Google Docs in one click.

Sync Bot-free meeting join

Unlike Otter or Fireflies, Notta can join Zoom/Meet/Teams without a bot joining the call — it listens through your mic so guests aren't weirded out.

Apps Mobile-first

Record in the field on iOS/Android, upload, get the transcript on desktop. The mobile app is genuinely the best in the category — beats Otter's mobile by a wide margin.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • 104 languages — the broadest coverage at this price
  • Bot-free meeting recording — no awkward 'Notta has joined the call'
  • Excellent mobile apps (iOS/Android) — field recording is buttery smooth
  • AI summaries are well-formatted and exported cleanly to Notion/Docs
  • $8/mo Business plan is cheap for the feature set

✗ What doesn't

  • Free tier is stingy (120 min/mo) — Otter's free is more generous
  • Speaker diarization (who-said-what) is weaker than Otter's
  • No built-in CRM sync (Otter and Fireflies do this better)
  • Real-time translation is still experimental

Verdict

Notta is the best pick for multilingual teams and field recorders. If you record interviews, podcasts, or international calls, the 104-language coverage and mobile polish beat Otter and Fireflies at the same price. Pick Otter if you need Salesforce sync; pick Fireflies if you need team analytics; pick Notta if you need the broadest language coverage and best mobile experience.
