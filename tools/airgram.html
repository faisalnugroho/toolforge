What it does

Airgram positions itself as the 'Notion of meetings' — it combines recording, transcription, shared agendas, action items, and a meeting notes wiki. Where Otter and Fireflies focus on the transcript, Airgram gives you a full meeting workspace where the team can prep, attend, and follow up in one app.

Who it's for: Product teams, agencies, and consulting firms that run 10+ structured meetings a week with shared agendas and clear action items. Less ideal for solo knowledge workers who just want a transcript.

Key features

WS Meeting workspace

Prep the agenda, attach docs, take shared notes, assign action items — all inside one persistent meeting page per meeting. Like a Notion board per call.

AI AI summaries & action items

Auto-extracts decisions, action items, and a 3-bullet summary from every recorded meeting. Action items sync to Asana, Trello, Linear, and Notion.

Trans Live transcription

Real-time transcription in 8 languages (English, Spanish, French, German, Portuguese, Italian, Japanese, Chinese). Speaker ID included.

Rec Cloud recording

Records Zoom, Google Meet, and Teams calls to the cloud — replays stay searchable forever on free tier (up to 5hrs/mo).

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Meeting workspace concept is unique — better than Otter for team meetings
  • Shared agendas + collaborative notes in real time
  • Action items sync to Asana/Trello/Linear — actually used
  • Cheaper than Fireflies Pro ($9/mo vs $19) at the same transcription quality
  • Free tier is functional for small teams

✗ What doesn't

  • Transcription accuracy is a notch below Otter (especially on accents)
  • AI summaries are shorter/less polished than Fireflies
  • No bot-free recording mode like Notta
  • Mobile app is web-wrapped — not native, feels slower than Notta

Verdict

Airgram is the right pick if your meetings are project-driven (product, agency, consulting) and you want one workspace for the whole meeting lifecycle — agenda, recording, notes, action items. If you just need a transcript, Otter or Notta are simpler. If you need deep CRM analytics, Fireflies wins. For structured team meetings, Airgram's workspace approach is genuinely better than the alternatives.
