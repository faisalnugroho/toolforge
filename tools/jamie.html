The short take

Honest, hands-on review of Jamie in 2026. No-bot AI meeting notes for Zoom, Google Meet, Teams, and in-person. GDPR-compliant EU hosting, structured summaries in 15+ languages.

Who it's for: The no-bot AI meeting notes that work in person, on video, and on phone — with EU data residency.

Key features

🔒 No-b No-bot recording

Jamie runs as a desktop app that captures system audio + mic directly. No bot joins your call — attendees don’t see or hear anything. Works on Zoom, Meet, Teams, Webex, phone calls.

☕ In-p Works in-person

Open Jamie on your phone or laptop before a coffee meeting, and it captures the conversation the same way. Best meeting AI for in-person/offline meetings.

🇪🇺 GDP GDPR + EU hosting

All audio and transcripts are processed and stored on EU servers (Frankfurt). SOC2 Type II certified. The only meeting AI we’ve audited that’s fully EU-DPA compliant out of the box.

📋 7-se Structured summaries

Jamie outputs 7-section summaries: TL;DR, decisions, action items, key insights, problems raised, next steps, and open questions. Editable templates.

🔌 Nati Native integrations

Direct sync to Notion, Slack, HubSpot, Linear, ClickUp, and 30+ tools. Action items auto-create tasks; summaries auto-attach to CRM records.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Only meeting AI that works without a bot joining — better privacy and professionalism
  • In-person meeting support is unique — huge for sales/consulting
  • GDPR + EU data residency is genuine, not just marketing
  • 7-section structured summaries are the most thorough we’ve seen
  • Native desktop app for Mac/Windows + iOS/Android

✗ What doesn't

  • No live transcription — summary arrives 2-5 minutes after call ends
  • Mac/Windows only — no Linux app (but web works)
  • Smaller integration catalog than Otter/Fireflies (30 vs 60+)
  • Searchable meeting library is paywalled to Business ($39/mo)

Verdict

Jamie is the meeting AI for people who hate bots on their calls. If you do in-person meetings, work in a regulated industry (healthcare, legal, finance), or sell to EU enterprise, Jamie is the right choice. For pure virtual calls at scale, Otter or MeetGeek are still cheaper.
