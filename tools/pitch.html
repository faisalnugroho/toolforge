The collaborative presentation tool built for modern teams

Pitch is the Notion-meets-Figma of presentation software. Real-time multiplayer editing (Google Docs-style), gorgeous templates, and AI features that turn outlines into decks. The free tier is unusually good: 5 active decks, unlimited viewers, and full AI features. The Berlin team behind it ships fast.

Who it's for: Startups, design-conscious agencies, and sales teams who want modern collaboration. Less ideal for enterprise PowerPoint workflows where file format fidelity matters most.

Key features

Co-edit Real-time multiplayer

Multiple people editing the same slide simultaneously — Google Docs-style. Cursor presence, comments, and version history. The smoothest collaborative slide editor we've tested.

AI GPT-powered deck generation

Type a topic and Pitch generates a full deck outline with suggested content per slide. Edit inline, accept/reject per section. Less "AI makes a whole deck" and more "AI gives you a strong first draft."

Video Built-in video recording

Record yourself presenting and embed the video into the slide as a thumbnail. Perfect for async updates — send a deck with you narrating each slide.

Share Notion-style sharing

Share via link, control access per person, password-protect decks, set expiry. Analytics show who viewed which slides and for how long. Pitch is the only deck tool with native share analytics this good.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Best-in-class collaborative editing — smoother than PowerPoint's co-author
  • Free tier is genuinely useful (5 decks, unlimited viewers, AI)
  • Video recording built in — saves a Zoom step
  • Share analytics show you who actually viewed your deck
  • Beautiful modern templates (more current than PowerPoint defaults)
  • Custom URLs, password protection, link expiry

✗ What doesn't

  • PPTX export loses some formatting — not ideal if your client lives in PowerPoint
  • No offline mode (web app only, though mobile apps are improving)
  • AI generation is more "outline assist" than "full deck" — Gamma is more generative
  • Free tier limits you to 5 active decks
  • Animation and chart options are narrower than PowerPoint
  • Some advanced typography requires manual tweaks

Verdict

For modern teams who don't have a corporate PowerPoint requirement, Pitch is the best collaborative presentation tool in 2026. The free tier beats Beautiful.ai's, and the multiplayer editing is years ahead of PowerPoint. Choose Beautiful.ai if you need strict PPTX fidelity; choose Gamma if you want one-prompt full-deck generation.
