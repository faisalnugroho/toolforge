Forms without the paywall

Tally flipped the form-builder model: the free plan includes unlimited forms, unlimited submissions, and most "pro" features rivals gate behind paid tiers. You type in a Notion-like editor, drop in fields, and publish. It's the fastest way to spin up a survey, waitlist, or application form at zero cost.

Who it's for: Founders, community builders, and makers who need forms now and don't want a monthly bill to start.

Key features

Free Unlimited forms

No cap on forms or submissions — the rare builder that's genuinely free to grow with.

Editor Notion-like writing

Type naturally and insert fields inline; the learning curve is close to zero.

Logic Calculations & branching

Add conditional logic, scoring, and calculated fields for quizzes and pricing.

Connect Integrations & API

Wire to Notion, Slack, Google Sheets, Zapier, and webhooks on Pro.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Truly unlimited free tier — unbeatable value
  • Delightfully fast, minimal interface
  • Most logic features free, not paid
  • Great Notion and Slack integrations
  • No branding on free plans

✗ What doesn't

  • Fewer design templates than JotForm/Typeform
  • Advanced team features need Pro
  • Smaller widget library
  • Less battle-tested at enterprise scale

Verdict

Tally is the obvious starting point for any new form. Stay free until you need integrations or removal of the Tally badge, then the $29 Pro plan is still a bargain.
