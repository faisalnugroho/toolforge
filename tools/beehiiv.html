What Beehiiv actually does

Beehiiv is the newsletter platform that took on Substack for the professional creator. Built by the Morning Brew team, it ships with AI-powered subject line scoring, an A/B testing engine, a referral program baked in, ad network access, and zero platform fees on paid subscriptions. Migrate from Substack, Mailchimp, or ConvertKit with one click.

Who it's for: Newsletter creators, indie media companies, and content entrepreneurs who want to grow and monetize without Substack's platform tax.
