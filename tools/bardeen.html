The honest take

AI automation that lives in your browser. Scrape, enrich, and act on any page with a single prompt.

Who it's for: Operations folks, growth marketers, recruiters — anyone drowning in copy-paste browser work.

Key features

Magic Magic Box prompts

Type 'scrape this LinkedIn search and add to my CRM' and Bardeen builds the automation. No code.

Scraper Browser scraper

Extract structured data from any website — even ones without APIs. Tables, lists, contact info, all in CSV/Sheets.

Connect 100+ app integrations

Google Sheets, Notion, HubSpot, Salesforce, Slack, Airtable, LinkedIn, and 100+ more. Real two-way sync.

Trigger Autopilot mode

Run automations on a schedule, on form submission, or when data changes. Real hands-off workflows.

Pros and cons

✓ What works

  • Easiest browser automation for non-developers
  • Magic Box actually understands vague instructions
  • Generous free tier (5 automations, 100 actions/mo)
  • Cheaper than Zapier for browser-based workflows

✗ What doesn't

  • Not as fast as Make/Zapier for backend automations
  • Websites that change markup break your automations
  • Pro tier is per-user, not per-workspace
  • Mobile experience is basically nonexistent

Verdict

Bardeen is what would happen if ChatGPT and Zapier had a baby and you could run it on any website. For sales ops, recruiting, and growth teams, this pays for itself in week one.
