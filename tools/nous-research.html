The fastest way to get more from Nous Research

Nous Research is the community lab behind the Hermes family — open-weight models with strong instruction-following, tool use, and structured output. DeepHermes adds explicit reasoning. Everything is auditable and self-hostable.

Who it's for: Builders and researchers who want open, auditable weights they can self-host or run privately — not a black-box API.

Key features

Hermes models Instruction-tuned

The Hermes 2/3 family pairs strong general ability with tool use and structured output — fully open weights.

DeepHermes Reasoning

A reasoning variant that thinks before answering, rivaling closed models on logic benchmarks.

Private by design Self-host

Run weights locally or on your own VPC — no data leaves your stack.

Nous API Hosted access

A simple API if you would rather not self-host, with the same open models behind it.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Genuinely open weights you can audit and self-host
  • DeepHermes reasoning is genuinely strong
  • Great community and frequent releases
  • API is cheap for what you get

✗ What doesn't

  • Not a consumer product — you wire it up yourself
  • Smaller ecosystem than OpenAI or Anthropic
  • Hosted API occasionally rate-limited
  • Benchmark marketing outpaces some real-world edge cases

Verdict

Nous Research is a pillar of the open-weight movement. If you care about owning your models and private inference, Hermes and DeepHermes are top-tier. Use the API for convenience or self-host for full control — either way, you are not locked in.
