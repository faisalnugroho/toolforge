The "video-to-anime" tool that took over TikTok

Domo AI is the platform that popularized video-to-anime style transfer. Drop in a clip of a person walking down the street, pick an anime preset, and you get a Studio Ghibli-style animation in under 3 minutes. The platform also animates still images (animate a portrait, get a 5-second loop) and does live-action style transfer.

Who it's for: TikTok and Instagram creators chasing viral anime trends, indie filmmakers doing style experiments, and animators prototyping concepts before committing to a full pipeline.

Key features

v2a Video to anime

Upload any clip (up to 60s on Pro). Pick from 30+ anime presets — Ghibli, Arcane, Cyberpunk, Shinkai, watercolor. Get a full anime-rendered video in 2-3 minutes.

i2v Image to video

Drop a portrait, a landscape, or a piece of concept art. Domo animates it with subtle motion, camera push-ins, hair sway, parallax. Outputs 4-10 second loops.

a2v Anime to live action

Reverse direction — convert anime clips into photorealistic live-action. The results are uncanny and polarizing. Great for AI film experiments.

REST API Programmatic access

REST API + Discord bot for batch processing. Studios use it to prototype dozens of style variations in a single afternoon.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Best video-to-anime quality in 2026 — beats Runway + Pika for this niche
  • 30+ style presets covering anime, cinematic, painterly, cyberpunk
  • Fast — most renders in 2-3 minutes for a 30s clip
  • Affordable entry ($9.99/mo) compared to Runway Gen-4
  • Active Discord community sharing presets and tips

✗ What doesn't

  • Output is 720p on Starter, 1080p only on Pro ($29.99/mo)
  • Character consistency is hit-or-miss on longer clips
  • Credit system can feel stingy — 250 credits/mo on Starter
  • Some style presets look generic, not the "Ghibli magic" you'd hope
  • No offline editing — fully cloud-based

Verdict

Domo AI is the easiest on-ramp to "AI video" in 2026. If you want to make your TikTok avatar dance in anime style, this is the tool. For professional filmmakers, the 720p limit on Starter and inconsistent character fidelity will frustrate — but the Pro tier at $30/mo is genuinely competitive with Runway and Pika for stylized output.
