Open video you control

Tencent's HunyuanVideo releases a full open text-to-video model with strong motion coherence and image-to-video support. Run it on your own GPUs, fine-tune with LoRAs, and avoid per-clip fees.

Who it's for: Studios and builders who want open, self-hostable, fine-tunable text-to-video without vendor lock-in.

Key features

Open weights Self-host

The full model is open — run it on your GPUs or via hosted partners.

Motion quality Coherent video

Strong temporal consistency and realistic motion versus earlier open models.

Image-to-video Animate stills

Seed it with an image and extend into a moving clip.

Fine-tunable LoRA support

Train custom styles and characters with community LoRAs.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Open and self-hostable
  • Impressive motion coherence
  • Image-to-video works well
  • Active open community
  • Free to run if you have GPUs

✗ What doesn't

  • Heavy GPU requirements
  • No polished consumer app
  • Prompting needs practice
  • Trails Sora/Kling on polish

Verdict

HunyuanVideo is the best open text-to-video model for teams that want to self-host and customize. Casual creators will prefer a hosted app like Kling or Sora, but for sovereignty and fine-tuning it leads the open field.
