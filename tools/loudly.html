Background music without the copyright headache

Loudly generates full, royalty-free tracks from a text prompt or a chosen genre, then hands you the individual stems so you can edit in your DAW. It is built for creators who need safe-to-monetize music for YouTube, Twitch, and client work without a licensing call.

Who it's for: YouTubers, podcasters, game devs, and indie hackers who ship video constantly and cannot risk a Content ID strike.

Key features

AI Music Generator Text to track

Describe a mood or pick a genre; Loudly composes a complete, finished track.

Stem separation Remix ready

Download individual stems to rearrange or mix in your DAW.

Royalty-free Safe to monetize

Use in YouTube, Twitch, and client work with no copyright worries.

Soundtracking Video fit

Auto-match length and energy to your footage.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Genuinely royalty-free output
  • Fast generation from a prompt
  • Stems make real editing possible
  • Friendly for non-musicians

✗ What doesn't

  • Tracks can sound generic at times
  • Less genre depth than a human composer
  • Free tier is watermarked
  • Smaller library than some rivals

Verdict

Loudly is the fastest way to get monetization-safe music under your video. At $9/mo it pays for itself the first time you avoid a copyright claim. For full songs with vocals, add Suno or Boomy to the stack.
