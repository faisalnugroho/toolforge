Your AI second brain for meetings

Context AI is a meeting intelligence tool that goes beyond transcription. It captures not just what was said, but the decisions made, action items assigned, and the broader context of ongoing projects. Ask it "what did we decide about the pricing model?" and it pulls from your last 20 meetings to give you the answer — with citations. For managers and team leads drowning in meetings, it's a memory augmentation layer.

Who it's for: Managers, executives, and team leads in meeting-heavy roles. Especially valuable for cross-functional teams where decisions span multiple conversations and stakeholders.

Key features

Memory Cross-meeting context

Context AI indexes every meeting and connects related conversations. Ask "what's the status of the Q3 launch?" and it synthesizes answers from standups, planning meetings, and 1:1s — with source references.

Actions Auto action items

Detects action items, assignments, and deadlines from conversation. Syncs them to your task manager (Linear, Jira, Asana, Notion) automatically. No more "can someone take notes?"

Decisions Decision log

Automatically builds a decision log — what was decided, by whom, and why. Searchable by topic. Solves the "didn't we already decide this?" problem that plagues growing teams.

Integrations Calendar & CRM sync

Connects to Google Calendar, Outlook, Zoom, Google Meet, Slack. Pulls CRM context (Salesforce, HubSpot) into meeting summaries so you always know the account history.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Cross-meeting search is genuinely useful — answers with citations
  • Decision log prevents re-litigating settled questions
  • Action item sync to task managers reduces follow-up overhead
  • Works across Zoom, Meet, Teams without switching tools
  • CRM context makes account reviews painless

✗ What doesn't

  • $18/mo is steep for individual contributors — best value for teams
  • Cross-meeting accuracy depends on consistent meeting habits
  • Privacy-conscious orgs may balk at indexing all conversations
  • Integrations can lag — Linear sync occasionally misses items
  • Decision detection sometimes flags discussions as decisions

Verdict

Context AI is the most sophisticated meeting memory tool we've tested — it goes beyond Otter and Fireflies by connecting conversations into a searchable knowledge graph. If your team has 20+ meetings a week and you lose track of decisions, it's worth the $18/mo. For solo professionals or light meeting loads, Otter's free tier is enough.
