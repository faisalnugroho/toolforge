Teal is the job search tool you didn't know you needed

Teal is the AI job search CRM that replaces your messy spreadsheet. Track every application, auto-extract job descriptions, get AI resume suggestions per role, and see exactly which companies ghost you. Built by ex-recruiters who got tired of spreadsheets.

Who it's for: Active job seekers, career changers, and anyone applying to 5+ jobs/week.

Key features

Track Application tracker

Track every application in one place — status, contact, follow-up dates. Auto-reminders.

AI Resume matcher

Paste any job description, get a side-by-side match score and bullet suggestions.

Chrome LinkedIn extension

One-click save any LinkedIn job into your tracker. Auto-fills application fields.

Stats Search analytics

See your application-to-interview conversion rate. Most users are at 2-3% — Teal gets you to 10%.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Free tier is genuinely useful — unlimited tracker, basic AI
  • Chrome extension is best-in-class — saves 2-3 min per application
  • Resume matcher shows exactly which keywords you're missing
  • Built by ex-recruiters — knows what hiring managers actually look for
  • Pro at $29/mo is expensive but pays for itself with one extra interview

✗ What doesn't

  • Pro tier ($29/mo) is the most expensive in this list
  • Resume builder templates are basic compared to Kickresume or Rezi
  • No cover letter AI — needs ChatGPT or Kickresume for that
  • Mobile app is web-only — no native iOS/Android

Verdict

Teal is the job search tool you didn't know you needed. The free tier alone beats a spreadsheet. For serious job seekers (5+ applications/week), the $29/mo Pro pays for itself with one extra interview. Pair with Kickresume for templates and you've got the best job search stack in 2026.
