The voice AI that actually understands emotion

Hume AI is the first major voice platform that doesn't just transcribe or generate speech — it actually measures emotional tone, with 48 distinct emotional states tracked across 25ms windows. Their EVI (Empathic Voice Interface) 2 model is the first conversational voice API to ship in production with sub-300ms latency, real turn-taking, and emotion-aware responses. It's the API behind the most realistic AI phone agents, therapy bots, and research tools in 2026.

Who it's for: Developers building voice-first products — call center AI, mental health apps, user research, accessibility tools, AI companions, and games. Not a no-code tool; you need to write code or use their playground.

Key features

EVI 2 Empathic Voice Interface

Hume's flagship model. Real-time voice conversation with emotion detection, natural turn-taking, and tone-mirrored responses. Sub-300ms latency, 30+ voice personas, and streaming responses. The only voice API that doesn't feel like talking to a robot.

48 Emotional dimensions

Not just 'happy' or 'sad' — Hume detects 48 distinct emotional states (confusion, excitement, hesitation, amusement, sympathy, anxiety) across 25ms audio windows. You get structured emotion data alongside the speech content.

Octave Text-to-speech with emotion

The TTS model that doesn't just say words — it acts them. Direct the emotional tone per-sentence or per-word, choose from 50+ voice personas, and generate speech that actually sounds like a real person who means it.

API Developer-first platform

Clean REST + WebSocket API, official Python/TypeScript/Go SDKs, on-device deployment options, and a Playground for prototyping. SOC 2 compliant, HIPAA-eligible, with EU data residency.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Best-in-class emotion detection — the only voice API that captures how people actually feel
  • EVI 2 voice conversation is the most natural-sounding AI voice on the market
  • Generous free tier (10K API calls/mo) for prototyping
  • Strong privacy and compliance posture (HIPAA, SOC 2, EU residency)
  • Active development, ships meaningful updates monthly

✗ What doesn't

  • Not as cheap as raw OpenAI TTS at scale — pricing is emotion-call-based
  • Smaller language support than ElevenLabs (30 languages vs 32+)
  • Free tier is good for dev but tight for production
  • No offline / on-device voice cloning

Verdict

Hume is the voice AI to pick in 2026 when you care about emotion, nuance, and how users actually feel — not just what they said. ElevenLabs is cheaper for pure TTS at scale; OpenAI is the safe default. But for empathic voice, there's nothing else close.
