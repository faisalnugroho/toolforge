The fastest way to the browser-based video editor with ai baked in.

Veed.io is what most non-editors actually need from a video editor. It runs in the browser (no download), has a Canva-like UI, and includes a full suite of AI tools — auto-subtitles, AI avatars, text-to-speech, eye contact correction, background removal. It won't replace Premiere for pro work, but it's the fastest way from "raw footage" to "published YouTube video."

Who it's for: Marketers making social videos, course creators, YouTubers, internal corporate comms teams, and anyone who doesn't want to learn DaVinci Resolve.

Key features

Auto-subtitles 99% accuracy

Generate subtitles in 100+ languages with one click. Style them, animate them, export as SRT. The killer feature for YouTube.

AI avatars No camera needed

Pick from 50+ stock AI avatars or create your own. Type a script, get a talking-head video. Great for explainer content.

Eye contact AI Look at the camera

Uses AI to redirect your eyes to the lens even if you're reading a teleprompter. Surprisingly effective.

Brand kit Save your style

Save colors, fonts, logos, and intro/outro as a brand kit. Apply to every new video with one click.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Browser-based — nothing to install
  • Auto-subtitles are shockingly accurate
  • Massive library of stock footage and music
  • Team collaboration built in (comments, version history)
  • Cheap Pro plan compared to Final Cut / Premiere

✗ What doesn't

  • Watermark on free tier is huge
  • 4K export is locked to Business plan ($59/mo)
  • Not a real NLE — no multi-track timeline like Premiere
  • Render times can be slow for long videos
  • AI avatars look uncanny in close-up shots

Verdict

For 90% of business and creator video needs in 2026, Veed.io is the right tool. If you're editing a feature film or doing color grading, you still need Premiere or DaVinci. For everything else, this is the fastest path to a finished video.
