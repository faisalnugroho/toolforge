ChatPDF is the perfect "I just need to read this one paper" tool

ChatPDF is the "no-signup-needed" AI PDF reader that became a student favorite overnight. Drop a PDF, get a chat box, ask questions. Free tier covers most use cases — and the paid plan at $5/mo is one of the cheapest in the category.

Who it's for: Students, casual researchers, anyone who wants to "just try it" without creating an account.

Key features

Free No signup

Drop a PDF and start chatting in 5 seconds. No email, no credit card, no friction.

Multi Multi-PDF chat

Upload multiple PDFs into one "folder" and ask cross-document questions.

Languages Multi-language

Works in 50+ languages — upload a German paper, ask questions in English.

Export Citation export

Export Q&A sessions with citations as Markdown or PDF. Good for study notes.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Best free tier in the category — 2 PDFs/day, 120 pages each, no signup
  • Cheapest paid plan — $5/mo for unlimited
  • No account needed for free use — try in 5 seconds
  • Good for students and casual users
  • Multi-language support is genuinely useful for non-English sources

✗ What doesn't

  • Citation accuracy is weaker than Humata — answers sometimes paraphrase without precise page refs
  • Plus tier limited to 2,000 pages/mo — heavy users hit the cap fast
  • No OCR on scanned PDFs (Humata handles this)
  • No team features — single-user only

Verdict

ChatPDF is the perfect "I just need to read this one paper" tool. The free tier is genuinely useful — no signup, no credit card. For serious research (50+ PDFs, citations, long documents), upgrade to Humata or ChatGPT Plus. For everyone else, ChatPDF + a coffee is the cheapest PDF-AI combo you can build.
