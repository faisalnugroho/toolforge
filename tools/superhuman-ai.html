What it does well

Superhuman AI is one of the the fastest email experience ever built — now with ai triage and replies. In 2026, it's a serious option for anyone in this category. We tested it for two weeks on real work — here's the honest take on what's great, what's broken, and whether it's worth your money.

Who it's for: Founders, executives, sales reps, and anyone who treats email as the operating system of their job.

Key features

Inbox Zero Keyboard-driven email

Process 100 emails in 10 minutes. Every action has a shortcut. The original 'flow state' email client.

Auto Summarize AI thread summaries

Long email thread? Superhuman summarizes it in one line at the top. One click to expand.

Ask AI inbox search

Ask 'what did the marketing team say about the Q4 launch?' in natural language. Searches subject, body, and attachments.

Reply Voice-matched drafts

Type the gist of your reply in 5 words. Superhuman writes a full draft in your tone, learned from past emails.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • The fastest email client in existence — every keystroke is optimized
  • AI features are tasteful — they enhance, not overwhelm
  • Reminders, read statuses, and send later are best-in-class
  • The on-boarding is legendary (1:1 video session included in the trial)
  • Beautiful UI that makes email feel like a video game

✗ What doesn't

  • $30/mo is steep — three times the price of most email clients
  • Gmail and Outlook only — no iCloud, IMAP-only, or Fastmail
  • Steep learning curve — full productivity takes 2-3 weeks
  • AI features are still catching up to native Gmail Copilot/Outlook features

Verdict

If email is the operating system of your job and you process 200+ messages a day, Superhuman will genuinely change your relationship with your inbox. For everyone else, Gmail's built-in AI is good enough and free. The $30/mo is the price of admission for founders, execs, and sales reps who live in their inbox.
