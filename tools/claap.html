Meeting recording and async video in one platform

Claap combines AI meeting recording (like Otter or Fireflies) with async video communication (like Loom) into a single platform. It records your Google Meet, Zoom, and Teams calls, generates AI summaries with action items, and lets you record screen videos for async updates. The result: fewer status meetings, better meeting notes, and a searchable video library of everything your team discussed.

Who it's for: Remote and hybrid teams (10-500 people) that want to reduce meetings while keeping everyone aligned. Particularly strong for product, engineering, and sales teams that run many recurring meetings.

Key features

AI Meeting Recorder Auto-record & summarize

Connects to Google Meet, Zoom, and Microsoft Teams. Auto-records every meeting, generates a summary with key decisions and action items, and identifies speakers. Summaries are surprisingly accurate — better than Fireflies in our tests.

Async Video Record screen videos

Record your screen and camera to share updates async. Viewers can leave timestamped comments and video replies. Replaces many status meetings — record a 3-minute update instead of scheduling a 30-minute call.

AI Chapters Auto-segmentation

Claap automatically segments meeting recordings into chapters by topic. Jump directly to the part where a decision was made or a specific task was discussed. Saves 10-15 minutes per meeting review.

Searchable Library Find anything said

All recordings are transcribed and searchable. Search for any term and Claap shows every meeting where it was mentioned, with timestamps. No more "when did we decide X?"

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Best of both worlds — meeting recording AND async video in one tool
  • AI summaries are accurate and include clear action items with owners
  • Auto-chapters save real time when reviewing long meetings
  • Integrates with Slack, Notion, and Linear — summaries auto-post to channels
  • Free tier includes 10 recordings/month — enough for small teams

✗ What doesn't

  • Video quality on async recordings is lower than Loom's
  • No mobile app for recording — desktop and browser only
  • Storage limits on lower tiers (10GB on Starter)
  • AI search can miss context — finds the word but not the conversation around it
  • Pricing scales steeply — Enterprise requires custom quotes

Verdict

Claap is the best tool we've tested for teams that want to combine meeting recording with async video. If you're already paying for both Otter and Loom, Claap replaces both for less money. The AI summaries are genuinely better than Fireflies, and the async video feature reduces meeting load. For pure meeting recording, Otter is still simpler. For pure async video, Loom has better video quality. But if you want both in one platform, Claap wins.
