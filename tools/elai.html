A spokesperson video from a paragraph

Elai is a text-to-video platform built around realistic AI avatar presenters. Paste a blog post, script, or PowerPoint and Elai generates a narrated video with a human-like avatar, 80+ languages, and avatar cloning for branded spokespeople. In 2026 it added interactive video elements and a 4K renderer, making it a favorite for L&D teams and agencies producing training and explainer content at scale.

Who it's for: Learning & development teams, agencies, and marketers who need lots of presenter-style videos without booking talent or studios.

Key features

🧑 80+ avatar presenters

Diverse stock avatars, or clone your own face and voice for a branded spokesperson.

🌍 80+ languages

Auto-translate scripts and dub into dozens of languages with lip-sync.

📊 PPT & URL import

Drop in a deck or paste a URL — Elai builds the storyboard automatically.

🎬 4K rendering

Export up to 4K with backgrounds, music, and on-screen elements.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Fastest path from script to spokesperson video
  • Avatar cloning for consistent brand presence
  • Strong language and dubbing coverage
  • Bulk generation for large training libraries
  • No camera or studio required

✗ What doesn't

  • Avatar realism still behind HeyGen's top tier
  • Per-minute pricing gets expensive at volume
  • Less suited to cinematic or creative video
  • Template-heavy look across videos

Verdict

The pragmatic choice for training and explainer videos at volume. HeyGen edges it on avatar realism, but Elai's language coverage and bulk tooling make it the L&D workhorse.
