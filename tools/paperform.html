Forms that don't look like forms

Paperform treats every form like a mini website. You get a rich text editor, custom theming, and the ability to drop products, quizzes, and calculators anywhere on the page. The result is a branded experience — lead pages, order forms, and intake surveys that match your site instead of screaming "I'm a Typeform clone."

Who it's for: Solopreneurs, agencies, and ecommerce brands who sell through forms and care about presentation.

Key features

Editor No-code builder

A Notion-style editor where forms, text, images, and products live on one flexible canvas.

Payments Stripe & PayPal

Collect one-time or subscription payments natively — great for order forms and donations.

Logic Conditional jumps

Show, hide, or redirect questions based on answers for smarter, shorter flows.

Themes Custom branding

Match fonts, colors, and CSS to your brand so the form feels like part of your site.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Gorgeous output — best-looking forms in the category
  • Native payments with Stripe and PayPal
  • Flexible editor beats rigid form builders
  • Strong logic and calculator fields
  • Unlimited forms even on entry plan

✗ What doesn't

  • No free forever plan — trial only
  • Smaller template library than JotForm
  • Advanced analytics need higher tiers
  • Less suited to high-volume enterprise intake

Verdict

Paperform is the pick when design matters as much as function. The $20 Essentials plan covers most small businesses selling through forms.
