The fastest way to get more done with Stability AI

Stability AI builds open generative models (Stable Diffusion, Stable Video, Stable Audio) and ships them as hosted APIs and open weights. It's the backbone for a huge share of indie image apps.

Who it's for: The company behind Stable Diffusion — APIs for image, video, audio, 3D.

Key features

SDXL API Images

Video + Audio Multimodal

Download models to run privately and cheaply at scale.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Open models — no lock-in
  • Broad multimodal coverage
  • Generous free credits
  • Huge third-party ecosystem

✗ What doesn't

  • UI is dev-focused, not consumer
  • Quality trails Midjourney on artistry
  • Rate limits on free tier

Verdict

If you're building with generative media, Stability's APIs are the open, cost-effective foundation. Use the platform to prototype, self-host to scale.
