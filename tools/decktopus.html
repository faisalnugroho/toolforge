"Pitch deck for our Series A in fintech, B2B SaaS, modern" — full deck in 60 seconds.

Decktopus is the AI presentation tool built for sales reps, marketers, and founders who need a polished deck fast. Type one sentence about what you want — "Series A pitch for B2B SaaS fintech company targeting CFOs" — and Decktopus generates a 10-12 slide deck with layouts, copy, stock imagery, icons, and speaker notes. You then edit in the drag-and-drop editor or regenerate individual slides with chat commands. The "Magic Forms" feature lets you embed lead-capture forms directly in your deck.

Who it's for: Sales reps, B2B marketers, founders, consultants, and agencies who need decent-looking decks fast. PowerPoint pros and design purists should look elsewhere.

Key features

AI Gen AI deck generator

Describe your deck in plain English, pick an audience and tone, and Decktopus builds a complete presentation — title, problem, solution, market, traction, ask — in under 60 seconds.

Design AI Smart layouts & images

AI picks layouts, color palettes, fonts, and stock images that match your topic. No more blank-deck paralysis. Adjust per slide or globally.

Magic Forms, polls & CTAs

Embed lead-capture forms, polls, and CTAs directly in slides. Capture viewer emails and feedback without leaving the deck — a killer feature for sales outbound.

Notes AI speaker notes

Auto-generates speaker notes for every slide. Edit them once and they stay synced if you regenerate the slide. Saves hours on prep.

Brand Custom branding & domains

Custom domain support, brand kit (logos, colors, fonts), and white-label decks for agencies and teams. New in 2026: brand-guideline-aware generation.

Export Export & share

Export to PowerPoint, PDF, or share via public link. Track views and engagement via built-in analytics (opens, time spent per slide).

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Fastest deck generation in the category — 60-second time-to-deck
  • Magic Forms are a real differentiator for sales reps doing outbound
  • Speaker notes AI is genuinely good — saves real prep time
  • Brand kit support is solid for agencies and teams
  • Free tier is generous (3 AI decks/month)
  • Solid export quality to PowerPoint

✗ What doesn't

  • Designs are "good" not "great" — beautiful.ai and Gamma still win on aesthetics
  • Stock images feel generic — no AI image generation (yet) beyond stock library
  • Less flexible than PowerPoint/Keynote for custom layouts
  • No real-time collaboration in 2026 (async only)
  • Analytics are basic vs dedicated presentation tools
  • Limited template library compared to Beautiful.ai

Verdict

Decktopus is the best AI presentation tool for sales teams and marketers who need a polished deck fast and want to capture leads directly from slides. Gamma wins on aesthetic minimalism, Beautiful.ai wins on design sophistication, but Decktopus wins on "deck in 60 seconds + built-in lead capture." Worth the $16/mo for any team doing outbound at volume.
