The fastest way to get production-ready 3D assets

Sloyd is a browser-based text-to-3D generator built for people who need game-ready or printable models without learning Blender. You describe what you want, adjust it with sliders, and export glTF, OBJ, or STL. It is not a sculpting tool — it is a generator, and that distinction is exactly why teams ship faster with it.

Who it's for: Game devs, product designers, 3D printing hobbyists, and indie creators who need assets fast and do not want a months-long modeling learning curve.

Key features

Text to 3D Prompt-based generation

Describe an object in plain English and Sloyd returns a watertight mesh you can rotate, inspect, and download immediately.

Parametric sliders Tweak without re-prompting

Adjust dimensions, density, and style with live sliders. Change a chair height without rewriting the prompt.

Clean topology Game-ready exports

Meshes come optimized for real-time engines — low poly counts, no floating geometry, UV-ready out of the box.

One-click export glTF, OBJ, STL

Export to the format your pipeline needs, including print-ready STL for 3D printers.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Genuinely fast — a usable model in under a minute
  • No 3D skill required to get good results
  • Clean, game-ready topology saves hours of cleanup
  • Free tier is enough to test real projects

✗ What doesn't

  • Stylized look — not for photoreal or organic sculpts
  • Limited control vs hand-modeling in Blender
  • Library leans toward objects, not characters
  • Pro required for commercial-scale output

Verdict

If you need 3D assets and you are not a modeler, Sloyd is the cheapest speed upgrade you can buy. The $20/mo Pro pays for itself the first time you skip a $300 freelancer gig for a prop.
