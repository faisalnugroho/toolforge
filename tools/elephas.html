GPT lives in your Mac's menu bar

Elephas is the Mac-native AI writing tool that brings GPT to every text field on your machine. Hit ⌥ Space in Mail, Notes, Safari, Slack, anywhere — and Elephas opens with context-aware AI. No app switching, no copy-pasting into ChatGPT tabs.

Who it's for: Mac power users who want AI in every text field without paying $20/mo for ChatGPT Plus. Especially useful for writers, founders, and knowledge workers who live in Mail and Notes.

Key features

Menu bar Global AI hotkey

Hit ⌥ Space in any app — Mail, Notes, Safari, Slack, anywhere — and Elephas opens with context-aware AI. The text you highlighted is automatically passed as context.

Local Bring-your-own keys

Use your own OpenAI, Claude, or local Ollama API keys. Pay the model cost directly — no Elephas markup. Power users save $100+/year vs ChatGPT Plus.

Super brain Personal knowledge base

Drop in your notes, PDFs, and docs. Elephas builds a searchable 'Super Brain' you can query from any text field. Local embeddings keep your data private.

Snippets Saved AI actions

Build a library of reusable prompts — 'rewrite this email,' 'summarize this article,' 'translate to Spanish.' One click to run them on selected text.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Best Mac-native AI experience — feels like Apple's own feature
  • BYOK means lower long-term cost than ChatGPT Plus
  • Privacy mode — your text isn't sent to Elephas servers

✗ What doesn't

  • Mac-only — no Windows or Linux version
  • Smaller user base than Grammarly/Notion AI
  • Setup requires API key (intimidating for non-technical users)

Verdict

If you're a Mac power user who wants AI in every text field without per-app integrations, Elephas is the best $7/mo you'll spend. The BYOK model means cheaper long-term cost than ChatGPT Plus — and Super Brain is genuinely useful once you feed it 6 months of notes.
