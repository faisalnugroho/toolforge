The automation layer for short-form

Repurpose.io connects your sources (YouTube, TikTok, podcast RSS) to your destinations (Reels, Shorts, LinkedIn, Twitter) and auto-posts. It is not a clipping tool — it is the distribution plumbing that keeps your accounts active without you touching each one.

Who it's for: Creators and social media managers who publish to many platforms and hate the manual cross-post grind.

Key features

Auto-distribute Set and forget

New YouTube videos auto-post to Shorts, Reels, and more.

Podcast to video RSS to social

Turn podcast episodes into audiograms and clips automatically.

Watermarking Branding

Stamp your logo on repurposed content on the fly.

Scheduling Queue control

Space posts out so accounts stay natural.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Saves hours of manual posting
  • Reliable cross-platform sync
  • Good for podcast audiograms
  • Pays for itself in time

✗ What doesn't

Verdict

Repurpose.io is the unglamorous tool that keeps your channels fed. If you publish to more than two platforms, the $15/mo is pure time tax you stop paying.
