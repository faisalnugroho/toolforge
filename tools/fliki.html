Text-to-video for non-editors

Fliki is the easiest way to turn any text — a blog post, a tweet, a product description, a script — into a polished video with voiceover, music, captions, and stock footage. It picked up the workflow that Lumen5 pioneered, then added 2,000+ AI voices in 75+ languages, better auto-storyboarding, and TikTok/Reels/Shorts export presets. By 2026, it's the default for marketers, course creators, and SEO shops that need 100 videos/month without hiring an editor.

Who it's for: Content marketers, social media managers, course creators, agencies, and anyone who needs a lot of short-form video fast. Not a creative tool for video pros — for that, use Descript or Premiere with AI plugins.

Key features

Script Idea-to-video

Paste a blog URL, a tweet, or a script. Fliki extracts the key points, generates scenes with stock footage, writes captions, and adds transitions. 60-second social video in under 5 minutes.

2,000+ AI voices

2,000+ voices in 75+ languages, with emotion/tone controls, voice cloning, and podcast-style multi-voice dialogues. Powered by ElevenLabs and Azure Neural TTS under the hood.

Stock 10M+ stock assets

Built-in library of 10M+ stock photos and clips (Pexels, Pixabay, Storyblocks) so you never have to leave the editor. AI image generation included on Standard+ plans.

Captions Auto subtitles & branding

Auto-generated animated captions in 50+ styles, brand kit support (logo, colors, fonts), and one-click resize for every platform (9:16, 1:1, 16:9).

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Fastest path from text to video — 5-minute turnaround for short social clips
  • 2,000+ voices in 75+ languages — best-in-class for localization
  • Stock library integrated — no tab-switching
  • Free tier is genuinely useful (5 min/mo with watermark)
  • Auto-captions save 30+ minutes per video

✗ What doesn't

  • Stock footage can feel generic — for premium aesthetics, you need a human editor
  • Limited creative control — can't fine-tune scene composition
  • Watermark on free tier is large and centered
  • No native AI avatar video (use Synthesia or HeyGen for that)

Verdict

If you need to ship 50+ short videos a month for social, SEO, or courses, Fliki will pay for itself in week one. It's not for cinematic work — for that, hire an editor or use Descript + Premiere. But for the 80% of marketing video that just needs to be competent, fast, and on-brand, Fliki is the best tool in 2026.
