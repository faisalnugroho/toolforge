The AI music tool built for content creators

Soundraw isn't trying to make the next Beatles album — it's trying to keep your YouTube channel monetized. Where Suno and Udio generate full songs with vocals (and copyright gray areas), Soundraw focuses on background music, intros, outros, and loops that you can safely use on monetized videos. The customization is what makes it special: you can tweak the mood, energy, length, and structure of every track.

Who it's for: YouTubers, TikTokers, podcasters, indie filmmakers, and anyone who needs unlimited original background music without copyright strikes.

Key features

🎚️ Deep customization

Pick a genre, mood, theme, length, tempo, and energy level. Then customize the structure — intro length, verse intensity, chorus drops. Your track, your call.

♾️ Unlimited generations

Unlike Suno or Udio, you can generate as many tracks as you want. No credits. The AI keeps generating until you find the one.

⚖️ Full commercial rights

Every track you generate is yours to monetize on YouTube, TikTok, podcasts, ads, games, films. No copyright strikes, ever. Cleared for monetization.

🎬 Stem exports

Export individual instrument stems (drums, bass, melody). Drop them into your DAW. Perfect for sound designers and video editors who want flexibility.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Unlimited generations on every paid plan
  • Copyright-safe for monetized YouTube channels
  • Stem export is a game-changer for video editors
  • Customization is more granular than Suno/Udio
  • No vocals = no AI music controversy

✗ What doesn't

  • No vocal generation (instrumental only)
  • Less "magical" than Suno's full-song generations

  • Free tier caps you at 10 downloads

Verdict

Soundraw is the AI music tool we recommend to every YouTuber and podcaster. The combination of unlimited generation, full commercial rights, and stem export makes it the safest bet for content creators. For full songs with vocals and emotional impact, Suno is still king. For background music that monetizes, Soundraw wins.
