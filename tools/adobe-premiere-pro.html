The editor pros already use, now AI-assisted

Premiere Pro is the NLE most studios standardize on. In 2025-2026 Adobe layered Firefly AI into the workflow: auto-transcription, AI-driven color and audio cleanup, generative filler B-roll, and object-aware masking. It's not a generative video toy — it's a mature editor that quietly automates the tedious 80% so you can focus on the cut.

Who it's for: Professional and aspiring video editors who need a deep, extensible timeline and AI that accelerates post, not replaces it.

Key features

✂️ AI transcription & captions

Auto-transcribe footage and generate captions in one click.

🎨 Auto color & audio

Firefly-assisted color matching and one-click audio cleanup.

🧠 Generative B-roll

Fill gaps with Firefly-generated filler clips (beta).

🔌 Deep plugin ecosystem

After Effects, Audition, and thousands of third-party integrations.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Industry-standard, endlessly capable
  • AI features accelerate tedious tasks
  • Unmatched plugin/ecosystem
  • Tight Adobe Creative Cloud sync

✗ What doesn't

  • Steep learning curve
  • Subscription only, not cheap
  • AI features still maturing vs dedicated tools
  • Heavy on system resources

Verdict

Premiere Pro remains the safe, deep choice for serious editing. The new AI features won't replace a skilled editor, but they shave hours off every project.
