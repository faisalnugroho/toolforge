The fastest voice cloning API

LMNT is the underdog of voice AI — founded by ex-Google engineers, it's the only major TTS API that streams under 150ms while still sounding natural. Built for real-time voice agents where latency kills the experience. ElevenLabs sounds better; LMNT responds faster.

Who it's for: Voice agent builders, game devs who need real-time NPC dialogue, app devs who need snappy TTS in their products.

Key features

150ms Sub-150ms latency

Streams audio chunks as fast as the model generates them. The fastest of any major TTS API. Critical for conversational agents where every 100ms matters.

Clone 10-second voice cloning

Record 10 seconds of clean audio, get a voice clone in under a minute. Voice quality is good — not ElevenLabs-quality, but close enough for production agents.

UE5 Unreal Engine plugin

Official Unreal Engine 5 plugin — generate NPC dialogue on the fly inside game engines. Unity SDK also available. Real use case for indie game devs.

$0.006/s Honest pricing

$0.006 per second of generated audio. Half the price of ElevenLabs' Creator tier for streaming. Free tier gives 12,500 chars/month forever.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Fastest TTS API on the market — sub-150ms streaming
  • Half the price of ElevenLabs for real-time use
  • Quality is good, not best-in-class
  • Official UE5 plugin is genuinely useful
  • Free tier that doesn't expire

✗ What doesn't

  • Voice quality is a tier below ElevenLabs
  • Smaller voice library (~50 stock voices vs 3,000+ on ElevenLabs)
  • No voice marketplace or community voices
  • Less mature than ElevenLabs — newer API, fewer integrations

Verdict

LMNT is the right pick when latency matters more than voice quality — voice agents, real-time apps, game dialogue. If you're building a polished audio product where voice quality is the entire experience, pay for ElevenLabs. For everything else, LMNT is fast, cheap, and good enough.
