Support that meets customers everywhere

Freshdesk pulls email, chat, phone, and social tickets into one inbox, then uses Freddy AI to suggest replies, summarize threads, and deflect common questions with a self-service portal. Rule-based automations route and prioritize tickets so a two-person support team can feel like ten.

Who it's for: Support teams at SaaS, ecommerce, and services companies that need multichannel helpdesk without enterprise pricing.

Key features

Omni Multichannel inbox

Email, chat, phone, and social tickets unified with context and history.

Freddy AI agent

AI suggestions, thread summaries, and deflection via the knowledge base.

Automation Rules & SLAs

Auto-assign, prioritize, and escalate tickets against SLA policies.

Portal Self-service

A branded help center and community forum to deflect repetitive tickets.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Free plan is genuinely usable for small teams
  • Omnichannel inbox is clean and fast
  • Freddy AI helps agents reply faster
  • Strong automation and SLA tools
  • Easy to roll out and train on

✗ What doesn't

  • Advanced AI and reporting need higher tiers
  • Customization can feel limited vs Zendesk
  • Per-agent pricing adds up as you scale
  • Some integrations are paid add-ons

Verdict

Freshdesk is the balanced pick for teams that want real omnichannel support without Zendesk's price tag. Start free, move to Growth ($15/agent/mo) when automations matter.
