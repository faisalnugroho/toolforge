AI music generation that actually sounds good

Suno AI turned the music world upside down. Type a prompt like "upbeat indie folk song about road trips" and get a full 3-minute track with vocals, harmonies, and production — in under 30 seconds. No instruments needed. No music theory required.

Who it's for: Content creators who need background music, indie musicians exploring ideas, podcasters wanting custom intros, and anyone who's ever wanted to make a song but can't play an instrument.

Key features

♫ Text-to-song generation

Describe the song you want in plain English. Specify genre, mood, tempo, and even lyrical themes. Suno generates complete tracks with vocals, instrumentation, and mixing in one go.

✍ Custom lyrics mode

Write your own lyrics and Suno will sing them. Great for personalizing birthday songs, jingles, or testing songwriting ideas without a recording studio.

🎨 Style & genre control

Choose from hundreds of genre tags — from K-pop to death metal to lo-fi jazz. Mix styles for unique results. Control vocal gender, energy level, and even accent.

🎛 Stem separation

Download individual stems (vocals, drums, bass, etc.) for remixing in your DAW. Pro plan only. Turns Suno from a toy into a legitimate production tool.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Incredible output quality — most listeners can't tell it's AI
  • Generous free tier with 50 songs/day
  • Fast generation — 30 seconds for a full track
  • Genre versatility is genuinely impressive
  • Community features let you share and discover songs

✗ What doesn't

  • Limited fine-grained editing — can't adjust individual notes or phrases
  • Songs capped at ~4 minutes
  • Voice cloning raises ethical concerns
  • Pro plan needed for commercial rights
  • Occasional garbled lyrics in non-English languages

Verdict

Suno is the ChatGPT of music — it makes creation accessible to everyone. If you need custom music for content, it's a no-brainer at $10/mo. Professional musicians will find it useful for ideation but not as a replacement for real production. The free tier is generous enough to try seriously before committing.
