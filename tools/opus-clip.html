The fastest way to turn long videos into 10 viral shorts with one click.

Opus Clip is the AI clipper that took over short-form in 2025. Paste a YouTube link or upload a long video, and it spits out 10-15 short, vertical, captioned clips — each scored for "virality potential." It auto-reframes to vertical, adds animated captions, and identifies the most quotable moments. For solo creators, it's the difference between posting 1 video a week and 10.

Who it's for: YouTubers repurposing long-form content, podcasters, coaches with webinar content, course creators. Anyone trying to feed the TikTok/Reels/Shorts beast without hiring an editor.

Key features

AI virality score Best moments first

Each clip gets a 0-100 virality score. Opus ranks them so you post the strongest ones first. Saves hours of manual scrubbing.

Auto-reframe 9:16 from 16:9

Speaker-tracking AI keeps the face in frame as it reframes landscape to vertical. Looks like a human did it.

Animated captions TikTok-style

Word-by-word karaoke captions, fully styled. Word highlighting, color changes on keywords. This is the style that took over Reels.

AI b-roll Cutaway inserts

Pulls relevant stock footage and inserts it at the boring parts. Optional, but a nice touch for talking-head content.
