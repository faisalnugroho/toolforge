The viral AI video model from China

Hailuo AI is the consumer-facing product from MiniMax, a Chinese AI lab whose video model went viral in early 2025 for producing photorealistic, cinematic 6-second clips from a text prompt. Where Sora felt like a polished research demo, Hailuo felt like a real product: short, fast, cheap, and good enough to ship on social. It's now one of the most-used AI video generators on the market.

Who it's for: Social media creators, marketers, and indie filmmakers who need fast, cheap, photoreal clips. Not great for long-form narrative content (clips cap at 6-10 seconds).

Key features

I2V Image-to-Video

Upload a single still image and Hailuo animates it. This is the killer feature — give it a portrait and it generates natural motion, breathing, hair movement, even lip-sync. Used by everyone from indie musicians to YouTube thumbnail animators.

T2V Text-to-Video

Type a prompt, get a 6-second 720p clip in ~60 seconds. Strong on cinematic camera moves, realistic humans, and dynamic lighting. Weaker on complex multi-character scenes.

Subject Ref Character consistency

Upload 1-4 reference images and Hailuo keeps the same character consistent across clips. Huge for storytelling — finally you can stitch a series of clips with the same protagonist.

Director Camera controls

Specify camera direction (pan, dolly, orbit), motion intensity, and style presets (cinematic, anime, realistic). Better prompt adherence than Runway for complex scene descriptions.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Best-in-class photorealism at this price point — beats Sora on realism in side-by-sides
  • Image-to-video is genuinely useful, not a gimmick
  • Free tier is generous (100 credits = ~10 clips)
  • Fast generation (~60 seconds for a 6-second clip)
  • Strong community on Discord and X, lots of prompt-sharing

✗ What doesn't

  • Clips cap at 6 seconds (10s on Pro) — useless for anything longer than social shorts
  • No native editing — you can't stitch clips together inside Hailuo
  • Complex multi-character scenes still glitch
  • Privacy: prompts and uploads are reviewed by MiniMax staff per their ToS
  • English prompt understanding is good but Chinese prompts are still more accurate

Verdict

If you need photoreal 6-second clips for social, Hailuo is the best price/quality point in 2026. For longer narrative work, you'll need to stitch clips externally with CapCut or Descript. The free tier is enough to try it without committing.
