The WordPress-native writer

Bertha.ai is a writing and image assistant that lives inside WordPress and as a Chrome extension. You highlight text in the editor and Bertha extends, rewrites, or generates — no tab-switching. It is the pragmatic pick for site owners who live in WP.

Who it's for: WordPress site owners, bloggers, and small teams who want AI without leaving the editor.

Key features

WP integration In-editor AI

Generate and edit copy directly in the block editor.

Browser extension Anywhere

Use Bertha on any web text field via Chrome.

Image generation Inline art

Create featured images and illustrations in place.

Long-form Blog drafts

Draft full posts with section-by-section help.
