The design tool built for things you can sell

Kittl is the design app that punches above its weight for merch, apparel, logos, and print. Think Canva, but with genuinely good typography, AI text-to-vector, and one-click text effects that would take an hour in Illustrator. It's the tool Etsy sellers and streetwear brands reach for first.

Who it's for: Print-on-demand sellers, merch designers, small brands, and creators who need pro-looking graphics without learning the Adobe suite.

Key features

AI AI text-to-vector

Describe a graphic and Kittl generates editable vector art. Tweak colors, shapes, and layout — it's real vectors, not a flat PNG.

Fonts 3,000+ premium fonts

A curated font library with distressed, vintage, and display typefaces you'd otherwise pay for separately.

FX One-click text effects

Badges, wavy text, split effects, and gold foil — applied instantly. The kind of detail that sells merch.

Mockups Mockups & background remover

Drop your design onto a hoodie, can, or sign with built-in mockups and a background remover. Export print-ready transparent PNGs.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Best-in-class typography for non-designers
  • AI text-to-vector produces editable art
  • Huge template library for merch and logos
  • Much faster than Illustrator for simple jobs
  • Free tier is usable for testing

✗ What doesn't

  • Pro ($10/mo) needed for commercial use and exports
  • Not a full Illustrator replacement for complex work
  • Less suited to long-form layouts or web design
  • Some trendy effects get overused across POD stores
  • No native print fulfillment — pair with Printful

Verdict

If you sell merch, run a brand, or just want logos that don't look like MS Paint, Kittl is the smartest $10/mo you'll spend in 2026. Start free, upgrade the moment you go commercial.
