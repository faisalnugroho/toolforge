One photo, one voice, one living character

Hedra 2 is a generative video platform that specializes in character animation. Upload a single image and an audio clip, and Hedra generates a video of that character speaking with synchronized lip movement, facial expressions, and head motion. It is the fastest way to create talking-head content for explainer videos, social media, and localization without a camera or actor.

Who it's for: Content creators, educators, marketers, and localization teams who need talking-head video content without filming.

Key features

🎭 Character video from image+audio

Upload a portrait and a voice clip. Hedra generates a video of the character speaking with realistic lip sync and expressions.

🗣️ Built-in voice cloning

Clone a voice from a short sample and generate speech in that voice, then animate it. Full pipeline in one tool.

🌍 Multilingual lip sync

The model handles 30+ languages with appropriate mouth shapes. Localize content without re-filming.

🎨 Style control

Cartoon, photorealistic, stylized, and 3D character modes. Not just photoreal faces.

⚡ Fast generation

A 30-second talking video renders in under 2 minutes. Faster than full video generation models.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Best-in-class single-image character animation
  • Integrated voice cloning, no separate tool needed
  • Multilingual lip sync handles 30+ languages
  • Generous free tier, 5 videos per month
  • Fast turnaround for talking-head content

✗ What doesn't

  • Limited to upper-body and face animation
  • Not a full video generation tool, cannot create arbitrary scenes
  • Free tier videos have a watermark
  • Voice cloning quality varies by source audio

Verdict

Hedra 2 is the best tool for creating talking-head video content without a camera. If you need explainer videos, social media talking content, or localization, it saves you from hiring actors and booking studio time. It is not a general-purpose video generator, but for character animation it is unmatched at this price point.
