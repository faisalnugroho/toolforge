The image toolkit, not just a generator

getimg.ai bundles text-to-image, inpainting, outpainting, and custom model training (DreamBooth) into one fast web app. It is the pragmatic choice when you need more than Midjourney allows but want a simpler life than running ComfyUI.

Who it's for: Creators, marketers, and indie devs who need flexible image generation plus editing without a local GPU.

Key features

Text to image 60+ models

Switch between SDXL, anime, and custom models instantly.

Inpainting Edit by mask

Paint the area you want changed and describe the result.

DreamBooth Train a face

Train a custom model on your subject in a few steps.

API Production use

Generate at scale via a clean REST API.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Huge model library
  • Editing tools built in
  • Free tier is generous
  • API is developer-friendly

✗ What doesn't

  • UI can feel busy
  • Custom training needs care
  • No native video
  • Pro needed for volume

Verdict

getimg.ai is the most complete browser image toolkit under $15/mo. If you outgrow Midjourney but do not want to self-host, this is the sweet spot.
