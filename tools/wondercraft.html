Studio-quality audio without a studio

Wondercraft is the fastest way to turn scripts, docs, or articles into polished spoken-word audio. No mic, no booth, no editor — just pick a voice and publish.

Who it's for: Authors, podcasters, educators, and marketers who need narrated audio but don't have studio time or a recording budget.

Key features

Text to speech

Paste a script or doc and get natural narration in 200+ voices across 20+ languages, with emotion and pacing controls.

AI voice cloning

Clone your own voice or pick from licensed voice actors for consistent, branded audio.

Podcast studio

Layer in music, sound effects, and multiple speakers to assemble a full episode without editing software.

Publish-ready export

Export MP3/WAV and chapterized audiobook formats ready for Audible, Spotify, and Apple Podcasts.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Studio-quality output in minutes, not days
  • Huge voice library with emotions and multilingual support
  • No recording gear or editing skills required
  • Great for repurposing blog content into audio

✗ What doesn't

  • Free trial is limited to ~3 minutes
  • Voice cloning requires a consent step
  • Not built for music production
  • Premium voices sit behind higher tiers

Verdict

If you publish audio regularly — audiobooks, podcasts, or narrated blog spin-offs — Wondercraft replaces a recording studio and an editor for a fraction of the cost. The $15/mo Creator plan pays for itself on the first episode.
