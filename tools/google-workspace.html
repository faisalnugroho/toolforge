AI inside your inbox

Google Workspace with Gemini bakes AI into Gmail, Docs, Sheets, and Meet: draft and summarize email, write and research docs, explain spreadsheets, and get live meeting notes and translation — all where your work already happens.

Who it's for: Teams and individuals living in Google apps who want AI assistance without adopting a new tool.

Key features

Gmail Smart compose + summarize

Draft replies, summarize threads, and surface action items automatically.

Docs Write + research

Generate and refine docs, build outlines, and cite with Gemini side-by-side.

Sheets Formula help

Explain data, write formulas, and build charts from plain prompts.

Meet Notes + translate

Live translated captions and auto-generated meeting notes.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • AI inside tools people already use
  • Strong email and docs assistance
  • Real-time collaboration still best-in-class
  • Translate and captions in Meet
  • Familiar, low training cost

✗ What doesn't

  • Gemini is a paid add-on
  • Less deep than dedicated AI apps
  • Privacy settings need attention
  • Best value on Business tiers

Verdict

Gemini in Workspace is the lowest-friction way to put AI into everyday email, docs, and meetings — no new app to learn. Power users wanting a dedicated writing or research tool will still want ChatGPT or Claude, but for the 90% workflow it is excellent.
