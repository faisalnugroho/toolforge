The corporate voiceover standard

Murf is the AI voiceover tool for explainer videos, e-learning, and corporate training. 200+ studio-quality voices in 20+ languages, with emphasis control, pause insertion, and pronunciation editing. For businesses that need polished voiceovers without hiring voice actors, Murf is the default.

Who it's for: L&D teams, marketing teams, e-learning creators needing professional voiceovers.

Key features

🎙️ 200+ studio voices

Professional voice actor quality, not 'AI voice' quality. Multiple tones per voice.

⏯️ Emphasis + pause control

Fine-tune how the voice delivers lines. Add pauses, change emphasis, adjust speed.

🗣️ Pronunciation editor

Type how words should sound (phonetic). Perfect for brand names and jargon.

Auto-sync voiceover to video. Adjust timing visually.
