The face enhancer that broke TikTok

Remini is the AI app that took over TikTok in 2023 with the "AI yearbook" trend. But underneath the viral filter is a genuinely powerful face restoration model. Drop in a blurry 2010 Facebook photo, and Remini reconstructs facial detail with uncanny accuracy. It's especially strong on faces (less so on full scenes or text). The free tier gives 5 enhancements/day, Pro is $9.99/wk (annual discount available).

Who it's for: Anyone restoring old family photos. Social media managers needing quick face cleanup. Dating profile photographers. Genealogists working with scanned archives. Less useful for full-scene enhancement — Topaz or Remini's "Enhance" mode handle that better.

Key features

Face Best-in-class face restoration

Trained on millions of face pairs. Reconstructs eyes, skin texture, and hair detail from inputs as small as 64x64. The "AI Yearbook" filter made this go viral for good reason — it works.

Mobile Native iOS & Android apps

Camera roll integration, real-time preview, one-tap enhance. The mobile UX is what Topaz and Lightroom don't have. Web version works but mobile is the primary surface.

Video Video face restoration (Pro)

Enhance face detail in low-res video. Useful for old home videos or security footage. Pro-only feature. Output is 1080p max.

Avatar AI avatar generator

Upload 8-10 selfies, get 50+ AI-generated portraits in different styles. Similar to Lensa / Aragon / Photo AI. Fun for social profiles.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Face restoration is genuinely best-in-class
  • Mobile-first UX is polished and fast
  • Free tier is usable for casual use
  • Viral templates drive engagement (yearbook, corporate headshot)

✗ What doesn't

  • Weekly pricing is aggressive ($9.99/wk ≈ $520/yr)
  • Privacy concerns: face data uploaded to Chinese-owned servers
  • Full-scene enhancement is weaker than Topaz
  • Free tier has aggressive ads and watermarks

Verdict

Remini is the best face-only enhancer in 2026. If you have a stack of old scanned family photos and want to print them at 8x10, Remini's free tier is enough. For ongoing professional use, the annual Pro plan is overpriced — Topaz Photo AI ($199 one-time) does more for less. The privacy question is real: read the policy before uploading sensitive photos.
