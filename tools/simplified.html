One subscription to replace four

Simplified is the marketing team Swiss Army knife. AI writer for blog posts and ad copy, Canva-style design editor with brand kits, link-short video editor with subtitles, and a Buffer-class social scheduler — all from one login, one billing, one invoice. If you're a freelancer or solopreneur paying for 4+ tools to handle marketing, Simplified is the consolidation play.

Who it's for: Solopreneurs, freelancers, and small marketing teams paying 4-5 separate subscriptions. Less ideal for enterprises with established creative workflows.

Key features

AI Writer 80+ templates

Blog posts, ad copy, emails, product descriptions, LinkedIn posts, video scripts. Pick a template, fill 3 fields, get 5 variations.

Design Studio Canva alternative

Drag-and-drop editor with 100K+ templates, brand kit (colors/fonts/logo), AI background removal, AI image generation.

Video Editor Subtitle tool built in

Trim, cut, add captions (auto-generated in 30+ languages), export to vertical or landscape. Targets Reels/TikTok workflows.

Scheduler Multi-platform social

Schedule to Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Facebook, Pinterest, YouTube. Calendar view, analytics, team collaboration.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Replaces 4-5 separate tools (writer + design + video + scheduler)
  • Generous free tier — 1,500 words/mo is enough to test
  • Brand kit keeps everything visually consistent
  • Built-in collaboration — real-time co-editing
  • AI image generation is solid for design mockups

✗ What doesn't

  • Each module is 'good', but none is best-in-class
  • Video editor is basic — heavy lifting still needs Premiere
  • Learning curve for the full feature set (8+ modules)
  • Some AI outputs still feel templated vs Jasper/Claude

Verdict

Simplified is the best value in marketing AI if you're consolidating tools. At $20/mo for the Pro plan, it undercuts buying Jasper ($49) + Canva Pro ($13) + Buffer ($6) + InVideo ($25) = $93/mo combined. The trade-off: each module is 'good enough', not 'best'. If you need elite-quality video or in-depth SEO, supplement with specialized tools.
