Motion from a prompt

Runway is the generative video studio behind award-winning AI film work. Gen-4 turns text and images into coherent video, while magic tools erase, inpaint, and upscale footage — all from the browser.

Who it's for: Filmmakers, designers, and creators who want controllable generative video and AI editing magic.

Key features

Gen-4 Text + image to video

Prompt or seed an image and Gen-4 produces coherent, controllable motion.

Motion tools Image to video

Animate stills, interpolate between frames, and drive camera moves with prompts.

Magic tools Edit with AI

Erase objects, inpaint, upscale, and restyle footage without green screens.

Director mode Fine control

Keyframes, motion brushes, and camera controls for shot-level direction.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Best-in-class controllability
  • Magic editing tools are genuinely useful
  • Strong free tier for testing
  • Active model releases (Gen-3/4)
  • Great for concept and previs work

✗ What doesn't

  • Credits run out fast on paid tiers
  • Still imperfect physics and hands
  • Learning curve on Director mode
  • Best output needs careful prompting

Verdict

Runway is the creative's generative video suite — text-to-video, image animation, and AI editing magic in one place. For concept art, previs, and social motion it is the tool to beat in 2026.
