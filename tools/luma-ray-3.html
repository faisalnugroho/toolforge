Ray 3 is Luma's third-generation video model, powering the Dream Machine platform. It's known for fast turnaround, smooth motion, and a distinctive stylized look that many creators prefer for animations and abstract sequences.

Who it's for: Creators who want quick iterations and a recognizable artistic style without waiting in long generation queues.

✓ What works
