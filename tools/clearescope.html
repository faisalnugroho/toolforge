What Clearscope is built for

Clearscope is the enterprise standard for content optimization. You paste a target keyword, it hands your writers the exact terms, headings, and readability to match the top content — and grades the result with a letter that correlates with ranking.

Who it's for: Enterprise SEO and content teams that need consistent, brand-safe optimization at scale.

Key features

Report Content Reports

Paste a target keyword, get the exact terms, headings, and readability to match top content.

Editor Docs & WordPress

Optimize inside the tools your team already uses — no context switching.

Grade Content Grade

An A–F grade that correlates with ranking potential, not vanity metrics.

Share Team workflows

Share reports, leave comments, and enforce brand voice across writers.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Best-in-class for enterprise SEO teams
  • Integrates with Docs and WordPress
  • Strong, defensible reporting
  • Trusted by major brands
  • Improves E-E-A-T signals

✗ What doesn't

  • Pricey at $170/mo+
  • Built for teams, overkill for solo bloggers
  • Slower iteration than Surfer
  • No built-in AI writer

Verdict

For a content team at scale, Clearscope is worth every dollar — its reports keep dozens of writers aligned and its grades hold up to scrutiny. Solo creators should start with Surfer; graduate to Clearscope when the team grows.
