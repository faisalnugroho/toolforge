The fastest way to rescue and upscale any footage

Topaz Video AI is the industry-standard desktop app for restoring and enhancing video with machine learning. Point it at a clip — old home movies, low-res screen recordings, drone footage — and its Proteus, Gaia, and Artemis models rebuild detail, kill noise, and smooth motion locally on your GPU.

Who it's for: Video editors, archival restorers, and creators who need 4K/8K output from imperfect source material.

Key features

Proteus Upscaling to 4K/8K

The Proteus model analyzes each frame and upscales SD/HD footage to 4K or 8K with genuine detail recovery, not blurry stretching.

Gaia & Artemis Noise & artifact removal

Strip compression artifacts, sensor noise, and film grain while preserving the detail that matters.

Apollo & Chronos Frame interpolation

Chronos generates new frames to convert 24/30fps into smooth 60fps; Apollo handles clean slow-motion.

Batch mode Whole-folder workflows

Drop a folder of clips, set a model stack, and walk away — ideal for archival restoration projects.
