Generate, design, schedule — all in one tab

Ocoya positions itself as the all-in-one AI social media tool that combines content generation, graphic design, and scheduling in a single platform. Its AI writer generates platform-specific copy, the built-in design tool creates graphics (no need for Canva), and the scheduler publishes across all major platforms. At $19/mo, it undercuts most competitors while offering more features.

Who it's for: Small businesses, solopreneurs, and social media managers who want one tool instead of three (scheduler + copywriter + Canva).

Key features

WriterAI Copywriter

Generate social media captions, ad copy, blog intros, and product descriptions. Supports 30+ languages and platform-specific formatting.

DesignGraphics Designer

Built-in Canva-like design tool with templates for every social platform. Generate graphics without leaving the app.

ScheduleMulti-Platform Scheduler

Schedule posts to Instagram, Facebook, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, TikTok, Pinterest, YouTube, and Google Business from one dashboard.

TagsHashtag Generator

AI suggests relevant hashtags based on your content, industry, and trending topics. Get the right mix of popular and niche tags.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Best value in the space — AI copy + design + scheduling at $19/mo
  • Replaces Canva + Buffer + Copy.ai for many use cases
  • E-commerce integrations (Shopify, WooCommerce) for product posts
  • AI-generated graphics are surprisingly decent
  • 30+ language support for international brands

✗ What doesn't

  • Jack of all trades, master of none — AI writing isn't as good as Jasper, design isn't as good as Canva
  • Smaller user base means less community support and fewer tutorials
  • Analytics are basic compared to Agorapulse or Sprout Social
  • Can be buggy — occasional sync issues with scheduled posts
  • No social listening features

Verdict

Ocoya is the Swiss Army knife of social media tools. If you want one tool that does copy, design, and scheduling — and you're on a budget — it's hard to beat at $19/mo. If you need best-in-class in any single category, dedicated tools (Buffer for scheduling, Jasper for copy, Canva for design) still win. But for small teams that want simplicity over perfection, Ocoya is a great choice.
