Understand any paper in plain language

Scispace (formerly Typeset) lets you search 200M+ papers, ask questions about any PDF, and get answers with citations. It is the research assistant that explains methodology and stats instead of just summarizing.

Who it is for: Grad students, academics, and R&D teams who read a lot of papers and need to extract methods, gaps, and related work fast.

Key features

Search Literature search

Semantic search across 200M+ papers returns relevant, not just keyword-matched, results.

Ask PDF Q&A

Upload a paper and ask what was the sample size, answers cite the exact line.

Explain Plain-language

Rewrites dense methods sections into readable summaries.

Cite Citation help

Find supporting or contradicting papers and format references automatically.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Genuinely useful paper Q&A with citations
  • Huge indexed corpus
  • Plain-language explanations save time
  • Free tier is usable
  • Good for literature reviews

✗ What does not

  • Premium needed for heavy use
  • Occasional hallucinated citations, verify
  • Less strong outside STEM
  • UI can feel busy

Verdict

Scispace is the fastest way to get through a reading list without losing the thread. For students and researchers it is near-essential. Keep Perplexity or Elicit alongside it: Scispace for deep paper Q&A, the others for broad synthesis.
