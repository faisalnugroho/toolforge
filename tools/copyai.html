From copywriting tool to full GTM AI platform

Copy.ai started in 2020 as a simple AI copywriting tool competing with Jasper. By 2026, it has pivoted hard into a "GTM AI" platform — a go-to-market system that handles prospecting, enrichment, cold outreach personalization, and content generation for sales and marketing teams. The copywriting features are still there, but the real value is in the sales workflow automation. If you're in B2B sales, this is now a serious alternative to Outreach and Salesloft — with AI doing the heavy lifting.

Who it's for: B2B sales and marketing teams that want AI to handle prospecting, outreach personalization, and content creation. Particularly strong for SDR teams that send high-volume cold email and need personalization at scale.

Key features

Prospecting AI lead finding

Describe your ideal customer and Copy.ai finds matching prospects from its database of 250M+ contacts. Enriches each lead with company info, recent news, and social signals — then writes a personalized cold email for each.

Outreach AI Personalized sequences

Generate multi-step email sequences where each message references the prospect's company, role, and recent activity. Far more personalized than template-based outreach tools. Integration with HubSpot, Salesforce, and Pipedrive.

Content Generation Marketing copy

The original copywriting features remain — blog posts, ad copy, social media content, product descriptions. 90+ templates for specific content types. Quality is good but not dramatically better than ChatGPT for one-off content.

Workflow AI Custom automation

Build custom AI workflows — "when a lead is added to HubSpot, research their company, write a personalized email, and draft a LinkedIn connection message." No-code workflow builder with 200+ integrations.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Prospecting + outreach + content in one platform — replaces 3-4 tools
  • AI-personalized cold emails get 2-3x higher reply rates than templates
  • Workflow builder is genuinely powerful for sales automation
  • Free tier includes 2,000 words/month — enough to test
  • Strong CRM integrations — HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive, Outreach

✗ What doesn't

  • Pivoted away from pure copywriting — now feels sales-first
  • Prospect database quality varies by industry — weaker outside tech/SaaS
  • Content generation quality lags Jasper for brand voice consistency
  • Pricing jumps from $49 (Starter) to $243/mo (Advanced) — big gap
  • Learning curve on workflow builder — not as intuitive as Zapier

Verdict

Copy.ai's pivot to a GTM platform makes it more valuable for B2B sales teams than its original copywriting focus. If your team does cold outreach at scale, the AI personalization alone justifies the price — reply rates improve 2-3x over templated sequences. For pure content writing, Jasper is still better. For pure prospecting data, Apollo and Clay have deeper databases. But if you want one tool that handles prospecting, outreach, and content, Copy.ai is the most integrated option.
