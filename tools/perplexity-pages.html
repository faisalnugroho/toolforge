The fastest way to publish a citation-backed article on any topic

Perplexity Pages is the publishing half of Perplexity — the search engine for AI. You type a prompt, Perplexity researches the live web, writes a structured article with inline citations, and gives you a shareable URL on perplexity.ai. In 2026 it is the highest-leverage tool for marketers, students, and journalists who need to publish well-researched content fast.

Who it's for: Content marketers who need research-backed posts, students writing essays with proper citations, journalists drafting explainers, and anyone who has ever wasted 3 hours producing a "thorough" article that was mostly AI hallucinations.

Key features

Research Live web + 20+ sources

Pulls from academic papers, news, government data, Reddit, YouTube transcripts. Citations link to every claim.

Structure Auto-formatted

H2/H3 hierarchy, callouts, images, tables, comparison blocks. No blank-page paralysis.

Edit Inline rewriting

Click any paragraph to regenerate, expand, simplify, or change tone. The edit is grounded in the same sources.

Visuals Auto images

Pulls relevant charts, photos, and infographics from open sources. Adds alt-text automatically.

Share Public URL

Every Page gets a free perplexity.ai/page/... URL. Pro gets custom domains and no branding.

Embed Embed in any site

iframe-embeddable. Use Pages as research widgets inside Notion, Substack, or your own site.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Citation quality is best-in-class — every claim is sourced
  • Auto-formatting saves 90% of the "blank page" work
  • Free tier is genuinely unlimited (with Perplexity branding)
  • Edit mode is grounded in sources — fewer hallucinations
  • Shareable URL is great for SEO and link building

✗ What doesn't

  • Limited style customization compared to Ghost or Jasper
  • Cannot export as Markdown cleanly (PDF and HTML only)
  • Long-form (>3000 words) Pages lose coherence
  • Pro pricing ($20/mo) high for what is essentially a research tool

Verdict

Perplexity Pages is the highest-leverage writing tool in 2026 for anyone whose job is to publish research-backed content. It will not replace your voice, but it will get you to a publishable first draft in 60 seconds. For pure SEO content, pair it with Surfer or Frase; for thought leadership, pair it with Claude for the final pass.
