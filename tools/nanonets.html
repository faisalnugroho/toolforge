The document AI that doesn't need templates

Most OCR tools (and even some AI ones) need you to define a template for every document layout. Got a new vendor invoice format? Recalibrate. Nanonets uses GPT-style multimodal AI to read documents like a human does. Throw 50 different invoice layouts at it — it just works. Built for finance teams drowning in AP, ops teams processing supplier docs, and anyone doing serious document automation.

Who it's for: Finance teams, accounts payable, accounts receivable, ops teams, and any business processing high volumes of unstructured documents.

Key features

📄 Zero-template OCR

Drop in PDFs, images, scans — even weirdly formatted ones. Nanonets extracts the right fields without you defining templates. New vendor? No retraining.

🧾 Pre-trained models

Invoices, receipts, passports, driver's licenses, W-2s, purchase orders, bank statements. Pre-trained out of the box. Just upload and go.

🔗 100+ integrations

Native connectors for QuickBooks, Xero, SAP, NetSuite, Salesforce, Workday, Google Drive, and 100+ more. No glue code.

🤖 Workflow automation

Approval flows, validation rules, exception handling. Build full AP automation without writing a line of code.
