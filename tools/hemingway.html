The writer's best friend for clarity

Hemingway Editor is the writing tool that makes your prose bold and clear. It flags adverbs, passive voice, complex sentences, and grade level. The new AI mode in 4.0 suggests rewrites for the worst offenders. For writers who care about clarity, Hemingway is the gold standard.

Who it's for: Writers, bloggers, marketers who want clear, bold prose.

Key features

📊 Readability scoring

Grade-level scoring for every paragraph. Aim for grade 6-8 for general audience.

🟡 Highlight issues

Yellow = hard to read, red = very hard, purple = complex words, blue = adverbs.

🤖 AI rewrites

Click any highlighted sentence, get an AI rewrite that's clearer. New in 4.0.

💻 Works offline

Desktop app works without internet. Great for distraction-free writing.
