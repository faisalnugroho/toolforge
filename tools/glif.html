Turn a prompt chain into a shareable app in minutes

Glif is a visual builder where you connect LLMs, image models, and logic blocks into little apps — then publish them so anyone can use them with one click. Think "IFTTT for generative AI," beloved by the AI-art community for its remix culture.

Who it's for: Artists, indie makers, and marketers who want to ship a fun AI tool without writing code — and love riffing on other people's creations.

Key features

Blocks Visual canvas

Drag LLM, image, and logic blocks onto a canvas. No code, instant preview.

Remix Fork anything

Every published glif can be cloned and tweaked. The community improves each other's work.

Share One-click apps

Publish to a clean URL anyone can use — no install, no signup wall.

Models Multi-model

Mix GPT, Claude, Stable Diffusion, and Flux blocks in one flow.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Genuinely fun, low-friction creative tool
  • Strong remix community drives discovery
  • Free tier is generous
  • Great for rapid prototyping of AI concepts

✗ What doesn't

  • Not built for production/enterprise use
  • Quality depends on the model blocks you wire
  • Occasional queue waits at peak times
  • No native API for most glifs

Verdict

Glif is the fastest way to go from "what if" to a working AI toy. Free, delightful, and perfect for creators who think in flows, not code.
