Finally, the keyword database has an LLM

Ahrefs built the world's second-largest search crawler (after Google itself). They've indexed 30B pages and 600M keywords. Now they're piping that data into GPT-4-class AI. The result: keyword clustering that actually understands intent, content grading that flags every paragraph that doesn't earn its place, and competitive analysis that reads like a senior strategist's notes.

Who it's for: Serious SEO teams, agencies managing 10+ clients, and publishers who treat organic traffic as a business channel.

Key features

Content Helper AI grading on existing posts

Paste your URL — get an A-F grade, paragraph-by-paragraph rewrite suggestions, missing subtopics, and target word count. Trained on what's actually ranking.

Keyword clustering Intent-grouped at scale

Upload 10K keywords — get them clustered by SERP overlap and intent in 2 minutes. Used to take a junior SEO a week.

Site Explorer AI Plain-English competitor analysis

Type 'why is competitor.com outranking me for X?' — get a real analysis citing their top pages, backlink gaps, content depth differences.

DR boost Backlink outreach AI

Finds link prospects in your niche, drafts personalized outreach emails in your brand voice, tracks responses. 3x reply rates vs manual.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Largest freshest backlink index of any SEO tool
  • AI Content Helper actually grades against ranking pages
  • Site Explorer remains the gold standard 15 years on
  • UI is fast and dense — power users love it
  • Ahrefs Webmaster Tools (free for site owners) is criminally underused

✗ What doesn't

  • $129/mo is the highest entry tier in the category
  • Keyword research UX is dense — steeper learning curve than SEMrush
  • AI features added in 2025-26 are still maturing
  • Limited social/search intent data outside Google

Verdict

If SEO is core to your business, Ahrefs is non-negotiable. The new AI features are a force multiplier on top of the best backlink data in the industry. If you're a hobby blogger, $129/mo is overkill — start with free Ahrefs Webmaster Tools and a $20/mo Mangools or SE Ranking subscription.
