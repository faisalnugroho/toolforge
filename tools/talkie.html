Characters that feel less like chatbots

Talkie is an AI companion platform focused on roleplay and character chat. Unlike a plain assistant, Talkie pairs each character with a visual, a voice, and persistent memory so conversations feel continuous. It sits in the same lane as Character.AI but leans harder into visuals and mobile.

Who it's for: People who want entertainment, roleplay, language practice, or just a low-stakes companion chat — not productivity users.

Key features

Visual characters Art per bot

Each character has a generated portrait that reacts to the scene.

Voice mode Spoken replies

Characters respond with synthesized speech, not just text.

Memory Persistent context

Bots remember prior chats for continuity across sessions.

Community hub User-made bots

Browse thousands of community-created characters.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Free to start
  • Strong mobile experience
  • Visuals + voice add immersion
  • Huge character library

✗ What doesn't

  • Not for work or research
  • Quality varies by creator
  • Free tier caps daily messages
  • Privacy is loose by design

Verdict

For entertainment and roleplay, Talkie is a polished, free entry point. Do not expect it to help with your spreadsheet — it is purely for fun.
