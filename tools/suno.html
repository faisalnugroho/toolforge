AI music generation that actually sounds like music

Suno broke through in 2024 with AI-generated full songs — vocals, instrumentals, the works. v4 (late 2025) added stem separation (isolate vocals, drums, bass, other), persona training (consistent voice across songs), and dramatically better musicality. The output is no longer 'AI slop' — it's actually good. Used by 10M+ creators, including professional musicians prototyping ideas and content creators who need royalty-free music that doesn't sound generic.

Who it's for: Content creators needing background music, songwriters prototyping ideas, podcasters who want intros/outros. Also hobbyists who always wanted to make music but couldn't play instruments.

Key features

🎤 Full Song Generation

Type 'upbeat indie rock about summer road trips' → 2-minute song with verse, chorus, bridge, vocals, and instrumentals. Not a loop — an actual song structure.

🎚️ Stem Separation (v4)

Isolate vocals, drums, bass, and other instruments. Use them in your DAW, remix, or create karaoke versions. Game-changer for producers.

🎭 Persona Training

Upload 3-5 of your own vocal samples. Suno learns your voice and uses it in generations. Consistent artist identity across tracks.

🎼 Instrumental Mode

Generate backing tracks without vocals. Perfect for video soundtracks, podcast backgrounds, and game music.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Most musical AI generator — actually understands song structure
  • Stem separation is industry-leading (was previously a $500/mo service)
  • Persona training enables consistent artist branding
  • Free tier is genuinely usable (50 credits/day = ~10 songs)
  • Output is royalty-free for commercial use on paid plans

✗ What doesn't

  • Quality varies — some outputs are amazing, some are clearly AI
  • Persona training requires good source material (clean vocals, no background music)
  • Lyrics generation is functional but not poetic — better to write your own
  • Vocal realism still slightly below real human singers in emotional nuance

Verdict

The default for AI music generation in 2026. The free tier is enough for casual use. Pro ($10/mo) is a no-brainer for content creators. The stem separation alone justifies the cost for producers.
