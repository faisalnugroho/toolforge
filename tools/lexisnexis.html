The fastest way to get more done with LexisNexis

LexisNexis is the incumbent legal-research database — case law, statutes, and secondary sources for nearly every jurisdiction. Its 2026 AI layer (Lexis+ AI) answers research questions, summarizes holdings, and drafts memos with inline citations you can verify.

Who it's for: Attorneys, paralegals, and law clerks who need authoritative, citable primary law.

Key features

Shepard's Citation check

The gold-standard citator tells you if a case is still good law.

AI Memo drafting

Lexis+ AI drafts research memos with clickable citations.

Search Natural language

Ask in plain English; results map to controlling authority.

Practical Guidance

Practice-area guides and forms alongside the cases.
