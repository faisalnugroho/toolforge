Predibase lets teams fine-tune and serve open-source LLMs with a serverless stack built on LoRAX. You get the control and privacy of self-hosting with the convenience of a managed platform — and you're never locked into someone else's model weights.

Who it's for: ML teams and enterprises that need custom, private, cost-efficient model serving without running their own GPU cluster.
