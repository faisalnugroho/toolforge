Notebooks that finally feel collaborative

Deepnote is a cloud notebook — like Jupyter, but multiplayer — with a built-in AI that writes and explains code, fixes errors, and queries your data in plain English. It's popular with data teams who need reproducible, shareable analysis.

Who it's for: Data scientists, analysts, and teams who want collaborative notebooks with AI assistance.

Key features

🤝 Real-time collab

Multiple people edit the same notebook live.

🤖 AI assistant

Writes, explains, and debugs notebook code.

🔌 Integrations

Connects to warehouses, S3, BigQuery, Snowflake.

📊 Interactive outputs

Publish dashboards and apps from notebooks.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • True multiplayer notebooks
  • Strong AI code help
  • Great integrations
  • Shareable published apps

✗ What doesn't

  • Free tier is limited
  • Less flexible than raw Jupyter for some
  • Cloud-only (no offline)
  • Heavier than a local script

Verdict

The best collaborative notebook with AI built in. If you live in Python data work and hate lonely Jupyter files, Deepnote is the upgrade.
