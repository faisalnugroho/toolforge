The AI analyst that reads what you can't

Hebbia is the AI tool that does what junior analysts and associates spend 80% of their day doing: reading and summarizing massive document sets. Drop in 500 contracts, a 10-K, or a pile of due diligence files — Hebbia reads them all in parallel, then answers natural-language questions with citations to specific pages and clauses. Used by 30%+ of the top hedge funds, 4 of the Big 4, and major law firms. It's not a search tool; it's a reasoning engine over your private data.

Who it's for: Investment analysts (hedge funds, PE, VC), lawyers (M&A, due diligence, compliance), consultants, and any knowledge worker drowning in documents.

Key features

Matrix Document matrix view

Hebbia's signature feature: load 50 contracts, and it auto-builds a matrix — every contract in a row, every clause/topic in a column, with AI-generated answers in each cell. Replaces 6 weeks of associate work.

Citations Click-to-verify citations

Every answer includes inline citations: 'The change-of-control clause is in Section 7.2 of Contract 14, page 23.' Click and jump straight to the source. Auditable, defensible, no hallucinations.

Multi-format PDF, DOCX, XLS, email, transcripts

Reads everything: 10-Ks, contracts, SEC filings, emails, call transcripts, Excel models, even scanned PDFs via OCR. Processes 500+ documents in 5 minutes.

Permissions SOC 2, on-prem, SSO

Enterprise-grade security: SOC 2 Type II, optional on-prem deployment, SSO, full audit logs, permission inheritance from your data sources. The only AI tool legal will approve.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Replaces 80% of associate/analyst document review work
  • Citations are auditable — actually usable in legal/financial workflows
  • Document matrix is a genuine breakthrough UX for analysts
  • Enterprise security and on-prem deployment are best-in-class
  • Customers report 5-10x faster deal cycles

✗ What doesn't

  • Enterprise-only — no self-serve, no SMB tier
  • Pricing is opaque and steep (~$30K+/yr, requires sales call)
  • Steep learning curve — analysts need training to use well
  • Not for casual document Q&A — overkill for 1-2 doc use cases
  • Web app only — no native integrations to common tools

Verdict

If you're a hedge fund, PE shop, law firm, or Fortune 500 deal team still doing document review the manual way, Hebbia will pay for itself in a single deal. If you're a solo founder, freelancer, or small business — you don't need this, use ChatGPT or Claude. It's not a tool for the masses; it's a tool for the people who do $100M+ decisions based on 1,000-page documents.
