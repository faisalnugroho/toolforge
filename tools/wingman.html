Coach every rep on every call, live

Wingman sits silently on your sales calls and pipes battlecard cues, objection handling, and talk-track nudges to the rep in real time, then scores the recording after. It is the manager who is in every meeting without being in every meeting.

Who it is for: Sales leaders running SDR and AE teams on high-volume video calls who want consistent talk tracks and faster ramp for new reps.

Key features

Live Real-time cues

Battlecards and next-best-actions surface during the call via a floating notifier.

Score AI scorecards

Every call is auto-scored on talk ratio, next steps, and competitor mentions.

Search Call library

Search every recording by keyword, pricing, competitor X, across your whole team.

Coach Snippets and comments

Managers drop timestamped coaching notes right on the recording.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Real-time coaching actually changes behavior
  • Strong search across the call library
  • Fast ramp for new reps
  • Integrates with Zoom, Meet, and Teams
  • Clean manager dashboard

✗ What does not

  • No published free tier
  • Pricing is per-seat and adds up
  • Can feel like surveillance to reps
  • Setup needs call-platform admin access

Verdict

Wingman is the rare conversation-intelligence tool reps do not hate, because the live cues help them in the moment. At about $35/seat it is an easy yes for any team doing 20+ discovery calls a week. Pair it with Apollo for list building.
