A game that writes itself around you

AI Dungeon is the original LLM game. You type what your character does; the AI narrates what happens next — no scripted branches, no dead ends. Spin up a fantasy epic, a sci-fi mystery, or a modern slice-of-life, alone or with friends. In 2026 it layers image generation and custom scenarios on top of sharper models, making it the most replayable writing toy on the web.

Who it's for: Writers, gamers, and creative souls who want a story that adapts to them instead of a fixed plot.

Key features

Story Infinite storytelling

No branches, no endings you can't reach. The AI responds to any action you type, so the story goes wherever you steer it.

Scenes Custom scenarios

Build your own worlds with lore, characters, and rules — or play millions of community-made scenarios.

Multi Multiplayer

Co-write a story with friends in real time. Each player controls a character; the AI weaves it together.

Art AI image generation

Generate illustrations for key moments, turning a text adventure into a visual storybook as you play.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Genuinely endless — limited only by your imagination
  • Great creative-writing warm-up and brainstorming tool
  • Huge community scenario library
  • Free tier lets you test the waters
  • Multiplayer co-writing is a blast

✗ What doesn't

  • Adventure tier ($14.99/mo) needed for the best models
  • AI can drift off-story or contradict earlier events
  • Moderation has occasionally clipped edgy content
  • Not a substitute for structured plotting tools
  • Quality varies with the model you select

Verdict

AI Dungeon is the most fun you can have with a language model in 2026, and a sneaky-good creativity trainer. Free to try; the Adventure tier unlocks the models that keep the story coherent. Use it to break writer's block, not to ship a manuscript.
