The publisher-grade AI detector

Originality.ai is the AI detector that SEO agencies and content publishers trust. Unlike GPTZero (built for teachers), Originality was built from day one for commercial use — vetting freelance writers, scanning guest posts, and protecting Google E-E-A-T scores. It bundles AI detection with plagiarism checking and (new in 2026) fact-checking against a web corpus.

Who it's for: SEO agencies managing teams of writers, content publishers vetting guest posts, editors verifying submissions, and freelancers who want to verify their own writing before delivering.

Key features

3-in-1 AI detect + plagiarism + fact-check

One scan gives you (1) AI probability score, (2) plagiarism matches across the web, and (3) factual claim verification against 10B+ indexed pages. Unique in the category.

Team Team management

Manage writers by team, share scan credits, see who's submitting AI-heavy content. The Google Docs add-on auto-scans everything your writers draft.

API Full API

Detect + plagiarism as a REST API. Used by content platforms (Writer.com, ContentAtScale, Surfer) to add detection to their own products.

Lit Readability scoring

Bonus: predicts the Flesch reading grade level and target SEO keyword density. Useful for agencies balancing AI efficiency with human readability.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • 3-in-1 scan (AI + plagiarism + fact-check) is unique in the category
  • Team management features are best-in-class for agencies
  • API access for embedding in your own product
  • Google Docs add-on auto-checks as writers type
  • Pay-as-you-go credits — no subscription required

✗ What doesn't

  • Higher false positive rate than GPTZero — editors report ~10% of human writing gets flagged on first scan
  • No free tier — must buy credits to even test
  • Credit-based pricing is opaque — agencies struggle to forecast costs
  • Same fundamental limit as all detectors: heavy editing defeats detection

Verdict

If you're a publisher or agency, Originality.ai is the right detector — the team features and bundled plagiarism check pay for themselves. For teachers and individuals, GPTZero's free tier is a better starting point.
