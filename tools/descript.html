Edit video by editing text

Descript is the video and audio editor that lets you edit by editing text. Upload your video, Descript transcribes it, you delete words from the transcript to cut the video. AI removes filler words ('um', 'uh'), corrects eye contact, dubs in 20+ languages, and generates short clips for social. For podcasters and YouTubers, Descript is the new default.

Who it's for: Podcasters, YouTubers, video creators, course creators, anyone editing spoken-word content.

Key features

✏️ Edit by editing text

Delete words from transcript, the video cuts. No timeline scrubbing.

🗑️ Filler word removal

AI removes 'um', 'uh', 'like', 'you know' automatically. Saves 15-30% of runtime.

🌍 AI dubbing

Translate to 20+ languages with your cloned voice. Lip-sync included.

📱 Short clip generator

Auto-generates 10 short clips from one long video. Perfect for social.
