AI that makes meetings actually useful

Zoom AI Companion is the AI assistant built directly into Zoom Workplace. It summarizes meetings, extracts action items, generates smart chapters for recordings, answers real-time questions during calls, and drafts follow-up messages. In 2026, Zoom added AI Companion 2.0 with agentic capabilities — it can now schedule follow-ups, create docs from meeting notes, and route action items to the right people automatically.

Who it's for: Anyone who spends more than 2 hours/day in Zoom meetings. Especially valuable for managers, project leads, and customer-facing teams who need to track decisions and action items without taking notes.

Key features

Smart Summaries Auto-generated meeting recaps

Get a structured summary with key topics, decisions, and action items seconds after the meeting ends. Shareable with attendees and absentees.

Real-time Q&A Ask questions during the call

Type a question like "What did Sarah say about the budget?" and get an answer from the live transcript. Works even if you joined late.

Smart Chapters Auto-segment recordings

Long recordings get split into topic-based chapters with timestamps. Skip to the part that matters instead of scrubbing through 60 minutes.

Agentic Actions Automated follow-ups

AI Companion 2.0 can draft emails, create tasks in project management tools, and schedule follow-up meetings based on what was discussed.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Free with all paid Zoom Workplace plans — no extra cost
  • Meeting summaries are surprisingly accurate and well-structured
  • Real-time Q&A is a game-changer for multi-hour calls
  • Works across Zoom Meetings, Team Chat, Phone, and Whiteboard
  • Multi-language support — summaries work in 30+ languages

✗ What doesn't

  • Not available on free Zoom plan — need at least Pro ($13.33/mo)
  • Summary quality drops with heavy accents or technical jargon
  • Privacy concerns — always-on AI listening in meetings
  • No integration with non-Zoom platforms (Teams, Meet, etc.)
  • Action item extraction misses nuance — needs human review

Verdict

If your company already uses Zoom, AI Companion is a no-brainer — it's included in the price. The meeting summaries alone save 30+ minutes per meeting in note-taking and follow-up. It won't replace a dedicated tool like Otter.ai or Fireflies for transcription-heavy workflows, but for the average knowledge worker, it's the best meeting AI that requires zero setup.
