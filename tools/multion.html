What MultiOn actually does

MultiOn is the most consumer-friendly AI browser agent. Tell it "find me a flight to NYC under $300 next Friday" and it actually goes to Google Flights, compares options, and books if you approve. The Chrome extension feels like having a personal assistant who lives in your browser.

Who it's for: Busy professionals who want to delegate routine web tasks — booking, ordering, research — to an AI.

Key features

Chrome Chrome extension

Installs in 10 seconds. MultiOn works on the page you're already looking at.

Voice Voice + text commands

Type or speak — "order my usual from DoorDash" just works.

Chain Multi-step workflows

Chains 5-20 actions to complete a goal autonomously.

Privacy Privacy-first

Agent runs in your browser context, not on a remote server scraping your logged-in pages.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Most accessible AI agent for non-developers
  • Works on sites you're logged into (unlike API-only tools)
  • Demo videos are genuinely mind-blowing
  • Free tier lets you try real tasks

✗ What doesn't

  • Still fails on ~10-15% of complex multi-step tasks
  • Limited to Chrome (no Safari/Firefox yet)
  • Not as reliable as dedicated scraping tools for data extraction
  • Newer company — long-term support risk

Verdict

The best on-ramp to the "AI does my web tasks" future. If you've never used an AI browser agent, start with MultiOn's free tier. For power users and devs, Skyvern gives you way more control.
