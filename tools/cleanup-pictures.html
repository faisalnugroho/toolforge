The fastest photobomber remover online

Cleanup.pictures is the lightweight sibling of the Clipdrop suite. Upload a photo, paint over the thing you want gone — a tourist, a power line, a wrinkle — and the AI inpaints a believable background in a second or two. No layers, no masking tutorials.

Who it's for: Ecommerce sellers, real-estate photographers, and casual users who need clean images without Photoshop.

Key features

Brush Point-and-remove

Paint over any object, person, or blemish and the model fills the gap with matching texture.

Resolution HD export (Pro)

Free exports are capped; Pro unlocks full-resolution downloads for print and listings.

No install Browser-native

Runs entirely in the browser — no app, no account needed for quick edits.

API Bulk workflows

Pro and enterprise tiers expose an API for cleaning thousands of product shots.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Genuinely one-click — no learning curve
  • Free tier is enough for occasional use
  • Results are clean on simple backgrounds
  • No signup friction for a quick edit

✗ What doesn't

  • Struggles on busy, high-detail backgrounds
  • Free export resolution is low
  • No layer or batch editing in the free tier
  • Less powerful than Photoshop Generative Fill

Verdict

For 90% of "remove this thing from my photo" jobs, Cleanup.pictures is faster than opening Photoshop. Keep it bookmarked. Reach for something heavier only when the background is genuinely complex.
