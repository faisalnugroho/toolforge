Sketching that feels human

Excalidraw is the open-source whiteboard with the charming hand-drawn look engineers and founders love for architecture diagrams and brainstorms. In 2026 its AI can generate diagrams from text, turn wireframes into code-ready shapes, and clean up messy boards.

Who it's for: Engineers, founders, and teachers who want fast, expressive diagrams without a heavy design tool.

Key features

AI Generate diagrams

Describe a system and Excalidraw AI produces a hand-drawn diagram you can edit.

Hand-drawn Signature style

The sketchy render makes diagrams feel approachable and human.

Collaboration Live multiplayer

Share a room link and sketch together in real time.

Export SVG, PNG, clipboard

Pull drawings into docs, slides, and code with clean exports.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Free and open source
  • Distinctive, friendly visual style
  • Great for architecture diagrams
  • Real-time collaboration
  • AI diagram generation is a delight

✗ What doesn't

  • Not a full design tool
  • No offline app by default
  • Limited beyond diagramming
  • File management is basic

Verdict

Excalidraw is the fastest way to make a diagram that people actually want to look at. Free, fun, and now AI-assisted, it earns a permanent spot in any engineer's or founder's toolkit.
