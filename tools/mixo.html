From idea to live landing page in 10 seconds

Mixo is the fastest path from "I have an idea" to "I have a waitlist." You type a sentence about your product, and its AI generates a branded landing page with copy, email capture, and a thank-you flow. No templates to wrestle, no builder to learn — it's deliberately thin so you can test before you build.

Who it's for: Indie founders, side-project builders, and marketers who need to validate demand before writing a line of production code.

Key features

AI builderInstant pages

One prompt becomes a complete, responsive landing page with hero, features, and CTA — styled and on-brand.

WaitlistEmail capture

Built-in subscriber forms and a lightweight CMS so you can collect and message early users immediately.

HostingZero-config deploy

Mixo hosts the site on its own domain or yours. No DNS headaches, no separate hosting bill.

GrowthFind-your-audience tools

Optional tools surface early adopters and let you run light campaigns without leaving the platform.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Genuinely the fastest idea-to-live-page flow
  • Zero learning curve — no builder UI to master
  • Free tier lets you validate before paying
  • Built-in waitlist and email capture
  • Managed hosting included

✗ What doesn't

  • Not a fit for complex, multi-page sites
  • Customization is limited vs Webflow/Framer
  • Lock-in if you outgrow the platform
  • AI copy needs editing for tone

Verdict

Mixo wins on speed. If you're validating a startup idea this weekend, it's the right $9/mo. The moment you need deep customization, graduate to a real builder — but by then Mixo has already told you the idea works.
