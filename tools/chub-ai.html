The character card library roleplayers rely on

Chub AI hosts hundreds of thousands of community character cards — portable definitions you can drop into SillyTavern, Agnaistic, or Chub’s own chat. It’s the de facto open registry for persona data, with tagging, versioning, and an API.

Who it's for: Roleplayers and frontend users who need a searchable, portable source of characters.

Key features

Card library Portable personas

Download character definitions usable across many frontends.

Tagging Find anything

Filter by fandom, style, model, and content rating.

API Programmatic access

Pull cards and metadata into your own tooling.

Versioning Fork history

Track edits and roll back character updates.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Open and portable — not locked to one app
  • Huge, actively growing card library
  • Free to browse and download
  • API for builders

✗ What doesn't

  • Requires a frontend (SillyTavern) to actually chat
  • Content quality and safety vary by author
  • Discovery can be overwhelming without good filters

Verdict

If you run SillyTavern or any compatible frontend, Chub AI is the best open source of characters in 2026. Free to use; Pro just adds storage and sync.
