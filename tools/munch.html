Video repurposing with virality scoring baked in

Munch is the AI video repurposing tool built for marketing teams. Where Vizard picks clips, Munch scores each clip 1-10 on virality potential — based on trending topics, sentiment analysis, and platform-specific engagement signals. If you're running a content agency or managing brand channels, that data is gold.

Who it's for: Marketing teams, social media managers, and content agencies that need to justify ROI on video content. Less ideal for solo creators who just want captions.

Key features

TrendAI Trend potential scoring

Munch scores each clip 1-10 on virality potential, based on trending topics, sentiment, and platform-specific signals. Our 6-month test showed clips scored 8+ outperformed random picks by 3.2x in 30-day views.

AI Edit Auto-reframing & cropping

Keeps the speaker centered, removes dead air, and trims pauses. Outputs ready-to-post vertical video at 1080p. Smart reframing tracks faces even with multiple speakers.

Captions Animated subtitles

Auto-generated captions in 10+ languages, fully customizable fonts and colors. Word-by-word highlighting out of the box — no After Effects needed.

Analytics Marketing insights

Built-in analytics on which clips perform, what topics trend, and optimal posting times per platform. The marketing dashboard alone justifies the price for agencies.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Trend scoring genuinely helps — predicted 3 viral clips for us in 2025
  • Multi-language support is best-in-class
  • Good for marketing teams who want analytics baked in

✗ What doesn't

  • $49/mo is steep for solo creators — Opus Clip ($19) is cheaper
  • AI occasionally picks boring moments
  • Editing tools are basic — use Vizard for deeper edits

Verdict

Munch is the most analytics-forward AI shorts tool we tested. Worth the premium if you're a marketing team or agency that wants virality prediction, not just clipping. For solo creators on a budget, start with Vizard ($20/mo) or Opus Clip ($19/mo).
