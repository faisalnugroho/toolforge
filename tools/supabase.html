Your AI app's backend, in an afternoon

Supabase wraps a managed Postgres database with auth, real-time subscriptions, storage, edge functions, and a pgvector-powered semantic search — the exact stack AI apps need. In 2026 it ships first-class AI helpers (embeddings, RAG templates, and an AI SQL editor) so builders can ship a grounded product without stitching six services together.

Who it's for: Indie hackers, startups, and teams who want a batteries-included backend with vectors and AI features without managing infrastructure.

Key features

🐘 Postgres core

A real relational database you can query with SQL.

🔎 pgvector search

Native vector embeddings for RAG and semantic search.

🔐 Auth & realtime

User management, row security, and live subscriptions.

🤖 AI helpers

Embeddings, AI SQL, and RAG starter templates.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Open source and portable
  • Vectors + relational in one place
  • Generous free tier

✗ What doesn't

Verdict

Supabase is the fastest way to give an AI app a real backend. If you're building RAG or an agent with memory, the pgvector + auth combo alone saves weeks.
