What Wispr Flow does best

The fastest voice-to-text dictation tool. Speak naturally at 3x typing speed. Works in every app — Gmail, Slack, Notion, anywhere you can type. The macOS voice dictation that actually works.

Who it's for: Writers, founders, developers, anyone who thinks faster than they type. Especially powerful for people with RSI, ADHD, or anyone who types 4+ hours/day.

Key features

🎙️ 3x faster Speak at typing speed x3

Average typing: 40 WPM. Average speech: 130 WPM. Wispr Flow hits 150+ WPM with 96% accuracy. Reply to email 3x faster than typing.

🌍 Works everywhere Universal text input

Hold a hotkey, speak, text appears in any focused text field. Gmail, Slack, Notion, Linear, code editors, terminal — anywhere you can type.

🧠 Context-aware Auto-edits as you speak

Says 'new line' to insert a paragraph break. 'Period' for punctuation. 'Delete that' to remove the last sentence. Wispr understands commands, not just dictation.

🔒 Private by default On-device processing option

Pro tier supports on-device transcription for sensitive content. Audio never leaves your Mac for confidential material.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • 3x faster than typing — measurable productivity gain
  • Works in every app, not just one (unlike Apple Dictation)
  • 96% accuracy out of the box, even with technical jargon
  • Auto-edits (new line, period, delete that) — feels natural
  • Free tier is generous for personal use

✗ What doesn't

  • macOS only (no Windows/Linux yet — Windows is in beta)
  • Background noise still throws it off — quiet environment helps
  • Heavy accents occasionally mistranscribe (improving fast)
  • $10/mo might feel expensive for what is essentially dictation

Verdict

Wispr Flow is a must-have for writers. The $10/mo pricing is fair, and the speak at typing speed x3 feature alone is worth trying the free tier.
