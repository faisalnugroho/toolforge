The controversial AI SDR

11x.ai promises to replace an SDR — not assist one. "Alice" runs the full outbound workflow: builds targeted lead lists, writes personalized emails, follows up across channels, replies to inbound, and books meetings straight on your sales reps' calendars. The price tag is steep, but the pitch is "cheaper than a $70K/yr hire."

Who it's for: B2B SaaS companies with mature ICP, established offer, and clear ICP-fit leads who want outbound without hiring. Not for early-stage founders still figuring out positioning.

Key features

🤖Alice — autonomous SDR

One digital rep. Handles 1,000+ personalized touches/day. Sources leads, sends emails, replies, qualifies, books — all without human input.

📧Multi-channel outreach

Email, LinkedIn, and phone (via integrated voice AI). Sequences adapt based on engagement — not a static drip.

🎯Personalization at scale

Every email is researched — funding rounds, hiring, tech stack, recent posts. Personalization is genuinely good, not "firstname."

📅Calendar booking

Books meetings straight into your reps' Calendly. Includes qualification questions, routing logic, and pre-meeting briefs.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Actually books meetings — average customer reports 20-40 qualified meetings/mo
  • Personalization quality is noticeably better than Apollo/Instantly sequences
  • Multi-channel orchestration handles the boring stuff
  • Slack-based "rep chat" — Alice pings you for human input only when needed

✗ What doesn't

  • $3K+/mo is steep — you need ≥$100K ARR to make the ROI math work
  • Domain warmup and email deliverability are still on you
  • Best results require a tight ICP — vague "SMB SaaS" targeting wastes the spend
  • Controversial in the SDR community — some prospects actively avoid 11x-sent emails

Verdict

If you've raised a Series A and your bottleneck is "we have more pipeline than people to call it," 11x.ai is worth piloting. If you're pre-revenue or still finding product-market fit, an Apollo + ChatGPT combo is a better $200/mo.
