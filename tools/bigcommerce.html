The fastest way to get more done with BigCommerce

BigCommerce is built for teams that want results without the enterprise sales call. We used it daily for several weeks across real workflows to separate the marketing from the muscle.

Who it's for: Mid-market and enterprise merchants who need B2B, multi-channel, and headless without per-sale fees.

Key features

Open SaaS Scale without limits

Hosted reliability plus the flexibility to go headless via APIs and GraphQL when you outgrow themes.

Native B2B Wholesale built in

Customer groups, quote management, and net terms — no separate app required.

Multi-channel Sell everywhere

List on Amazon, eBay, Instagram, and Google from one catalog.

No fees Keep more margin

BigCommerce charges zero transaction fees, unlike many competitors.

SEO core Rank out of the box

Clean URLs, structured data, and sitemaps that help products get found.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • No transaction fees ever
  • Strong native B2B and wholesale
  • Headless-ready for enterprise
  • Scales without replatforming

✗ What doesn't

  • Steeper learning curve than Shopify
  • Fewer themes on lower tiers
  • Useful add-ons add cost
  • Standard plan caps annual sales

Verdict

For merchants scaling past Shopify's ceiling — especially B2B or headless — BigCommerce removes fee drag and lock-in. The $39/mo Standard plan is a fair on-ramp.
