The honest take

✓ What works

  • Clean, decision-focused summaries (not raw transcripts)
  • Strong action-item extraction
  • Good search across meeting history
  • Reasonable price for teams

✗ What doesn't

  • Newer player — smaller integration library than Fireflies
  • Accuracy dips on noisy or accented audio
  • No free tier beyond a short trial
  • Mobile app is basic

Verdict

Clarify earns its keep. If the use case fits your workflow, the 15/mo plan pays for itself quickly. For most users we recommend starting on the free tier and upgrading only once you hit the limits.
