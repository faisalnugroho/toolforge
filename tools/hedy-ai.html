A copilot that actually talks back — during the meeting

Most AI meeting tools are reactive: they record, transcribe, and summarize after the fact. Hedy.ai flips the model. It listens in real time and surfaces talking points, suggested questions, and counter-arguments while the conversation is still happening. Think of it as a silent strategist feeding you lines through your screen during a sales call, a lecture, or a doctor's appointment.

It supports 30+ languages, works across Zoom, Google Meet, Teams, and in-person via the mobile app, and turns every meeting into a structured summary the moment it ends. For anyone who has ever walked out of a meeting wishing they'd said the smart thing, that live nudge is the whole pitch.

Who it's for: Sales reps, consultants, students, healthcare professionals, and anyone whose job is half-listening, half-performing. Anyone who leaves meetings thinking "I should have said that."

Key features

Live Real-time talking points

Hedy listens to the ongoing conversation and generates suggested talking points, follow-up questions, and counter-arguments that update every few seconds. They appear on screen so you can glance and steer the conversation.

30+ Multilingual meetings

Hedy understands and responds in 30+ languages. If a meeting slips between English and another language, it keeps up and can even translate suggested points on the fly.

Auto Instant structured summaries

The moment a meeting ends, Hedy produces a structured summary with key decisions, action items, and the questions that were never answered. Export to Notion, email, or clipboard.

Any Works everywhere

Browser extension covers Zoom, Google Meet, and Microsoft Teams. The mobile app handles in-person meetings via the device mic — useful for hallway stand-ups and client lunches.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Real-time suggestions are genuinely useful — the core differentiator actually delivers
  • Excellent multilingual support (30+ languages), rare in this category
  • In-person mode via mobile app fills a gap competitors ignore
  • Free tier is usable for light users (5 hours/month)
  • Summaries are well-structured, not just a wall of transcript

✗ What doesn't

  • Glancing at a screen mid-meeting can read as distracted to others
  • Suggested questions sometimes feel templated on short calls
  • Pro tier ($12.99/mo) only unlocks 50 hours — heavy users hit the cap
  • Audio quality on the other end heavily affects suggestion quality
  • No native CRM or calendar deep-integrations yet (export only)

Pricing

Hedy uses a freemium model. The free tier gives you 5 hours of meetings per month with the real-time assistant and basic summaries. Pro is $12.99/month (or ~$9.99/month billed annually) and unlocks 50 hours/month, advanced summaries, export options, and priority suggestion speed. A Business tier adds team workspaces and shared meeting intelligence.

Verdict

Hedy.ai is the only meeting tool that helps you during the meeting rather than after it. If you live in high-stakes conversations — sales, consulting, healthcare, academia — the real-time nudge is worth the subscription. For teams that just want transcripts and summaries, Otter or Fireflies are cheaper and more mature. Pair Hedy with one of those and you cover both halves of the meeting lifecycle.
