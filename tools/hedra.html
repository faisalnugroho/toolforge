The fastest way to make a face talk

Hedra is the consumer product built on Character-1, a Stanford-originated model that animates a single still face into a talking video synced to any audio input. It went viral in 2024 for the "talking baby" meme trend and has since become a workhorse for creators making avatar content, course videos, and AI-generated UGC at scale.

Who it's for: Educators, course creators, social media teams, and anyone making avatar-based UGC. Also great for prototyping character designs before commissioning voice actors.

Key features

Char-1 Character-1 model

The fastest talking-head model on the market. Upload a face, upload audio (your voice, ElevenLabs, or any TTS), and get a synced video in under 30 seconds. Lip sync accuracy is best-in-class.

60s Long-form output

Unlike most talking-head tools (which cap at 10-30 seconds), Hedra generates clips up to 60 seconds on the Creator plan. Enough for a full course intro or product pitch.

Multi Multi-character scenes

Drop in two characters and Hedra will animate a conversation between them, with turn-taking lip sync and natural head movement. Unique in the category.

API Programmatic access

REST API for batch generation. Used by UGC agencies to spin up hundreds of personalized avatar videos per day for ad creative testing.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Lip sync is best-in-class — beats Heygen and D-ID on naturalness
  • Free tier is genuinely usable (500 credits = ~10 short clips)
  • Multi-character scenes are unique — no other tool does this well
  • API access for batch UGC generation
  • Fast rendering (~30 seconds for a 30-second clip)

✗ What doesn't

  • Backgrounds are static — you can't put characters in custom scenes without compositing
  • Emotion control is limited — characters feel slightly flat compared to live actors
  • Image quality depends heavily on input — low-res faces produce low-res videos
  • Creator plan is $9/mo for limited credits; power users will need Studio at $99/mo

Verdict

If you need to put words in a face's mouth — for course videos, UGC ads, or meme content — Hedra is the fastest and most natural option in 2026. The free tier is enough to evaluate it; power users should budget for the Studio plan.
