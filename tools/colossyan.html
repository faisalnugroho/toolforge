The L&D team's AI avatar platform

Colossyan carved out a niche in the crowded AI avatar market by going all-in on corporate learning and development (L&D). Where HeyGen targets creators and Synthesia targets generic enterprise, Colossyan ships features specifically for training: SCORM export, LMS integration, auto-translation across 80+ languages, and a "bring your own avatar" feature for company spokespeople. In 2026, it's the default pick for L&D teams producing compliance, onboarding, and product training videos.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Best-in-class for L&D — features like SCORM and LMS export that Synthesia doesn't have
  • Auto-translation across 80+ languages is genuinely production-quality
  • Custom avatars look and sound great — better lip-sync than Synthesia in our tests
