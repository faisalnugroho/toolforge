What it does well

Rask AI is the fastest way to make your video content multilingual. Upload a video in English, get a perfectly dubbed version in Spanish, Japanese, or Arabic — with lip-sync that makes it look like the speaker actually speaks that language. It's used by YouTubers, educators, and enterprise content teams.

Who it's for: Content creators, educators, and businesses that need to reach global audiences without re-recording videos in each language.

Key features

Voice Cloning Keep your voice in any language

Rask clones the speaker's voice and re-synthesizes it in the target language. The result sounds like the same person speaking Spanish, Japanese, or Hindi. Remarkably natural.

Lip-Sync Mouths match the new audio

AI adjusts the speaker's lip movements to match the dubbed audio. This is the feature that makes dubbed videos look professional instead of awkward.

130+ Languages Broadest language support

More languages than any competitor. Covers major markets (English, Spanish, Chinese, Hindi, Arabic) plus niche languages (Finnish, Thai, Swahili) for global reach.

Subtitles Auto-generated subtitles

Generates subtitle files in all supported languages. Download SRT files for YouTube, social media, or your LMS platform.
