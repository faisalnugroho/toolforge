Script in, video out, in minutes

Steve.ai is the pragmatic text-to-video tool for people who are not video editors. Feed it a script or blog URL and it picks scenes, adds voiceover, captions, and music. It leans on stock + animated assets rather than generative avatars, which keeps output clean and on-brand for social and training videos.

Who it's for: Marketers, coaches, and small teams who need lots of short videos without hiring an editor.

Key features

Script to video Type it out

Paste a script; Steve builds the scene timeline automatically.

Blog to video Repurpose

Drop a URL and turn a post into a narrated video.

Stock + animated Clean assets

Large library of licensed stock and animated scenes.

Brand kits Consistent

Save colors, logos, and intros for on-brand output.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Fastest path from script to finished video
  • No editing skills needed
  • Good stock/animation library
  • Affordable starting price

✗ What doesn't

  • Not as cinematic as Synthesia/HeyGen avatars
  • Stock-heavy look
  • Voice choice is limited vs ElevenLabs
  • Long videos get pricey

Verdict

Steve.ai is the best "I have a script and need a video now" tool for non-editors. For avatar-led training, go Synthesia; for quick social repurposing, Steve.ai wins on speed and price.
