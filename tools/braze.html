Orchestrate every message, everywhere

Braze is the customer-engagement platform behind many of the apps you use daily. It unifies push, email, in-app, SMS, and web messaging around a real-time user profile, then uses AI to pick the right channel and time.

Who it is for: Growth and lifecycle marketing teams at scaling product companies who have outgrown basic email tools and need orchestration across channels.

Key features

Profile Real-time user data

A live customer profile updates on every event, driving instantly personalized messages.

Canvas AI orchestration

Visual journeys adapt using BrazeAI, timing, channel, and content chosen per user.

Channels Omni-channel

Push, email, in-app, SMS, content cards, and web all from one canvas.

ML Predictive segments

Churn and affinity models auto-build segments you can message.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Best-in-class real-time orchestration
  • True omni-channel from one profile
  • Strong AI for timing and content
  • Enterprise-grade reliability
  • Great developer APIs

✗ What does not

  • No self-serve free tier, enterprise pricing
  • Implementation is non-trivial
  • Overkill for small teams
  • Steeper learning curve

Verdict

Braze is the engagement platform you graduate to when sending a newsletter is no longer enough. If you have real engineering and marketing resources, it is best-in-class. Smaller teams should start on HubSpot or Klaviyo and move to Braze later.
