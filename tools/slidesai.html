The fastest way to draft a Google Slides deck

SlidesAI is the Google Slides add-in that launched in 2022 and now has 2M+ users — primarily teachers, students, and consultants who need to turn an outline or article into a presentation fast. The premise is simple: paste text, click "Generate Slides," and SlidesAI builds a 10-slide deck with headings, bullets, and basic layouts in 30 seconds. By 2026 it supports 100+ languages and integrates with Google Slides themes. It's not a replacement for Gamma or Beautiful.ai on design-heavy decks, but for the 80% of presentations that are content-driven, it's the fastest tool available.

Who it's for: Teachers, students, sales engineers, and anyone who needs to turn a document or outline into a usable Google Slides deck without spending 2 hours on layout.

Key features

📝Text to slides

Paste an outline, article, or notes → get a complete Google Slides deck in 30-60 seconds. AI determines slide count, layout, and bullet hierarchy. You then edit and refine in Slides.

🎨Theme matching

Matches your existing Google Slides theme — fonts, colors, master layouts. The output looks like your brand, not like a generic AI template.

🌐100+ languages

Generate slides in any language — Spanish, French, German, Japanese, Arabic. Useful for international education and global teams.

🖼️AI image generation

Auto-generates relevant images per slide (Pro plan only). Quality is decent for stock-photo-style imagery — better than placeholder gradients, worse than Midjourney.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Dramatically faster than building slides manually — 5 minutes vs. 2 hours
  • Lives inside Google Slides — no new tool to learn
  • Affordable at $10/mo — much cheaper than Gamma or Beautiful.ai
  • Handles 100+ languages — best in class for international users
  • 3 free presentations to try before paying

✗ What doesn't

  • Output is a "first draft" — requires editing for client-facing or polished decks
  • Design quality is functional, not beautiful — Gamma wins on aesthetics
  • Google Slides only — no PowerPoint export (use Plus AI for that)
  • No collaboration features — single-user workflow
  • Free tier is limited to 3 presentations

Verdict

For Google Slides users who need to turn text into a presentation quickly, SlidesAI is the most practical AI tool of 2026. It's particularly valuable for teachers and non-designers who create weekly decks. For client-facing pitch decks where design matters, use Gamma or Beautiful.ai instead.
