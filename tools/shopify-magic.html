AI that ships with your store

Shopify Magic is the umbrella for all the AI features built into Shopify — and unlike every other tool on this list, it's included free with your subscription. The flagship feature is Sidekick, a Shopify-aware AI assistant that can answer analytics questions, generate reports, draft email campaigns, and even edit your store's liquid theme code. Plus the original Magic suite: AI product descriptions, AI image background remover, and AI-generated email subject lines.

Who it's for: Every Shopify merchant, from first-timers to 8-figure brands. The free price point means there's literally no reason not to use it. The advanced features (Sidekick) are most useful for solo operators and small teams who can't afford a data analyst.

Key features

Sidekick AI store assistant

Ask "what were my top 10 products last month?" and Sidekick queries your store, generates the report, and can email it to your team. It can also draft marketing emails, create discount codes, and edit theme code.

Magic AI Product descriptions

Generate SEO-friendly product descriptions in seconds. Tone control (expert, playful, sophisticated), keyword input, and multi-language support. Used by 1M+ merchants to write 100M+ descriptions.

Magic Image AI photo editor

Background removal, scene generation, and product photo enhancement built into the product page. No Photoshop, no third-party apps. The free competitor to remove.bg and Photoroom.

Magic Email AI subject lines

AI-generated subject lines for Shopify Email campaigns, personalized to each subscriber. Plus auto-segmented customer lists based on purchase behavior. Built into Shopify Marketing.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • FREE with any Shopify plan — no extra cost
  • Sidekick is genuinely useful: answers analytics questions in plain English
  • Product description generation saves hours per launch
  • Built into the platform — no third-party apps to install or pay for
  • Shopify-aware: knows your catalog, customers, and orders
  • Image editing is solid and instant

✗ What doesn't

  • Sidekick is still developing — complex queries sometimes miss
  • AI descriptions are good but generic — top brands still use human copy
  • Limited integration with non-Shopify channels (Amazon, eBay, etc.)
  • Smaller merchants may not realize these features exist
  • AI image generation isn't as flexible as Midjourney/DALL-E for hero shots

Verdict

If you're a Shopify merchant and not using Shopify Magic, you're leaving free money on the table. The product description writer alone is worth 10+ hours per launch. Sidekick is the killer feature for solo operators who want a "data analyst on call" without the salary. For power users, the limits show, but the price (free) makes it essential.
