Notion Calendar has earned a spot in the ToolForge directory because it does one job exceptionally well and keeps the rest out of your way. Below is our hands-on breakdown of what works, what doesn't, and who should reach for it.

Key features

Multi-calendar Everything in one view

Overlay Google, Apple, and Outlook calendars so you see conflicts instantly.

Notion links Docs meet time
