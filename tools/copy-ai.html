The workflow engine for AI content

Copy.ai started as an AI copywriter but has evolved into something more powerful: a workflow automation platform for marketing content. Instead of generating one piece at a time, you build workflows that auto-generate weeks of content in minutes. Pull from a Google Sheet of products, generate 30 LinkedIn posts, 30 tweets, 30 email subject lines, and 30 ad headlines — all consistent, all on-brand, all in a few minutes.

Who it's for: Marketing teams that need to scale content production 10x. Agencies managing multiple clients. Growth marketers running hundreds of A/B test variations.

Key features

⚙️ Workflows

Build multi-step content pipelines. Input → process → output. Pull from Sheets, generate, format, push to your CMS. Runs on autopilot.

📝 Templates

90+ pre-built templates for every marketing format. Email sequences, blog posts, LinkedIn carousels, ad copy, product descriptions, press releases.

🌍 Brand Voice

Train Copy.ai on your existing content. The workflow maintains voice consistency across hundreds of generated pieces.

🔌 Integrations

Native integrations with HubSpot, Salesforce, Google Sheets, Slack, Zapier, and 50+ other tools. Workflows plug into your existing stack.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Workflows are genuinely 10x — they automate what used to be days of work
  • Best template library in the AI writing space (90+)
  • Free tier is generous (2,000 words/mo)
  • Integrations make it fit into existing marketing stacks
  • Team collaboration features for agencies

✗ What doesn't

  • Pricier than ChatGPT ($49/mo) — justified at scale, steep for solo creators
  • Output quality can vary — workflows need human review for final approval
  • Learning curve for the workflow builder — not as simple as 'type a prompt'
  • Customer support response times have slipped as they've scaled

Verdict

The right choice for marketing teams that need to ship 50+ pieces of content a month. For solo creators, Jasper or ChatGPT + good prompts is a better fit. But for teams that want to put content production on autopilot, Copy.ai's workflows are unmatched.
