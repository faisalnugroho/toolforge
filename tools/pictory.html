Turn blogs into videos automatically

Pictory is the AI tool that turns blog posts, articles, and scripts into videos. Paste a blog URL, Pictory extracts the key points, finds stock footage, adds captions and music, and outputs a 2-3 minute video. For content marketers who want to repurpose blog content for YouTube, TikTok, and Instagram, Pictory is the obvious tool.

Who it's for: Content marketers, bloggers, course creators, anyone repurposing written content for video.

Key features

📝 Blog to video

Paste a URL, get a video. AI extracts key points, finds stock footage, adds captions.

🎬 Script to video

Type a script, get a video. AI generates visuals, voiceover, music.

📱 Auto-short clips

Generate 10+ short clips from one long video. Perfect for TikTok, Reels, Shorts.

🎵 Stock library

Built-in 3M+ stock footage and music clips. No need for separate subscriptions.
