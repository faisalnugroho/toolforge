The bottom line

Grain is the meeting tool that Zoom-native teams love. Records your Zoom calls with one click (or runs as a bot on Meet/Teams), generates AI summaries, and — most distinctively — turns meetings into 60-second shareable video clips. Perfect for coaching, customer research, social proof, and async updates. 90% of what Gong does for 15% of the price, with a free tier that's actually usable.

Who it's for: Startup sales teams (5-50 reps), customer research teams, and anyone who wants to share meeting moments internally or externally.

Key features

🎬 One-Click Clip Sharing

Highlight any 30-second moment in a call and share it as a video clip with a single link. The killer feature — turns 60-min calls into shareable proof points for coaching, customer research, and social.

🤖 AI Meeting Summary

Auto-generates summary, action items, and key topics for every call. Less deep than Gong's deal intelligence, but 10x faster to set up.

📝 Live Transcription

95%+ accurate English transcription with speaker ID. Searchable transcript that you can scrub like a video timeline.

🎯 Coaching Workflows

Scorecards, highlight libraries, and shared folders for rep onboarding. Managers can leave timestamped comments on calls.

🔌 Integrations

HubSpot, Salesforce, Slack, Notion, Zapier. Auto-logs call recordings and summaries to CRM. Webhooks for custom workflows.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Best free tier in the category: 20 meetings/mo with full AI features, no credit card
  • Clip-sharing is genuinely the best in any meeting tool — turns 1-hour calls into shareable moments
  • Lighter UI than Gong or Chorus — new reps are productive in 5 minutes
  • Best pricing for early-stage sales teams: $15/mo Pro is a tenth of Gong's price
  • Strong customer research use case: product marketers love the highlight reel feature

✗ What doesn't

  • No revenue intelligence layer (can't tell you which deals are at risk or which reps are top performers)
  • Limited Salesforce/HubSpot deal intelligence — you'll still need manual CRM updates
  • No market intelligence (aggregated customer language across calls)
  • Smaller integration catalog than enterprise tools
  • Free tier caps at 20 meetings/mo — teams above 30 calls/week will need Pro

Verdict

Grain is the right conversation intelligence tool for early-stage B2B sales teams (under 50 reps). At $15/mo vs Gong's $100+/user/mo, it's a no-brainer for startups — you get call recording, transcription, AI summaries, and the best clip-sharing feature in any meeting tool. The free tier is generous enough to use for 6+ months before paying. Once you cross 50 reps and need revenue intelligence, market analytics, and Salesforce deal risk signals, you'll graduate to Gong or Chorus. Until then, Grain wins on price, UX, and the shareable clip feature alone.
