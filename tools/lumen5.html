The text-to-video tool built for marketers

Lumen5 has been around since 2017, which makes it a dinosaur in AI-video years. But it survives because it solves one job better than anyone else: turning an article, blog post, or script into a presentable marketing video with zero video editing skills. Paste a URL or text, Lumen5's AI picks sentences, finds matching stock footage from a 10M+ clip library, adds captions, and ships a 60-second video you can post on LinkedIn by lunch.

Who it's for: Marketing teams, agencies, and solo content marketers who need to repurpose blogs/articles into LinkedIn, Instagram, and TikTok videos. Less ideal for creators wanting cinematic output.

Key features

URL→Vid Blog to video

Paste a blog URL or article text. Lumen5's NLP engine picks the most quotable lines, builds a scene-by-scene storyboard, and matches each scene with stock footage. Real 5-minute workflow, not a 5-hour tutorial.

10M+ Stock library

Shutterstock-powered library of 10M+ stock clips and images, all cleared for commercial use. No separate stock subscription needed for Basic tier; HD+ for higher tiers.

Auto Captions + layouts

Animated captions in 30+ styles (vertical, square, widescreen). Brand kits lock in fonts, colors, and logos so every video matches your brand. One click to reformat for IG Reels, TikTok, YouTube Shorts.

AI Script Talkthrough videos

Type a prompt like "60-second explainer on cold email" and get a full script + storyboard. Add your own voice or use the 50+ AI voices in 25 languages.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Genuinely 5-minute blog→video workflow, not marketing hype
  • Best stock library of any AI video tool at this price point
  • Brand kit + caption styles = consistent content at scale
  • Auto reformatting for IG/TikTok/YouTube saves a separate tool
  • Mature product with 8+ years of UX polish
  • 50+ AI voices in 25 languages — strong for global teams

✗ What doesn't

  • Not a creator tool — no cinematic output, no advanced timeline
  • Stock footage match can be hit-or-miss for niche topics
  • Free tier watermarks videos and caps at 720p
  • No true avatar feature (Synthesia territory)
  • Voice quality is good, not ElevenLabs-good for narration
  • No native scheduling to socials — export and upload elsewhere

Verdict

Lumen5 is the Honda Civic of AI video tools: not flashy, not the fastest, but it does the daily commute reliably for years. If your job is "turn this blog into a LinkedIn video by 3pm," Lumen5 is still the fastest path in 2026. The stock library and brand kit lock-in are the moat. Skip it if you're a creator making hero content — go Pictory, HeyGen, or Runway instead.
