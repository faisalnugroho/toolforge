The fastest way to the ai calendar that actually protects your focus time.

Reclaim.ai is the AI calendar layer that sits on top of Google Calendar and Outlook. It auto-blocks time for your tasks, habits, and breaks. When someone sends a meeting invite, Reclaim negotiates — moving your focus blocks around to find a slot that works. It's the productivity tool that finally made me stop ending days with 6 hours of meetings and 0 hours of actual work.

Who it's for: Knowledge workers with 4+ hours of meetings/day, managers, founders, anyone who feels like their calendar owns them.

Key features

Smart 1:1s AI-scheduled recurring meetings

Set "weekly 30-min 1:1 with Sarah" and Reclaim finds the slot that protects both your priorities. No more "what time works" emails.

Habits & tasks Auto-blocked recurring time

Tell Reclaim you want to work out 3x/week, write 1 hour daily, and review analytics weekly. It blocks the time and shifts it when conflicts come up.

Meeting cost Time-equivalent in dollars

Shows the dollar cost of every meeting. That "quick 30-min sync" costs $187 in engineering time. Reclaim surfaces this gently.

Calendar sync Google + Outlook

Two-way sync with Google Calendar and Outlook 365. No need to migrate.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Genuinely reclaims 5-10 hours/week of focus time
  • Beautiful UI with stats dashboard
  • Free tier is actually usable for individuals
  • Integrates with Slack, Asana, Linear, Notion
  • Constantly shipping new features (recently added time-tracking)

✗ What doesn't

  • Power features require paid plan ($10/mo)
  • Initial setup takes 30-60 minutes
  • Some users report aggressive "smart" rescheduling
  • No native iOS widget for upcoming focus blocks
  • Sync delays of 5-10 seconds with Google Calendar

Verdict

If you have more than 3 hours of meetings per day, Reclaim.ai is the highest-ROI subscription you can buy. The free tier works for light users; the paid tier pays for itself in week one for managers and ICs alike.
