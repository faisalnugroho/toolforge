Your whole AI toolkit, one sidebar away

Sider bolts a ChatGPT-style sidebar onto every webpage. Select text and it summarizes, translates, explains, or rewrites it. Ask questions about the page you're reading. Switch between GPT-4o, Claude, and Gemini without opening a new tab. It's the closest thing to having a research assistant glued to your browser.

Who it's for: Researchers, students, writers, and bilingual professionals who live in the browser and want AI without tab-switching.

Key features

Sidebar Universal sidebar

A persistent AI panel on every page. Highlight any text and act on it — summarize, translate, explain, or improve — without leaving the tab.

Models Multi-model access

Route prompts to GPT-4o, Claude 3.5, Gemini, and Llama. Pick the best model per task from one interface.

Reader AI reader & summarizer

Paste any URL or PDF and Sider produces a structured summary with key points, saving you from reading 20-page docs end to end.

Write Inline AI writer

A writing panel drafts emails, posts, and docs in your tone. Built-in grammar and style suggestions rival standalone editors.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Works on any website — no copy-paste between tabs
  • Access to multiple top models in one place
  • Strong free tier with daily quota
  • Excellent translation across 100+ languages
  • Clean, non-intrusive UI

✗ What doesn't

  • Premium ($10/mo) required for heavy daily use
  • Sidebar can feel cramped on small screens
  • Quality depends on the underlying model you pick
  • No fully offline mode
  • Some features overlap with free ChatGPT sidepanel

Verdict

For anyone who spends their day in a browser, Sider is the most convenient AI layer you can add in 2026. The free tier covers casual use; power users will want Premium. It's the fastest way to stop alt-tabbing to ChatGPT.
