The most realistic AI music yet

Suno v7 is a massive leap forward in AI music generation. The v7 vocal engine produces singing that is indistinguishable from human performers — no artifacts, no robotic phrasing, no uncanny valley. Combined with full-song structure generation (verse, chorus, bridge) and stem export, Suno v7 has become the go-to tool for musicians, content creators, and marketers who need original music fast.

Who it's for: Musicians, content creators, marketers, podcasters, and anyone who needs original music without hiring a composer or licensing tracks.

Key features

v7 Vocal Engine

Studio-quality vocals with natural phrasing, breath control, and emotional delivery. The v7 model eliminates the robotic artifacts that plagued earlier AI music — listeners can't tell it's AI.

🎵 Full-Song Generation

Generate complete songs with proper structure — verse, chorus, bridge, outro — from a single text prompt. No more stitching together 30-second clips. Songs up to 3 minutes long.

🔀 Genre Blending

Mix genres that shouldn't work together but do. Jazz with trap. Folk with electronic. Classical with hip-hop. Suno v7 handles cross-genre fusion better than any other tool.

🎚️ Stem Export

Export separated stems — vocals, drums, bass, melody — for mixing in your DAW. Essential for producers who want to remix or build on AI-generated foundations.

📄 Commercial License

The Pro tier ($24/mo) includes royalty-free commercial use. Use your generated songs in videos, ads, podcasts, and films without worrying about copyright claims.

🔄 Extend & Remix

Add new sections to existing songs, change the style mid-track, or extend a 30-second clip into a full 3-minute production. Iterative creation that feels like working with a producer.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Best-in-class vocal quality — indistinguishable from human singers
