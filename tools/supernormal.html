What it does well

Supernormal is the AI meeting tool that silently captures every detail so you can stay present in conversations. In 2026, it's a serious option for anyone who attends more than 5 meetings per week. We tested it for two weeks on real meetings — here's the honest take.

Who it's for: Busy professionals who want meeting notes without the overhead of a visible bot joining calls.

Key features

Auto-Capture Silent recording on Google Meet

Supernormal lives natively inside Google Meet — no bot joins your call. It records, transcribes, and summarizes automatically. Also supports Zoom and Teams with bot-based recording.

AI Summaries Structured meeting notes

Generates clean, structured notes with action items, decisions, and key discussion points. Notes are customizable with templates for standups, 1:1s, sales calls, and more.

CRM Sync Auto-fill Salesforce, HubSpot

For sales teams: automatically pushes meeting notes, action items, and call summaries into your CRM. No more manual note entry after calls.

Search Search across all meetings

Ask questions across your entire meeting history. "What did the client say about Q3 pricing?" gets an instant, citation-linked answer.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Native Google Meet integration means no visible bot — clients don't see it
  • Free tier is generous (up to 1,000 minutes/month)
  • Notes are cleaner and better structured than most competitors
  • CRM sync saves 15-20 minutes per sales call
  • Works across Google Meet, Zoom, and Microsoft Teams

✗ What doesn't

  • Zoom and Teams integrations still use a visible bot (unlike Google Meet)
  • Speaker identification is less accurate than Otter in overlapping speech
  • No mobile app for on-the-go recordings
  • Advanced features (CRM sync, custom templates) require Pro plan

Verdict

Supernormal is the best choice for teams that live in Google Meet. The native integration is seamless — no bot joining your calls, no awkward "is this being recorded?" moments. The free tier is generous enough for most individuals, and the CRM integration alone justifies the Pro price for sales teams. If you're on Zoom or Teams, Fireflies or Otter may be better bets. For Google Meet power users, Supernormal is the clear winner.
