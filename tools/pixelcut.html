The fastest way to make product photos that sell

Pixelcut is the AI photo editor built for ecommerce sellers, marketplace flippers, and content creators who need studio-quality images without a studio. Upload a product shot and it strips the background, adds realistic shadows, and can even generate dozens of on-model and lifestyle variants from a single source image.

Who it's for: Ecommerce sellers, Etsy/Shopify store owners, and social creators who ship high volumes of product imagery and cannot afford a photographer.

Key features

BG Remover Instant background removal

One click removes the background with clean, accurate edges — even on hair and translucent objects. Batch mode handles hundreds of SKUs at once.

Magic Eraser Object & defect eraser

Paint over anything you want gone — wires, blemishes, price tags — and Pixelcut fills the space with context-aware pixels.

AI Shadows Shadows & lighting

Add soft, hard, or colored shadows and relight the product so it looks like a real studio shot on a white sweep.

Product Gen AI product photo generator

Describe a scene and Pixelcut generates on-model and lifestyle backgrounds, turning one flat-lay into 30 listing-ready variants.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Background removal is near-instant and genuinely accurate
  • Batch processing is a lifesaver for large catalogs
  • Mobile app is just as capable as the web app
  • Generative backgrounds replace a photographer for many SKUs

✗ What doesn't

  • Free exports carry a visible watermark
  • Subscription adds up if you only edit occasionally
  • Less useful for heavy retouching / compositing work
  • Some generative scenes look obviously AI on close inspection

Verdict

If you sell anything online, Pixelcut pays for itself in the first batch of listings. The $7.99/mo Pro plan removes watermarks and unlocks batch + generative modes. For one-off edits, the free tier is enough; for a store, it is a no-brainer.
