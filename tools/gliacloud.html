The video engine behind news publishers

GliaCloud is not for creators. It's for media companies and enterprise marketing teams that need to turn 50-500 blog posts per week into videos automatically. Bloomberg, Reuters, and Yahoo use it. The magic: paste an RSS feed URL, pick an avatar and template, and GliaCloud generates, edits, and renders a finished video per post. Edit one in the dashboard, push to YouTube, done.

Who it's for: News publishers, content marketing teams, and e-commerce stores that need to scale video output 10x without scaling headcount.

Key features

RSS Feed-to-video pipeline

Paste any RSS feed. GliaCloud ingests new articles and produces a finished video per post — typically under 90 seconds.

AI Summary Script extraction

GPT-class summarizer extracts the headline, key points, and a 60-90s spoken script. Sounds like a news anchor.

Multi-avatar Avatars in 40 languages

200+ avatars covering most demographics. Multi-language templates auto-translate voice + captions.

Branding Per-publisher style

Upload your logo, fonts, color palette, intro/outro sting. Every video matches your brand without manual editing.

Auto-publish YouTube/TikTok push

Direct integrations with YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, and Facebook. Schedule releases or publish on trigger.

Editor In-browser timeline editor

When you need to override the AI, the timeline editor is genuinely usable — not a stripped-down afterthought.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Best-in-class for feed-to-video — the AI summarizer is sharp and on-brand
  • Proven at scale — Bloomberg, Reuters, Yahoo aren't easy to impress
  • Auto-publishing saves 30+ min per video in distribution alone
  • Multi-language video from one source post is huge for international teams
  • Per-publisher branding is enforced — no rogue colors, no off-brand templates

✗ What doesn't

  • $99/mo is overkill for solo creators — GliaCloud is for teams producing 20+ videos/mo
  • 14-day trial requires credit card and a sales call to extend
  • Avatar library is smaller than HeyGen/Vidnoz — quality over quantity
  • No screen recording or webcam capture — you can't make a vlog in GliaCloud

Verdict

GliaCloud is the right answer if you're a publisher or marketing team that needs to ship 20-100 videos a week from existing text content. It's not a creator tool — it's an industrial video pipeline. For solo YouTubers, this is overkill; for media companies, it's the best in the category.
