Spreadsheets in the browser — now AI-assisted

Google Sheets is the collaborative spreadsheet of choice. Gemini adds natural-language formula generation, automatic insights, and chart suggestions, so you can ask 'why did revenue drop in Q2?' instead of writing a VLOOKUP. It's the fastest way to get a shared, live spreadsheet working.

Who it's for: Analysts, operators, and teams who live in spreadsheets and want AI to write formulas and surface insights.

Key features

🤖 Gemini formula help

Describe what you want; Sheets writes the formula.

📊 Auto insights & charts

Gemini suggests the right visualization from your data.

👥 Live collaboration

Share, comment, and edit together in real time.

🔌 Apps Script & add-ons

Automate with scripts and a huge add-on library.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Free and instantly collaborative
  • Gemini lowers the formula barrier
  • Huge add-on ecosystem
  • Accessible anywhere

✗ What doesn't

  • Gemini insights need a paid tier for some
  • Not built for heavy data modeling
  • Large sheets get slow
  • AI can hallucinate formula logic

Verdict

Sheets with Gemini is the most approachable spreadsheet for teams. For serious data science, graduate to a dedicated tool — but for 80% of business tracking, it's enough.
