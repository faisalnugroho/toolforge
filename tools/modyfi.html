Photoshop, but everything is a prompt

Modyfi is a real-time, browser-native image editor that fuses generative AI with traditional compositing. Select an area and type what you want; it inpaints, restyles, or generates. Because it's in the browser with version history, teams can collaboratively art-direct without a 2GB desktop app.

Who it's for: Designers, marketers, and creative teams who want generative editing without leaving a collaborative canvas.

Key features

🖌️ Generative editing

Inpaint, restyle, or extend any selection with a prompt.

🧅 Non-destructive layers

Every edit is reversible, like a pro editor.

👥 Real-time collaboration

Multiple people art-direct the same canvas.

🌐 Browser-native

No installs, runs anywhere with a GPU.
