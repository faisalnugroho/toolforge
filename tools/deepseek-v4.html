Frontier reasoning, open weights, low price

DeepSeek V4 is the July 2026 release from the lab that has consistently punched above its weight since the original R1. V4 closes most of the gap to the closed frontier: it matches or beats GPT-5.1 on math and code, sits a notch behind GPT-5.2 and Claude Opus 5 on the very hardest reasoning, and does it all with open weights and an API that costs roughly a tenth of OpenAI's.

For builders, that math is the whole story. If your product runs millions of model calls, routing the 80% that don't need frontier reasoning to DeepSeek V4 slashes your bill without users noticing. For everyone else, the free chat is a genuinely good daily driver.

Who it's for: Cost-conscious developers and teams running high-volume inference. Also great for students, researchers, and self-hosters who want a capable model they can run locally or fine-tune.

Key features

Open Open weights

Full model weights released under a permissive license. Run it on your own GPUs, fine-tune it, or inspect it — no vendor lock-in, no per-seat pricing.

Reasoning MoE reasoning core

A mixture-of-experts architecture with a dedicated reasoning head delivers GPT-5.1-class math and logic at a fraction of the activated-parameter cost.

256K 256K context

Long enough for whole-document and medium-codebase work, with recall that holds up better than most open-weight peers at this length.

Code Strong coding

Beats every other open-weight model on HumanEval and SWE-bench in its class, and trades blows with closed frontier models on real refactors.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Best quality-per-dollar in 2026 — by a wide margin
  • Open weights mean real ownership — self-host, fine-tune, audit
  • Math and code are genuinely frontier-class, not "good for the price"
  • Free chat tier is generous and a solid daily driver for casual use
  • Active community producing fine-tunes, quants, and tool integrations

✗ What doesn't

  • Lags GPT-5.2 and Claude Opus 5 on the hardest, most nuanced reasoning
  • Weaker English writing voice — competent but not as natural as Claude
  • No first-party agents/computer-use stack — you build that yourself
  • Official API reliability has improved but still trails the big three for uptime

Verdict

DeepSeek V4 is the model that makes "frontier-quality AI" cheap enough to put in every product. If you're building at scale, route your traffic here by default and reserve GPT-5.2 or Claude Opus 5 for the queries that genuinely need them. If you're an individual, the free chat is one of the best no-cost AI experiences available in 2026.
