The AI music leader

Suno is the AI music platform that made AI-generated songs go viral. In v4.5, it added stem separation (split vocals, drums, bass, etc.), better song structure, and longer generation. For creators who want beats, jingles, and experimental music, Suno is the original and still the most versatile. Udio is better for full songs, Suno wins for sound design.

Who it's for: Content creators, podcast producers, experimental musicians, anyone needing AI music.

Key features

🎵 Songs with vocals

Generate full songs with lyrics and vocals in any style.

🎚️ Stem separation

Split your generated song into vocals, drums, bass, other. Use stems in your DAW.

🎼 Style variety

Rock, pop, hip-hop, electronic, classical, jazz, world music. Hundreds of styles.

📥 Export stems

Download individual stems for use in Logic, Ableton, FL Studio.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Best sound design and variety
  • Stem separation is unique
  • Generous free tier
  • Active community

✗ What doesn't

  • Vocals less consistent than Udio
  • Less structured than Udio for full songs
  • Some style mixing can be weird
  • No commercial use on free tier

Verdict

Use Suno for sound design, experimental music, beats, and short loops. Use Udio for full songs with vocals. For most content creators, Suno is the right starting point. The stem separation is unique in the industry.
