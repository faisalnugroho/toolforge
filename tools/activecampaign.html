What ActiveCampaign actually does

ActiveCampaign is the automation-first email platform. Its visual automation builder is the best in the business — branching logic, conditional splits, goal tracking, and 800+ pre-built recipes. The new AI features include predictive sending, content generation, and lead scoring. Built-in CRM means sales teams work in the same platform as marketers.

Who it's for: B2B SaaS companies, ecommerce brands, and agencies running complex multi-step automation campaigns.

Key features

Auto Visual automations

Drag-and-drop builder with if/then branching, wait conditions, and goal tracking. 800+ recipes.

CRM Built-in CRM

Sales pipeline with deal tracking, lead scoring, and email automation tied to deal stage.

AI Predictive content

AI picks the right email content per contact based on past engagement and predicted intent.

Score Lead scoring

Custom scoring models that update in real-time based on behavior and demographic data.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Best automation builder in any email platform at this price
  • Built-in CRM eliminates the need for separate HubSpot or Pipedrive
  • AI predictive content genuinely improves open and click rates
  • Sales + marketing in one platform is rare and powerful

✗ What doesn't

  • No free tier (only 14-day trial)
  • Pricing is per contact — gets expensive fast at scale
  • UI is functional but not as polished as Mailchimp
  • Limited landing page builder compared to Mailchimp

Verdict

For B2B SaaS and ecommerce companies running complex automations, ActiveCampaign is the best value under $500/mo. The automation builder + built-in CRM is the unfair combo. For simpler email blasts and newsletters, Mailchimp or Beehiiv are cheaper and easier.
