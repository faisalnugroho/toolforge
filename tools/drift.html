Turn chats into pipeline

Drift pioneered conversational marketing: a chatbot on your site that greets visitors, answers questions, qualifies them with AI, and routes hot leads straight to a rep's calendar. In 2026 the AI handles most FAQs automatically, so humans only jump in for deals that matter.

Who it's for: B2B marketing and sales teams who want to capture and qualify site traffic in real time instead of letting it leak to a form nobody fills.

Key features

AI chat Auto answers

The bot handles common questions and books meetings without a human.

Routing Right rep, fast

Match visitors to the best rep by account, intent, or territory.

Playbooks Guided flows

Build branching conversation paths for demos, pricing, and support.

Integrations Your stack

Connect Salesforce, HubSpot, and Slack so conversations sync everywhere.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Proven lift in meeting-booked rate
  • AI handles tier-1 questions automatically
  • Deep Salesforce/HubSpot integration
  • Strong analytics on conversation quality
  • Good for high-intent B2B traffic

✗ What doesn't

  • Pricing is enterprise-level, not transparent
  • Setup and playbook design take effort
  • Overkill for low-traffic sites
  • Can feel pushy if misconfigured

Verdict

Drift pays for itself only when you have real inbound traffic to convert. If you do, the AI routing books meetings your forms never would. Budget for the enterprise tier.
