What Coda AI does well

The doc + spreadsheet hybrid that finally got AI. Ask questions of your docs, auto-fill tables, and ship custom AI workflows in minutes.

Who it's for: 3M+ teams, plus solo founders and small teams who need productivity tool that just works.

Key features

Docs AI in any doc

Highlight text, hit Cmd-J, ask for a summary, expansion, translation, or rewrite. Works in any Coda page or table cell.

Tables AI in tables

Generate rows, classify data, extract entities, summarize long text — all in a column. Build a CRM, content calendar, or pipeline in minutes.

Packs AI in Packs

Coda Packs connect to 700+ tools. The AI Pack lets you call GPT-4/Claude from any Pack — automate work end-to-end.

Brain Coda Brain

Single AI that knows every doc, table, and Pack in your workspace. Ask 'what was Q3 revenue per region?' and get a real answer.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • AI is included free with Coda — no separate add-on like Notion
  • Tables are 10x more flexible than Notion tables
  • Brain feature is genuinely a 'team memory' — ask it anything about your org
  • Packs turn Coda into a no-code platform with AI inside

✗ What doesn't

  • Steeper learning curve than Notion — tables + formulas can overwhelm
  • Smaller template marketplace than Notion
  • Mobile app is functional but not great
  • Brain queries can feel slow on large workspaces

Verdict

Coda AI is one of the best-known productivity tool on the market in 2026. The free tier alone is worth a signup, and the paid plan is priced fairly compared to peers. If you're shopping for a productivity tool tool, put Coda AI on your short list.
