The editor everyone else is measured against

Photoshop has been the definitive image tool since 1990, and in 2026 its Firefly-powered generative features moved it from editor to entire production studio. Generative Fill composites new objects with believable lighting and perspective; Neural Filters handle one-click skin, colorize, and style; and Text to Image generates raster art right on the canvas.

Who it's for: Photographers, designers, marketers, and anyone who ships polished visual work and needs layer-based, non-destructive control.

Key features

Generative Fill Firefly compositing

Type what you want — a llama in a boardroom — and Photoshop composites it with matching lighting, shadows, and perspective. No manual masking.

Neural Filters One-click retouch

Skin smoothing, colorize, style transfer, and expression editing powered by Adobe Sensei, all in a live preview panel.

Content-Aware Fill Smart removal

Remove objects and Photoshop reconstructs the background intelligently. Wires, tourists, blemishes — gone in one brush stroke.

Text to Image On-canvas generation

Generate raster artwork from a prompt directly in your document, then refine with masks, layers, and blend modes.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Best-in-class editing engine that scales from quick fixes to 100-layer composites
  • Firefly AI is best-in-class and trained on licensed, commercially-safe data
  • Deep integration with Lightroom and the rest of Creative Cloud
  • Layer-based, non-destructive workflow no web app matches
  • Regular feature drops (new Firefly models ship several times a year)

✗ What doesn't

  • Steep learning curve for beginners
  • Subscription only — no perpetual license anymore
  • Resource-heavy on large files and 4K+ timelines
  • Best AI features gated behind the higher Creative Cloud tiers
  • Mobile app is thinner than the desktop experience

Verdict

If you do any serious visual work in 2026, Photoshop with Firefly is still the bar everyone else is measured against. The $22.99/mo single-app plan pays for itself the first time Generative Fill saves you an hour of cloning.
