What MagicSchool actually does

MagicSchool is purpose-built AI for teachers. Where ChatGPT gives generic outputs that a teacher has to massage, MagicSchool ships 60+ teacher-specific generators — IEP accommodations, rubric builders, reading-level rewriters, standards-aligned lesson plans, SEL activities, parent email drafts. Used by 3M+ educators across 50,000+ schools. Built by a former teacher who got tired of staying up until midnight writing IEPs.

Who it's for: K-12 teachers drowning in planning, IEPs, differentiation, and parent communication. Especially valuable for special education teachers and ESL/ELL teachers.

Key features

📚 60+ teacher-specific generators

Lesson plan, rubric, IEP accommodations, quiz, worksheet, reading passage, parent email, SEL activity, project idea, vocabulary list, exit ticket, and many more. Each generator is purpose-built.

🎯 Standards alignment

Lesson plans automatically tagged to Common Core, NGSS, state standards. Pick a state + grade level and outputs cite the specific standards they're aligned to.

⚖️ Differentiation built in

Every generator has a "Differentiate for ELL, IEP, or advanced learners" toggle. Generates three versions in one click — what used to take an hour takes 10 seconds.

🏫 School-safe + FERPA compliant

District SSO integration, no student data shared with model providers, student information stripped before generation. Adopted by LAUSD, NYC DOE, Chicago Public Schools.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Generators are pre-tuned for teacher voice and structure — output is ready to use
  • Free tier is genuinely useful (50 generations/month, no credit card)
  • Standards alignment saves the most painful 30 minutes of lesson planning
  • School district SSO + bulk licensing is the strongest in the category
  • Active community — teacher-requested features ship monthly

Verdict

MagicSchool is the default AI tool for K-12 teachers in 2026. If you're a teacher not using it, you're spending 5+ hours/week on work it can do in seconds. The free tier is enough to try it. The $9/mo premium is worth it the moment you write your first IEP accommodation with it.
