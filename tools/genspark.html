The search engine that builds the page for you

Genspark flips the search paradigm on its head. Instead of returning a list of links for you to click through, it uses multiple specialized AI agents to research your query in parallel, then synthesizes the answer into a custom "Sparkpage" — a single, ad-free mini-website with text, images, tables, and interactive widgets tailored to your exact question.

Who it's for: Researchers, marketers, trip planners, shoppers, and anyone tired of clicking through 5 SEO-stuffed articles to find a real answer.

Key features

Sparkpages Custom mini-websites per query

Every search returns a fresh, structured page with curated info, comparison tables, embedded videos, and an AI co-pilot. No SEO spam, no ads, no popups — just the answer.

Agents Multi-agent research

Behind the scenes, Genspark spawns 8-10 specialized agents that research different angles of your question in parallel, then a synthesis agent combines the best findings. Faster than Perplexity, deeper than ChatGPT search.

Verified citations Every claim is sourced

Inline source links on every fact. Click the [1] marker to see exactly which page the claim came from. No more "trust me bro" AI answers.

Sparkpages shareable Make it a permanent page

Found a great Sparkpage? Pin it to your library or share via link. Other users can search and find your pinned Sparkpage. Great for travel itineraries, gift guides, and team research.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Sparkpages are genuinely 10x better than scrolling 10 links
  • Multi-agent research is fast and thorough — usually beats Perplexity on depth
  • Free tier is generous — 200 Sparkpages/day
  • Great for product comparisons, travel planning, and complex research queries
  • No ads, no SEO bait — feels like a breath of fresh air vs Google

✗ What doesn't

  • Still slower than a quick Google search for simple lookups ("weather in Paris")
  • Sparkpage generation can take 15-30 seconds — patience required
  • Some queries (especially niche local info) return thinner results than Perplexity
  • Library and sharing features are still rough around the edges
  • Pro tier ($20/mo) is steep if you mainly use it for simple searches

Verdict

Genspark is the most ambitious AI search product of 2026. If you find yourself opening 8 tabs every time you research a purchase, plan a trip, or compare options, the Sparkpage format will feel like the future. If you just need to check the weather or look up a phone number, stick with Google. For everything else, give Genspark a real try.
