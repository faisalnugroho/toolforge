The email tool that pays for itself in week one

SaneBox has been around since 2010 — older than every other AI productivity tool in this directory. While Superhuman charges $30/mo for a new email client, SaneBox layers onto your existing inbox and uses machine learning to sort, snooze, and defer emails. You keep your email app; SaneBox just makes it dramatically less stressful.

Who it's for: Knowledge workers drowning in email, executives managing 200+ emails/day, anyone who refuses to switch email clients but desperately needs triage.

Key features

SaneLater AI triage folder

Non-urgent emails (newsletters, FYIs, automated notifications) auto-move to @SaneLater. Your main inbox only shows what actually matters. Train it with drag-and-drop — it learns in days.

Snooze Defer emails

Hit snooze on an email and it disappears, then returns to your inbox at the time you choose — tomorrow, next week, when you get back from vacation. Like Gmail's snooze, but cross-client.

SaneBlackHole Unsubscribe magic

Drag a sender into @SaneBlackHole and every future email from them goes straight to trash before you see it. Works for newsletters, cold sales, vendors — anyone you'd rather not hear from.

Reminders Follow-up tracking

CC'd on something that needs a reply in 3 days? Set a reminder and SaneBox pings you if nobody responds. Perfect for sales follow-ups and project check-ins.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Works with literally any IMAP email — Gmail, Outlook, Fastmail, iCloud, ProtonMail
  • No new app to learn — SaneBox lives inside your existing client via folder labels
  • Set-and-forget AI — takes about a week to learn your preferences, then runs itself
  • 14-day free trial, no credit card required
  • SaneBlackHole unsubscribe is more reliable than Gmail's built-in

✗ What doesn't

  • Need to give SaneBox IMAP credentials (security-conscious users may hesitate)
  • No real-time push — triage can lag 1-2 minutes
  • $7/mo feels steep if you only get ~50 emails/day
  • Mobile experience relies on folder rules, less polished than desktop
  • No AI-generated draft replies (that's Superhuman's territory)

Verdict

If your email is a source of dread and you don't want to switch clients, SaneBox is the lowest-friction fix that exists. It's a 14-day free trial with no card, so try it for one work week — you'll know by Friday if it's worth $7/mo. For most knowledge workers, the math is easy: 30 minutes/day saved = $250/day at $100/hr consulting rates.
