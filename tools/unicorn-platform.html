Landing pages that don't look like templates

Unicorn Platform sits between pure-AI builders (type a prompt, get a page) and full editors (drag everything). Its AI scaffolds a startup-grade landing page — hero, features, pricing, footer — and then you fine-tune with a clean block editor. It's especially loved by indie hackers and newsletter authors who ship a new page every week.

Who it's for: Indie hackers, startup founders, and newsletter creators who need attractive launch pages fast and often.

Key features

AI scaffoldPrompt to page

Describe your product and get a structured landing page with sensible sections.

BlocksReal editor

Unlike pure generators, you can restyle and rearrange with a proper block system.

BlogBuilt-in CMS

Add a changelog or blog without a separate platform — handy for SEO.

ExportOwn your code

Paid tiers let you export clean HTML/CSS so you're never fully locked in.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • AI speed plus a genuine editor
  • Startup-first templates look modern
  • Blog/CMS helps with SEO
  • Code export reduces lock-in
  • Affordable at $12/mo

✗ What doesn't

  • Smaller template library than giants
  • Not ideal for large multi-page sites
  • AI output still needs brand polish
  • Fewer integrations than Webflow

Verdict

Unicorn Platform is the sweet spot for founders who want AI speed but refuse to be stuck with a rigid generator. At $12/mo it's a daily-driver launch page tool — pair it with Mixo if you only need a waitlist, or graduate to Webflow when the site grows up.
