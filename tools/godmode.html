Point it at a goal, walk away

Godmode is the open-source 'agentic' web app that takes a high-level objective and chains model calls, web browsing, and self-reflection to get there — adjusting its plan as it learns. Bring your own API key and let it research, draft, and iterate on open-ended tasks.

Who it's for: Researchers, founders, and tinkerers who want an autonomous assistant for messy, multi-step goals.

Key features

🎯 Goal-driven

Describe the outcome, not the steps.

🌐 Web browsing

Researches live as it works.

🔁 Self-reflect

Replans when stuck.

🔓 Open source

Bring your own model key.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Great for open-ended tasks
  • Free and extensible
  • Fun, fast experimentation
  • Model-agnostic

✗ What doesn't

  • Can go off the rails
  • Needs your API key
  • Not reliable for critical work
  • UI is experimental

Verdict

Godmode is the sandbox for autonomous agents — impressive on research and brainstorming, best treated as a tireless intern, not a guarantee.
