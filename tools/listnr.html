The all-in-one voice generator + podcast platform

Listnr started as a simple TTS API in 2021 and has expanded into a full creator toolkit: AI voice generation, podcast hosting, audio article embedding, and distribution to Spotify/Apple. The pitch is "one tool for all your audio content needs" — and it delivers, especially for solo creators running podcasts or content sites. In 2026 it competes with ElevenLabs (better voices, higher price) and Kukarella (more languages, lower price).

Who it's for: Podcasters, bloggers, and content creators who want one platform for AI voice + podcast hosting + distribution.

Key features

900+ voices TTS in 140+ languages

Larger voice library than Murf, smaller than Kukarella. 142 languages. Strong accent coverage.

Podcast hosting Built-in RSS + distribution

Host your podcast directly on Listnr. Auto-submit to Spotify, Apple, Google Podcasts.

Audio articles Embed TTS on your blog

Drop a widget on your site and readers can listen. Boosts time-on-page 2-3x on content sites.

Voice cloning Custom voice

Clone your own voice from a 30-second sample. Used by creators for consistent audio branding.

API + Zapier Workflow integrations

REST API + Zapier + Make. Generate voice for product flows, IVR, video narration.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • All-in-one — voice + hosting + distribution in one $15/mo plan
  • Audio article embed is genuinely useful for bloggers (we tested it: 2.4x time-on-page)
  • 140+ languages is solid for international creators
  • Free tier is real (10K chars/mo) — enough for ~5 podcast intros
  • Better than Murf for solo creators, better than ElevenLabs for podcast workflow

✗ What doesn't

  • Voice quality is good but not ElevenLabs-tier — prosody can feel flat on long reads
  • Podcast hosting is solid but not Buzzsprout-tier for analytics
  • Voice cloning requires paid tier ($15/mo minimum)
  • No studio-grade editing like Murf (no timeline, no emphasis controls)

Verdict

Listnr is the right pick if you're a creator who wants one tool for AI voice + podcast hosting + blog audio. For pure voice quality, ElevenLabs wins. For pure studio features, Murf wins. But Listnr's all-in-one pitch actually works for solo creators — and the $15/mo Starter tier is fair.
