Why Sourcegraph Cody stands out in 2026

Key features

Codebase-wide context Whole-repo awareness

Cody indexes your entire codebase with Sourcegraph's search backend, so answers come with real file and symbol references — not guesses from the open tab.

Multi-model Pick your model

Swap between Claude, GPT-4o, Mixtral, and local models. Enterprise teams can point Cody at their own self-hosted models.

Inline + chat Edit in place

Highlight code, describe the change, and Cody rewrites the selection. Jump to a chat sidebar for architecture questions.

Autocomplete Contextual completions

Whole-line and multi-line completions trained on your repo's patterns, not just the current file.
