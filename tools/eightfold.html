What Eightfold actually does

Eightfold AI is the deep-learning platform for HR. The Talent Intelligence Platform unifies recruiting, employee development, internal mobility, and diversity hiring on one neural network trained on the global job market. Eightfold claims to hold the world's largest talent graph: 1.2B+ profiles, 1M+ skills, real-time labor market data. Used by Walmart (replaced 13 separate HR tools), Vodafone, Merck, and Hilton to hire faster, develop existing employees, and identify skills gaps.

Who it's for: Mid-market and enterprise HR teams (1,000+ employees) looking to consolidate ATS, LMS, skills taxonomy, and career mobility on one AI platform.

Key features

🧠 Deep-learning talent graph

1.2B+ profiles, 1M+ skills. Matches candidates to roles based on capability, not just keywords. Understands transferable skills (e.g., a Java developer can be a Python developer with 2 weeks ramp-up).

🔄 Internal mobility + career development

Suggests internal candidates for open roles before external recruiting. Closes the #1 reason employees quit: "no growth opportunities." Walmart used this to fill 50% of corporate roles internally.

⚖️ Diversity hiring controls

Blind screening mode, bias-audited models, structured interview kits. Customers report 30-40% improvement in diverse candidate progression through funnel.

🔌 ATS + HRIS integrations

Native Workday, SAP SuccessFactors, Greenhouse, Lever integrations. Replaces ~13 separate HR tools in enterprise deployments — that's the actual value prop.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Deep-learning skill matching is meaningfully better than keyword ATS — fewer mis-hires
  • Internal mobility is the killer app — fills roles 60% faster and 70% cheaper than external
  • Replaces 10+ legacy HR tools, simplifying the HR tech stack
  • Strong diversity hiring controls — bias auditing is built-in, not bolt-on
  • SOC 2 + ISO 27001 + GDPR — passes enterprise security review

✗ What doesn't

  • Enterprise-only pricing ($5k+/mo minimum, usually $50-200k/yr) — inaccessible to SMB
  • Implementation takes 6-9 months. Requires clean data + change management across HR/IT
  • Model is a black box — explaining why a candidate was rejected requires support tickets
  • Less mature for hourly / frontline hiring — better for knowledge workers

Verdict

Eightfold is the right tool if you're an enterprise (1,000+ employees) drowning in legacy HR tech and want to consolidate. The internal mobility feature alone justifies the cost — Walmart filled half its corporate roles internally, saving millions in recruiting costs. If you're an SMB or hiring hourly workers, look at Greenhouse or HireEZ instead.
