Key features

AI Search Smart job matching

Upwork's AI matches you to jobs based on your skills, past work, and success rate — not just keyword filters.

Moment AI Proposal generation

Generate tailored proposals in seconds. The AI reads the job post and drafts a pitch using your profile and portfolio.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • AI proposal generation saves 20-30 minutes per pitch
  • Smart job matching surfaces relevant gigs you'd miss manually
  • Market insights help you price competitively
  • Built into the existing Upwork platform — no separate subscription

✗ What doesn't

  • AI-generated proposals can sound generic if not edited

Verdict

If you're already freelancing on Upwork, the AI features are worth enabling — especially proposal generation, which can 3x your application speed. Just don't send AI proposals unedited; clients can tell. The $14.99/mo Plus plan pays for itself with one extra client win.
