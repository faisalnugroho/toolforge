Stunning design out of the box — now with AI that gets your brand.

Squarespace has always been the design-first website builder — the one designers recommend to non-designers. In 2024 they shipped Blueprint AI: describe your business in plain English, and Squarespace generates a custom site tailored to your style, products, and copy. Unlike Wix's AI generator (which optimizes for breadth), Squarespace's Blueprint AI optimizes for "this looks like a brand, not a template."

Who it's for: Photographers, designers, restaurants, wedding vendors, artists, boutique e-commerce, consultants — anyone who wants a website that looks expensive without hiring a designer.

Key features

Blueprint AI site generator

Describe your business, pick a vibe, and Blueprint AI builds a custom site with sections, layouts, color palette, and copy that matches your brand. Iterate by chatting — "make it more minimal" or "add a testimonials section."

Design AI Design intelligence

AI auto-adjusts layouts, spacing, and typography as you edit. Suggests color palettes and font pairings based on your industry. The Fluid Engine editor makes pixel-perfect responsive edits without code.

Commerce Built-in commerce

Squarespace Commerce for product businesses — inventory, payments, abandoned cart recovery, subscription products, and POS integration. Less powerful than Shopify but more beautiful.

Marketing Email, SEO & analytics

Built-in email marketing (Squarespace Email Campaigns), AI-powered SEO suggestions, and Squarespace Analytics. No external tools needed for the basics.

Scheduling Acuity scheduling built in

Acuity Scheduling (owned by Squarespace) handles bookings, consultations, classes, and payments — integrated directly. No plugin needed.

Member areas Members & courses

New in 2025-2026: native member areas for paid content, online courses, and gated downloads. Native video hosting included.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Best-in-class design — sites look "designed" out of the box, not templated
  • Blueprint AI is the best AI site generator for brand-aware output in 2026
  • All-in-one: hosting, domain, email, commerce, scheduling, courses, analytics
  • Strong typography defaults — uses Adobe Fonts and Google Fonts seamlessly
  • Native Acuity integration for service businesses
  • Excellent 24/7 support and a thriving designer community

✗ What doesn't

  • Less flexible than Webflow or Wix for highly custom layouts
  • Commerce is solid but limited vs Shopify (no native multi-warehouse, weaker app ecosystem)
  • No real free tier — only a 14-day trial
  • AI features are still maturing vs Wix's broader AI suite
  • Locked into Squarespace ecosystem — hard to migrate
  • Personal plan ($16/mo) doesn't include a custom domain or e-commerce

Verdict

Squarespace is the right choice for creatives and brand-conscious small businesses. Where Wix wins on breadth and Shopify wins on commerce scale, Squarespace wins on "this site looks like a real designer built it." Blueprint AI is the best in class for designers, photographers, restaurants, wedding vendors, and consultants. Skip it if you need deep commerce or full layout freedom.
