10x faster than LinkedIn Recruiter for passive sourcing

hireEZ (formerly Hiretual) is the AI sourcing platform that enterprise recruiting teams use to find passive candidates that LinkedIn Recruiter misses. It aggregates 800M+ profiles across LinkedIn, GitHub, Stack Overflow, Behance, Dribbble, and 50+ other sources — searchable from one tab.

Who it's for: In-house recruiters and talent acquisition teams at companies hiring 10+ engineers/designers/marketers per quarter. Especially powerful for technical roles where LinkedIn is incomplete.

Key features

800M 800M+ candidate profiles

Search across LinkedIn, GitHub, Stack Overflow, Behance, Dribbble, and 50+ other sources from one tab. No more tab-switching to find developers on GitHub, designers on Dribbble.

AI AI candidate scoring

hireEZ scores every candidate 1-100 on fit for your role. Surfaces best matches first — no more sifting through 500 profiles to find the 5 worth messaging.

Outreach Multi-channel outreach
