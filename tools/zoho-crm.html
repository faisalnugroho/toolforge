CRM without the enterprise tax

Zoho CRM is the backbone of the broader Zoho One suite. It covers lead and contact management, pipeline stages, omnichannel outreach, and workflow automation — then connects natively to Zoho's email, desk, and finance apps. For small businesses that want one vendor instead of ten, it's hard to beat on price.

Who it's for: Small and mid-size businesses that want an affordable CRM now and a clear upgrade path into a full business suite.

Key features

Leads Pipeline management

Track leads through custom stages with forecasts, scoring, and assignment rules.

Channels Omnichannel outreach

Email, phone, social, and live chat unified in one customer timeline.

Blueprint Process automation

Enforce consistent sales processes with guided Blueprints and macros.

Zia AI assistant

Zoho's AI surfaces anomalies, predicts deal outcomes, and suggests next actions.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Unbeatable price-to-feature ratio
  • Deep integration with the Zoho suite
  • Free tier for tiny teams
  • Strong automation and AI (Zia)
  • Highly customizable modules

✗ What doesn't

  • Interface is dated next to modern CRMs
  • Customization can get complex fast
  • Support quality varies by plan
  • Best value requires buying into Zoho ecosystem

Verdict

Zoho CRM is the smart default for cost-conscious teams that expect to grow into a full suite. The $14 Standard plan is enough for most small sales teams.
