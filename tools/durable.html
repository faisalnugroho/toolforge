The bottom line

Durable is the fastest way to get a small business online. Type your business type and city, and Durable generates a complete website — homepage, about, services, contact — in under 30 seconds. The 2026 version adds a built-in CRM, AI marketing tools, and an invoicing module, turning Durable from a website builder into a full small-business platform.

Who it's for: Solo service businesses — plumbers, photographers, consultants, coaches, cleaners, barbers — who need a professional web presence in an afternoon, not a quarter.

Key features

30s AI website generator

Enter your business type, name, and location. Durable ships a complete, mobile-responsive, copy-complete site in 30 seconds. The AI writes the headlines, services, and about page from scratch.

Built-in CRM Customer tracking

Every contact form, booking, and lead lands in the integrated CRM. Tag, segment, and follow up. No Zapier required.

AI Marketing Autopilot ads + emails

Durable's AI can write and launch Google Ads, send email campaigns, and post to social — all driven by the same business profile that built your site.

Invoicing Get paid

Send invoices, take card payments, and track who owes you. Replaces FreshBooks for the smallest 30% of small businesses.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Fastest time-to-publish in the industry — 30 seconds is real, not marketing
  • All-in-one: site + CRM + marketing + invoicing on one $15/mo plan
  • No design skills required — the templates genuinely look professional
  • Best small-business value in 2026 (Framer/v0 are for designers; Durable is for owners)

✗ What doesn't

  • Less design flexibility than Framer, Webflow, or v0 — you work within templates

Verdict

Durable is the best AI website builder for service businesses in 2026. It's not the prettiest, it's not the most flexible, but it gets a real business online and profitable in an afternoon.
