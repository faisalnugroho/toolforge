AI that remembers your whole book

Novelist is purpose-built for long fiction. Unlike generic chatbots that forget the chapter you wrote yesterday, it tracks your plot, characters, and style across an entire manuscript so generated prose stays consistent.

Who it's for: Indie novelists, fanfic writers, and serialized-fiction authors who need continuity across tens of thousands of words.

Key features

Plot Outline & beats

Plan acts, chapters, and beats; the AI drafts toward them.

Cast Character memory

Persistent character sheets keep voices and facts consistent.

Voice Style lock

Match your established tone so new chapters don’t drift.

Draft Chapter generation

Expand beats into full scenes you then edit.

The honest take

  • Character and plot memory actually work
