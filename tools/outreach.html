The short take

Honest, hands-on review of Outreach in 2026. The enterprise sales engagement platform with AI deal scoring, Kaia conversation intelligence, and sequence automation.

Who it's for: The enterprise sales engagement platform with AI deal scoring and conversation intelligence.

Key features

📈 Deal AI deal scoring (Deal IQ)

Outreach’s model scores every open deal on win likelihood, stuck-ness, and next-best-action. Trained on 5B+ sales interactions from the Outreach customer base.

👂 Kaia Kaia conversation intelligence

Records, transcribes, and analyzes sales calls. Surfaces competitor mentions, pricing pushback, and next steps. Built-in agenda, slide-share, and live coaching.

⛓️ Seq Sequence automation

Multi-step, multi-channel sequences (email, call task, LinkedIn task, manual task). A/B test subject lines, body copy, send times, and CTAs at the sequence level.

📊 Fore Forecast & pipeline analytics

Pipeline-by-stage forecasting with configurable weights. AI-suggested commit/most-likely/best-case numbers per rep. Salesforce-native sync.

🛡️ SOC Enterprise governance

SOC2, GDPR, HIPAA, FedRAMP-ready. Role-based access, audit logs, email policy enforcement. The default at Fortune 500 B2B sales orgs.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Most mature enterprise sales engagement platform — 10+ years, 6,000+ customers
  • Deal IQ scoring genuinely predicts wins/losses
  • Kaia conversation intelligence is on par with Gong, half the price
  • Enterprise governance (SOC2, HIPAA, FedRAMP) wins large deals
  • Native Salesforce + Microsoft Dynamics integration

✗ What doesn't

  • Expensive — $100-200/user/month typical, minimum 5 seats
  • Steep learning curve — admins needed for full config
  • Overkill for teams under 10 reps — use Instantly or Lemlist
  • Email deliverability requires dedicated admin attention
  • Recent AI features still maturing vs Gong/Chorus

Verdict

Outreach is the right choice when you have 10+ AEs and need enterprise-grade sales engagement with AI deal scoring. For startups and SMBs, Instantly ($30/mo) or Lemlist ($59/mo) deliver 80% of the value at 10-20% of the price. The $100/user/month Pro plan is the entry point.
