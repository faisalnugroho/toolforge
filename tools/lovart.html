Tell Lovart your brand idea — get a full design system back

Lovart is the first AI design tool that feels like hiring a junior designer. Describe your brand or product, and it generates a full design system — logo, color palette, typography, social posts, landing page mockup, and product shots — in 5 minutes. It's not just an image generator; it plans, iterates, and delivers design assets that actually look like a cohesive brand. Made by the team behind the viral 'design agent' demo videos of 2025.

Who it's for: Founders, marketers, indie hackers, and small teams who can't afford a $5K/mo design agency but need more than a DALL-E image dump.

Key features

Brand kits Full brand kit generation

Type 'organic skincare brand for Gen Z women, sustainable, playful' — Lovart delivers logo, palette, typography, packaging mockup, and 5 social posts. Editable in Figma export.

Campaigns Multi-asset campaigns

Ask for 'a Black Friday launch campaign' and get Instagram posts, story templates, email headers, landing page hero, and TikTok thumbnails. All on-brand, all consistent.

Iterations Conversational revisions

Hate the logo? Say 'make the logo more minimal, swap teal for sage green' and Lovart regenerates only the affected assets, keeping everything else on brand.

Exports Figma, PSD, SVG exports

Export to Figma (editable layers), PSD (Photoshop), or SVG (vectors). No more pixel-pushing in a PNG. Designers can refine in their native tools.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Best 'AI design partner' experience in 2026 — actually plans, not just generates
  • Full brand kits in 5 minutes vs 2-3 days with a designer
  • Figma export is editable — designers don't have to start from scratch
  • Great for non-designers who need on-brand assets fast
  • Free tier (3 projects) is enough to try a full campaign

✗ What doesn't

  • Still hit-or-miss on very specific brand styles (luxury fashion, medical)
  • Logo generation is decent but won't replace a senior designer for serious brands
  • Iterating 10+ times on a campaign can hit Pro tier limits fast
  • Some exports lose layer structure in Figma (working on it)
  • No live collaboration — designed for solo use, not teams

Verdict

Lovart isn't going to replace your design team, but it will absolutely replace the 4-day 'quick social campaign' rush jobs and the $500 logo contest cycle. For founders shipping MVPs and marketers running small campaigns, Lovart pays for itself in week one. The free tier is a real free tier — try it before paying.
