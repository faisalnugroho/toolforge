The most headshots, plus tools to fix them

Secta AI generates 300+ professional headshots from 25+ selfies — more than any competitor. But what sets it apart is the Remix tool: after generation, you can change backgrounds, swap outfits, adjust expressions, fix closed eyes, and retouch individual headshots. It's the closest thing to having a photographer and editor in one.

Who it's for: People who want maximum options and the ability to fine-tune results. Job seekers, speakers, real estate agents, and anyone who's been frustrated by AI headshots that are "almost right" but need one small fix.

Key features

300+ Maximum output

Get 300+ headshots in a single batch — the most of any AI headshot tool. More options means better odds of finding the perfect shot.

Remix Post-generation editing

The standout feature. Change backgrounds, outfits, and expressions on any headshot after it's generated. Fix closed eyes, adjust smiles, swap suits.

Retouch Fine-tune details

Built-in retouching tools for blemishes, stray hair, skin smoothing, and teeth whitening. Edit like a pro without Photoshop.

4K High-res downloads

Download in 4K resolution with commercial usage rights. Suitable for LinkedIn, websites, print, and large displays.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • 300+ headshots is the most in the category — more options to choose from
  • Remix tool is genuinely useful — fix backgrounds, outfits, expressions post-generation
  • Built-in retouching saves a Photoshop trip
  • Good diversity of styles, backgrounds, and lighting setups
  • 4K commercial downloads included

✗ What doesn't

  • $49 is the most expensive one-time option (Aragon is $29)
  • Requires 25+ selfies — more upload friction than ProPhotos (5 photos)
  • Remix results can be inconsistent — outfit swaps sometimes look unnatural
  • Generation takes longer (2–3 hours) due to the larger batch size

Verdict

Secta AI is the best choice if you want maximum headshots and the ability to edit them afterward. The Remix tool alone justifies the $49 price — no other AI headshot tool lets you swap backgrounds and outfits after generation. If you just need 100 quick headshots, Aragon is cheaper. If you want to fine-tune, Secta wins.
