Competitive intel for the rest of us

WatchMyComp is the scrappy, affordable answer to Crayon and Klue. It tracks competitor websites, pricing, and changes and delivers digestible weekly intelligence — without the six-figure enterprise contract. For small GTM teams, it's the difference between 'we heard they changed something' and 'here's exactly what and when'.

Who it's for: Startups and SMBs with 1-20 person GTM teams who need competitor awareness but not a full CI platform.

Key features

Monitor Website monitoring

Tracks competitor pages for pricing, feature, and copy changes.

Digest Weekly digest

A clean summary of what each rival did this week, delivered to your inbox.

Compare Side-by-side

Compare your positioning against competitors at a glance.

Alerts Change alerts

Get pinged when a rival launches or reprices.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Genuinely affordable vs enterprise CI tools
  • Fast setup, no taxonomy project required
  • Clean weekly digest anyone can read
  • Free tier to validate

✗ What doesn't

  • Smaller signal coverage than Crayon/Klue
  • Less depth on social and review signals
  • Best for a handful of competitors, not hundreds
  • No deep CRM integration

Verdict

WatchMyComp is the best entry-level competitive intelligence tool in 2026 for small teams that can't justify a five-figure platform. It won't replace Crayon at scale, but at $99/mo it pays for itself the first time it warns you about a rival price drop before a deal slips.
