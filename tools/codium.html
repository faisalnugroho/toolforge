AI that actually improves code integrity

Qodo (you may still know it as Codium AI) focuses on the unglamorous part of shipping software: tests and code review. Its AI analyzes your code, proposes meaningful test suites that catch edge cases, and reviews pull requests for logic bugs before a human ever looks. It's the difference between 'it compiles' and 'it's correct'.

Who it's for: Engineering teams who want higher test coverage and fewer escaped bugs without hiring a QA army.
