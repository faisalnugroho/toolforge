The short take

Moises is the AI audio engineer in your pocket. Upload any MP3/WAV/YouTube link and get studio-quality stem separation in seconds — vocals, drums, bass, guitar, keys, and other.

Who it’s for: DJs, remix producers, music teachers, cover singers, audio engineers, karaoke creators, and music students who need clean stems from any track.

Key features

🎚️ Stem Stem separation

Industry-leading 5-stem separation (vocals, drums, bass, guitar, other) plus a 2-stem “vocals/instrumental” mode for karaoke. Output is studio-quality 24-bit WAV.

🎚️ Pitch Pitch & tempo

Real-time pitch shifting (-12 to +12 semitones) and tempo change (0.5x to 2.0x) without artifacts. Rehearse tricky passages, learn songs in different keys, or remix at 128 BPM.

🥁 Beat Smart metronome

Auto-detects BPM and downbeats, then inserts a click track at any tempo — perfect for practicing instruments or recording covers.

🔗 YouTu YouTube link import

Paste any YouTube/SoundCloud URL and Moises pulls stems directly. No need to download MP3s or worry about DRM.

💾 Expo Stem export

Download individual stems or the full multitrack session. Integrates with Ableton, Logic Pro, FL Studio via drag-and-drop.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Best-in-class stem quality (better than free tools like LALAL.AI demo)
  • Real-time pitch & tempo without artifacts
  • Mobile app is fully featured (iOS + Android)
  • Free tier gives 5 separations/month
  • API for enterprise music tech companies

✗ What doesn’t

  • 5-stem mode is gated to paid plans ($12/mo Premium)
  • Very long songs (>10 min) can take 2-3 minutes to process
  • Drum stem sometimes bleeds with bass-heavy tracks
  • No native MIDI extraction (use Audioshake for that)

Verdict

Moises is the de-facto standard for AI stem separation in 2026. The free tier is generous enough for casual covers, and Premium at $12/mo is a steal for working producers. Better than LALAL.AI for music producers, better than Audacity for everyone.
