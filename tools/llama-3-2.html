Small, fast, multimodal

Llama 3.2 extended the family with tiny 1B and 3B text models that run on phones and laptops, plus 11B and 90B vision-language models that can read images. It's the practical choice when you need on-device inference or cheap multimodal understanding without a cloud bill.

Who it's for: Mobile and edge developers, and teams needing cheap vision understanding on private infrastructure.

Key features

The honest take

✓ What works
