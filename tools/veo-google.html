Google's video engine, everywhere

Google Veo is the umbrella name for DeepMind's video generation models now embedded across YouTube (Dream Track, video effects), Gemini, and Google Ads. It's the engine making AI clips a default creative option inside Google's products.

Who it's for: Marketers and creators publishing to YouTube and Google surfaces who want AI video without leaving the platform.

Key features

📺 YouTube native

Built into YouTube creation and Shorts effects.

🧠 Gemini connected

Generate and edit video from Gemini prompts.

🎯 Ads ready

Used in Google Ads for quick creative variants.

🔒 Brand-safe

Enterprise controls for commercial use.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Deep YouTube and Google integration
  • Brand-safe for advertisers
  • Continuously improving
  • Broad availability

✗ What doesn't

  • Capabilities vary by product surface
  • Less of a standalone creative app
  • Pricing tied to Google plans
  • Less control than dedicated tools

Verdict

Google Veo is the right engine if your content lives on YouTube and Google surfaces. For a dedicated creative studio, a standalone video tool gives more control.
