Video you can actually query

Twelve Labs turns raw video into embeddings you can search with plain English ('find the shot where the chef lifts the pan'). It also generates highlights and clips from prompts — no manual scrubbing.

Who it's for: Developers and media teams building searchable video libraries, highlight reels, or moderation pipelines.

Key features

Marengo Embed video
