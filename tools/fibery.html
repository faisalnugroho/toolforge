One workspace instead of eight tabs

Fibery is the 'build-your-own-Notion-meets-Linear' platform. You model your own entities (bugs, features, clients, deals) and connect them — then ask the AI to summarize, draft, or auto-link. For small product teams drowning in tool sprawl, it replaces Notion, Jira, and a CRM in one.

Who it's for: Product and ops teams who want a single source of truth without buying six subscriptions.

Key features

AI Summaries Instant recaps

Ask Fibery AI to summarize any entity, workspace, or filtered view into plain-English briefings.

Entity modeling Build your own DB

Create typed entities and relations (Feature → Bug → Customer) with no code. AI suggests fields from your writing.

AI in rich text Draft where you write

Generate and rewrite text inline; the AI knows the surrounding entity's context.

Automations Rules + AI

Trigger actions on field changes; use AI to classify, label, or enrich records automatically.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Truly flexible — model almost any workflow
  • AI is contextual, not a disconnected chatbot
  • Replaces 3-5 separate tools for small teams
  • Generous free tier for tiny teams
  • Strong import from Notion/Jira

✗ What doesn't

  • Steep learning curve — flexibility is a double-edged sword
  • UI can feel dense for non-technical users
  • Mobile app is limited
  • AI features need the paid plan to scale

Verdict

If your team lives in five tools and hates the copy-paste between them, Fibery is the closet thing to a unified workspace with AI that actually understands your data. The $10/user/mo plan pays off the moment you cancel two other subscriptions.
