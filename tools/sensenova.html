An enterprise-grade model family

SenseNova is SenseTime’s foundation-model product line: a chatbot front end plus vision and language APIs aimed at enterprises that need to deploy AI inside regulated, China-based environments.

Who it's for: Enterprises and developers in China needing a trustworthy local model vendor with vision + language under one roof.

Key features

Chat SenseChat

A general-purpose assistant for writing, analysis, and coding.

Vision Multimodal

Strong computer-vision heritage feeds document and image understanding.

API Open platform

REST APIs with SDKs and enterprise SLAs.

Deploy On-prem option

Private deployment for data-sensitive workloads.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Backed by a major CV/AI research leader
  • Vision + language from a single vendor
  • On-prem and private-cloud options
  • Enterprise support and SLAs

✗ What doesn't

  • Less known outside China
  • Pricing is enterprise-quote based
  • English performance lags domestic rivals’ best

Verdict

SenseNova is a credible enterprise choice in China, especially where vision and on-prem deployment matter. Global teams will likely default elsewhere.
