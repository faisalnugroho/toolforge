The ecommerce AI writer that ships product pages

Hypotenuse AI is the AI writing tool that ecommerce teams reach for when they have 5,000 SKUs and a copy deadline. Unlike ChatGPT or Jasper, it's tuned for product descriptions, category pages, and SEO content — with direct integrations to Shopify, Amazon, and most PIMs. If you've ever tried to write 1,000 product descriptions with ChatGPT, you know the pain. Hypotenuse was built for that exact pain.

Who it's for: Ecommerce managers, Shopify store owners, Amazon sellers, and content teams producing high-volume product copy. Less useful for long-form blog content.

Key features

📦 Bulk product descriptions

Upload a CSV of product names and attributes. Get SEO-friendly descriptions for 1,000+ products in under 10 minutes. The killer feature for any ecommerce team.

🛍️ Shopify integration

Native Shopify app. Pull products directly from your store, generate copy in bulk, and push it back. No CSV shuffling.

🎨 Brand voice training

Feed it 10-20 of your best product pages. It learns your tone, vocabulary, and structure. Output sounds like your team wrote it, not like AI.

🔍 SEO keyword mode

Input target keywords. Hypotenuse weaves them in naturally — title tags, meta descriptions, and on-page SEO baked in. Beats ChatGPT for ecommerce SEO.

  • Shopify and Amazon integrations actually work
