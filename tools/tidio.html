The SMB-friendly AI support chatbot

Tidio started as a simple live chat widget for Shopify stores in 2013, and has grown into the most popular AI customer support platform for small and mid-size ecommerce. The flagship product is Lyro — an AI agent trained on your store's data (FAQ, product pages, past tickets) that autonomously resolves 70%+ of customer questions. By 2026, Tidio powers live chat for 300,000+ businesses and is one of the top-installed apps on the Shopify App Store. Compared to enterprise tools like Intercom Fin and Decagon, Tidio is dramatically cheaper and easier to set up — at the cost of some advanced features.

Who it's for: Shopify, WooCommerce, and BigCommerce store owners getting 50-500 support tickets/week who want AI deflection without $500+/mo enterprise tools.

Key features

🤖Lyro AI agent

Conversational AI that learns from your help docs, products, and past tickets in 2 minutes. Resolves up to 70% of common questions (order status, shipping, returns) without a human. Only answers what it knows — gracefully hands off to humans when unsure.

💬Live chat + email

Full live chat with visitor list, typing preview, and shortcuts. Email integration. Mobile apps for iOS/Android so you can respond on the go.

🛒Shopify/WooCommerce deep integration

Pre-built flows: cart abandonment, order tracking, return requests. Pulls order data, customer history, and product info automatically. The Shopify app installs in 60 seconds.

💰Affordable pricing

$29/mo Starter is dramatically cheaper than Intercom Fin ($0.99/resolution) or Decagon ($3K+/mo). For SMBs doing $1-10M/year GMV, Tidio is the right price tier.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Setup is genuinely 5 minutes — connect Shopify, train Lyro on your FAQ, ship
  • Lyro resolves 50-70% of common questions — measurable ticket deflection
  • Free tier is useful (20 Lyro conversations/month, unlimited live chat)
  • Affordable for small businesses — $29-89/mo vs. $500+/mo for enterprise
  • Native ecommerce integrations beat generic chat tools

✗ What doesn't

  • Lyro conversation caps on lower tiers (20-100/month) — you'll outgrow the Starter plan
  • Limited to English, Spanish, Portuguese, French (Intercom Fin handles 50+ languages)
  • No voice — Tidio is text-only (no phone/SMS AI agent)
  • Reporting is basic — Decagon and Intercom win on analytics
  • Not HIPAA compliant — don't use for healthcare

Verdict

For Shopify/WooCommerce stores getting 50-500 tickets/week, Tidio is the best price-to-performance AI support tool of 2026. Lyro pays for itself in the first week if you're paying even one part-time support person. For larger ecommerce ($50M+ GMV) or complex B2B support, evaluate Intercom Fin, Decagon, or Ada — they offer deeper customization at higher prices.
