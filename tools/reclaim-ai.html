The calendar that schedules itself

Reclaim (by Dropbox) is the AI calendar assistant that took off in 2024-2026 by solving the one problem every productivity app ignores: actually finding time. Drop in tasks, habits, and meetings, and Reclaim defends your focus blocks and shuffles the rest automatically.

Who it's for: Knowledge workers, founders, and managers drowning in meetings. Best for anyone whose calendar fills up before their real work gets a slot.

Key features

Smart scheduling Auto-time-blocking

Add tasks with due dates; Reclaim finds the optimal open slot and guards it.

Habit defense Protected focus

Recurring deep-work and habit blocks that meetings cannot overwrite.

Priority engine Graceful reshuffling

When the calendar gets full, low-priority work moves; high-priority stays put.

Slack & Zoom sync Cross-tool scheduling

Auto-schedules standups, 1:1s, and buffers pulled from your connected tools.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Genuinely saves hours every week
  • Excellent for protecting focus time
  • Tight Google Calendar integration
  • Useful free personal tier

✗ What doesn't

  • Google Calendar only (no Outlook on free)
  • Team features add up in cost
  • Occasional surprise reshuffles

Verdict

If your calendar runs your life, Reclaim gives you some of it back. The $8/mo Starter plan is the cheapest therapy for meeting overload we've tested.
