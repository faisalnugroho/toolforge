The only AI built for novelists, not marketers

Sudowrite is the rare AI writing tool that doesn't try to write ad copy. It was built by and for fiction writers — novelists, screenwriters, short story authors. It understands plot beats, POV consistency, sensory detail, dialogue rhythm. Hit 'Write' and it continues your scene in your voice. Hit 'Rewrite' and it suggests 5 alternatives in different tones. Hit 'Brainstorm' and it generates plot twists like a workshop partner who never sleeps.

Who it's for: Novelists, screenwriters, fanfiction writers, NaNoWriMo participants, and any writer working on long-form fiction. Less useful for blog posts or technical writing.

Key features

Write Scene continuation

Highlight the last paragraph, click Write, get 100-1000 words in your voice, plot, and POV. Not a generic chatbot — it reads your manuscript.

Rewrite Tone-shifted alternatives

Pick any sentence, get 5 rewrites in different tones: more descriptive, more tense, more lyrical, more dialogue-heavy, more internal-monologue.

Brainstorm Plot doctor on call

Stuck on chapter 12? Brainstorm gives you 10 plot directions, character motivation maps, twist ideas, and pacing options.

Story Bible Long-form consistency

Tracks your characters, locations, plot threads across a 100K-word novel. Catches continuity errors before your editor does.
