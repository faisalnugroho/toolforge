The AI built for fiction writers

Sudowrite is what happens when AI writing tools are designed by novelists, for novelists. Where Jasper and Copy.ai focus on marketing copy, Sudowrite focuses on fiction — novels, screenplays, short stories. The 'Story Bible' maintains character and plot consistency across 100,000-word manuscripts. The 'Rewrite' tool reworks prose with specific tone control. The 'Describe' tool suggests sensory details. Used by 100,000+ writers, including several published authors who credit it with helping them finish drafts.

Who it's for: Novelists, screenwriters, and long-form fiction writers. Less useful for short marketing copy or business writing where Jasper or Copy.ai shine.

Key features

📖 Story Bible

Maintain character arcs, plot points, world-building details, and writing style across 100,000+ word manuscripts. The AI never loses track of who killed the butler in chapter 5.

✍️ Rewrite

Select any paragraph. Rewrite it in a different tone, POV, or style. 'Make this more noir,' 'switch to first person,' 'add more tension.' The AI understands craft, not just synonyms.

🎨 Describe

Highlight a noun. Get sensory-rich alternatives. 'The door' becomes 'The warped oak door with the brass handle worn smooth by decades of use.' Built for show-don't-tell.

🌳 Tree & Canvas

Visualize your story structure. Branch from any chapter, explore alternate plot directions, or just see your manuscript's pacing at a glance.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Purpose-built for fiction — features that ChatGPT simply doesn't have
  • Story Bible maintains consistency across 100K+ word manuscripts
  • Built by working novelists who understand the craft
  • Used by published authors — credible, not gimmicky
  • Free trial is generous (10,000 words is enough to write a short story)

✗ What doesn't

  • Niche product — useless if you don't write fiction
  • AI output is starting point, not finished prose — requires editing
  • More expensive than ChatGPT Plus for the same word count
  • No team collaboration features (single-author focused)

Verdict

Essential for any fiction writer. If you're writing a novel, short story, or screenplay, Sudowrite will save you dozens of hours and produce genuinely useful creative suggestions. For business or marketing writing, Jasper or Copy.ai are better picks.
