What Supermaven does best

The fastest AI code completion tool. Built by the team behind Tabnine, acquired by Cursor in 2024. Sub-100ms autocomplete that handles 1M token contexts.

Who it's for: Software developers who want sub-100ms AI autocomplete in any IDE, especially those using VSCode, JetBrains, or Neovim

Key features

⚡ Speed Sub-100ms completions

The fastest AI autocomplete on the market. Cursor's autocomplete was built on Supermaven's tech. Feels like magic compared to Copilot's 200-400ms latency.

📚 1M context Massive context window

Babble LLM handles 1M token contexts. Your entire codebase fits in the model's memory — it understands patterns across files better than Copilot.

🔌 Multi-IDE Works everywhere

VSCode, JetBrains (IntelliJ, PyCharm, WebStorm), Neovim, Zed, Emacs. Pick your favorite editor, get the same AI.

💰 Free tier 2,000 completions/month

Generous free tier for hobbyists and side projects. Pro is $10/mo for unlimited completions, half the price of Copilot.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Fastest AI autocomplete in 2026 — measurably faster than Copilot
  • 1M token context window handles large codebases
  • $10/mo Pro is half the price of Copilot Pro
  • Works in VSCode, JetBrains, Neovim, Zed
