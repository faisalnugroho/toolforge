What Komo does best

the AI search engine that took the Perplexity playbook and stripped out everything annoying: no ads, no SEO spam in the sources, no 'related searches' carousel hijacking your screen. Just type, get a cited answer, and click through to sources if you want to dig deeper. The interface is the fastest in the AI search space — answers start streaming in under a second.

Who it's for: Researchers, students, and anyone who uses Perplexity or ChatGPT search but hates the clutter. Especially good for quick factual questions where you need sources but not a 10-paragraph essay.

Key features

⚡ Sub-second answers

Streams responses in 800ms-1.2s. Fastest AI search engine we've tested.

📚 Source citations

Every claim links to its source. Click through to verify — no hallucinated references.

🛡️ Ad-free by design

No ads, no 'sponsored results', no SEO gaming. Komo makes money on premium, not attention.

💬 Three modes

Search (quick answer), Research (deep dive), Chat (conversational follow-ups). One toggle.

🔒 Private by default

No tracking cookies, no query history stored, no account required to use.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Fastest AI search we've used (slightly faster than Perplexity)
  • Actually ad-free (Perplexity now shows ads in some answers)
  • Clean, minimal UI — no clutter or upsells
  • Free tier is full-featured (no artificial limits)
  • Privacy-first: no account needed, no tracking

✗ What doesn't

  • Smaller index than Perplexity or Google — niche queries can miss
  • No image or video generation (search only)
  • Fewer 'deep research' features than Perplexity Pro
  • No mobile app yet (mobile web only)
  • Smaller company — long-term viability uncertain

Verdict

the AI search engine for people who want Perplexity's quality without the ad-pocalypse or $20/month price tag. For quick factual questions (what's the capital of X, who won Y, when did Z happen), it's now our default over Google. For deep research tasks, Perplexity Pro still wins.
