The video model that shocked the world

Kling AI, from Chinese lab Kuaishou, stunned the industry with its realistic physics, coherent motion, and long-generation capabilities. The 1.6 generation improved prompt adherence, faces, and camera control. Creators use it for cinematic shots, product videos, and effects that rival far more expensive pipelines. It offers both a web app and an API.

Who it's for: Filmmakers, marketers, and creators who need high-fidelity AI video without a render farm.
