Stock photos that don't look like stock photos

Lummi combines a huge library of AI-generated, commercially-usable images with a prompt-based generator and a lightweight editor. No more spending $30 on a generic hero image — describe it, generate it, download it, done. Everything is free for commercial use.

Who it's for: Designers, marketers, and founders who need on-brand imagery fast and don't want to dance around stock licensing.

Key features

AI generate Prompt to image

Describe the shot and get multiple options in seconds, royalty-free.

Curated library Hand-picked

Browse a growing set of community and AI images, all free to use.

Editor In-browser

Recolor, upscale, and tweak without leaving Lummi.

Plugins Figma & more

Drop Lummi assets straight into your design tools.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • 100% free, commercial-friendly license
  • Fast generation with good prompt adherence
  • No licensing headaches
  • Decent built-in editor
  • Figma integration speeds design work

✗ What doesn't

  • Smaller library than Unsplash/Pexels for real photos
  • AI artifacts appear on close inspection
  • Style consistency needs careful prompting
  • Export options are basic

Verdict

Lummi is the free image workhorse for 2026 — generate a hero shot in 30 seconds, no budget needed. Keep Midjourney or Krea for hero art that needs to be perfect.
