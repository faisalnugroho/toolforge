Long video in, shorts out

Klap analyzes a long-form video, detects the most engaging moments, then auto-cuts, reframes (face tracking), and captions them for TikTok, Reels, and Shorts. It is the repurposing layer that turns one upload into a week of short-form content.

Who it's for: YouTubers, podcasters, and marketers who want a short-form presence without a video editor.

Key features

Auto clip selection

AI scores moments by likely engagement and cuts them.

Smart reframing

Face-centered framing keeps subjects in frame on vertical video.

Animated captions

On-trend caption styles that boost retention.

One-click export

Push to TikTok, Reels, and Shorts-ready formats.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Turns 1 video into 10+ shorts fast
  • Reframing saves manual keyframing
  • Captions are genuinely good
  • No editing skills required

✗ What doesn't

  • Picks aren't always the best moments
  • Heavy editing still needs CapCut
  • Monthly credit caps
  • Brand-heavy videos need cleanup

Verdict

Klap is the cheapest leverage you can buy for a short-form strategy in 2026. Pair it with Opus Clip if you need finer control, but for most creators Klap is enough.
