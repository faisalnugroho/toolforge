Your whole Microsoft suite, with an AI in it

Microsoft Copilot sits inside Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook, and Teams. Ask it to draft a memo, build a pivot table from a prompt, or recap a meeting, and it acts on your actual files.

Who it's for: Enterprises and SMBs standardized on Microsoft 365 who want AI without adopting a new tool.

Key features

Word Draft and rewrite

Generate first drafts and rewrite in your brand voice.

Excel Prompt-driven analysis

Build formulas, charts, and insights from plain English.

PowerPoint One-prompt decks

Turn a doc or topic into a full slide deck.

Teams Meeting recaps

Live takeaways, action items, and chat answers.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Native in tools people already use
  • Enterprise security
  • Strong Excel help
  • Recap in Teams
  • Free web tier

✗ What doesn't

  • $20/mo per seat adds up
  • Needs M365 license for full value
  • Quality varies by app
  • Data residency questions for some orgs

Verdict

For organizations already on Microsoft 365, Copilot is the path of least resistance to AI. The $20/mo Pro plan is worth it the first time it builds a deck from a Word doc. Just budget per seat.
