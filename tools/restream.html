Multistreaming without the OBS headache

Restream is the multistreaming standard for creators and companies in 2026. One camera, one scene, thirty destinations — plus a browser-based studio that replaces OBS for most streamers. If you're streaming to a single platform, you're leaving half your audience on the table.

Who it's for: Creators, educators, and brands who want maximum reach. Perfect when you'd rather not wrangle OBS or a capture rig.

Key features

Multistream 30+ destinations

Stream to Twitch, YouTube, LinkedIn, and X simultaneously from a single source — no extra hardware.

Studio Browser overlay editor

Add logos, lower-thirds, screens, and guests in a built-in editor. No OBS download required.

AI Script Generated scripts

Draft stream scripts, titles, and talking points from a prompt before you go live.

Analytics Unified chat + stats

One chat window across every platform plus viewer analytics in a single dashboard.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Saves enormous time versus per-platform streaming
  • Free tier actually works (2 channels)
  • No OBS needed for most use cases
  • Clean unified chat

✗ What doesn't

  • Higher tiers get pricey for teams
  • Minor latency on some platforms
  • Studio has a learning curve

Verdict

For any creator serious about reach, Restream is the default. The $16/mo Standard plan is the point where multistreaming stops being a hassle and starts being automatic.
