Lindy is the no-code AI agent builder from the team behind Python productivity tools. You describe what you want an agent to do in plain English ('Triage my inbox, schedule meetings with VIPs, and follow up on unanswered emails after 3 days'), and Lindy builds the agent, connects it to your Gmail/Calendar/Slack, and runs it autonomously. Think Zapier + GPT-4 in a single tool, but smarter.

Who it's for: Founders, EAs, sales teams, and anyone drowning in email/calendar admin. Anyone who tried Zapier and found it too rigid. Teams that want an AI assistant that can actually take actions (not just chat).

Key features

Build Plain English agent builder

Describe what you want in natural language. Lindy's agent builder converts it to a multi-step workflow with conditions, branching, and integrations. No code, no flowchart tool.

Connect 300+ integrations

Gmail, Outlook, Google Calendar, Slack, Notion, HubSpot, Salesforce, Linear, Airtable, plus webhooks for everything else. The integrations work — most competitors break on real auth flows.

Run Autonomous scheduling

Lindy agents run 24/7 in the background. They read incoming emails, decide what's a meeting request, check your calendar, propose times, and book — all without you.

Team Multi-agent teams

Lindy Trios let you chain multiple agents (e.g., 'inbox triage agent' → 'draft response agent' → 'review agent') with a human in the loop where you want one.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Genuinely no-code — natural language agent builder works
  • Real Gmail/Calendar integration (not just API stubs)
  • Agents actually run autonomously and take actions
  • Cheaper than hiring an EA for basic triage work
  • Free tier lets you test with 40 tasks/month

✗ What doesn't

  • Free tier is too limited for serious use (40 tasks/mo runs out fast)
  • Pro tier is $49/mo — same price as ChatGPT Team
  • Agents can hallucinate actions (still need human oversight for important tasks)
  • Steeper learning curve than ChatGPT for non-technical users
  • Some integrations are read-only (limited write access to certain apps)

Verdict

Lindy is the best no-code agent builder in 2026 if you want AI that actually does things, not just chats. For $49/mo you get an EA-grade inbox assistant. The free tier is enough to try, but you'll need Pro to get real value. Worth it for any founder or EA drowning in admin.
