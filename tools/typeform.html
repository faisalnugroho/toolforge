What Typeform actually does

Typeform turned boring form-filling into a conversation. One question per screen, full-bleed images, beautiful typography. The result: completion rates 2-3x higher than Google Forms. The 2026 version adds AI form generation (describe your form, get a finished draft), smart conditional logic, and native video responses.

Who it's for: Marketers, designers, recruiters, and product teams who care about completion rates and brand experience.

Key features

💬 One-question-at-a-time UX

The signature Typeform flow. Reduces friction, increases completion, and feels like a conversation.

🪄 AI form generator

Type "customer feedback survey for a SaaS product" and get a 12-question form ready in 30 seconds.

🎬 Video & audio responses

Ask open-ended questions, get back video. Used by researchers, recruiters, and customer success teams.

🔗 700+ integrations

Zapier, HubSpot, Salesforce, Slack, Google Sheets, Notion — connect to anything in two clicks.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • The most beautiful form UX on the market
  • AI form generation is genuinely time-saving
  • Logic jumps are more powerful than Google Forms
  • Video responses are unique to the space
  • Native video calls and surveys in one tool

✗ What doesn't

  • Free tier is brutal (10 responses/month)
  • $25/mo for the basic paid plan feels steep
  • No offline mode
  • Reporting is weaker than SurveyMonkey for research

Verdict

If completion rate matters more than cost, Typeform wins. For lead-gen, customer feedback, and brand-sensitive forms, the 2-3x completion lift pays for the $25/mo within a week. For internal forms or surveys with 100+ responses/month, Google Forms is more practical.
