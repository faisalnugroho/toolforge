Infographics and reports made stupid-easy. Templates for data stories that don't need a designer.

Venngage has focused relentlessly on one thing since 2012: making infographics anyone can build. While competitors sprawled into video and full design suites, Venngage doubled down on data visualization — process diagrams, statistical infographics, and reports. In 2026 it's the quiet favorite of comms teams and nonprofits who need to make numbers legible.

Who it's for: Businesses, nonprofits, and educators who need to communicate data without a designer. Annual reports, process diagrams, infographics, and flyers. The 'I have a spreadsheet and need it to look like a story' use case.

Key features

Infographics Infographic templates

The deepest infographic template library in the category — process, comparison, statistical, timeline, and geographic. Start from a proven layout, drop in your data.

Charts Smart charts

Build charts from a spreadsheet import. Venngage auto-suggests the right chart type for your data — bar, pie, radar, or map.

Reports Reports & docs

Turn dry reports into branded, visual documents. Annual reports, proposals, and one-pagers that stakeholders actually read.

Brand Brand kit

Set brand colors, fonts, and logos so every visual matches. Team templates keep everyone consistent.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • The best infographic-specific template library (process, statistical, geographic)
  • Smart charts guide you to the right visualization for your data
  • Cheaper than Visme or Canva Pro for pure infographic work ($19/mo)
  • Non-designers get professional results fast
  • Strong for reports and proposals, not just social graphics

✗ What doesn't

  • Narrower than Canva — weak for social posts and video
  • Free tier is watermarked and limited
  • Less interactive than Visme (mostly static output)
  • Heavy custom illustration isn't its strength

Verdict

Venngage is the infographic tool. If your job is turning data and processes into visuals — reports, diagrams, annual summaries — it beats Canva on templates and beats Visme on price. For general social and marketing design, Canva is the better all-rounder. Pick Venngage when the deliverable is a data story.
