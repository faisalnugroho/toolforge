The most generous free tier in AI video

Haiper is the AI video generator from the ex-Stability AI team (the same crew behind Stable Video Diffusion). It launched in 2024 and quietly became the best free AI video tool in 2026 — 10 free 4-second videos per day is more than Runway, Pika, and Kling combined.

Who it's for: Casual creators who want to try AI video without a credit card. Hobbyists making TikTok content. Anyone who wants a runway alternative that doesn't charge per generation.

Key features

Text Text-to-video

Type a prompt, get a 4-second video clip. Up to 1080p output on the Pro plan. Solid prompt adherence — handles motion, camera movement, and lighting cues reasonably well.

Image Image-to-video

Upload a still image and Haiper animates it. Great for bringing product photos and concept art to life. The image-to-video pipeline is faster than Sora's.

Repaint Video repaint

Change the style of an existing video clip — turn daytime footage into nighttime, pencil sketch into oil painting. This is Haiper's unique superpower that nobody else has matched well.

Free Generous free tier

10 free 4-second videos per day at 720p. Way more than competitors like Runway or Pika give away. Enough for casual content creators who don't want to pay.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Best free tier in AI video — 10/day is more than Runway, Pika, and Kling combined
  • Built by ex-Stability AI team — solid foundation
  • Repaint feature is unique and powerful

✗ What doesn't

  • 4-second max clip length on free tier
  • Less photorealistic than Sora or Veo 3
  • Smaller community than Runway

Verdict

If you want to try AI video without paying, Haiper's free tier is the best in 2026. 10 videos a day is enough for a week of TikTok content. Upgrade to Pro when you need longer clips or higher resolution. Power users should still pay for Sora or Runway for serious work.
