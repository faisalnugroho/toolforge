Key features

KWFinder KWFinder keyword research

Best UX in the keyword research category. 500+ suggestions, difficulty score, SERP overview, all in a clean UI.

SERPChecker SERP analysis

See exactly who ranks for a keyword — DR, backlinks, traffic, on-page signals. Local SEO support in 50K+ locations.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Cheapest serious SEO toolkit on the market — $29/mo gets you everything

  • Free browser extension with SiteProfiler — useful even without paying
