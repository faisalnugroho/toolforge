Photoshop-level results without Photoshop

Luminar Neo (by Skylum) is the AI photo editor that made "just fix it" a real workflow in 2024-2026. Sky replacement, relighting, portrait retouching, and one-click Looks — all driven by AI that reads your actual photo instead of applying a flat filter.

Who it's for: Photographers, ecommerce sellers, and marketers who need great images fast. Ideal when Photoshop is overkill or too slow.

Key features

Sky AI One-click skies

Replace skies realistically; the scene relights to match the new light.
