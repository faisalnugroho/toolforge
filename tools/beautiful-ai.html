The "I don't know design" presentation tool

Beautiful.ai solves the problem every non-designer has: you need a deck tomorrow, you have content, but you have no idea how to make it look professional. Drop in your text and it auto-applies smart layouts, proper spacing, color harmony, and visual hierarchy. Add a bullet point and the surrounding elements re-flow like magic.

Who it's for: Consultants, sales reps, marketers, and startup founders who need pitch decks, status updates, and proposals that look great without a design team. Less ideal for creative decks where you want full layout control.

Key features

Smart Auto-layout slides

The killer feature. Add a third bullet and the slide rearranges itself. Add a chart and the title, legend, and source text snap into proper hierarchy. No manual alignment, ever.

Dall-E AI image generation

Type "futuristic office with plants" and Beautiful generates 4 options via DALL-E 3. Edit them with built-in tools. No more hunting Unsplash for hours.

Brand Brand kits & themes

Set your company colors, fonts, and logo once. Every deck inherits them. The Team plan syncs brand kits across the org — no more off-brand slides from new hires.

Export PowerPoint export

Export to PPTX without losing layout (mostly). Animations convert. Charts stay editable. Important for clients still on PowerPoint — and yes, that's still most of them.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Best-in-class auto-layout — every slide looks designed
  • Real PPTX export that doesn't fall apart in PowerPoint
  • Team plan with shared brand kits is excellent
  • Designer-quality templates out of the box
  • Live collaboration works smoothly (Google Docs-style)
  • Reasonable $12/mo for the Pro tier

✗ What doesn't

  • Less generative than Gamma — Beautiful optimizes layout, Gamma writes the whole deck
  • Limited free tier — 3 decks then paywall
  • Custom layouts are clunky — you're fighting the "smart" system
  • No offline mode
  • Animation options are sparse compared to PowerPoint
  • Mobile editor is read-only

Verdict

If you make 5+ presentations a year and want them to look professional without learning design, Beautiful.ai is the safest bet. For one-off pitch decks or exploratory brainstorming, try Gamma first (it's more generative). For ongoing internal reporting where brand consistency matters, Beautiful wins on discipline.
