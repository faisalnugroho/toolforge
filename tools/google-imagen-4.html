The image model that finally gets text right

Google Imagen 4 is the search giant's answer to Midjourney and DALL-E, and it does something neither does well: it renders text inside images correctly. Signs, posters, book covers, and UI mockups come out readable, not garbled. Combined with photorealistic detail and strong prompt understanding, Imagen 4 is the best model for marketing assets, social media graphics, and any image that needs legible words.

Who it's for: Marketers, social media managers, and designers who need images with readable text, product mockups, or photorealistic marketing assets.

Key features

📝 Best-in-class text rendering

Signs, posters, logos, and UI elements render with correct spelling. No more gibberish text in your AI images.

📸 Photorealistic detail

Skin, fabric, and metal textures look real. Lighting and shadows are physically plausible.

🎨 Style flexibility

Photorealistic, illustration, 3D render, watercolor, and more. Prompting for style is intuitive.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Unmatched text rendering inside images
  • Photorealistic quality competitive with Midjourney v6
  • Accessible via Gemini Advanced subscription

Google Imagen 4 is the image model to use when your image needs words. Marketing assets, social posts, mockups, and presentations all benefit from its text rendering. For pure art, Midjourney still wins on aesthetics, but for practical business imagery, Imagen 4 is the most useful model available.
