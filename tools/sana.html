The unified AI layer for enterprise knowledge work

Sana is the AI platform that combines three things that used to be separate products: enterprise search (find anything across your SaaS apps), AI-powered learning (onboarding, training, courses), and workflow automation (agents that complete multi-step tasks). Used by Merck, Bank of America, and 200+ enterprises. It's positioned as the 'AI knowledge layer' — sits on top of your existing Notion, Slack, Drive, Salesforce stack and makes it all searchable and actionable.

Who it's for: Mid-to-large enterprise knowledge workers, L&D teams, and IT departments who need a single AI platform that works across all their SaaS apps and complies with SOC 2/HIPAA/GDPR.

Key features

Search Unified enterprise search

Search across Notion, Slack, Google Drive, Salesforce, Confluence, GitHub, Linear, 100+ apps. Permissions inherited. The 'Google for your company' that actually respects access controls.

Agents Workflow automation agents

Build AI agents that complete multi-step tasks: 'Onboard a new hire' triggers account creation, doc access, training enrollment, and IT tickets. No code, runs in 1-2 hours.

Learning AI-powered learning platform

Generate courses from your docs, video, and meeting recordings. Auto-translate to 50+ languages. Track skill gaps, recommend learning paths. The L&D team's new best friend.

Compliance SOC 2, HIPAA, GDPR, on-prem

Enterprise-grade security from day 1. SOC 2 Type II, HIPAA, GDPR, ISO 27001, optional on-prem deployment. The only AI platform legal and IT will approve without a 6-month review.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Unified search across 100+ apps actually works — not a marketing claim
  • AI agents are more capable than typical RPA — handle ambiguity well
  • Learning platform is a real differentiator (Glean doesn't do this)
  • Enterprise security and compliance are best-in-class
  • Customers report 30-50% reduction in 'where do I find X' tickets

✗ What doesn't

  • Enterprise-only — no SMB tier, no self-serve
  • Pricing is opaque (~$15K+/yr) and requires sales calls
  • Onboarding takes 2-4 weeks for a typical 500-person company
  • Not as deep on search as specialized tools like Glean (by design)
  • Some connectors still in beta (especially niche SaaS apps)

Verdict

Sana is the 'just works' AI platform for enterprises that don't want to buy 5 different point solutions. If you're a 500+ person company drowning in SaaS apps and your employees can't find anything, Sana is the right platform. If you're a startup under 100 people, use Glean's free tier or Notion AI — Sana is overkill.
