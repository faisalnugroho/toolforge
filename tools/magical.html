TextExpander + AI + scraper, in one Chrome extension

Magical is the productivity tool everyone who tries it falls in love with. Type /intro in any text field, get your full intro email. Type /followup in a CRM, get a contextual follow-up draft. The killer feature is Autofill from Anywhere: Magical scrapes data from a source (a website, a spreadsheet, a CRM record) and pastes it into a target form. It works in every web app — Salesforce, HubSpot, LinkedIn, Gmail, anywhere. No API integrations needed.

Who it's for: Recruiters, SDRs, support agents, ops folks, and anyone who copies data between 5 tabs 50 times a day. Free tier is genuinely useful, not a crippled demo.

Key features

/shortcuts Text expansion

The TextExpander basics: /sig → signature, /intro → intro email. Magical adds variables (date, name from a CRM record) and conditional logic. Unlimited on free.

Autofill Cross-app data transfer

The killer. Open a LinkedIn profile, click "transfer to Salesforce" — Magical reads the page and fills every Salesforce field. No Zapier, no API, no engineering. 7+ hours saved per week per user.

AI Drafts Contextual messages

Pull from a CRM record or LinkedIn profile, generate a personalized message. Better than copy-paste with variables — Magical writes the whole thing.

Sharing Team snippet library

Share your best snippets with the team. New hires get the full playbook on day 1. Standardize messaging without losing personalization. Built-in analytics on usage.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Free tier is the most generous in the category — actually useful, not a 7-day trial
  • Autofill feature is unique — no competitor does cross-app data transfer this cleanly
  • Works in every web app — no API keys, no Zaps, no engineering
  • AI drafts use context (CRM record, page content) — not just generic templates
  • Team sharing makes onboarding 10x faster for SDRs and recruiters

✗ What doesn't

  • Browser-only — no desktop app for non-browser tasks (yet)
  • Autofill can break when sites change their DOM — needs occasional "remapping"
  • AI drafts are good but not great — still need human editing for high-stakes messages
  • Free tier caps at 100 AI messages/mo — heavy users need $13/mo Pro
  • Chrome-first, Safari/Firefox support lags

Verdict

Magical is the highest-ROI productivity tool in 2026. The free tier alone saves most users 2-3 hours per week. The Autofill feature is genuinely magical (yes, the name fits) — once you've used it to move LinkedIn data to your CRM in 3 seconds, you can't go back. For anyone doing repetitive typing, form-filling, or data entry in a browser, install it today. The 60-second install is the highest-ROI 60 seconds of your week.
