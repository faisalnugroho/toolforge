Why Qwen-Image stands out in 2026

Qwen-Image is Alibaba's flagship text-to-image model. It's notable for rendering legible text inside images and strong prompt adherence — and the weights are open.

Who it's for: Designers and devs who want controllable, text-accurate image generation they can run privately.

Key features

Text Legible text

Renders words and labels inside images far better than most.

Open Weights public

Self-host or use via DashScope.

Control High adherence

Follows complex multi-subject prompts.

Edit In/outpainting

Refine regions without regenerating the whole frame.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Open weights — no per-image fee
  • Excellent in-image text
  • Strong composition
  • Active community

✗ What doesn't
