The time tracker that agencies actually love

Harvest has been around since 2006 — practically ancient in SaaS years. But it survives because it does one thing exceptionally well: tracking time and turning that data into invoices, budgets, and reports. No bloated feature bloat, no "AI project management" buzzwords — just solid, reliable time tracking.

Who it's for: Agencies, consultancies, law firms, and freelance teams that bill by the hour. If you need to track billable time and send professional invoices, this is your tool.

Key features

Timer Time Tracking

Start/stop timer or log time manually. Track by project, task, and client. Timesheet reminders, weekly approvals, and mobile app for on-the-go tracking.

Invoice Billing & Invoicing

Convert tracked hours into professional invoices with one click. Retainer billing, fixed-fee invoicing, expense tracking, and online payments via Stripe/PayPal.

Budget Project Budgets

Set hourly or fee-based budgets per project. Get alerts at 50%, 75%, 90% spent. Visual reports show profitability and team utilization at a glance.

Reports Analytics

Deep reporting on project profitability, team capacity, client revenue, and billable vs. non-billable ratios. Export to CSV or send weekly summaries.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Fast, reliable time tracking — the timer just works
  • Invoicing is seamless — convert time to invoice in 2 clicks
  • Deep integrations with Asana, Jira, Trello, Slack, QuickBooks
  • Beautiful, detailed reports that clients understand
  • Mobile app is excellent — track time from anywhere

✗ What doesn't

  • No built-in project management — you need Asana/Jira alongside it
  • Pricing adds up fast ($11/seat/mo + features)
  • No AI features yet — feels a bit "old school"
  • UI hasn't changed much in years (some see this as a feature)
  • Limited customization for invoice templates

Verdict

Harvest is the gold standard for time tracking and invoicing. It's not trying to be a project management tool or an AI assistant — it's a purpose-built time tracker that does its job flawlessly. If your business bills by the hour, Harvest pays for itself in the first week.
