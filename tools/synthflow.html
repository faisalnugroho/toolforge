The SMB-friendly voice agent platform

Synthflow is the no-code AI voice agent platform built specifically for small and mid-size businesses who want phone automation without hiring developers. Founded in Berlin in 2023, Synthflow grew to 5,000+ customers by 2026 by being the simplest way to deploy an AI receptionist, appointment scheduler, or lead qualifier. The platform comes with pre-built templates for the most common SMB voice use cases (bookings, lead qualification, FAQs, surveys) and ships with bundled Twilio telephony — no separate phone carrier needed.

Who it's for: Local service businesses (dental, legal, real estate, med spa), small agencies, and SMBs handling 50-500 phone calls/month who want AI handling without $5K+/mo enterprise platforms.

Key features

🛠️Visual agent builder

Drag-and-drop agent designer. Pre-built blocks for booking, FAQ, lead qualification, transfer to human, calendar integration. Ship a working agent in an afternoon, not a sprint.

📞Bundled telephony

Twilio integration included. Local number provisioning, call recording, SMS follow-up, international numbers. No need to bring your own carrier — unlike Vapi/Retell.

📅Calendar & CRM integrations

Native Cal.com, Calendly, Google Calendar, HubSpot, Salesforce integrations. Agent books appointments, qualifies leads, logs to CRM — all without Zapier.

🌍Multi-language voices

ElevenLabs-quality voices in 30+ languages out of the box. Clone a custom voice for $5 one-time. No model fine-tuning required.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Lowest barrier to entry — non-developers can ship a voice agent same-day
  • Bundled telephony saves $100+/mo on Twilio setup
  • Pre-built SMB templates (bookings, FAQ) cut setup from weeks to hours
  • $29/mo Starter includes 100 minutes — competitive pricing
  • Good voice quality with ElevenLabs backend

✗ What doesn't

  • Less flexible than Vapi/Retell for complex multi-step workflows
  • Concurrency limits on lower tiers (3-10 concurrent calls)
  • Limited voice library customization vs. Vapi's full voice provider choice
  • Smaller community and ecosystem than Vapi
  • No HIPAA compliance tier yet (2026 roadmap item)

Verdict

For SMBs and local service businesses that want an AI voice agent live in days, not weeks, Synthflow is the easiest on-ramp of 2026. The bundled telephony and pre-built templates remove the two biggest barriers for non-technical buyers. For high-volume enterprise calling (1M+ minutes/month) or developer-led voice AI builds, Vapi or Retell remain stronger. For most SMB voice automation needs, Synthflow is the right choice.
