From topic to course in minutes

Mindsmith is an AI-first course creation platform that generates complete learning modules from a single prompt. Type "Onboarding for new sales hires" and it produces a structured lesson with slides, text, quizzes, and interactive elements. For HR teams, teachers, and course creators, it eliminates the blank-page problem and cuts course development time by 80%+.

Who it's for: HR and L&D teams, educators, and course creators who need to produce training content quickly. Best for corporate training, compliance modules, and structured educational content.

Key features

AI Gen AI lesson generation

Describe your topic, audience, and learning objectives. Mindsmith generates a complete lesson with sections, content, and assessment questions in under 60 seconds.

Quiz Auto-generated quizzes

AI creates quiz questions from lesson content — multiple choice, true/false, and short answer. Edit, add, or remove questions with one click.

SCORM SCORM export

Export courses as SCORM packages for any LMS — Canvas, Moodle, Blackboard, or corporate LMS. Maintains tracking and completion data.

Branch Branching scenarios

Create interactive decision-tree scenarios where learner choices determine the path. Perfect for compliance training and soft skills development.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • AI generates genuinely useful course structure, not just filler
  • Quiz auto-generation saves hours of manual work
  • SCORM export works with any LMS
  • Free tier is enough to test and build a full course
  • Branching scenarios are easy to set up

✗ What doesn't

  • AI-generated content needs human review — not always accurate
  • Limited customization compared to Articulate or Captivate
  • No video generation — text and image only
  • Template variety is still growing
  • Pro plan ($19/mo) needed for team features and unlimited courses

Verdict

Mindsmith is the fastest way to go from "we need training on X" to a publishable course. The AI generation quality is impressive for structured content, and SCORM export means it fits into any existing L&D stack. It won't replace Articulate for polished bespoke courses, but for 80% of corporate training needs, it's the right tool.
