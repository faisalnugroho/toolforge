Video editing that lives in the browser

Kapwing is the collaborative video editor for teams that don't touch Premiere. Cut clips, auto-generate subtitles, remove backgrounds, and clean audio — all in a shared, browser-based workspace your whole team can comment on.

Who it's for: Social media teams, educators, and creators who need fast turnaround and teamwork.

Key features

Subtitles Auto captions

AI transcribes speech and burns in accurate captions — essential for silent-scroll social.

Smart Cut Silence removal

Detect and delete dead air across a long recording automatically.

Clean Audio Noise removal

Strip background hum and echo from rushed recordings.

Collaborate Shared workspace

Comment, assign, and version videos with your team in real time.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Zero install — runs in any browser
  • AI captions are fast and accurate
  • Great for team review workflows
  • Free tier covers casual use

✗ What doesn't

  • Watermark on free exports
  • 4K and some AI tools gated to Pro
  • Not built for long-form/complex edits
  • Render speeds vary with file size

Verdict

Kapwing is the pragmatic choice for social and team video. The free tier is fine for testing; $16/mo Pro removes the watermark and unlocks the AI that actually saves time.
