AI video that lives where you already work

Adobe Firefly Video is Adobe's AI video generation model, integrated directly into Premiere Pro, Express, and the Firefly web app. Generate video clips from text prompts, animate still images, or extend existing footage. The big advantage: it's commercially safe (trained only on licensed content), and it's built into the Creative Cloud workflow that millions of creatives already use.

Who it's for: Video editors, motion designers, and marketing teams already in the Adobe ecosystem who need AI-generated B-roll, clip extensions, or quick motion graphics without worrying about copyright issues.

Key features

Text-to-Video Generate from text

Type a description and Firefly generates a 5-second 1080p video clip. Choose from cinematic, 3D animation, or stock-footage styles. Quality is competitive with Runway Gen-3 for most prompts.

Image-to-Video Animate still images

Upload an image (or use a Firefly-generated one) and add motion — camera pans, zooms, parallax, or subject movement. Great for bringing product photos or illustrations to life.

Generative Extend Extend clips

Short on B-roll? Firefly extends any video clip by up to 2 seconds — seamlessly matching the lighting, motion, and content. Works inside Premiere Pro directly.

Commercial Safe Copyright-safe

Firefly is trained only on Adobe Stock, openly licensed, and public domain content. Every generation is commercially safe — no copyright worries for brands and agencies.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Commercially safe — trained only on licensed content, no copyright risk
  • Seamless Premiere Pro integration — generate and edit in one workflow
  • Generative Extend is genuinely useful for fixing cut clips
  • Text-to-video quality is solid and improving rapidly
  • Cheaper than Runway for Adobe subscribers already paying for Creative Cloud

✗ What doesn't

  • 5-second clips feel short compared to Runway's longer generations
  • No audio generation — you need a separate tool for sound
  • Complex prompts with multiple subjects or actions can produce artifacts
  • Credit system is confusing — heavy users burn through plans fast

Verdict

If you're already on Adobe Creative Cloud, Firefly Video is a no-brainer add-on — it's the safest AI video tool for commercial work. The Premiere Pro integration alone saves hours. For standalone AI video, Runway and Kling still edge ahead on quality and length, but Adobe wins on safety and workflow.
