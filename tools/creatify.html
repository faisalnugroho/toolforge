URL → 10 video ads in 60 seconds

Creatify is purpose-built for one thing: making paid social ads faster. Paste a product URL (or upload a product image), and it scrapes the page, generates 5-10 script variations, picks the best voice, matches an AI avatar, and renders a 15-30 second ad. Each variant tests a different hook or angle. Marketers ship 5x more creative, kill underperformers, scale winners. CPA drops 30-50% in most accounts.

Who it's for: Performance marketers, DTC brands, ecom stores, and agencies running paid social at scale. Not for brand-film-quality content.

Key features

URL Import Scrape → script → video

Paste a Shopify, Amazon, or any product URL. Creatify reads the page, extracts the value prop, and generates scripts in the style of top-performing ads. 60 seconds from URL to first render.

Avatars 750+ AI spokespeople

Pick from 750+ stock avatars (diverse ages, ethnicities, settings) or upload a custom face. Lip-sync to script is solid — better than D-ID, behind Tavus.

A/B Variants Hook testing at scale

Generate 5-10 variants per product, each with a different opening hook, body angle, or CTA. Run them as separate ad sets, let Meta/TikTok pick the winner.

Editor Quick tweaks

Don't love the script? Edit inline. Want to swap the avatar? One click. The editor is built for speed — not Premiere, but 80% of the changes you'd want to make take 30 seconds.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • URL-to-video pipeline is genuinely 10x faster than manual creation
  • A/B variant generation unlocks the "creative volume" play that top ad accounts rely on
  • Avatar library is huge and the lip-sync is good for short-form
  • Built-in optimization hints (which hook to use, ideal length, etc.)
  • Affordable — $19/mo Starter is enough for most solo marketers

✗ What doesn't

  • Output is UGC-style, not premium — fine for paid social, not for TV/YouTube pre-roll
  • Script quality varies — some hooks are gold, some are generic
  • Limited control over visual style — you get what the AI picks
  • No native publishing to Meta/TikTok — download, then upload manually
  • Best for short-form; long-form (60s+) is a weak spot

Verdict

If you run paid social ads and you're not using Creatify (or a similar AI ad tool) in 2026, you're burning budget on creative fatigue. The math is simple: more variants tested = lower CPA. Creatify makes that math work. For brand-quality hero content, you still need a real production. For the 50 UGC-style ads per month you need to keep Meta's algorithm happy, Creatify is the new default.
