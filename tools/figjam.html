Whiteboarding that thinks with you

FigJam is the brainstorming canvas from the makers of Figma. In 2026 its AI turns rough sticky notes into organized boards, generates flowcharts from prompts, and summarizes a messy jam into action items — without leaving the canvas.

Who it's for: Product, design, and remote teams running workshops and retros.

Key features

AI organize Tidy the jam

Cluster scattered stickies into themes automatically.

AI generate From prompt

Describe a diagram or framework; FigJam drafts it on the board.

Jambot ChatGPT on canvas

Spawn a ChatGPT widget anywhere to draft or remix ideas.

Figma sync Design handoff

Push frames and components straight into Figma files.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Free tier is genuinely useful
  • Best-in-class real-time collaboration
  • AI tidy/organize is a real time-saver
  • Tight Figma integration
  • Familiar to design teams

✗ What doesn't

  • AI features gated behind the $3/mo plan
  • Less powerful than Miro for huge boards
  • Needs Figma account ecosystem
  • Not a vector design tool

Verdict

FigJam is the whiteboard to beat for teams already in Figma. The $3/mo AI add-on pays for itself the first time it organizes a 200-sticky retro into coherent themes.
