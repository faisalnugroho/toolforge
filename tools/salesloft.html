The sales engagement platform for orchestrated outbound at scale. Cadences, coaching, and conversation intelligence.

Salesloft pioneered the sales-engagement category back in 2011, turning 'spray and pray' cold outreach into coordinated, measurable cadences. In 2026 it's the platform of record for mid-market and enterprise revenue teams that need cadence automation, conversation intelligence, and manager dashboards in one system. Acquired by Flywheel (formerly Apollo) in 2024, it remains the coaching-and-orchestration leader.

Who it's for: Revenue teams running coordinated, multi-channel outbound at scale. Mid-market and enterprise orgs with dedicated SDR and CSM functions who need cadence automation, manager visibility, and conversation intelligence — not just a dialer.

Key features

Cadence Multi-channel cadences

Orchestrate email, call, SMS, and social touches in one sequence. Reps get a single prioritized to-do list; managers see exactly where every prospect is.

Coaching Conversation intelligence

Call recordings auto-transcribed and scored against playbooks. Managers coach from data, not vibes. Surfaces winning talk tracks across the team.

Rhythm Revenue workflows

Deal monitoring, forecast signals, and 'buyer signals' that flag at-risk or hot accounts. Connects sales activity to pipeline outcomes.

Forecast Analytics & reporting

Team-wide dashboards for activity, conversion, and rep performance. Drill from forecast to the exact email that moved a deal.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • The most mature cadence engine for enterprise outbound (multi-channel, multi-team)
  • Conversation intelligence is genuinely useful for coaching at scale
  • Deep Salesforce and Gong integrations — drops into existing RevOps stacks
  • Manager visibility is excellent — no more 'what did the rep do all week?'
  • Reliable and secure (SOC 2, enterprise SSO) for big orgs

✗ What doesn't

  • Expensive ($125/user/mo+) — overkill for small teams
  • Steeper learning curve than lighter tools like Close or Outreach
  • Pricing isn't public — you'll talk to sales
  • Heavier than needed if you just want a dialer + simple sequences

Verdict

Salesloft is the enterprise sales-engagement standard. If you run a 50+ person SDR org with managers who need visibility and coaching data, it's worth the premium over lighter tools. For a 5-person outbound team, it's overkill — Close or Outreach will do 80% of the job for a third of the price. Match the tool to your team size, not your ambition.
