A whole design team in one tab

Designs.ai bundles several AI generators — Logo Maker, Video Maker, Design Maker, and a Brand Kit tool — into one subscription. Describe your business and it produces a coherent visual identity, social posts, and short videos without opening Photoshop or hiring an agency.

Who it's for: Solopreneurs, small businesses, and marketers who need branded assets fast but don't have a designer on staff.

Key features
