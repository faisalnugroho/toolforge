AI video with real cinematic camera work

Luma's Dream Machine generates 5-second to 2-minute AI videos from text or images. What sets it apart: the camera work actually feels cinematic. Tracking shots, dolly moves, rack focus — it understands cinematography in a way most AI video tools don't.

Who it's for: Filmmakers pre-visualizing scenes, content creators who care about camera work, product videographers, and anyone tired of AI video that looks like a slideshow with morphing.

Key features

🎥 Cinematic camera understanding

Prompt with camera language — "dolly zoom on a crumbling castle" — and Dream Machine delivers. The best camera work in any AI video tool we've tested.

⚡ Fast generation

Generates 5-second clips in under 30 seconds on paid plans. One of the fastest AI video generators available. No waiting in queues.

🖼 Image-to-video

Upload any image — photos, AI art, sketches — and bring it to life. Preserves the style and composition of the original while adding natural motion.

🔗 Extend & chain

Extend any generated clip or chain multiple clips together. Build longer sequences by describing what happens next. Great for storyboarding.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Best camera work of any AI video tool
  • Very fast generation — under 30 seconds
  • Clean, photorealistic output quality
  • Good at maintaining consistency across frames
  • Easy-to-use interface, no learning curve

✗ What doesn't

  • Free tier is limited (30 generations/month)
  • Faces still occasionally glitch at extreme angles
  • No native audio or music generation
  • Max 5 seconds per generation (extend for longer)
  • Less creative/artistic flexibility than Runway

Verdict

Dream Machine is the video tool for people who care about cinematography. If you've ever wished AI video looked more like actual footage and less like a fever dream, this is your answer. Pair it with Suno for audio and you've got a complete AI video production pipeline.
