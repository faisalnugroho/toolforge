Voices that talk back in real time

Rime AI is the voice engine behind a new wave of real-time conversational products. Where most TTS APIs add a noticeable delay, Rime streams lifelike speech with sub-300ms latency — fast enough for natural back-and-forth voice agents. We built a live phone bot with it in an afternoon.

Who it's for: Developers shipping voice agents, AI call centers, and conversational apps where latency and naturalness both matter.

Key features

Streaming Real-time TTS

Token-by-token streaming delivers audio with sub-300ms first-byte latency — natural enough for live phone and voice-agent conversations.

Cloning Custom voices

Clone a brand or real voice from minutes of audio, with consent controls and watermarking for responsible deployment.

Emotion 20+ expressive voices

Voices carry real emotion and prosody — not the robotic monotone of legacy TTS. Anger, excitement, hesitation all land.

API Drop-in REST + WebSocket

One API call to synthesize, streaming WebSocket for real-time. SDKs for Python, Node, and Go get you live fast.
