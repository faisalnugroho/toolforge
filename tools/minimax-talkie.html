The model lab punching above its weight

MiniMax is one of China's leading foundational AI labs, and in 2024-2026 it shipped a remarkably complete stack: a 4-million-token context model (MiniMax-Text-01), Sora-class video (Hailuo), and studio-grade speech (T2A). A free chat assistant sits on top, and the API is aggressively cheap.

Who it's for: Builders who need huge context windows or cheap multimodal inference, and creators curious about Hailuo video. Note the China-based data residency.

Key features

MiniMax-Text-01 4M-token context

Ingest entire codebases or books in one prompt — far beyond typical limits.

Hailuo video Text/image-to-video

Generate Sora-class clips from prompts or stills, free tier included.

T2A-01 audio Voice & cloning

Studio-grade multilingual text-to-speech and voice cloning via API.

Open API Cheap + compatible

Generous free quota, low per-token pricing, OpenAI-compatible endpoints.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Massive 4M-token context
  • Free, high-quality video generation
  • Very cheap API inference
  • Strong multilingual support

✗ What doesn't

  • China-based (data residency concerns)
  • English weaker than US labs
  • Some docs primarily in Chinese

Verdict

For builders who need context or cheap multimodal inference, MiniMax is a genuine frontier alternative — and Hailuo alone is worth the signup. Free to start; pay only when you scale the API.
