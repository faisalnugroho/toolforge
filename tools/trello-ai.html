What it is

Trello AI is the easiest way to add AI to an existing kanban workflow.

Who it's for: Project managers, small teams, kanban users.

Key features

Cards Smart card generation

Type a one-line idea and Trello AI generates a full card with description, due date, assignee, and checklist.

Summary Board summarization

Get a 3-bullet summary of a board, list, or sprint. Perfect for standups and async status reports.

Action Meeting → tasks

Paste a meeting transcript; Trello AI extracts action items, creates cards, and assigns them.

Search Semantic search

Find cards by meaning, not just keywords. "Show me everything about the Q3 launch" actually works.

The honest take

✓ What works

  • Free Trello tier is still very capable
  • AI is opt-in per card — doesn't bloat boards
  • Integrates with Jira, Confluence, Slack
  • Atlassian Intelligence also works in those products
  • Mobile app has full AI features

✗ What doesn't

  • AI is gated behind $5/user/mo Power-Up
  • Smaller automation ecosystem than Asana or ClickUp
  • No native Gantt chart — kanban only
  • Atlassian Cloud-only; no self-host option

Verdict

Trello AI is the easiest way to add AI to an existing kanban workflow. If your team already runs on Trello, the $5/user/mo Power-Up pays for itself in the first sprint. For larger teams needing timeline views and resource management, Asana or ClickUp are better AI investments.
