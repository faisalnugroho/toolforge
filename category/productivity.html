Frequently asked questions

Are AI productivity tools worth the subscription cost?

For busy professionals billing $100+/hr, yes — Motion at $34/mo pays for itself if it saves 30 minutes a day. For casual users, the free tiers of Notion AI and Mem are usually sufficient.

Which is better: Mem or Notion AI?

Mem for personal knowledge management and discovery. Notion AI for team workspaces and structured databases. They serve different purposes — many power users use both.

Can AI calendar tools really plan my day?

Motion does a surprisingly good job. It understands task duration, deadlines, meetings, and priorities. The result isn't perfect — you'll still override sometimes — but it eliminates 30+ minutes of daily planning for most users.
