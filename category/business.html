Frequently asked questions

Is Make better than Zapier?

For complex workflows, yes — Make handles multi-branch logic, error handling, and data transformations that Zapier needs premium tiers for. For simple linear automations, Zapier's UI is friendlier. Make is also significantly cheaper at scale.

Do I need coding knowledge to use these tools?

No — that's the point. Make and Zapier are visual builders. You drag and drop blocks. Basic JSON understanding helps for complex data transformations, but 90% of workflows are buildable by non-developers.

Can AI agents replace business process outsourcing?

For repetitive, rules-based work, yes — and it's already happening. Customer support tier 1, data entry, lead enrichment, and report generation are all automatable. For complex judgment calls and relationship management, humans remain essential.
