Frequently asked questions

Can AI writing tools replace human writers?

No — and that's not their goal. The best AI writers in 2026 are productivity multipliers. They handle first drafts, research, and SEO optimization, freeing human writers to focus on strategy, brand voice, and creative direction. A skilled writer using Jasper or Copy.ai ships 5-10x more content than one working unassisted.

Which AI writer has the best free tier?

Writesonic offers 10,000 free words per month — the most generous free tier in the category. Notion AI includes basic AI features in its free workspace plan (limited to ~20 AI actions per month). Jasper and Copy.ai only offer free trials.

Are AI-written articles bad for SEO?

In 2026, Google's helpful content update specifically targets low-quality AI content. But high-quality AI-assisted content (fact-checked, edited, with human expertise added) ranks just as well as fully human-written content. The key is treating AI as a drafting tool, not a replacement for editorial oversight.
