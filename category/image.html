Frequently asked questions

Which AI image generator is best for commercial use?

Adobe Firefly is the safest for commercial use because it's trained only on Adobe Stock and public domain content. Midjourney and DALL-E are also commercial-safe on paid plans, but check the latest terms. Stable Diffusion requires you to manage the model license yourself.

Can AI image generators do text in images yet?

In 2026, yes — but inconsistently. Midjourney v7 and DALL-E 4 can render readable text in most cases, especially short words. Long paragraphs still fail. For product mockups or book covers with text, plan to do 3-5 generations and pick the best.

Which is better for anime/illustration style?

Midjourney v7 produces the most aesthetic anime/illustration. Leonardo.AI with a fine-tuned model can match it. NovelAI is a specialist option for anime specifically. Stable Diffusion with custom checkpoints (e.g., Anything V5) is the power-user choice.
