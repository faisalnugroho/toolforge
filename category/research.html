Frequently asked questions

Is Perplexity better than Google?

For finding specific answers, yes — Perplexity synthesizes a direct response with citations. For browsing the latest news, discovering new websites, or finding local businesses, Google is still better. Use Perplexity as a research accelerator, not a complete Google replacement.

Can AI research tools find misinformation?

They try — Perplexity cites sources so you can verify. Consensus only shows peer-reviewed studies. But AI can still hallucinate or cite low-quality sources. Always check the cited source. AI research tools are starting points, not final answers.

What's the best free AI research tool?

Perplexity's free tier (unlimited basic searches) and Consensus' free tier (20 searches/month) are the best. For academic research, Consensus is essential. For general knowledge, Perplexity free is good enough for most queries.
