Frequently asked questions

How long can AI video generators make clips?

Runway Gen-4 does 10 seconds, Pika 2.0 does 3-4 seconds, Sora promises 20+ seconds. For longer videos, you chain clips together in an editor. The "seamless" stitching is improving but still requires manual review.

Can AI video replace real video production?

For social content, yes. For product demos, mostly. For cinematic/narrative content, no — not yet. AI video is a tool in the production pipeline, not a full replacement. Use it for B-roll, transitions, and stylized shots; use real cameras for primary footage.

Are AI-generated videos detectable?

Increasingly hard. Watermarking standards like C2PA are being adopted by major generators. Some detectors exist (Hive, Sensity) but are 60-80% accurate. For commercial use, always disclose AI involvement where required by law or platform terms.
