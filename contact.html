FAQ

Do you accept paid reviews?

No. Never. We don't accept payment for reviews, rankings, or inclusion on the site. Affiliate links (where we may earn a commission if you sign up) are clearly disclosed, but they don't influence our editorial coverage.

How long does it take to review a submitted tool?

30 days minimum — we won't review a tool before we've used it for at least a month. If accepted, expect a 4-8 week turnaround from submission to published review.

Can I suggest a tool that's not on the list?

Yes! Use the form or email us. We add 3-5 new tools every month. If your suggestion is good, it might be in next month's update.

I work at a tool company. Can we partner?

We work with select partners for sponsored newsletter features, but never for review content. Email us with "Partnership" in the subject line and we'll send our media kit.
